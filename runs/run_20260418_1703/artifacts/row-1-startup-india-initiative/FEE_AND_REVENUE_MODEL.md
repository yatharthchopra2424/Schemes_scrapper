## Pricing Principles
Our pricing principles are designed to ensure that our services are accessible to all eligible startups under the Startup India Initiative. We consider the risk profile of this scheme, which involves providing recognition and tax benefits to startups, and the processing time for applications. The typical processing time for startup recognition is 2-7 business days, and for tax exemptions, it may take longer. We will work closely with our clients to ensure that they receive the benefits they are eligible for under the scheme.
