# Pitch & Sales Scripts
## Philosophy
We are advisors, not salespeople. We diagnose first. We only pitch a scheme if it fits the client. We never oversell success rates.

## 1. Discovery Call Script (First Contact — 20 min)
### Opening (2 min)
Hello, I'm [TO BE FILLED BY CONSULTANT], and I'm excited to learn more about your startup and see if the Startup India Initiative can help you achieve your goals. The Startup India Initiative is designed to support startups in India, and I'd love to explore how it can benefit your business.

### Discovery Questions (6-8 questions tailored to this scheme's eligibility criteria)
1. What is your startup's incorporation date, and how long have you been in operation?
2. What is your startup's annual turnover, and do you expect it to exceed INR 100 Cr in the near future?
3. Can you tell me about your innovative products, services, or processes that have the potential to generate employment and create wealth?
4. Are you working on a scalable business model with high potential for growth?
5. Have you received any funding or investments, and if so, from whom?
6. What are your biggest challenges as a startup, and how do you think the Startup India Initiative can help address them?

### Qualification Checklist (internal — don't read aloud)
- Is the startup incorporated as a Private Limited Company, LLP, or Registered Partnership?
- Is the startup's annual turnover less than INR 100 Cr?
- Is the startup working towards innovation, improvement, or development of products, services, or processes?
- Does the startup have a scalable business model with high potential for employment generation or wealth creation?

### Transition to Pitch (2-3 lines that bridge from discovery to pitching THIS specific scheme — use the real scheme name and benefit amount)
Based on what you've shared, I think the Startup India Initiative could be a great fit for your startup. This scheme offers benefits like DPIIT recognition, tax exemptions, and funding opportunities up to INR 20 Lakhs from the Startup India Seed Fund Scheme. Let me tell you more about how this scheme can support your startup's growth.

## 2. Scheme Pitch (5 min)
### Structure: Problem → Scheme → Value → Proof → Next Step
**Problem framing:** As a startup, you face challenges like limited access to funding, complex regulatory compliance, and difficulty in scaling your business. The Startup India Initiative addresses these challenges by providing a supportive ecosystem for startups to grow and thrive.

**Scheme intro:** The Startup India Initiative, launched by the Department for Promotion of Industry and Internal Trade (DPIIT), aims to foster a robust startup ecosystem in India. The scheme offers various benefits, including DPIIT recognition, tax exemptions under Section 80-IAC, and funding opportunities through the Fund of Funds for Startups.

**Why they qualify (personalised):** Based on our conversation, I believe your startup meets the eligibility criteria for the Startup India Initiative. You have an innovative product/service, a scalable business model, and the potential to generate employment and create wealth.

**Proof:** We've helped [N] similar businesses get approved for this scheme. [TO BE FILLED BY CONSULTANT]

**Next step:** The next step would be to conduct a free eligibility check to ensure your startup meets the scheme's requirements. If eligible, we can assist you in applying for the scheme and guide you through the process.
