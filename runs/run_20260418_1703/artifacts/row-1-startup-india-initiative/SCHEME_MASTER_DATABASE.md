### Startup India Initiative
- **Scheme ID:** SCH-001
- **Category:** Recognition
- **Geography:** Pan-India
- **Administering Body:** Department for Promotion of Industry and Internal Trade (DPIIT), Ministry of Commerce and Industry
- **Portal / Application URL:** https://www.startupindia.gov.in/
- **Current Status:** Open
- **Window:** Rolling basis — no fixed deadline. Applications are accepted year-round.

#### Eligibility Criteria
- Business type: Private Limited Company, LLP, or Registered Partnership
- Age of business: Less than 10 years from the date of incorporation
- Sector: Any sector
- Revenue cap: Annual turnover should not exceed INR 100 Crores
- Employee count: No specific limit
- Other: The entity should be working towards innovation, improvement, or development of products, services, or processes, and have the potential to generate employment or create wealth.

#### What It Offers
- Amount / Benefit: Up to INR 20 Lakhs from SISFS for early-stage validation, and tax exemptions
- Disbursement mode: Direct credit
- Duration of benefit: 3-year income tax exemption under 80-IAC

#### Documents Required
1. Certificate of Incorporation / Registration
2. PAN of the entity
3. Pitch deck or business description
4. Authorization letter from authorized signatory
5. Details of funding received (if any)

#### Application Process
1. Register on startupindia.gov.in
2. Fill DPIIT recognition form with business description
3. Upload Certificate of Incorporation, PAN, pitch deck
4. Submit and await approval (typically 2-7 business days)
