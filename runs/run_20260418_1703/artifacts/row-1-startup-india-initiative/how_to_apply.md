## Prerequisites
To be eligible for the **Startup India Initiative**, a company must meet the following criteria:
* ✅ Be incorporated as a Private Limited Company, a Registered Partnership Firm, or a Limited Liability Partnership
* ✅ Have an annual turnover of less than Rs. 100 crore
* ✅ Be less than 10 years old from the date of incorporation
* ✅ Work towards innovation, improvement of existing products, services, and processes, and have a scalable business model with high potential for employment generation or wealth creation
* ✅ Not be formed by splitting up or reconstructing an existing business

## Documents Required
The following documents are required for the application process:
1. Memorandum of Association and Articles of Association
2. Certificate of Incorporation
3. PAN Card
4. GSTIN (if applicable)
5. Bank Account details
6. Audited Financial Statements (if applicable)
7. Income Tax Returns (if applicable)
8. Proof of innovation and scalability (e.g., patent filings, prototype development)
9. Business plan and pitch deck
10. Proof of investment (if applicable)

## Step-by-Step Application Process
1. Create an account on the **Startup India** portal
    a. Fill in the registration form with basic details
    b. Verify email address and mobile number
2. Fill in the application form
    a. Provide company details and documents
    b. Answer questions about innovation and scalability
    c. Upload required documents
3. Submit the application
    a. Review and confirm the application
    b. Pay the application fee (if applicable)
4. Wait for verification and approval
    a. The application will be reviewed and verified by the **Startup India** team
    b. Approval may take several days to several weeks

## Timeline & Deadlines
The application process typically takes several weeks to several months. There are no specific deadlines for application, but it is recommended to apply as soon as possible to take advantage of the benefits.

## Common Mistakes to Avoid
1. Incomplete or inaccurate application form
2. Insufficient documentation
3. Lack of clarity on innovation and scalability
4. Not meeting the eligibility criteria
5. Not following up on the application status

## After Submission
After submitting the application, the **Startup India** team will review and verify the documents. If approved, the company will receive a recognition certificate and access to various benefits, including tax exemptions, funding opportunities, and mentorship.
