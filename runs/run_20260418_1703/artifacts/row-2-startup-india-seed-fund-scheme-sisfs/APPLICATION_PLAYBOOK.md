# Application Playbook
## How to Use This File
For each new client case, assign it a Case ID, pick the target scheme, and follow the steps below.

## Stage 1: Eligibility Verification (Day 1–3)
### Checklist
✅ Registration type: Private Limited Company or Registered Partnership Firm or Limited Liability Partnership
✅ Turnover threshold: Not exceeded INR 100 crore
✅ Employee count: No minimum requirement
✅ Sector: Any sector, with preference for social impact, waste management, water management, financial inclusion, education, agriculture, food processing, biotechnology, healthcare, energy, mobility, defense, space, railways, oil and gas, textiles, etc.
✅ Age of business: Incorporated not more than 2 years ago at the time of application
✅ Other conditions:
	* Recognized by DPIIT
	* Business idea to develop a product or service with market fit, viable commercialization, and scope of scaling
	* Using technology in its core product or service, or business model, or distribution model, or methodology to solve the problem being targeted
	* Shareholding by Indian promoters in the startup should be at least 51% at the time of application to incubator for the scheme

## Stage 2: Document Collection (Day 3–10)
### Universal Documents (always required)
* Certificate of Incorporation
* PAN of the entity
* GST registration certificate
* Udyam registration certificate
* Bank statements
* ITR (if applicable)

### Scheme-Specific Documents
* Pitch deck or business description
* Authorization letter from authorized signatory
* Details of funding received (if any)

### Document Quality Check
* File size: Less than 5MB
* Name matching: Ensure all documents have the same name as the startup
* Certification requirements: Self-certified documents are acceptable
* Language: English or Hindi

### Client Communication Template
"Dear [Client], we are applying for the Startup India Seed Fund Scheme. Please find attached the required documents. If you have any questions or concerns, please do not hesitate to reach out to us. We will keep you updated on the progress of the application. Thank you for your cooperation."

## Stage 3: Application Drafting (Day 10–18)
### Project Report / DPR (if required)
Not required for this scheme

### Application Form Filling Protocol
1. Ensure all fields are complete and accurate
2. Use the correct funding instrument (Grant/Debt/Debenture)
3. Select the correct incubator preferences
4. Ensure all attachments are in the correct format (PDF)
5. Review and verify all information before submission
6. Use the Startup India portal to submit the application
7. Keep a record of the application reference number

### Internal Review Gate
* All fields complete: Yes
* Docs attached: Yes
* Declarations signed: Yes

## Stage 4: Submission & Follow-Up (Day 18–30)
### Submission Protocol
1. Submit the application through the Startup India portal
2. Take a screenshot of the submission confirmation
3. Store the reference number for future reference
4. Set follow-up reminders for the incubator's response

### Follow-Up Script
"Dear [Incubator], we have submitted our application for the Startup India Seed Fund Scheme. Our application reference number is [Reference Number]. Could you please update us on the status of our application? Thank you for your time and consideration."

### Escalation Path
- Level 1: [Portal helpdesk](https://seedfund.startupindia.gov.in/contact) or helpline (if available)
- Level 2: [Nodal officer](mailto:dipp-startups@nic.in) or [TO BE FILLED BY CONSULTANT]
- Level 3: RTI filing if no response within statutory SLA

## Stage 5: Post-Approval (Day 30–90+)
### Approval Actions
* Verify sanctioned amount: Yes
* Utilization certificate: Yes
* Invoice success fee: Yes
* Approval letter: Yes

Application portal URL: https://seedfund.startupindia.gov.in/
