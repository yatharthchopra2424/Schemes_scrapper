## Pricing Principles
Our pricing principles are based on the following:
1. **Value-based pricing**: Our fees are based on the value we bring to our clients.
2. **Tiered pricing**: We offer different pricing tiers to cater to the diverse needs of our clients.
3. **Transparency**: We are transparent about our pricing and ensure that our clients understand what they are paying for.
4. **Risk-sharing**: We share the risk with our clients by offering a success-based fee structure.

The risk profile of this scheme is moderate, considering the processing time and evidence of success from the crawled data. The scheme has a well-defined evaluation process, and the incubators are responsible for selecting and monitoring the startups.
