# Pitch & Sales Scripts
## Philosophy
We are advisors, not salespeople. We diagnose first. We only pitch a scheme if it fits the client. We never oversell success rates.

## 1. Discovery Call Script (First Contact — 20 min)
### Opening (2 min)
Hello, I'm [Your Name] from [Your Company]. We specialize in helping startups like yours navigate the complex world of government schemes. The Startup India Seed Fund Scheme (SISFS) is one such initiative that provides financial assistance to early-stage startups. Are you familiar with it?

### Discovery Questions (6-8 questions tailored to this scheme's eligibility criteria)
1. Can you tell me a little about your startup and what problem you're trying to solve?
2. How long has your startup been incorporated?
3. Do you have a business idea with a clear market fit and potential for scaling?
4. Are you using technology in your core product or service to solve the problem?
5. Have you received any funding or support from other government schemes?
6. What is your current shareholding structure, and is it at least 51% owned by Indian promoters?
7. Are you looking for funding to validate your proof of concept, develop a prototype, or conduct product trials?
8. Do you have a clear plan for how you would utilize the seed funding if granted?

### Qualification Checklist (internal — don't read aloud)
- Recognized by DPIIT
- Incorporated not more than 2 years ago
- Business idea with market fit and scaling potential
- Using technology to solve the problem
- Not received more than Rs 10 lakh from other government schemes
- At least 51% owned by Indian promoters
- Clear plan for seed funding utilization

### Transition to Pitch (2-3 lines that bridge from discovery to pitching THIS specific scheme — use the real scheme name and benefit amount)
Based on what you've shared, I believe the Startup India Seed Fund Scheme could be a great fit for your startup. This scheme provides up to Rs. 20 lakhs as a grant for validation of proof of concept, prototype development, or product trials, and up to Rs. 50 lakhs for market entry, commercialization, or scaling up. Would you like to learn more about how this scheme can support your startup's growth?

## 2. Scheme Pitch (5 min)
### Structure: Problem → Scheme → Value → Proof → Next Step
**Problem framing:** Many startups struggle to find the necessary funding to validate their proof of concept, develop prototypes, or conduct product trials, which are critical stages in their growth journey.
**Scheme intro:** The Startup India Seed Fund Scheme (SISFS) is designed to address this challenge by providing financial assistance to early-stage startups. The scheme is implemented by the Department for Promotion of Industry and Internal Trade (DPIIT), Ministry of Commerce and Industry, Government of India.
**Why they qualify (personalized):** Based on our conversation, your startup meets the eligibility criteria for the SISFS, including being a DPIIT-recognized startup, having a business idea with market fit and scaling potential, and not having received more than Rs 10 lakh from other government schemes.
