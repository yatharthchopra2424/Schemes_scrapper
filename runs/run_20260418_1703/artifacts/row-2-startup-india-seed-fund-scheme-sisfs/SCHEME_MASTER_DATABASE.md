### Startup India Seed Fund Scheme (SISFS)
- **Scheme ID:** SCH-001
- **Category:** Grant
- **Geography:** Pan-India
- **Administering Body:** Department for Promotion of Industry and Internal Trade (DPIIT), Ministry of Commerce and Industry
- **Portal / Application URL:** https://seedfund.startupindia.gov.in/
- **Current Status:** Open
- **Window:** Rolling basis, with no fixed deadline

#### Eligibility Criteria
- Business type: Private Limited Company or Registered Partnership Firm or Limited Liability Partnership
- Age of business: Incorporated not more than 2 years ago at the time of application
- Sector: All sectors (sector-agnostic)
- Revenue cap: No specific revenue cap mentioned
- Employee count: No specific employee count mentioned
- Other: 
  - The startup must have a business idea to develop a product or service with market fit, viable commercialization, and scope of scaling.
  - The startup should be using technology in its core product or service, or business model, or distribution model, or methodology to solve the problem being targeted.
  - Preference would be given to startups creating innovative solutions in sectors such as social impact, waste management, water management, financial inclusion, education, agriculture, food processing, biotechnology, healthcare, energy, mobility, defense, space, railways, oil and gas, textiles, etc.
  - The startup should not have received more than Rs 10 lakh of monetary support under any other Central or State Government scheme.
  - Shareholding by Indian promoters in the startup should be at least 51% at the time of application to the incubator for the scheme.

#### What It Offers
- Amount / Benefit: Up to Rs. 20 Lakhs as grant for validation of Proof of Concept, or prototype development, or product trials, and up to Rs. 50 Lakhs of investment for market entry, commercialization, or scaling up through convertible debentures or debt or debt-linked instruments
- Disbursement mode: Grant and debt/debenture
- Duration of benefit: One-time (grant) and up to 5 years (debt/debenture)

#### Documents Required
1. Certificate of Incorporation
2. PAN of the entity
3. Pitch deck or business description
4. Authorization letter from authorized signatory
5. Details of funding received (if any)

#### Application Process
1. Online application on the Startup India portal
2. Evaluation by the Incubator Seed Management Committee (ISMC)
3. Selection by the Experts Advisory Committee (EAC)
