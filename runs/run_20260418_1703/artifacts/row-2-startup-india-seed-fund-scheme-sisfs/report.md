## 📋 Executive Overview
The Startup India Seed Fund Scheme (SISFS) aims to provide financial assistance to startups for proof of concept, prototype development, product trials, market entry, and commercialization. The scheme is sector-agnostic and will support startups across all sectors.


---

## 🎯 Objectives
- Provide financial assistance to startups for proof of concept, prototype development, product trials, market entry, and commercialization
- Enable startups to graduate to a level where they can raise investments from angel investors or venture capitalists, or seek loans from commercial banks or financial institutions
- Support startups in creating innovative solutions in various sectors

## ✅ Eligibility
A startup, recognized by DPIIT, incorporated not more than 2 years ago at the time of application, with a business idea to develop a product or service with market fit, viable commercialization, and scope of scaling


**Target Beneficiaries:** startups
**Geographic Scope:** Pan-India

---

## 🎁 Benefits
Financial assistance up to Rs. 20 Lakhs as grant for validation of Proof of Concept, or prototype development, or product trials, and up to Rs. 50 Lakhs of investment for market entry, commercialization, or scaling up through convertible debentures or debt or debt-linked instruments


---

## 💳 Financial Support
The Seed Fund will be disbursed to eligible startups through eligible incubators across India


---

## 📝 How to Apply

## Application Process
Online application on the Startup India portal, with evaluation by the Incubator Seed Management Committee (ISMC) and selection by the Experts Advisory Committee (EAC)


**Application Portal:** [Apply Here](https://seedfund.startupindia.gov.in/)

## Required Documents
- Certificate of Incorporation
- PAN of the entity
- Pitch deck or business description
- Authorization letter from authorized signatory
- Details of funding received (if any)


## ⏰ Deadlines
Rolling basis, with no fixed deadline


---

## 🏢 Implementing Agency
Department for Promotion of Industry and Internal Trade (DPIIT), Ministry of Commerce and Industry


## 📞 Contact Details
Email: dipp-startups@nic.in


---

## ⚠️ Caveats & Conditions
- The startup should not have received more than Rs 10 lakh of monetary support under any other Central or State Government scheme
- The startup should have at least 51% shareholding by Indian promoters at the time of application to the incubator for the scheme
