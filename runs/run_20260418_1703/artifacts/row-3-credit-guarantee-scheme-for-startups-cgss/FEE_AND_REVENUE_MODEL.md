## Pricing Principles
Our pricing principles are based on the following:
1. **Value-based pricing**: Our fees are based on the value we bring to our clients.
2. **Risk-reward sharing**: We share the risk and reward with our clients, ensuring that our interests are aligned.
3. **Transparency**: We provide clear and transparent pricing to our clients, with no hidden costs or surprises.
4. **Flexibility**: We offer flexible pricing options to accommodate the unique needs and circumstances of each client.

The Credit Guarantee Scheme for Startups (CGSS) has a relatively low risk profile due to the guarantee cover provided by the National Credit Guarantee Trustee Company (NCGTC). However, the processing time for the scheme can be lengthy, which may impact our revenue projections.
