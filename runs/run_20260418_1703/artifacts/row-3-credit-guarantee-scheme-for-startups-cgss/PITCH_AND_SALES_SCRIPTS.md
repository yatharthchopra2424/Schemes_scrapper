# Pitch & Sales Scripts
## Philosophy
We are advisors, not salespeople. We diagnose first. We only pitch a scheme if it fits the client. We never oversell success rates.

## 1. Discovery Call Script (First Contact — 20 min)
### Opening (2 min)
Hello, I'm [TO BE FILLED BY CONSULTANT], and I'm excited to learn more about your startup and see if we can help you with the Credit Guarantee Scheme for Startups (CGSS). This scheme provides a credit guarantee to loans extended by Member Institutions, with a maximum guarantee cover of ₹20 crore per borrower.

### Discovery Questions
1. What is your startup's current stage, and what are your funding requirements?
2. Can you tell me about your business model and revenue streams?
3. Have you explored any funding options previously, and if so, what were the outcomes?
4. What are your thoughts on debt funding, and have you considered it as an option for your startup?
5. Are you recognized by the Department for Promotion of Industry and Internal Trade (DPIIT) as a startup?
6. Do you have any existing loans or debt obligations that we should be aware of?
7. What are your plans for using the funds if you were to receive a loan under the CGSS?
8. Can you share your financial projections and growth plans for the next few years?

### Qualification Checklist
- Is the startup recognized by DPIIT?
- Is the startup not in default to any lending/investing institution and not classified as Non-Performing Asset as per RBI guidelines?
- Does the startup have a viable business model and growth potential?
- Is the startup in need of debt funding for growth or working capital?
- Does the startup meet the eligibility criteria for the CGSS?

### Transition to Pitch
Based on our discussion, it seems like your startup could benefit from the Credit Guarantee Scheme for Startups. This scheme can provide you with the necessary funding to grow your business without requiring collateral. We specialize in helping startups navigate this process and secure the funding they need. Would you like to learn more about how we can assist you with the CGSS?

## 2. Scheme Pitch (5 min)
### Structure: Problem → Scheme → Value → Proof → Next Step
#### Problem framing:
Many startups struggle to secure funding due to lack of collateral or credit history. This can hinder their growth and ability to compete in the market.

#### Scheme intro:
The Credit Guarantee Scheme for Startups (CGSS) is designed to provide credit guarantees to loans extended by Member Institutions to finance eligible startups. The scheme offers a maximum guarantee cover of ₹20 crore per borrower, with 85% of the amount in default covered for loan amounts up to ₹10 crore, and 75% for loan amounts exceeding ₹10 crore.

#### Why they qualify (personalised):
Based on our previous discussion, your startup meets the eligibility criteria for the CGSS, including being recognized by DPIIT and not being in default to any lending institution. Your business model and growth plans also align with the scheme's objectives.

#### Proof:
We've helped [N] similar businesses get approved for this scheme. [TO BE FILLED BY CONSULTANT]

#### Next step:
The next step would be to conduct a free eligibility check to confirm your startup's eligibility for the CGSS. If eligible, we can guide you through the application process and help you prepare the necessary documents.
