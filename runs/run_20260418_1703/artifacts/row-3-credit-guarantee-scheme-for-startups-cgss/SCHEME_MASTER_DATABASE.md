### Credit Guarantee Scheme for Startups (CGSS)
- **Scheme ID:** SCH-001
- **Category:** Loan
- **Geography:** Pan-India
- **Administering Body:** Department for Promotion of Industry and Internal Trade (DPIIT), Ministry of Commerce and Industry
- **Portal / Application URL:** https://jansamarth.in/
- **Current Status:** Rolling basis
- **Window:** Always Open

#### Eligibility Criteria
- Business type: Startups recognized by DPIIT
- Age of business: Not specified
- Sector: All sectors
- Revenue cap: No specific revenue cap mentioned, but the startup should not be in default to any lending/investing institution
- Employee count: Not specified
- Other: The startup should be recognized by DPIIT as per Gazette Notifications issued from time to time and should not be in default to any lending/investing institution and not classified as Non-Performing Asset as per RBI guidelines.

#### What It Offers
- Amount / Benefit: Credit guarantee up to a specified limit against loans extended by Member Institutions to finance eligible startups, with a maximum guarantee cover of ₹20 crore per borrower.
- Disbursement mode: The guarantee cover is provided to the lending institution, which then extends the loan to the eligible startup.
- Duration of benefit: The guarantee cover is valid for the tenure of the loan.

#### Documents Required
1. DPIIT recognition certificate
2. Incorporation certificate
3. KYC documents
4. Business plan
5. Financial statements

#### Application Process
1. Startups can apply for the scheme through the Jan Samarth Portal.
2. Alternatively, startups can approach the nearest branch of any Member Lending Institution directly.
3. The lending institution will evaluate the startup's eligibility and creditworthiness.
4. If eligible, the lending institution will apply for the guarantee cover on behalf of the startup.
5. The guarantee cover will be provided by the National Credit Guarantee Trustee Company (NCGTC).
