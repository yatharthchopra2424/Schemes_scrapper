## Prerequisites
To apply for the **Credit Guarantee Scheme for Startups (CGSS)**, the following prerequisites must be met:
* The startup must be recognized by the Department for Promotion of Industry and Internal Trade (DPIIT) as per the Gazette Notification issued by the Department for Promotion of Industry and Internal Trade (DPIIT) and amended from time to time.
* The startup should not be in default to any lending/investing institution and not classified as Non-Performing Asset as per RBI guidelines.
* The startup's eligibility must be certified by the Member Institution for the purpose of guarantee cover.

## Documents Required
The following documents are required to apply for the CGSS:
1. **DPIIT Recognition Certificate**: A certificate issued by DPIIT recognizing the startup.
2. **Business Plan**: A detailed business plan outlining the startup's goals, objectives, and financial projections.
3. **Financial Statements**: Audited financial statements for the last three years, if applicable.
4. **Bank Account Details**: Details of the startup's bank account, including account number and IFSC code.
5. **Identity Proof**: Identity proof of the startup's promoters, such as Aadhaar card or PAN card.
6. **Address Proof**: Address proof of the startup's registered office, such as utility bills or rent agreement.
7. **Incorporation Documents**: Incorporation documents, such as certificate of incorporation, memorandum of association, and articles of association.
8. **Resolution**: A resolution passed by the startup's board of directors authorizing the application for CGSS.

## Step-by-Step Application Process
To apply for the CGSS, follow these steps:
1. **Visit the Jan Samarth Portal**: Visit the Jan Samarth Portal website and click on the "Apply Now" button.
2. **Fill in the Online Application Form**: Fill in the online application form with the required details, including startup information, business plan, and financial projections.
3. **Upload Required Documents**: Upload the required documents, including DPIIT recognition certificate, business plan, financial statements, and identity proof.
4. **Submit the Application**: Submit the application and pay the required fees, if any.
5. **Wait for Processing**: Wait for the application to be processed and verified by the concerned authorities.
6. **Receive the Guarantee Cover**: Receive the guarantee cover from the National Credit Guarantee Trustee Company (NCGTC) if the application is approved.

## Timeline & Deadlines
The timeline and deadlines for the CGSS application process are as follows:
* **Application Submission**: The application can be submitted at any time, and there is no specific deadline.
* **Processing Time**: The processing time for the application is typically 15-30 days, depending on the complexity of the application and the speed of verification.
* **Guarantee Cover**: The guarantee cover is typically issued within 7-10 days of approval of the application.

## Common Mistakes to Avoid
To avoid common mistakes, ensure that:
* **All required documents are uploaded**: Make sure all required documents, including DPIIT recognition certificate, business plan, and financial statements, are uploaded.
* **The application form is filled correctly**: Ensure that the application form is filled correctly and completely, with all required information.
* **The startup meets the eligibility criteria**: Ensure that the startup meets the eligibility criteria, including recognition by DPIIT and not being in default to any lending/investing institution.
