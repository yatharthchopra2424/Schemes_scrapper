## 📋 Executive Overview
The Credit Guarantee Scheme for Startups (CGSS) provides credit guarantees to loans extended by Member Institutions to finance eligible startups, with a maximum guarantee cover of ₹20 crore per borrower.


---

## 🎯 Objectives
- To provide credit guarantee up to a specified limit against loans extended by Member Institutions to finance eligible startups
- To support eligible startups in accessing debt funding from Scheduled Commercial Banks, Non-Banking Financial Companies, and Venture Debt Funds
- To enhance the credit availability for startups and encourage entrepreneurship

## ✅ Eligibility
The startup should be recognized by DPIIT as per Gazette Notifications issued from time to time, and should not be in default to any lending/investing institution and not classified as Non-Performing Asset as per RBI guidelines.


**Target Beneficiaries:** startups
**Geographic Scope:** Pan-India

---

## 🎁 Benefits
The scheme provides a credit guarantee of up to ₹20 crore per borrower, with a guarantee cover of 85% of the amount in default for loan amounts up to ₹10 crore, and 75% of the amount in default for loan amounts exceeding ₹10 crore.


---

## 💳 Financial Support
The scheme provides a credit guarantee to loans extended by Member Institutions, with a maximum guarantee cover of ₹20 crore per borrower.


---

## 📝 How to Apply

## Application Process
Startups can apply for the scheme through the Jan Samarth Portal or by approaching the nearest branch of any Member Lending Institution directly.


**Application Portal:** [Apply Here](https://jansamarth.in/)

## Required Documents
- DPIIT recognition certificate
- Incorporation certificate
- KYC documents
- Business plan
- Financial statements


## ⏰ Deadlines
Rolling basis

## ⚠️ Caveats & Conditions
- The startup should not be in default to any lending/investing institution and not classified as Non-Performing Asset as per RBI guidelines.
- The guarantee cover is subject to a maximum of ₹20 crore per borrower.
