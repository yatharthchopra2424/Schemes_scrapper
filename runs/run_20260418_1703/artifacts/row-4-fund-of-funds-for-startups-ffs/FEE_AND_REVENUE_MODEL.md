## Pricing Principles
Our pricing principles are designed to be fair, transparent, and aligned with the needs of our clients. We consider the following factors when determining our pricing:
1. **Value-based pricing**: Our fees are based on the value we bring to our clients, taking into account the complexity of the scheme, the level of expertise required, and the potential benefits to the client.
2. **Risk-based pricing**: We consider the level of risk associated with each scheme, including the potential for delays, disputes, or other complications, and adjust our fees accordingly.
3. **Market-based pricing**: We research and analyze market rates for similar services to ensure our fees are competitive and reasonable.
4. **Client-centric pricing**: We work closely with our clients to understand their needs, goals, and budget constraints, and tailor our pricing to meet their specific requirements.

The Fund of Funds for Startups (FFS) scheme has a relatively low risk profile due to its focus on supporting startups through SEBI-registered AIFs. However, the processing time for the scheme can be lengthy, which may impact our revenue projections.
