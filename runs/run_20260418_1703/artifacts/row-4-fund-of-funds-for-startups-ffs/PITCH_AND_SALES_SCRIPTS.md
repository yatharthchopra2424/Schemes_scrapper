# Pitch & Sales Scripts
## Philosophy
We are advisors, not salespeople. We diagnose first. We only pitch a scheme if it fits the client. We never oversell success rates.

## 1. Discovery Call Script (First Contact — 20 min)
### Opening (2 min)
Hello, we're excited to learn more about your startup and explore how the Fund of Funds for Startups (FFS) scheme can support your growth. As a startup founder, you're likely aware of the challenges in accessing funding. The FFS scheme, administered by the Small Industries Development Bank of India (SIDBI), provides a unique opportunity for startups like yours to secure funding and take your business to the next level.

### Discovery Questions (6-8 questions tailored to this scheme's eligibility criteria)
1. What is your startup's incorporation date, and how long have you been in operation?
2. Can you share your startup's annual turnover for the last financial year?
3. What is the nature of your business, and how does it align with the FFS scheme's focus on innovation and improvement of existing products or services?
4. Do you have any existing funding or investments, and if so, how do you plan to utilize the funding from the FFS scheme?
5. How do you plan to use the funding from the FFS scheme to drive growth and expansion in your business?
6. Can you walk us through your team's experience and expertise in your industry?
7. How do you envision your startup contributing to the Indian economy and creating jobs?
8. Are you registered with the Startup India initiative, and if so, can you share your recognition number?

### Qualification Checklist (internal — don't read aloud)
- Incorporated as a private limited company or registered as a partnership firm or limited liability partnership
- Turnover less than INR 100 Crores in any of the previous financial years
- Entity shall be considered as a startup up to 10 years from the date of its incorporation
- Working towards innovation or improvement of existing products, services, and processes
- Has the potential to generate employment or create wealth

### Transition to Pitch (2-3 lines that bridge from discovery to pitching THIS specific scheme — use the real scheme name and benefit amount)
Based on our discussion, it seems like your startup aligns with the eligibility criteria for the Fund of Funds for Startups (FFS) scheme. This scheme provides funding to startups through SEBI-registered Alternative Investment Funds (AIFs), with a total corpus of INR 10,000 crores. We believe the FFS scheme can be a great fit for your startup, providing the necessary funding to drive growth and expansion.
