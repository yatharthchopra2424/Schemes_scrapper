### Fund of Funds for Startups (FFS)
- **Scheme ID:** SCH-001
- **Category:** Government grants
- **Geography:** Pan-India
- **Administering Body:** Small Industries Development Bank of India (SIDBI)
- **Portal / Application URL:** https://www.startupindia.gov.in/
- **Current Status:** Open
- **Window:** Always Open

#### Eligibility Criteria
- Business type: Startups
- Age of business: Less than 10 years
- Sector: All sectors
- Revenue cap: ₹100 Crores
- Employee count: 
- Other: The startup should be working towards innovation, improvement of existing products, services, and processes, and should have the potential to generate employment or create wealth.

#### What It Offers
- Amount / Benefit: Funding to startups through SEBI-registered AIFs
- Disbursement mode: Investment in AIFs
- Duration of benefit: One-time

#### Documents Required
1. Business plan
2. Financial projections
3. Registration certificate
4. PAN card
5. Bank account details

#### Application Process
1. Startups can apply for funding under the FFS scheme through the Startup India website.
2. The application process involves submitting a business plan, financial projections, and other relevant documents.
