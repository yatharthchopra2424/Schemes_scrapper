## Prerequisites
To apply for the **Fund of Funds for Startups (FFS)**, the following prerequisites must be met:
* The startup must be registered as a private limited company or a limited liability partnership in India.
* The startup must have a unique product or service that has the potential for high growth.
* The startup must have a scalable business model with a high potential for employment generation or wealth creation.
* The startup must be less than 10 years old from the date of incorporation.
* The startup must have a turnover of less than ₹100 crore in the last financial year.
* The startup must not have been formed by splitting up or reconstructing an existing business.

## Documents Required
The following documents are required to apply for the **Fund of Funds for Startups (FFS)**:
1. Certificate of Incorporation
2. Memorandum of Association (MoA) and Articles of Association (AoA)
3. PAN card of the company
4. GST registration certificate
5. Audited financial statements for the last two years
6. Detailed business plan, including market analysis, product or service description, marketing and sales strategy, financial projections, and management team profiles
7. Proof of innovative product or service, such as patent or trademark registration
8. Proof of scalable business model, such as market research reports or customer testimonials
9. Bank statement for the last six months
10. Income tax returns for the last two years

## Step-by-Step Application Process
1. Register on the Startup India portal and obtain a DPIIT recognition number.
2. Prepare a detailed business plan, including market analysis, product or service description, marketing and sales strategy, financial projections, and management team profiles.
3. Gather all the required documents, including certificate of incorporation, MoA and AoA, PAN card, GST registration certificate, audited financial statements, and bank statement.
4. Fill out the online application form on the Startup India portal and upload all the required documents.
5. Pay the application fee, if applicable.
6. Submit the application and wait for the verification process to be completed.
7. Once the application is verified, the startup will be eligible to receive funding from the **Fund of Funds for Startups (FFS)**.

## Timeline & Deadlines
The application process for the **Fund of Funds for Startups (FFS)** is open throughout the year, and there are no specific deadlines. However, the startup must ensure that it meets the eligibility criteria and has all the required documents before applying.

## Common Mistakes to Avoid
1. Not meeting the eligibility criteria, such as not being registered as a private limited company or limited liability partnership in India.
2. Not having a unique product or service that has the potential for high growth.
3. Not having a scalable business model with a high potential for employment generation or wealth creation.
4. Not providing all the required documents, such as certificate of incorporation, MoA and AoA, PAN card, GST registration certificate, audited financial statements, and bank statement.
5. Not filling out the online application form correctly or not uploading all the required documents.

## After Submission
After submitting the application, the startup will receive an acknowledgement email from the Startup India portal. The application will then be verified, and the startup will be eligible to receive funding from the **Fund of Funds for Startups (FFS)**. The startup will also receive regular updates on the status of its application.

## Help & Support
For any queries or issues related to the application process, the startup can contact the Startup India portal support team at [support@startupindia.gov.in](mailto:support@startupindia.gov.in) or call the helpline number at 1800 103 5147.
