## Pricing Principles
Our pricing principles are designed to ensure that our services are accessible and affordable for startups and MSMEs. We offer a tiered pricing structure, with lower retainers and success fees for smaller clients. Our success fees are based on the value of the scheme, and we offer a discount for larger schemes. We also offer a custom pricing option for enterprise clients.

Note: The risk profile of the Startup India Fund of Funds 2.0 scheme is relatively low, as it is a government-backed scheme with a fixed funding amount. However, the processing time for the scheme can be lengthy, which may impact our revenue projections.
