# Pitch & Sales Scripts
## Philosophy
We are advisors, not salespeople. We diagnose first. We only pitch a scheme if it fits the client. We never oversell success rates.

## 1. Discovery Call Script (First Contact — 20 min)
### Opening (2 min)
Hello, I'm [TO BE FILLED BY CONSULTANT], and I'm excited to learn more about your startup and see if we can help you with the Startup India Fund of Funds 2.0 scheme. This scheme provides financial support to startups, and I'd love to explore if your business qualifies.

### Discovery Questions (6-8 questions tailored to this scheme's eligibility criteria)
1. What is your startup's incorporation date, and what type of entity is it (Private Limited Company, LLP, or Registered Partnership)?
2. What is your startup's annual turnover, and what products or services do you offer?
3. Can you describe your innovative product or service and how it addresses a specific problem in the market?
4. Do you have any existing funding or investments, and if so, how much?
5. What are your plans for growth and expansion in the next 2-3 years?
6. Have you applied for any other government schemes or funding programs?
7. Can you share your team's experience and expertise in the industry?
8. What are your biggest challenges currently, and how do you think this scheme could help?

### Qualification Checklist (internal — don't read aloud)
- Incorporated as a Private Limited Company, LLP, or Registered Partnership
- Turnover less than INR 100 Cr
- Incorporated less than 10 years ago
- Working on innovative products or services
- Not formed by splitting up or reconstructing an existing business

### Transition to Pitch (2-3 lines that bridge from discovery to pitching THIS specific scheme)
Based on what you've shared, I think the Startup India Fund of Funds 2.0 scheme could be a great fit for your startup. This scheme provides funding support to startups, and I'd like to walk you through the details and see if we can help you with the application process.

## 2. Scheme Pitch (5 min)
### Structure: Problem → Scheme → Value → Proof → Next Step
#### Problem framing:
As a startup, accessing funding can be a significant challenge, especially when you're trying to scale and grow your business.

#### Scheme intro:
The Startup India Fund of Funds 2.0 scheme is a government-backed initiative that provides funding support to startups. The scheme has a corpus of INR 10,000 Cr, managed by SIDBI, and invests in SEBI-registered Alternative Investment Funds (AIFs) that fund startups.

#### Why they qualify (personalised):
Based on our conversation, I believe your startup qualifies for this scheme because:
* You're incorporated as a Private Limited Company
* Your annual turnover is less than INR 100 Cr
* You're working on an innovative product or service
* You're incorporated less than 10 years ago

#### Proof:
We've helped [N] similar businesses get approved for this scheme. [TO BE FILLED BY CONSULTANT]

#### Next step:
The next step would be to conduct a free eligibility check to confirm your startup's eligibility for the scheme. If eligible, we can assist you with the application process.
