## Why This Scheme Matters
The **Startup India Fund of Funds 2.0** scheme matters because it addresses the critical issue of funding for startups in India. Many startups struggle to secure funding, which hinders their growth and ability to scale. This scheme provides a much-needed boost to the startup ecosystem by offering financial support, thereby enabling startups to focus on innovation, job creation, and wealth generation.

## What You Get
The top 5 benefits of the **Startup India Fund of Funds 2.0** scheme are:
* **Financial Support**: Access to funding through the Fund of Funds, which invests in SEBI-registered Alternative Investment Funds (AIFs) that, in turn, invest in startups.
* **Diversified Portfolio**: The scheme allows AIFs to invest in a diverse range of startups across various sectors, reducing risk and increasing potential returns.
* **Mentorship and Guidance**: Startups can benefit from the expertise and guidance of experienced fund managers and industry experts.
* **Networking Opportunities**: The scheme provides a platform for startups to connect with other entrepreneurs, investors, and industry stakeholders, potentially leading to valuable partnerships and collaborations.
* **Government Support**: The scheme is backed by the Government of India, which can help build credibility and trust among investors and startups.

## Who Qualifies
To qualify for the **Startup India Fund of Funds 2.0** scheme, startups must meet the eligibility criteria set by the Department for Promotion of Industry and Internal Trade (DPIIT). This includes being recognized as a startup by DPIIT, having a turnover of less than INR 100 crores, and being incorporated as a private limited company or a limited liability partnership.

## Financial Support
The **Startup India Fund of Funds 2.0** scheme has a corpus of INR 10,000 crores, with the Government of India contributing INR 5,000 crores. The scheme provides financial support to startups through investments in SEBI-registered AIFs, which can invest up to INR 50 crores in a single startup.

## How to Get Started
To get started with the **Startup India Fund of Funds 2.0** scheme, follow these steps:
1. **Check Eligibility**: Ensure your startup meets the eligibility criteria set by DPIIT.
2. **Register with DPIIT**: Get your startup recognized by DPIIT.
3. **Find an AIF**: Research and identify a SEBI-registered AIF that invests in startups like yours.
4. **Apply for Funding**: Submit an application to the AIF, providing detailed information about your startup, business plan, and funding requirements.
5. **Due Diligence**: The AIF will conduct due diligence on your startup, and if successful, invest in your company.
