# Pitch & Sales Scripts
## Philosophy
We are advisors, not salespeople. We diagnose first. We only pitch a scheme if it fits the client. We never oversell success rates.

## 1. Discovery Call Script (First Contact — 20 min)
### Opening (2 min)
Hello, I'm [TO BE FILLED BY CONSULTANT], and I'm calling from [TO BE FILLED BY CONSULTANT]. We specialize in helping startups and entrepreneurs navigate the complex world of government schemes and incentives. I came across your company, [Client Company], and was impressed with the innovative work you're doing in [industry/sector]. The Startup India Initiative, launched by the Government of India, aims to foster entrepreneurship and start-ups, providing various benefits such as tax exemptions, funding opportunities, and simplified regulatory compliance. I'd love to explore how our expertise can support your growth and success.

### Discovery Questions (6-8 questions tailored to this scheme's eligibility criteria)
1. Can you tell me a little bit about your company and what motivated you to start it?
2. How long has your company been in operation, and what's your current turnover?
3. Are you working on any innovative products, services, or processes that could potentially disrupt the market or create new opportunities?
4. Have you explored any government schemes or incentives that could support your business growth?
5. What are your biggest challenges right now, and how do you see our expertise helping you overcome them?
6. Are you aware of the benefits provided by the Startup India Initiative, such as income tax exemption for three years, or the fast-tracking of patent applications?
7. Have you considered how the Credit Guarantee Scheme for Startups could help you access funds for growth and development?
8. How do you envision your company scaling up in the next few years, and what kind of support do you think you'll need to get there?

### Qualification Checklist (internal — don't read aloud)
- Is the company incorporated as a Private Limited Company, Registered Partnership Firm, or Limited Liability Partnership?
- Is the company's turnover less than INR 100 Crores?
- Is the company working towards innovation, improvement of existing products, services, and processes, and has the potential to generate employment or create wealth?
- Is the company recognized by the Department for Promotion of Industry and Internal Trade (DPIIT)?

### Transition to Pitch (2-3 lines that bridge from discovery to pitching THIS specific scheme)
Based on what you've shared, I believe the Startup India Initiative could be a great fit for your company. This scheme offers a range of benefits, including tax exemptions, simplified regulatory compliance, and funding opportunities, which could help support your growth and success. I'd love to tell you more about how we can help you navigate this scheme and maximize its benefits for your business.

## 2. Scheme Pitch (5 min)
### Structure: Problem → Scheme → Value → Proof → Next Step
#### Problem framing:
As a startup, one of the biggest challenges you face is accessing the right resources and support to drive growth and innovation. The current regulatory environment can be complex and time-consuming, taking away from the time and energy you could be spending on developing your product or service.

#### Scheme intro:
The Startup India Initiative is a flagship program launched by the Government of India to foster entrepreneurship and start-ups. The scheme provides various benefits, including:
- Income tax exemption for three years
- Fast-tracking of patent applications
- Simplified regulatory compliance
- Funding opportunities through the Fund of Funds for Startups
- Access to a network of mentors, investors, and peers through the Startup India Hub

#### Why they qualify (personalised):
Based on our conversation, I believe your company meets the eligibility criteria for the Startup India Initiative. Your innovative approach to [industry/sector] and your potential for generating employment and creating wealth make you an ideal candidate for this scheme.

#### Proof:
We've helped [N] similar businesses get approved for this scheme. [TO BE FILLED BY CONSULTANT]

#### Next step:
The next step would be to conduct a free eligibility check to confirm your company's eligibility for the scheme. If eligible, we can support you in preparing and submitting your application. Our success fee model ensures that you only pay if your application is successful.
