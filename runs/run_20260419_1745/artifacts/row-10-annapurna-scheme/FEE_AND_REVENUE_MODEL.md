## Pricing Principles
Our pricing principles are based on the following:
1. **Risk Profile**: The Annapurna Scheme has a relatively low risk profile as it is backed by the State Bank of India and has a repayment period of up to 7 years.
2. **Scheme Benefits**: The scheme offers a loan amount of up to Rs. 50,000 to Rs. 50 lakhs, with a competitive interest rate and a moratorium period of up to 6 months.
3. **Client Segments**: Our pricing tiers are designed to cater to different client segments, including micro, MSME, and startup businesses.
4. **Success Fee**: Our success fee is based on the scheme value and is designed to incentivize our consultants to deliver high-quality services to our clients.
