# Pitch & Sales Scripts
## Philosophy
We are advisors, not salespeople. We diagnose first. We only pitch a scheme if it fits the client. We never oversell success rates.

## 1. Discovery Call Script (First Contact — 20 min)
### Opening (2 min)

I'm calling today to discuss the Annapurna Scheme, a loan program specifically designed for women entrepreneurs in the food industry. ### Discovery Questions (6-8 questions tailored to this scheme's eligibility criteria)
1. Are you a woman entrepreneur in the food industry?
2. What is your current age?
3. Do you have a good credit history?
4. Do you have a viable business plan?
5. What is the current status of your business (e.g., startup, existing, expanding)?
6. Are you looking for financing to support your business growth?
7. Have you explored any other financing options or government schemes?
8. What are your primary goals for your business in the next 6-12 months?

### Qualification Checklist (internal — don't read aloud)
- Women entrepreneurs in the food industry
- Minimum age of 21 years and maximum age of 55 years
- Good credit history
- Viable business plan
- Loan amount between Rs. 50,000 to Rs. 50 lakhs
- Repayment period of up to 7 years

### Transition to Pitch (2-3 lines that bridge from discovery to pitching THIS specific scheme)
Based on your interests and business needs, I believe the Annapurna Scheme could be a great fit for you. This scheme offers competitive interest rates and a moratorium period of up to 6 months, making it an attractive option for women entrepreneurs in the food industry. Would you like to learn more about how this scheme can support your business goals?

## 2. Scheme Pitch (5 min)
### Structure: Problem → Scheme → Value → Proof → Next Step
#### Problem framing:
As a woman entrepreneur in the food industry, accessing financing can be challenging, especially when looking to expand or establish your business. Traditional financing options may have strict eligibility criteria or high interest rates, making it difficult to secure the funds you need.

#### Scheme intro:
The Annapurna Scheme, offered by the State Bank of India, is designed to address these challenges. With loan amounts ranging from Rs. 50,000 to Rs. 50 lakhs and repayment periods of up to 7 years, this scheme provides the financial support you need to grow your business.

#### Why they qualify (personalised):
Given your background in the food industry and your business goals, you may qualify for this scheme. The scheme's eligibility criteria include being a woman entrepreneur in the food industry, having a good credit history, and a viable business plan, all of which align with your current situation.
