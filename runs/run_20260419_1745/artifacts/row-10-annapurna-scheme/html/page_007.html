How to Apply for a Credit Card through YONO SBI App?

13 Nov, 2023

credit card

Numerous benefits come with owning an SBI Credit card, which makes it among the best in the market. Also, these cards facilitate cashless transactions, making payment and shopping processes easier. SBI is associated with appealing reward programs that allow you to get points for a rupee spent and which can be utilized towards attractive offers and promotions. Besides, they accompany you with offers and discounts in different outlets that are affiliated with the company, making you get more worth for your money. So, can we apply for SBI credit cards online through YONO SBI? Below is a step-by-step guide on how to apply for an SBI credit card through YONO SBI. To know more, read the following.

What is YONO SBI App?

Before we get into details, let's take a quick look at the YONO SBI app. The State Bank of India provides an all-in-one digital platform called YONO, which stands for "You Only Need One." It makes it extremely easy for SBI users to manage their accounts, complete transactions, and apply for different products like credit cards by combining a wide range of banking and financial services under one roof. Explore the 17 variants of SBI credit cards on YONO SBI to discover their features before applying for a credit card based on your needs. This ensures a seamless and hassle-free digital experience.

How to Apply for SBI Credit Card through YONO SBI App

Now that you're aware of the advantages of having an SBI credit card, let's proceed with the step-by-step guide on how to apply for an SBI Credit Card online through the YONO SBI app:

Step 1: Download the YONO SBI App

If you haven't already, go to the App Store (for iOS users) or the Google Play Store (for Android users) and download the YONO SBI app. After that, start the application and proceed to the login process using the credentials from SBI Internet banking. New users can register and create an account with ease.

Step 2: Explore the 'Cards' Section

To access the "Credit Cards" section, log in and tap on the "Cards" option in the app. Here, you'll find detailed information about different SBI credit card options, including their features, benefits, and eligibility requirements.

Step 3: Choose the Right Credit Card for You

Choose the ideal credit card to suit your needs and lifestyle with SBI's diverse range of credit cards tailored for various customer segments, including travel, shopping, dining, and fuel.

Step 4: Check Eligibility

Before you apply for a credit card, you can find out if you are eligible by checking the requirements on the app. These requirements usually include things like your age, how much money you make, your credit score, and your existing relationship with the bank.

Step 5: Fill in the Application

After you have selected the right credit card and ensured that you meet the requirements, the next step is to fill in an application form. This form will need your basic details, income and other related details. Make sure that you have provided accurate information to avoid any processing delays.

Step 6: Review and Submit

Kindly take a moment to review the details you've entered before submitting your application. Before submitting the application, make sure that all the information is accurate.

Step 7: Application Processing

Once you have applied, SBI cards team will process it and assess your eligibility based on the credit card approval criteria. This may take a few business days, and you will receive the status through the app or by email/SMS.

Step 8: Receive Your Credit Card

Get ready for an instant virtual credit card upon approval ! Moreover, a brand new SBI credit card is on its way to your registered address.

Tips for a Successful Credit Card Application

To apply for SBI credit card, follow these tips:

  • Make sure you meet the eligibility criteria for the credit card you're applying for.
  • Fill out accurate and up-to-date information in the application form.
  • Keep a healthy credit score, as it has a significant impact on credit card approval chances.

Conclusion

Applying for an SBI credit card through the YONO SBI app is an easy and convenient process that gives you access to a wide range of rewards and benefits. With a variety of credit cards designed to meet different needs, you're certain to find one that suits your lifestyle perfectly.

Embrace the digital revolution and apply for your ideal credit card through the YONO SBI app today. Access a world of financial opportunities and maximize the benefits of your purchases with SBI's excellent credit card options!
