## 📋 Executive Overview
The Annapurna Scheme is a loan scheme offered by the State Bank of India (SBI) to support women entrepreneurs in the food industry. The scheme provides financing for setting up or expanding food businesses, with a focus on women-owned and operated enterprises.


---

## 🎯 Objectives
- To support women entrepreneurs in the food industry
- To provide financing for setting up or expanding food businesses
- To promote women-owned and operated enterprises

## ✅ Eligibility
Women entrepreneurs in the food industry, with a minimum age of 21 years and a maximum age of 55 years, are eligible to apply for the scheme. The applicant should have a good credit history and a viable business plan.


**Target Beneficiaries:** women entrepreneurs, food industry
**Geographic Scope:** Pan-India

---

## 🎁 Benefits
The scheme offers a loan amount of up to Rs. 50,000 to Rs. 50 lakhs, with a repayment period of up to 7 years. The interest rate is competitive, and the scheme also offers a moratorium period of up to 6 months.


---

## 💳 Financial Support
The scheme provides financing for setting up or expanding food businesses, including working capital and term loans.
