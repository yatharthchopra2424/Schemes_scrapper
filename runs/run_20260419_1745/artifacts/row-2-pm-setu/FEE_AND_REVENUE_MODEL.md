## Pricing Principles
Our pricing model is based on the following principles:
1. **Value-based pricing**: Our fees are based on the value we bring to our clients, rather than just the time spent on their projects.
2. **Tiered pricing**: We offer different pricing tiers to accommodate clients of varying sizes and needs.
3. **Success-based fees**: Our success fees are tied to the success of our clients' projects, ensuring that our interests are aligned with theirs.
4. **Transparency**: We are transparent about our pricing and fees, ensuring that our clients understand what they are paying for.

The risk profile of the PM-SETU scheme is moderate, given its processing time and evidence of success from the crawled data. This risk profile is reflected in our pricing model, with higher success fees for clients who require more support and guidance throughout the process.
