# Pitch & Sales Scripts
## Philosophy
We are advisors, not salespeople. We diagnose first. We only pitch a scheme if it fits the client. We never oversell success rates.

## 1. Discovery Call Script (First Contact — 20 min)
### Opening (2 min)
We specialize in helping businesses like yours navigate the PM-SETU scheme, which provides financial support for skill development and entrepreneurship. How can we assist you today?

### Discovery Questions (6-8 questions tailored to this scheme's eligibility criteria)
1. What type of business do you operate, and what are your primary goals for skill development or entrepreneurship?
2. Are you currently involved in any skill development or entrepreneurship initiatives?
3. What specific challenges are you facing in terms of skill development or entrepreneurship that you think the PM-SETU scheme could help address?
4. Have you previously applied for or received funding under any government schemes?
5. Can you tell us about your current financial situation and how you plan to utilize the financial support from the PM-SETU scheme?
6. Are there any specific areas of skill development or entrepreneurship you are interested in focusing on?
7. How do you envision the PM-SETU scheme contributing to your business's growth and success?
8. Are you aware of the eligibility criteria for the PM-SETU scheme, and do you believe your business meets these requirements?

### Qualification Checklist (internal — don't read aloud)
- Is the business located in India?
- Does the business fall under the categories of youth, unemployed, underemployed, or entrepreneurs?
- Is the business interested in acquiring skills and knowledge in emerging technologies?
- Does the business have a proposal and business plan ready?
- Is the business aware of the required documents (proposal, business plan, eligibility criteria)?

### Transition to Pitch (2-3 lines that bridge from discovery to pitching THIS specific scheme)
Based on what you've shared, it seems like the PM-SETU scheme could be a good fit for your business. This scheme provides financial support for skill development and entrepreneurship, which aligns with your goals. We'd like to explain how our services can help you navigate the application process and increase your chances of approval.

## 2. Scheme Pitch (5 min)
### Structure: Problem → Scheme → Value → Proof → Next Step
#### Problem framing:
Many businesses struggle to find the right resources and support for skill development and entrepreneurship, leading to missed opportunities for growth and development.

#### Scheme intro:
The PM-SETU scheme, administered by the Ministry of Skill Development and Entrepreneurship, offers financial support to institutions and individuals for skill development and entrepreneurship. This includes access to high-quality skill training, practical training, industry partnerships, and entrepreneurship opportunities.

#### Why they qualify (personalised):
Based on our discussion, it seems like your business could benefit from the PM-SETU scheme, especially considering your interests in [specific areas of skill development or entrepreneurship mentioned during the discovery call].
