## 📋 Executive Overview
The PM-SETU scheme is a flagship program of the Government of India, launched by the Ministry of Skill Development and Entrepreneurship, with the objective of creating a robust ecosystem for skill development and entrepreneurship. The scheme aims to strengthen the existing infrastructure for imparting skilling, with a focus on emerging technologies, and to create new institutions for skilling in areas where there are no ITIs. The scheme also aims to create a network of institutions that can provide high-quality skill training, with an emphasis on practical training and industry partnerships.


---

## 🎯 Objectives
- To create a robust ecosystem for skill development and entrepreneurship
- To strengthen the existing infrastructure for imparting skilling
- To create new institutions for skilling in areas where there are no ITIs
- To create a network of institutions that can provide high-quality skill training
- To provide practical training and industry partnerships

## ✅ Eligibility
The eligibility criteria for the PM-SETU scheme include youth, unemployed, underemployed, and entrepreneurs who are interested in acquiring skills and knowledge in emerging technologies.


**Target Beneficiaries:** youth, unemployed, underemployed, entrepreneurs
**Geographic Scope:** Pan-India

---

## 🎁 Benefits
The benefits of the PM-SETU scheme include access to high-quality skill training, practical training, industry partnerships, and entrepreneurship opportunities.


---

## 💳 Financial Support
The PM-SETU scheme provides financial support to institutions and individuals for skill development and entrepreneurship.
