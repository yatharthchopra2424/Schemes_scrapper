# Application Playbook
## How to Use This File
For each new client case, assign it a Case ID, pick the target scheme, and follow the steps below.

## Stage 1: Eligibility Verification (Day 1–3)
### Checklist
✅ Is the startup recognized by DPIIT?
✅ Is the startup incorporated not more than 2 years ago at the time of application?
✅ Does the startup have a business idea to develop a product or service with market fit, viable commercialization, and scope of scaling?
✅ Is the startup using technology in its core product or service, or business model, or distribution model, or methodology to solve the problem being targeted?
✅ Has the startup not received more than Rs 10 lakh of monetary support under any other Central or State Government scheme?
✅ Does the startup have at least 51% shareholding by Indian promoters at the time of application to the incubator for the scheme?

## Stage 2: Document Collection (Day 3–10)
### Universal Documents
* Certificate of Incorporation
* PAN of the entity
* GST registration certificate
* Bank statements
* Udyam registration certificate
* ITR (Income Tax Return) of the startup
### Scheme-Specific Documents
* Pitch deck or business description
* Authorization letter from authorized signatory
* Details of funding received (if any)
### Document Quality Check
* File size: less than 5MB
* File name: should match the document name
* Certification requirements: self-certified documents are accepted
* Language: English or Hindi
### Client Communication Template
"Dear [Client], we are applying for the Startup India Seed Fund Scheme. Please find attached the required documents. If you have any questions or concerns, please do not hesitate to reach out to us. Thank you for your cooperation."

## Stage 3: Application Drafting (Day 10–18)
### Project Report / DPR
Not required for this scheme.
### Application Form Filling Protocol
1. Use the Startup India portal to fill the application form.
2. Ensure all fields are complete and accurate.
3. Upload required documents.
4. Review and submit the application.
5. Take a screenshot of the submission confirmation.
6. Store the reference number for future reference.
7. Set follow-up reminders for the next steps.
### Internal Review Gate
* All fields are complete
* Documents are attached
* Declarations are signed

## Stage 4: Submission & Follow-Up (Day 18–30)
### Submission Protocol
1. Submit the application on the Startup India portal.
2. Take a screenshot of the submission confirmation.
3. Store the reference number for future reference.
4. Set follow-up reminders for the next steps.
### Follow-Up Script
"Dear [Nodal Officer], we have submitted our application for the Startup India Seed Fund Scheme. Could you please update us on the status of our application? Thank you for your time and assistance."
### Escalation Path
- Level 1: [Portal helpdesk](https://seedfund.startupindia.gov.in/contact) or helpline (1800-11-5565)
- Level 2: [Nodal Officer](dipp-startups@nic.in) or [TO BE FILLED BY CONSULTANT]
- Level 3: RTI filing if no response within statutory SLA

## Stage 5: Post-Approval (Day 30–90+)
### Approval Actions
* Verify sanctioned amount
* Utilization certificate
* Invoice success fee
* Follow up with the client for feedback and testimonials
