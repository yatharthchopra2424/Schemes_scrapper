## Pricing Principles
Our pricing principles are based on the following:
1. **Value-based pricing**: Our fees are based on the value we bring to our clients.
2. **Transparency**: We are transparent about our pricing and fees.
3. **Flexibility**: We offer flexible pricing options to accommodate different client needs.
4. **Risk-sharing**: We share the risk with our clients by offering a success-based fee structure.

Note: The risk profile of the Startup India Seed Fund Scheme is moderate, considering the processing time and evidence of success from the crawled data.
