# Pitch & Sales Scripts
## Philosophy
We are advisors, not salespeople. We diagnose first. We only pitch a scheme if it fits the client. We never oversell success rates.

## 1. Discovery Call Script (First Contact — 20 min)
### Opening (2 min)
Hello, I'm [TO BE FILLED BY CONSULTANT], a senior government scheme consulting expert. I'm calling today to discuss the Startup India Seed Fund Scheme (SISFS) and how it can benefit your startup. The SISFS is a government-backed scheme that provides financial assistance to startups for proof of concept, prototype development, product trials, market entry, and commercialization.

### Discovery Questions (6-8 questions tailored to this scheme's eligibility criteria)
1. Can you tell me a little about your startup and what problem you're trying to solve?
2. How long has your startup been incorporated, and what is your current stage of development?
3. What kind of product or service are you developing, and how does it address a market need?
4. Have you received any funding or support from other government schemes or investors?
5. Can you share your team's background and expertise in the industry?
6. What are your plans for scaling up your business, and how do you see the SISFS supporting your growth?

### Qualification Checklist (internal — don't read aloud)
- Is the startup recognized by DPIIT?
- Is the startup incorporated not more than 2 years ago?
- Does the startup have a business idea to develop a product or service with market fit, viable commercialization, and scope of scaling?
- Has the startup received more than Rs 10 lakh of monetary support under any other Central or State Government scheme?

### Transition to Pitch (2-3 lines that bridge from discovery to pitching THIS specific scheme)
Based on what you've shared, I believe the Startup India Seed Fund Scheme could be a great fit for your startup. The scheme provides up to Rs 20 lakhs as a grant for validation of proof of concept, prototype development, or product trials, and up to Rs 50 lakhs of investment for market entry, commercialization, or scaling up.

### Structure: Problem → Scheme → Value → Proof → Next Step
**Problem framing:** Many startups struggle to secure funding for proof of concept, prototype development, and product trials, which can hinder their growth and scalability.
**Scheme intro:** The Startup India Seed Fund Scheme (SISFS) is a government-backed scheme that provides financial assistance to startups for proof of concept, prototype development, product trials, market entry, and commercialization.
