### Startup India Seed Fund Scheme (SISFS)
- **Scheme ID:** SCH-001
- **Category:** Grant
- **Geography:** Pan-India
- **Administering Body:** Department for Promotion of Industry and Internal Trade (DPIIT), Ministry of Commerce and Industry
- **Portal / Application URL:** https://www.startupindia.gov.in/
- **Current Status:** Open
- **Window:** Always Open

#### Eligibility Criteria
- Business type: Startups
- Age of business: Not more than 2 years old
- Sector: All sectors
- Revenue cap: No specific revenue cap mentioned
- Employee count: No specific employee count mentioned
- Other: The startup should have a business idea to develop a product or service with market fit, viable commercialization, and scope of scaling. The startup should not have received more than Rs 10 lakh of monetary support under any other Central or State Government scheme.

#### What It Offers
- Amount / Benefit: Up to Rs. 20 Lakhs as grant for validation of Proof of Concept, or prototype development, or product trials, and up to Rs. 50 Lakhs of investment for market entry, commercialization, or scaling up through convertible debentures or debt or debt-linked instruments
- Disbursement mode: The grant will be disbursed in milestone-based installments, and the debt or convertible debentures will be provided at a rate of interest of not more than the prevailing repo rate.
- Duration of benefit: The grant will be disbursed over a period of time, and the debt or convertible debentures will have a tenure of not more than 60 months (5 years).

#### Documents Required
1. Certificate of Incorporation
2. PAN of the entity
3. Pitch deck or business description
4. Authorization letter from authorized signatory
5. Details of funding received (if any)

#### Application Process
1. Startups can apply for the scheme on the Startup India portal.
2. The application will be evaluated by the Incubator Seed Management Committee (ISMC) based on criteria such as market size, feasibility, potential impact, novelty, team strength, and fund utilization plan.
3. The ISMC will select startups for seed support, and the selected startups will receive seed funding from the incubator.
