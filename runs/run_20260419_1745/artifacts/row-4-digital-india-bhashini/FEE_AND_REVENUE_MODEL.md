## Pricing Principles
Our pricing principles are based on the following:
1. **Value-Based Pricing**: Our fees are based on the value we bring to our clients, taking into account the complexity and potential impact of the scheme on their business.
2. **Transparency**: We maintain complete transparency in our pricing, ensuring that our clients are aware of all costs and fees associated with our services.
3. **Risk Profile**: Given the Digital India Bhashini Scheme's focus on AI and technology, there is a moderate risk profile associated with its implementation and success. This risk is factored into our pricing strategy.
4. **Market Competitiveness**: Our pricing is competitive with the market, ensuring that our clients receive the best possible value for their investment.
