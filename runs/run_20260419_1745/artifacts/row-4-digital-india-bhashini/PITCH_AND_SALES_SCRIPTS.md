# Pitch & Sales Scripts
## Philosophy
We are advisors, not salespeople. We diagnose first. We only pitch a scheme if it fits the client. We never oversell success rates.

## 1. Discovery Call Script (First Contact — 20 min)
### Opening (2 min)

### Discovery Questions (6-8 questions tailored to this scheme's eligibility criteria)
1. Can you tell me a little about your business and what you do?
2. How long has your business been operational?
3. Are you currently utilizing any digital services or platforms for your business operations?
4. Have you explored any government schemes or initiatives for support?
5. What are your primary challenges or areas where you think you could use some support or innovation?

- Is the business based in India?
- Is the business a startup, entrepreneur, or MSME?
- Does the business have potential for digital innovation or improvement?
- Is the business open to exploring government schemes for support?

## 2. Scheme Pitch (5 min)
### Structure: Problem → Scheme → Value → Proof → Next Step
#### Problem framing:
Many businesses in India face challenges in accessing digital services, improving their online presence, and leveraging technology for innovation and growth. This can hinder their ability to compete effectively and reach their full potential.

#### Scheme intro:
The Digital India Bhashini Scheme is a government initiative designed to address these challenges by providing support for the development of digital infrastructure, enhancing digital literacy, and fostering an environment conducive to innovation and entrepreneurship. With a focus on citizens, startups, entrepreneurs, and MSMEs, this scheme offers a comprehensive approach to bridging the digital divide and promoting economic growth.

#### Why they qualify (personalised):
Given your business's focus on [specific area of business], and your interest in digital innovation, I believe you qualify for the benefits offered by the Digital India Bhashini Scheme. Specifically, the scheme's emphasis on [aspect of the scheme that aligns with the business's interests or challenges] could directly address [specific challenge or goal mentioned by the business].

#### Proof:
We've helped [N] similar businesses get approved for this scheme. [TO BE FILLED BY CONSULTANT]

The next step would be to conduct a free eligibility check to confirm your business's qualification for the scheme. This process is straightforward and will give us a clear understanding of how the Digital India Bhashini Scheme can support your business objectives. We work on a success fee-only model, so you only pay if we successfully secure the scheme's benefits for your business.
