NCW Women’s Helpline

NCW 24×7 Women Helpline – 14490 is intended to provide Digital Complaint Registration System for women affected by violence. The Helpline platform is developed by Digital India Corporation. The Helpline is a vital service provided by the National Commission for Women, offering assistance and support to women facing various challenges. Available round-the-clock, the helpline provides counseling, legal assistance and guidance on issues such as domestic violence, sexual harassment and emotional distress.

It also connects women to police, One Stop Centre, hospital and authorities for further support and protection. Whether dealing with personal crises or needing legal advice, the helpline ensures women in need have access to help and resources at any time. This Women Helpline is being operated from the premises of the National Commission for Women, New Delhi from 21st July 2021.
