## 📋 Executive Overview
Digital India is a flagship programme of the Government of India, launched on July 1, 2015, by Honourable Prime Minister Shri Narendra Modi with the vision to transform India into a digitally empowered society and knowledge economy.

- To promote e-governance and digital literacy
- To enhance digital empowerment and inclusive growth
- To provide digital services and access to information
- To foster innovation and entrepreneurship in the digital economy
