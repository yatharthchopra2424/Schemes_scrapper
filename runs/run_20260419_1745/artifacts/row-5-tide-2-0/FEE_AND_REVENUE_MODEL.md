## Pricing Principles
Our pricing principles are based on the following:
1. **Value-based pricing**: Our fees are based on the value we bring to our clients.
2. **Transparency**: We are transparent about our fees and pricing structure.
3. **Flexibility**: We offer flexible pricing options to accommodate different client needs.
4. **Risk-sharing**: We share the risk with our clients by offering performance-based fees.

Note: The TIDE 2.0 scheme has a relatively long processing time, which may affect our revenue lag assumption.
