### TIDE 2.0
- **Scheme ID:** SCH-001
- **Category:** Grant
- **Geography:** Pan-India
- **Administering Body:** Ministry of Electronics and Information Technology (MeitY)
- **Portal / Application URL:** https://msh.meity.gov.in/schemes/tide
- **Current Status:** Open
- **Window:** Rolling basis, with releases of funds based on progress and recommendations of PRSG

#### Eligibility Criteria
- Business type: Institutions of higher learning and R&D organizations having incubation facilities and established linkages with large companies, industry bodies, and industry clusters
- Age of business: Not specified
- Sector: ICT startups using emerging technologies such as IoT, AI, Block-chain, Robotics, etc.
- Revenue cap: Not specified
- Employee count: Not specified
- Other: Incubators to maintain separate audited accounts for the project, Intellectual property and rights to be agreed upon between participating organizations

#### What It Offers
- Amount / Benefit: Up to Rs. 40 Lakh for product development and market outreach
- Disbursement mode: Grant-in-aid
- Duration of benefit: Five years

#### Documents Required
1. Proposal
2. Utilization Certificate
3. Technical and Financial Progress Report
4. Audited statement of accounts

#### Application Process
1. Proposals to be submitted by institutions of higher learning and R&D organizations
2. Evaluation by a Project Review and Steering Group (PRSG)
3. Recommendations of PRSG for funding
