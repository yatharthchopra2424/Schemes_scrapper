## 📋 Executive Overview
The Technology Incubation and Development of Entrepreneurs (TIDE) 2.0 scheme aims to promote tech entrepreneurship through financial and technical support to incubators engaged in supporting ICT startups using emerging technologies such as IoT, AI, Block-chain, Robotics, etc. in seven pre-identified areas of societal relevance.


---

## 🎯 Objectives
- Promote tech entrepreneurship through financial and technical support to incubators
- Support ICT startups using emerging technologies
- Create a holistic ecosystem to support technology startups and incubation centers
- Identify and create necessary linkages for leveraging complementary strengths
- Handhold approximately 2000 tech start-ups over a period of five years

## ✅ Eligibility
Institutions of higher learning and R&D organizations having incubation facilities and established linkages with large companies, industry bodies, and industry clusters


**Target Beneficiaries:** startups, incubators, ICT startups
**Geographic Scope:** Pan-India

---

## 🎁 Benefits
Financial support, technical support, mentorship, and access to networks and resources


---

## 💳 Financial Support
Grant-in-aid of up to Rs. 264.62 Crores to be released over a period of five years

## 📝 How to Apply

## Application Process
Proposals to be submitted by institutions of higher learning and R&D organizations, evaluated by a Project Review and Steering Group (PRSG)

## Required Documents

- Utilization Certificate

## ⏰ Deadlines
