### Startup India Scheme
- **Scheme ID:** SCH-001
- **Category:** Government grants
- **Geography:** Pan-India
- **Administering Body:** Ministry of Commerce and Industry
- **Portal / Application URL:** https://www.startupindia.gov.in/
- **Current Status:** Open
- **Window:** Always Open

#### Eligibility Criteria
- Business type: Startups in the manufacturing sector
- Age of business: Up to 10 years from the date of incorporation/registration
- Sector: All sectors
- Revenue cap: Turnover not exceeding ₹100 crore
- Employee count: 
- Other: The entity should be working towards innovation, development, or improvement of products or processes or services, or if it is a scalable business model with a high potential of employment generation or wealth creation.

#### What It Offers
- Amount / Benefit: Funding support through a Fund of Funds with a corpus of ₹10,000 crore
- Disbursement mode: Fund of Funds
- Duration of benefit: 4 years

#### Documents Required
1. Certificate of Incorporation
2. Memorandum of Association
3. Articles of Association
4. PAN card
5. Aadhaar card
6. Bank account details

#### Application Process
1. Register on the Startup India portal
2. Fill out the application form
3. Upload required documents
4. Submit the application
5. Wait for verification and approval
