# Application Playbook
## How to Use This File
For each new client case, assign it a Case ID, pick the target scheme, and follow the steps below.

## Stage 1: Eligibility Verification (Day 1–3)
### Checklist
✅ Registration type: Private limited company, partnership firm, or limited liability partnership
✅ Turnover threshold: Less than ₹100 crore
✅ Employee count: No specific limit
✅ Sector: Any sector, with a focus on innovation and scalability
✅ Age of business: Up to 10 years from the date of incorporation/registration
✅ Other conditions: The entity should be working towards innovation, development, or improvement of products or processes, or have a scalable business model with high potential for employment generation or wealth creation.

## Stage 2: Document Collection (Day 3–10)
### Universal Documents
* PAN card
* ITR (Income Tax Return)
* GST (Goods and Services Tax) registration
* Bank statements
* Udyam registration (if applicable)

### Scheme-Specific Documents
* DIPP (Department of Industrial Policy and Promotion) recognition certificate (if applicable)
* Patent or intellectual property registration documents (if applicable)
* Business plan and pitch deck

### Document Quality Check
* File size: Less than 5 MB
* File name: Should include the company name and document type
* Certification requirements: Documents should be self-certified by the applicant
* Language: English or Hindi

### Client Communication Template
"Dear [Client], 
We are excited to support your application for the GENESIS scheme. Please find attached the required documents and checklist. If you have any questions or concerns, please do not hesitate to reach out to us. 
Best regards, 
[Your Name]"

## Stage 3: Application Drafting (Day 10–18)
### Project Report / DPR
Not required for this scheme.

### Application Form Filling Protocol
1. Ensure all fields are complete and accurate.
2. Use the correct format for dates and numbers.
3. Attach all required documents.
4. Ensure the application is submitted online through the Startup India portal.
5. Keep a record of the application submission, including the date and time.
6. Ensure the application is submitted before the deadline.
7. Use a clear and concise language in the application.
8. Ensure all declarations are signed and stamped (if required).

### Internal Review Gate
* All fields are complete and accurate.
* All required documents are attached.
* The application is submitted online through the Startup India portal.

## Stage 4: Submission & Follow-Up (Day 18–30)
### Submission Protocol
* Take a screenshot of the submission confirmation page.
* Store the reference number and application ID.
* Set follow-up reminders for the next steps.

### Follow-Up Script
"Dear [Nodal Officer],
We are following up on our application for the GENESIS scheme, submitted on [Date]. Our application ID is [ID]. Could you please provide us with an update on the status of our application?
Best regards, 
[Your Name]"

### Escalation Path
- Level 1: [Portal helpdesk](https://www.startupindia.gov.in/)
- Level 2: [Nodal officer](mailto:startupindia@nic.in)
- Level 3: RTI (Right to Information) filing, if no response within the statutory SLA.

## Stage 5: Post-Approval (Day 30–90+)
### Approval Actions
* Verify the sanctioned amount and utilization certificate.
* Ensure successful invoice and payment.
* Calculate and pay the success fee.

Please note that the above playbook is based on the provided evidence and may require modifications based on the specific requirements of the GENESIS scheme and the client's needs.
