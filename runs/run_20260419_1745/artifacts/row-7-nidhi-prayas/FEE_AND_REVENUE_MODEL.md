## Pricing Principles
Our pricing principles are based on the following:
1. **Value-based pricing**: Our fees are based on the value we bring to our clients.
2. **Risk-sharing**: We share the risk with our clients by offering a success-based fee structure.
3. **Transparency**: We are transparent about our fees and costs.
4. **Flexibility**: We offer flexible pricing options to accommodate different client needs.

The GENESIS scheme has a relatively low risk profile due to its focus on supporting innovative startups in Tier II and Tier III cities. However, the processing time for the scheme can be lengthy, which may affect our revenue lag assumption.
