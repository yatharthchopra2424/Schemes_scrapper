# Pitch & Sales Scripts
## Philosophy
We are advisors, not salespeople. We diagnose first. We only pitch a scheme if it fits the client. We never oversell success rates.

## 1. Discovery Call Script (First Contact — 20 min)
### Opening (2 min)
Hello, I'm [TO BE FILLED BY CONSULTANT], a senior government scheme consulting expert. I'm calling today to discuss the GENESIS scheme, a flagship initiative of the Ministry of Electronics and Information Technology (MeitY) to nurture and support startup ecosystems in Tier-II and Tier-III cities in India. Our goal is to empower startups to grow through innovation and design.

### Discovery Questions (6-8 questions tailored to this scheme's eligibility criteria)
1. Can you tell me about your startup and its current stage of development?
2. What problem does your startup solve, and how does it address the needs of your target market?
3. What is your startup's revenue model, and what are your projected growth plans?
4. Have you received any funding or investments to date?
5. Can you describe your team's experience and expertise in the industry?
6. Are you currently based in a Tier-II or Tier-III city in India?
7. What specific challenges is your startup facing, and how do you think the GENESIS scheme could support your growth?
8. Are you interested in learning more about the GENESIS scheme and how it can benefit your startup?

### Qualification Checklist (internal — don't read aloud)
- Is the startup based in a Tier-II or Tier-III city in India?
- Does the startup have a scalable business model with high potential for employment generation or wealth creation?
- Is the startup working towards innovation, development, or improvement of products or processes?
- Does the startup have a turnover of less than INR 100 crore?

### Transition to Pitch (2-3 lines that bridge from discovery to pitching this specific scheme — use the real scheme name and benefit amount)
Based on our conversation, I believe your startup may be eligible for the GENESIS scheme, which provides funding support of up to INR 10,000 crore to startups in Tier-II and Tier-III cities. This scheme can help your startup grow and scale, and I'd be happy to guide you through the application process.

## 2. Scheme Pitch (5 min)

**Problem framing:** Many startups in Tier-II and Tier-III cities face challenges in accessing funding, mentorship, and resources, hindering their growth and scalability.
**Scheme intro:** The GENESIS scheme is a flagship initiative of the Ministry of Electronics and Information Technology (MeitY) that provides funding support of up to INR 10,000 crore to startups in Tier-II and Tier-III cities. The scheme aims to nurture and support startup ecosystems, empowering them to grow through innovation and design.
