### GENESIS Scheme
- **Scheme ID:** SCH-001
- **Category:** Government grants
- **Geography:** Pan-India
- **Administering Body:** Ministry of Electronics and Information Technology (MeitY)
- **Portal / Application URL:** https://www.startupindia.gov.in/
- **Current Status:** Open
- **Window:** Always Open

#### Eligibility Criteria
- Business type: Startups in Tier II and Tier III cities
- Age of business: Less than 10 years
- Sector: All sectors
- Revenue cap: Less than ₹100 crore
- Employee count: Not specified
- Other: The startup should be working towards innovation, development, or improvement of products or processes or services, or if it is a scalable business model with a high potential of employment generation or wealth creation.

#### What It Offers
- Amount / Benefit: Funding support through a Fund of Funds with a corpus of ₹10,000 crore
- Disbursement mode: Fund of Funds
- Duration of benefit: Not specified

#### Documents Required
1. Certificate of Incorporation
2. Memorandum and Articles of Association
3. PAN and TAN of the company
4. Audited financial statements for the last three years
5. Detailed project report

#### Application Process
1. Register on the Startup India portal
2. Fill out the application form
3. Upload required documents
4. Submit the application
5. Wait for the verification and approval process
