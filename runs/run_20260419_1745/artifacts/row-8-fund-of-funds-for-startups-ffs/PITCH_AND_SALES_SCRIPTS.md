# Pitch & Sales Scripts
## Philosophy
We are advisors, not salespeople. We diagnose first. We only pitch a scheme if it fits the client. We never oversell success rates.

## 1. Discovery Call Script (First Contact — 20 min)
### Opening (2 min)
Hello, I'm [TO BE FILLED BY CONSULTANT], and I'm excited to learn more about your business and see if we can help you with the GENESIS scheme, which supports innovative startups in Tier II and Tier III cities in India. Can you tell me a little bit about your company and what you're working on?

### Discovery Questions (6-8 questions tailored to this scheme's eligibility criteria)
1. What problem does your startup solve, and how does it address the needs of your target market?
2. Can you tell me about your team and their experience in the industry?
3. What is your current revenue, and what are your projected growth plans?
4. Are you incorporated as a private limited company, partnership firm, or limited liability partnership in India?
5. What is the date of incorporation, and have you been in operation for less than 10 years?
6. Can you describe your innovative product or service and how it is scalable?
7. Do you have any existing funding or investments, and if so, how much?
8. Are you based in a Tier II or Tier III city in India, and if so, which one?

### Qualification Checklist (internal — don't read aloud)
- Incorporated in India as a private limited company, partnership firm, or limited liability partnership
- Less than 10 years old
- Turnover less than INR 100 crore
- Working towards innovation, development, or improvement of products or processes
- Scalable business model with high potential for employment generation or wealth creation

### Transition to Pitch (2-3 lines that bridge from discovery to pitching THIS specific scheme — use the real scheme name and benefit amount)
Based on what you've shared, I think the GENESIS scheme could be a great fit for your startup. This scheme provides funding support to innovative startups in Tier II and Tier III cities, with a total corpus of INR 10,000 crore. We've helped several similar businesses successfully apply for this scheme, and I'd love to explore how we can support you in accessing up to INR 2,500 crore in funding.

## 2. Scheme Pitch (5 min)
### Structure: Problem → Scheme → Value → Proof → Next Step
**Problem framing:** Many innovative startups in Tier II and Tier III cities struggle to access funding, which hinders their growth and scalability.
**Scheme intro:** The GENESIS scheme, launched by the Ministry of Electronics and Information Technology, aims to support innovative startups in these cities by providing funding support through a Fund of Funds with a corpus of INR 10,000 crore.
**Why they qualify (personalised):** 
- Your startup is incorporated in India and is less than 10 years old.
- You have a scalable business model with high potential for employment generation or wealth creation.
- Your startup is working towards innovation, development, or improvement of products or processes.
- You are based in a Tier II or Tier III city in India.
