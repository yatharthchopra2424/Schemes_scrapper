## Pricing Principles
Our pricing principles are based on the following:
1. **Transparency**: We provide clear and transparent pricing to our clients.
2. **Value-based pricing**: Our pricing is based on the value we bring to our clients.
3. **Risk-sharing**: We share the risk with our clients through our success fee structure.
4. **Flexibility**: We offer flexible pricing tiers to cater to different client needs.

Note: The Udyam Registration Portal has a risk profile of low to moderate, as it is a government-backed scheme with a relatively straightforward registration process. However, the processing time and success rate may vary depending on the client's documentation and eligibility.
