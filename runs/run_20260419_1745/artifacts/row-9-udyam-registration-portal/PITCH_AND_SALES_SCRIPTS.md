# Pitch & Sales Scripts
## Philosophy
We are advisors, not salespeople. We diagnose first. We only pitch a scheme if it fits the client. We never oversell success rates.

## 1. Discovery Call Script (First Contact — 20 min)
### Opening (2 min)
Hello, I'm [Your Name] from [Your Company]. We specialize in helping businesses like yours navigate the Udyam Registration Portal scheme. This scheme is designed to provide benefits to micro, small, and medium enterprises in India. Can you tell me a little bit about your business and what challenges you're currently facing?

### Discovery Questions (6-8 questions tailored to this scheme's eligibility criteria)
1. What type of business do you have (manufacturing/services)?
2. How long has your business been operational?
3. What is your annual turnover?
4. What is your investment in plant and machinery or equipment?
5. Do you have an Aadhaar card and PAN card?
6. Have you previously registered under any other scheme or with the Udyam Registration Portal?
7. Are you looking to access credit or other financial benefits for your business?
8. Do you have all the required documents ready, such as bank account details and GSTIN (if applicable)?

### Qualification Checklist (internal — don't read aloud)
- Is the business a micro, small, or medium enterprise based on investment and turnover?
- Does the business have the required documents (Aadhaar, PAN, bank account details)?
- Is the business looking for benefits that the Udyam Registration Portal can offer?

### Transition to Pitch (2-3 lines that bridge from discovery to pitching THIS specific scheme)
Based on what you've shared, it seems like your business could greatly benefit from the Udyam Registration Portal scheme. This scheme can provide you with priority sector lending, easier access to credit, and other incentives. Would you like to learn more about how we can assist you in registering and maximizing the benefits of this scheme?

## 2. Scheme Pitch (5 min)
### Structure: Problem → Scheme → Value → Proof → Next Step
#### Problem framing:
Many micro, small, and medium enterprises in India face challenges in accessing credit and other benefits due to lack of recognition. The Udyam Registration Portal scheme is designed to address this issue by providing a unified platform for registration and offering various benefits.

#### Scheme intro:
The Udyam Registration Portal scheme is a government initiative that allows micro, small, and medium enterprises to register and become recognized. This recognition opens up opportunities for priority sector lending, easier access to credit, and other incentives that can help businesses grow.

#### Why they qualify (personalised):
Based on our conversation, your business seems to fit the criteria for the Udyam Registration Portal scheme, considering your investment in plant and machinery/equipment and annual turnover. You also mentioned looking for easier access to credit, which this scheme can facilitate.

#### Proof:
We've helped [TO BE FILLED BY CONSULTANT] similar businesses get approved for this scheme. Our expertise ensures a smooth registration process, maximizing the benefits you can receive.

#### Next step:
The next step would be for us to conduct a free eligibility check. If eligible, we can proceed with the registration process. Our success fee model ensures that you only pay if your registration is successful.
