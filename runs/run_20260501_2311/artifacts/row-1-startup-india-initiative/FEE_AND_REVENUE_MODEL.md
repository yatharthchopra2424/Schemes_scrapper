## Pricing Principles
The pricing principles are based on the Startup India initiative, which aims to promote entrepreneurship and innovation in India. The scheme provides various benefits, including tax exemptions, funding support, and simplified regulatory compliance. The pricing tiers are designed to cater to different types of clients, from micro and small enterprises to larger startups. The success fee is based on the scheme value, which is typically up to ₹20 lakhs for early-stage validation.
