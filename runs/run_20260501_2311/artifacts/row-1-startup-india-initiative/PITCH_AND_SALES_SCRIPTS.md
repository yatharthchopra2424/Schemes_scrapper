# Pitch & Sales Scripts
## Philosophy
We are advisors, not salespeople. We diagnose first. We only pitch a scheme if it fits the client. We never oversell success rates.

## 1. Discovery Call Script (First Contact — 20 min)
### Opening (2 min)
Hello, I'm [TO BE FILLED BY CONSULTANT], a senior government scheme consulting expert. I'm calling today to discuss the Startup India Initiative, a scheme designed to support startups in India. Our goal is to help entrepreneurs like you navigate the complexities of government schemes and unlock the benefits that can help your business thrive.

### Discovery Questions (6-8 questions tailored to this scheme's eligibility criteria)
1. Can you tell me a little about your startup and what motivated you to start your business?
2. What is your current annual turnover, and how long has your company been incorporated?
3. Are you working on innovative products, services, or processes that have the potential to generate employment and create wealth?
4. Do you have any existing funding or investments, and if so, can you tell me a bit about them?
5. Have you considered applying for any government schemes or incentives to support your startup's growth?
6. What are your biggest challenges right now, and how do you think a scheme like the Startup India Initiative could help address them?

### Qualification Checklist (internal — don't read aloud)
- Is the company a private limited company, LLP, or registered partnership?
- Is the annual turnover less than INR 100 Cr?
- Has the company been incorporated for less than 10 years?
- Is the company working on innovative products, services, or processes?
- Does the company have the potential to generate employment and create wealth?

### Transition to Pitch (2-3 lines that bridge from discovery to pitching THIS specific scheme — use the real scheme name and benefit amount)
Based on what you've shared, I believe the Startup India Initiative could be a great fit for your business. This scheme offers benefits like DPIIT recognition, tax exemptions, and access to funding of up to INR 20 Lakhs from the Startup India Seed Fund Scheme. Would you like to hear more about how this scheme can support your startup's growth and success?

## 2. Scheme Pitch (5 min)
### Structure: Problem → Scheme → Value → Proof → Next Step
**Problem framing:** As a startup, accessing funding and navigating regulatory complexities can be significant challenges. The Startup India Initiative addresses these challenges by providing a platform for recognition, funding, and tax benefits.

**Scheme intro:** The Startup India Initiative is a flagship program by the Government of India, aimed at promoting and supporting startups in the country. The scheme offers DPIIT recognition, which provides access to various benefits, including tax exemptions under Section 80IAC, easier public procurement norms, and faster patent examination.

**Why they qualify (personalised):** Based on our conversation, it seems your startup meets the eligibility criteria for the Startup India Initiative, including being a private limited company, having an annual turnover of less than INR 100 Cr, and working on innovative products or services.

- The company is a private limited company.
- The annual turnover is less than INR 100 Cr.
- The company is working on innovative products or services.
- The company has the potential to generate employment and create wealth.
