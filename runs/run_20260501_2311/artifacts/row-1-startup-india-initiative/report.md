## 📋 Executive Overview
The Startup India Initiative is a flagship program launched by the Government of India to foster innovation, support entrepreneurs, and drive sustainable economic growth. The initiative provides various benefits, including tax exemptions, easier compliance, and funding opportunities, to eligible startups.


---

## 🎯 Objectives
- Create a robust startup ecosystem with regulatory ease
- Provide tax incentives to eligible startups for 3 years
- Foster innovation and entrepreneurship in India
- Support entrepreneurs and drive sustainable economic growth


---

## 💰 Financial Summary

| Parameter | Value |
|---|---|
| Total Fund Corpus | ₹ 10000 Cr |
| Max Per Entity | Up to INR 20 Lakhs from SISFS for early-stage validation |

---

## ✅ Eligibility
Private Limited Company, LLP, or Registered Partnership with turnover < INR 100 Cr, incorporated < 10 years ago, working on innovative products/services/processes.


**Target Beneficiaries:** startups, entrepreneurs, MSME
**Geographic Scope:** Pan-India

---

## 🎁 Benefits
DPIIT recognition, 3-year income tax exemption under 80-IAC, self-certification under 9 labour laws, faster patent examination at 80% rebated fees.


---

## 💳 Financial Support
Fund of Funds for Startups (FFS) of INR 10,000 Cr managed by SIDBI invests in SEBI-registered Alternative Investment Funds (AIFs) that fund startups.


---

## 📝 How to Apply

## Application Process
1. Register on startupindia.gov.in 2. Fill DPIIT recognition form with business description 3. Upload Certificate of Incorporation, PAN, pitch deck 4. Submit and await approval (typically 2-7 business days).


**Application Portal:** [Apply Here](https://www.startupindia.gov.in/)

## Required Documents
- Certificate of Incorporation / Registration
- PAN of the entity
- Pitch deck or business description
- Authorization letter from authorized signatory
- Details of funding received (if any)


## ⏰ Deadlines
Rolling basis — no fixed deadline. Applications are accepted year-round.


---

## 🏢 Implementing Agency
Department for Promotion of Industry and Internal Trade (DPIIT), Ministry of Commerce and Industry


## 📞 Contact Details
Email: startupindia@dipp.gov.in | Helpline: 1800-11-5565


---

## ⚠️ Caveats & Conditions
- Tax benefits under 80-IAC require separate Inter-Ministerial Board (IMB) approval
- Startup status expires when turnover exceeds INR 100 Cr or after 10 years
