# Pitch & Sales Scripts

## Philosophy
We are advisors, not salespeople. We diagnose first. We only pitch a scheme if it fits the client. We never oversell success rates.

---

## 1. Discovery Call Script (First Contact — 20 min)

### Opening (2 min)
Good morning/afternoon! I specialize in helping Indian startups access government recognition and benefits through the Startup India Initiative by DPIIT. Many founders I speak with are focused on product development and market traction but may not be aware of the tax exemptions, compliance relaxations, and funding pathways available to them. I’d like to understand your startup’s current stage and goals to see if the Startup India Initiative aligns with your needs.

### Discovery Questions
1. When was your company incorporated or registered?
2. What is your current annual turnover, and has it ever exceeded INR 100 crore in any financial year since incorporation?
3. Are you incorporated as a Private Limited Company, Registered Partnership Firm, Limited Liability Partnership, or another eligible entity type under the Companies Act, 2013 or Partnership Act, 1932?
4. Is your business focused on innovation, development, or improvement of products, processes, or services—or do you have a scalable model with high potential for employment or wealth creation?
5. Has your entity been formed by splitting up or reconstructing an existing business?
6. Are you seeking benefits such as tax exemptions under Section 80-IAC, fast-tracked patent applications, or easier public procurement norms?
7. Are you aware of the Startup India Seed Fund Scheme (SISFS) or Credit Guarantee Scheme for Startups (CGSS) for funding support?
8. Have you previously applied for DPIIT recognition or any startup-related government scheme?

### Qualification Checklist (internal — don't read aloud)
- [ ] Incorporated or registered in India as a private limited company, partnership firm, LLP, Multi-State Cooperative Society, or State/UT Cooperative Society  
- [ ] Within 10 years of incorporation/registration (or up to 20 years for Deep Tech startups)  
- [ ] Turnover not exceeding INR 100 crore in any financial year since incorporation (or INR 300 crore for Deep Tech)  
- [ ] Working towards innovation, development, or improvement of products/processes/services, or has a scalable business model with high potential for employment generation or wealth creation  
- [ ] Not formed by splitting up or reconstruction of an existing business  

### Transition to Pitch
Based on your responses, it appears your startup may meet the core eligibility criteria for the Startup India Initiative. This recognition unlocks access to significant benefits—including up to three years of tax exemption under Section 80-IAC, fast-tracked IPR processing, and eligibility for funding schemes like SISFS (up to INR 20 lakhs) and CGSS (up to INR 20 crore). Would you like me to walk you through how this works and what the next steps would be?

## 2. Scheme Pitch (5 min)

### Structure: Problem → Scheme → Value → Proof → Next Step

**Problem framing:**
Early-stage startups in India often face high compliance costs, difficulty accessing collateral-free funding, delays in IPR protection, and barriers in public procurement due to lack of prior experience or turnover—diverting focus from innovation and growth.

**Scheme intro:**
The Startup India Initiative, launched by the Department for Promotion of Industry and Internal Trade (DPIIT), Ministry of Commerce and Industry, provides DPIIT recognition to eligible startups, enabling access to a wide range of benefits including tax exemptions, compliance relaxations, IPR fast-tracking, and funding support through schemes like the Startup India Seed Fund Scheme (SISFS) and Credit Guarantee Scheme for Startups (CGSS). The initiative operates on a rolling basis with no fixed deadline, and applications are processed via the National Single Window System (NSWS) at www.nsws.gov.in.
