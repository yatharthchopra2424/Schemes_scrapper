### Startup India Initiative
- **Scheme ID:** SCH-001
- **Category:** Recognition
- **Geography:** Pan-India
- **Administering Body:** Department for Promotion of Industry and Internal Trade (DPIIT), Ministry of Commerce and Industry
- **Portal / Application URL:** https://www.startupindia.gov.in/
- **Current Status:** Rolling
- **Window:** Always Open

#### Eligibility Criteria
- Business type: Private limited company, partnership firm, limited liability partnership, Multi-State Cooperative Society, or Cooperative Society
- Age of business: Up to 10 years from incorporation/registration (up to 20 years for Deep Tech Startups)
- Sector: Innovation, development, or improvement of products/processes/services; scalable business model with high potential for employment generation or wealth creation
- Revenue cap: Turnover not exceeding ₹200 crore for any financial year since incorporation (₹300 crore for Deep Tech Startups)
- Employee count: 
- Other: Must not be formed by splitting up or reconstruction of an existing business; must be working towards innovation/development/improvement of products/processes/services or have a scalable business model with high potential for employment generation/wealth creation

#### What It Offers
- Amount / Benefit: Access to tax exemptions (Section 80-IAC: 100% tax exemption on profits for 3 financial years; Section 56(2)(VIIB): Angel Tax exemption), self-certification under 9 labour and environment laws, 80% rebate on patent fees, relaxed public procurement norms, faster winding up (90 days), access to National Funding Schemes (Fund of Funds, Seed Fund Scheme, Credit Guarantee Scheme), private funding avenues, ecosystem initiatives (National Startup Awards, BHASKAR, MAARG), and State/UT incentives
- Disbursement mode: Tax offset, direct credit, reimbursement, facilitation
- Duration of benefit: One-time recognition; benefits vary (e.g., tax exemption for 3 years, patent rebate perpetual, winding up facility available as needed)

#### Documents Required
1. Certificate of Incorporation / Registration
2. PAN of the entity
3. Memorandum of Association for Pvt. Ltd. / LLP Deed
4. Board Resolution (if any)
5. Annual Accounts of the startup for the last three financial years
6. Income Tax returns for the last three financial years
7. Pitch deck or business description
8. Authorization letter from authorized signatory
9. Details of funding received (if any)

#### Application Process (numbered steps)
1. Register on the Startup India portal (www.startupindia.gov.in).
2. Apply for DPIIT recognition via the National Single Window System (NSWS) at www.nsws.gov.in by adding 'Registration as a Startup' to your dashboard.
3. Fill the startup application form with entity details, innovation description, and scalability aspects.
4. Upload required documents including Certificate of Incorporation/Registration, PAN, Memorandum of Association/LLP Deed, Board Resolution (if any), Annual Accounts, and Income Tax returns.
5. Submit the application and await approval (typically issued within 2 working days).
6. Upon recognition, access benefits via the Startup India portal and apply for specific schemes like tax exemptions, seed fund, or credit guarantee through respective portals.
