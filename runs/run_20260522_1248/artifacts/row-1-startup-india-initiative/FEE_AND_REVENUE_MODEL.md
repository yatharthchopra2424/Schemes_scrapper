## Pricing Principles
1. Value-based pricing aligned with the financial benefits unlocked for clients (e.g., tax exemptions, funding access).
2. Transparent fee structures with clear milestones for retainer and success fees.
3. Risk-adjusted pricing reflecting the scheme’s moderate processing time (2–7 business days for DPIIT recognition) and high success rate based on 170,743 recognised startups as of the portal data.
4. Scheme-specific note: The Startup India Initiative offers high-value, non-dilutive benefits (tax holidays, funding access, credit guarantees) with minimal financial risk to clients, justifying success fees in the 6–8% range for MSME/startup tier clients.
