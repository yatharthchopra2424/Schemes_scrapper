# Pitch & Sales Scripts

## Philosophy
We are advisors, not salespeople. We diagnose first. We only pitch a scheme if it fits the client. We never oversell success rates.

---

## 1. Discovery Call Script (First Contact — 20 min)

### Opening (2 min)
Good morning/afternoon! I specialize in helping startups and early-stage ventures access government support through the Startup India Initiative. Given your focus on innovation and growth, I’d like to understand if DPIIT recognition under this initiative could unlock benefits like tax exemptions, funding access, and compliance ease for your business.

### Discovery Questions
1. When was your company incorporated or registered?
2. What is your current annual turnover?
3. Are you incorporated as a private limited company, partnership firm, or LLP?
4. Are you working on innovation, development, or improvement of products/services with potential for employment or wealth creation?
5. Has your entity been formed by splitting up or reconstructing an existing business?
6. Are you seeking tax benefits under Section 80-IAC or exemption under Section 56(2)(VIIB)?
7. Do you need support for patent filing, easier public procurement, or faster winding up?
8. Are you interested in accessing the Fund of Funds, Seed Fund Scheme, or credit guarantee support?

### Qualification Checklist (internal — don't read aloud)
- [ ] Incorporated or registered in India as a private limited company, partnership firm, or LLP  
- [ ] Within 10 years of incorporation/registration (up to 20 years for DeepTech startups)  
- [ ] Annual turnover not exceeding INR 100 crore (INR 300 crore for DeepTech)  
- [ ] Working towards innovation, development, or improvement of products/services or has a scalable business model with high potential for employment generation or wealth creation  
- [ ] Not formed by splitting up or reconstructing an existing business  

### Transition to Pitch
Based on your responses, it appears you may qualify for DPIIT recognition under the Startup India Initiative, which unlocks access to benefits like up to 3 years of tax exemption on profits, credit guarantees up to ₹20 crore, and support from the Fund of Funds. Let me walk you through how this works.

## 2. Scheme Pitch (5 min)

### Structure: Problem → Scheme → Value → Proof → Next Step

**Problem framing:**
Startups face high compliance costs, difficulty accessing funding, delays in patent approvals, and barriers in government tenders due to lack of recognition and support structures.

**Scheme intro:**
The Startup India Initiative, administered by the Department for Promotion of Industry and Internal Trade (DPIIT), Ministry of Commerce and Industry, provides DPIIT recognition that enables access to a host of benefits including tax exemptions, easier compliance, IPR fast-tracking, and funding support. The initiative includes the Fund of Funds for Startups with a corpus of INR 10,000 crore, the Startup India Seed Fund Scheme (SISFS) offering up to INR 20 lakhs for early-stage validation, and the Credit Guarantee Scheme for Startups (CGSS) providing credit guarantees up to ₹20 crore per borrower.

**Why they qualify (personalised):**
- Incorporated as a private limited company, partnership firm, or LLP  
- Within 10 years of incorporation (or up to 20 years for DeepTech)  
- Annual turnover below INR 100 crore (or INR 300 crore for DeepTech)  
- Engaged in innovation, development, or improvement of products/services with scalability and employment/wealth creation potential
