### Startup India Initiative
- **Scheme ID:** SCH-001
- **Category:** Recognition
- **Geography:** Pan-India
- **Administering Body:** Department for Promotion of Industry and Internal Trade (DPIIT), Ministry of Commerce and Industry
- **Portal / Application URL:** https://www.startupindia.gov.in/
- **Current Status:** Rolling
- **Window:** Always Open

#### Eligibility Criteria
- Business type: Private limited company, partnership firm, or limited liability partnership
- Age of business: Within 10 years of incorporation/registration (up to 20 years for DeepTech startups)
- Sector: Innovation, development, or improvement of products/services or scalable business model with high potential for employment generation or wealth creation
- Revenue cap: Annual turnover not exceeding INR 100 crore (INR 300 crore for DeepTech)
- Employee count: 
- Other: Entity must not be formed by splitting up or reconstructing an existing business; must be incorporated or registered in India

#### What It Offers
- Amount / Benefit: Access to tax benefits including 100% tax exemption on profits for 3 consecutive years under Section 80-IAC; exemption under Section 56(2)(VIIB) for investments above fair market value up to INR 25 crore; self-certification under 9 labour and 3 environment laws; fast-tracking of patent applications with 80% rebate in fees; exemption from Earnest Money Deposit in government tenders; relaxed norms in public procurement; faster winding up within 90 days; access to Fund of Funds and Seed Fund Scheme; credit guarantee support; participation in MAARG mentorship platform, BHASKAR knowledge registry, and National Startup Awards
- Disbursement mode: Tax offset / Direct credit / Reimbursement (varies by benefit)
- Duration of benefit: One-time (recognition); tax exemption for 3 years; other benefits vary

#### Documents Required
1. Certificate of Incorporation / Registration
2. PAN of the entity
3. Memorandum of Association for Pvt. Ltd. / LLP Deed
4. Board Resolution (if any)
5. Annual Accounts of the startup for the last three financial years
6. Income Tax returns for the last three financial years
7. Pitch deck or business description
8. Authorization letter from authorized signatory
9. Details of funding received (if any)

#### Application Process (numbered steps)
1. Register on the Startup India portal (startupindia.gov.in)
2. Apply for DPIIT recognition via the National Single Window System (NSWS) at nsws.gov.in by adding 'Registration as a Startup' to approvals
3. Fill the application form with entity details, innovation description, and scalability
4. Upload required documents: Certificate of Incorporation/Registration, PAN, Memorandum of Association/LLP Deed, Board Resolution (if any), annual accounts and income tax returns for last three financial years
5. Submit the application and await approval (typically 2-7 business days)
6. Upon recognition, access benefits via Startup India portal and apply for specific schemes like Seed Fund Scheme or tax exemptions through respective portals
