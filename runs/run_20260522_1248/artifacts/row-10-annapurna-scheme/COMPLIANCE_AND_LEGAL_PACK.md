**Data Classification:** All client data is classified as Confidential and handled according to the highest security standards.

**Storage:** All data is stored in encrypted cloud storage with access controls. Encryption at rest and in transit using industry-standard protocols (AES-256).

**Access Control:** Access is limited to authorized personnel only. Role-based access control (RBAC) is enforced. Multi-factor authentication (MFA) is required for all access.

**Device Policy:** No personal devices are permitted for accessing or storing client data. All work must be conducted on firm-issued, secured devices.

**Retention:** Client data is retained for a period of two (2) years post case closure, after which it is securely deleted unless otherwise required by law or regulatory obligation.

**Breach Notification:** In the event of a data breach, the firm will notify affected clients and relevant authorities within 24 hours of discovery, in accordance with applicable data protection laws.

**Scheme-specific note:** All documents submitted to State Bank of India under Annapurna Scheme must be retained for a minimum of 3 years for audit purposes.

**Disposal:** Secure deletion methods are used for data disposal, ensuring data cannot be recovered.

**Compliance:** This policy complies with the Information Technology Act, 2000 and the SPDI Rules, 2011, and aligns with global standards such as ISO 27001 where applicable.
