## Pricing Principles
1. Value-based pricing aligned to the financial benefit delivered (₹50,000 max loan under Annapurna Scheme).
2. Transparent fee structure with no hidden charges; all fees disclosed upfront.
3. Success fee contingent on loan approval and disbursement, reducing client risk.
4. Scheme-specific note: The Annapurna Scheme has a moderate risk profile due to its collateral-free nature and reliance on applicant creditworthiness; processing time is typically 2–4 weeks based on SBI’s standard loan disbursement timelines, supporting a 3-month lag assumption for approvals in revenue modeling.

## Invoice Templates

### Retainer Invoice
**Description:** Retainer fee for consulting services related to the Annapurna Scheme loan application support, including eligibility assessment, document preparation, and application submission guidance.

### Success Fee Invoice
**Description:** Success fee for securing loan approval under the Annapurna Scheme implemented by State Bank of India, payable upon disbursement of the sanctioned loan amount to the client’s account.
