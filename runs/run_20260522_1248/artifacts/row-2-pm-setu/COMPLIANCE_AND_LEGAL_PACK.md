**Policy Statement:**  
[Firm Name] is committed to protecting the confidentiality, integrity, and availability of client
data. This policy outlines the minimum standards for handling sensitive personal and financial
information collected during consulting engagements.

**Data Storage:**  
All client data shall be stored in encrypted cloud storage environments with access controls
aligned to industry standards (e.g., ISO 27001, SOC 2). Encryption shall apply both at rest and in
transit.

**Access Control:**  
Access to client data is limited to authorized personnel only, based on role necessity. Multi-factor
authentication (2FA) is mandatory for all system access.

**Device Security:**  
No personal devices may be used to access, store, or transmit client data. All work must be
conducted on firm-issued, secured devices with updated antivirus and endpoint protection.

**Retention:**  
Client data shall be retained for a period of two (2) years after case closure, after which it shall
be securely deleted unless longer retention is required by law or scheme-specific guidelines.

**Breach Notification:**  
In the event of a data breach, [Firm Name] shall notify affected clients and relevant authorities
within 24 hours of confirmation.

**Scheme-specific note:**  
All documents submitted to Directorate General of Training (DGT), Ministry of Skill Development and Entrepreneurship (MSDE) under PM-SETU
must be retained for a minimum of 3 years for audit purposes.

**Review:**  
This policy shall be reviewed annually or as required by changes in regulations or scheme
guidelines.
