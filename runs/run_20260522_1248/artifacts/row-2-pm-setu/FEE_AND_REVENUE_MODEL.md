## Pricing Principles
1. **Value Alignment**: Fees are structured to reflect the tangible outcomes delivered, such as successful enrollment, certification, and placement support under PM-SETU.
2. **Risk Sharing**: Success fees are contingent on measurable results (e.g., trainee enrollment and certification), aligning consultant incentives with client success.
3. **Transparency**: All fee components are clearly communicated upfront, with retainers covering initial assessment and application support, and success fees tied to verified outcomes.
4. **Scheme-Specific Note**: PM-SETU has a rolling admissions process with batch-based commencements, leading to variable processing times (typically 1–3 months from application to enrollment). The scheme offers 100% funding for training costs and stipends for eligible groups, resulting in high perceived value but moderate operational risk due to placement not being guaranteed and stipend availability varying by project and trainee category. This supports a mid-range success fee structure where value is clear but outcomes depend on external factors like training center capacity and trainee eligibility.

## Invoice Templates

### Retainer Invoice
**Description**: Retainer fee for initial eligibility assessment, document verification, course counseling, and application submission support under the PM-SETU (Pradhan Mantri Kaushal Vikas Yojana - Skill Employment and Training Upgradation) scheme implemented by the Directorate General of Training (DGT), Ministry of Skill Development and Entrepreneurship (MSDE).

### Success Fee Invoice
**Description**: Success fee for successful enrollment of the trainee in an approved PM-SETU course, completion of training, and certification awarded by NCVT or SSC, under the PM-SETU scheme implemented by the Directorate General of Training (DGT), Ministry of Skill Development and Entrepreneurship (MSDE).
