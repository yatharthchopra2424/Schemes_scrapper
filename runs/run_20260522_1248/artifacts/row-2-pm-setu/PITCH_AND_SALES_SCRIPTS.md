# Pitch & Sales Scripts

## Philosophy
We are advisors, not salespeople. We diagnose first. We only pitch a scheme if it fits the client. We never oversell success rates.

---

## 1. Discovery Call Script (First Contact — 20 min)

### Opening (2 min)
Good morning/afternoon. I’m reaching out because I specialize in helping individuals and organizations access government skill development initiatives like the **PM-SETU scheme**, which provides **100% funded training** in industry-aligned courses with placement support. I’d like to understand your background and goals to see if this could be a fit for you or someone you know.

### Discovery Questions
- What is your current age, and are you between 15 and 45 years old?  
- What is your highest level of education completed (e.g., 5th pass, 10th pass, 12th pass)?  
- Are you currently employed, unemployed, or looking to switch careers or upskill?  
- Do you belong to any of the following categories: SC, ST, OBC, Minority, or Person with Disability (PwD)?  
- Are you interested in gaining certification in a specific trade or sector (e.g., IT, healthcare, manufacturing, retail)?  
- Would you benefit from a stipend during training, particularly if you are a woman, SC/ST, or PwD candidate?  
- Are you located near an ITI, PMKK, or accredited training center where you could attend in-person sessions?  
- Are you seeking support for job placement or self-employment after training?

### Qualification Checklist (internal — don't read aloud)
- [ ] Applicant is an Indian national  
- [ ] Age between 15 and 45 years  
- [ ] Minimum educational qualification met (as per specific job role: 5th pass to 12th pass or equivalent)  
- [ ] Belongs to any category (SC/ST/OBC/Minorities/PwD or general)  
- [ ] Meets eligibility criteria for the specific trade or course under PM-SETU  
- [ ] Willing to attend training at an accredited center (ITI/PMKK/partner)  
- [ ] Able to submit required documents (Aadhaar, educational certificates, DOB proof, etc.)  
- [ ] Interested in placement assistance or self-employment support post-training  

### Transition to Pitch
Based on your responses, it looks like you may be eligible for the **PM-SETU scheme**, which offers **free NSQF-aligned skill training** with potential stipend and placement support. Let me share how this works and how we’ve helped others like you get approved.

## 2. Scheme Pitch (5 min)

### Structure: Problem → Scheme → Value → Proof → Next Step

**Problem framing:**
Many youth, especially from disadvantaged backgrounds, face barriers to employment due to lack of industry-relevant skills, access to quality training, or financial constraints. Even when willing to upskill, they struggle to find free, certified programs that lead to real job opportunities or self-employment.

**Scheme intro:**
The **PM-SETU (Pradhan Mantri Kaushal Vikas Yojana - Employability and Skill Training Initiative)** is a **subsidy-based scheme** implemented by the **Directorate General of Training (DGT)** under the **Ministry of Skill Development and Entrepreneurship (MSDE)**. It provides **100% funding for training costs**, including trainer remuneration, raw materials, assessment, and certification fees. In eligible cases, a **stipend is provided during training** for women, SC/ST, and persons with disabilities. The scheme offers **free skill training in NSQF-aligned courses**, placement assistance, post-placement tracking, and certification recognized by **NCVT or SSC**.

**Why they qualify (personalised):**
- You are an Indian national aged between **15 and 45 years**  
- You meet the **minimum educational requirement** (ranging from 5th pass to 12th pass, depending on the course)  
- You belong to a **target beneficiary group** (youth, unemployed, school dropout, woman, SC/ST, minority, or PwD)  
- You are seeking **industry-aligned training with placement support** in a sector covered under PM-SETU  

**Proof:**

We offer a **free, no-obligation eligibility check** — we’ll review your profile and documents, confirm your fit for PM-SETU, and guide you through the application. We work on a **success fee only model**: you pay nothing unless we secure your enrollment and certification.
