### PM-SETU
- **Scheme ID:** SCH-001
- **Category:** Subsidy
- **Geography:** Pan-India
- **Administering Body:** Directorate General of Training (DGT), Ministry of Skill Development and Entrepreneurship (MSDE)
- **Portal / Application URL:** https://dgt.gov.in/en/scheme-guidelines-of-pmsetu
- **Current Status:** Rolling
- **Window:** Always Open (admissions open throughout the year based on batch commencement schedules at training centers)

#### Eligibility Criteria
- Business type: Not applicable (individual beneficiary scheme)
- Age of business: Not applicable
- Sector: All sectors (skill training across industries as per NSQF-aligned courses)
- Revenue cap: Not applicable (individual beneficiary scheme)
- Employee count: Not applicable
- Other: Indian nationals aged between 15 and 45 years; minimum educational qualification as per specific job role (5th pass to 12th pass or equivalent); belonging to any category including SC/ST/OBC/Minorities/PwD; meeting eligibility criteria defined for the specific trade or course

#### What It Offers
- Amount / Benefit: 100% funding for training costs (trainer remuneration, raw materials, assessment, certification fees); stipend during training (where applicable, especially for women, SC/ST, and persons with disabilities)
- Disbursement mode: Direct credit (to training providers for costs; stipend directly to eligible trainees' bank accounts)
- Duration of benefit: Duration of training course (varies by job role and sector; typically ranging from a few months to up to 2 years)

#### Documents Required
1. Aadhaar Card
2. Educational Certificates (Mark sheets and passing certificates)
3. Date of Birth Proof
4. Caste Certificate (if applicable)
5. Disability Certificate (if applicable)
6. Passport-sized photographs
7. Bank account details (for stipend, if applicable)
8. Domicile certificate (if required by state)

#### Application Process (numbered steps)
1. Identify the desired skill course and nearest training center (ITI/PMKK/accredited partner)
2. Visit the center or apply online through the State Skill Development Mission portal
3. Fill out the application form with personal and educational details
4. Submit required documents for verification
5. Undergo counseling and pre-assessment if required
6. Get enrolled in the selected course
7. Attend training as per schedule
8. Appear for final assessment
9. Receive certification and placement support
