# Comprehensive Scheme Masterclass & File Guide

## Scheme Deep Dive

### Scheme Overview
PM-SETU (Pradhan Mantri Skill Employment and Training for Upliftment) is a centrally sponsored skill development scheme implemented by the Directorate General of Training (DGT) under the Ministry of Skill Development and Entrepreneurship (MSDE). It operates on a pan-India basis with rolling admissions throughout the year, aligned with batch schedules at accredited training centers.

**Application Portal:** [https://dgt.gov.in/en/scheme-guidelines-of-pmsetu](https://dgt.gov.in/en/scheme-guidelines-of-pmsetu)

### Objectives
The scheme aims to:
- Provide industry-relevant skill training to enhance youth employability
- Align training programs with the National Skills Qualification Framework (NSQF)
- Ensure placement linkage for at least 70% of certified trainees
- Promote inclusion of disadvantaged and marginalized groups (SC/ST, minorities, women, persons with disabilities)
- Strengthen public-private partnerships in vocational training delivery
- Upgrade infrastructure and training capacity of ITIs and PMKKs
- Monitor and evaluate training outcomes via a robust Management Information System (MIS)

### Eligibility Matrix
| Criteria | Details |
|---------|--------|
| **Age** | 15 to 45 years (inclusive) |
| **Nationality** | Indian nationals only |
| **Educational Qualification** | Minimum as per specific job role: ranging from 5th pass to 12th pass or equivalent |
| **Social Category** | Open to all categories: SC/ST/OBC/Minorities/Persons with Disabilities (PwD) |
| **Target Groups** | Youth, unemployed, school dropouts, women, SC/ST, minorities, persons with disabilities |
| **Additional Requirements** | Must meet eligibility criteria defined for the specific trade or course under the scheme |

> **Key Caveat:** Eligibility varies by trade/course; applicants must verify specific educational and age requirements for their desired skill program.

### Benefits & Financial Support
| Benefit Category | Details |
|------------------|--------|
| **Training Costs** | 100% funding covering: trainer remuneration, raw materials, assessment fees, certification fees |
| **Stipend** | Provided during training period for eligible candidates (women, SC/ST, PwD) as per project norms; **not universal** |
| **Certification** | Nationally recognized certificate awarded by NCVT (National Council for Vocational Training) or SSC (Sector Skill Council) upon successful assessment |
| **Placement Support** | Placement assistance and post-placement tracking facilitated through industry linkages |
| **Industry Exposure** | On-the-job training opportunities and industry engagement |
| **Self/Wage Employment** | Support for both wage employment and self-employment pathways |

> **Important Notes:**
> - Stipend is **not guaranteed** for all courses; depends on project design and trainee category
> - Placement is **facilitated but not guaranteed**
> - Certification is **contingent on successful assessment completion**
> - Minimum attendance norms must be maintained to remain in the program

### Required Documents Checklist
| Document | Requirement Status | Notes |
|---------|-------------------|-------|
| Aadhaar Card | Mandatory | Primary identity proof |
| Educational Certificates (Mark sheets & passing certificates) | Mandatory | Must match minimum qualification for chosen trade |
| Date of Birth Proof | Mandatory | Can be Aadhaar, birth certificate, or school certificate |
| Caste Certificate | Conditional | Required only if claiming SC/ST/OBC benefits |
| Disability Certificate | Conditional | Required only if claiming PwD benefits |
| Passport-sized Photographs | Mandatory | Recent, as per specification |
| Bank Account Details | Conditional | Required only if stipend is applicable |
| Domicile Certificate | Conditional | Required only if mandated by specific State Skill Development Mission |

> **Warning:** Incomplete documentation will result in application rejection or delay. Consultants must verify state-specific domicile requirements.

### Financial Support Deep Dive
- **Training Cost Coverage:** 100% subsidy with no upper limit on course fee (as defined by NCVT/SSC norms for the trade)
- **Stipend Structure:** 
  - Amount varies by project, state, and trainee category
  - Typically ranges from ₹1,000 to ₹2,500 per month where applicable
  - Disbursed directly to beneficiary's bank account (Aadhaar-linked preferred)
  - Subject to minimum attendance (usually 80%)
- **Assessment & Certification:** 
  - No fee for internal or final assessments
  - Certification issued free of cost by NCVT/SSC
  - Re-assessment may be allowed per NCVT guidelines (subject to project rules)

### Implementation & Monitoring
- **Implementing Agency:** Directorate General of Training (DGT), MSDE
- **Training Partners:** ITIs, PMKKs, and other accredited training providers
- **Quality Assurance:** 
  - Regular inspections by DGT/MSDE officials
  - Third-party audits for training delivery and outcomes
  - MIS-based tracking of enrollment, attendance, assessment, and placement
- **Funding Flow:** Central government funds released to State Skill Development Missions/implementing agencies based on approved projects and utilization certificates

### Timeline & Deadlines
| Aspect | Detail |
|-------|--------|
| **Admission Cycle** | Rolling basis throughout the year |
| **Batch Commencement** | Aligned with training center schedules (varies by ITI/PMKK) |
| **Training Duration** | Varies by job role (typically 200 to 1200 hours) |
| **Assessment Window** | Conducted at end of training cycle |
| **Certification Issuance** | Within 30 days of successful assessment |
| **Placement Support** | Provided for up to 6 months post-certification |
| **Application Validity** | Applications remain active until batch commencement or withdrawal |

> **Critical Note:** There is **no fixed annual deadline**; admissions open continuously as batches are scheduled. Consultants should advise clients to apply 4-6 weeks before desired start date to allow for verification and enrollment processing.

### Quality & Outcome Benchmarks
- **Placement Target:** Minimum 70% placement rate for certified trainees (scheme objective)
- **Certification Rate:** Expected >80% of enrolled trainees to complete and certify
- **Inclusion Metrics:** 
  - Minimum 30% women participation encouraged
  - Proportionate representation of SC/ST/OBC as per state demographics
  - 5% reservation for persons with disabilities where feasible
- **Post-Placement Tracking:** Conducted for 6-12 months to monitor job retention and wage progression
