## Pricing Principles
1. **Value-Based Pricing**: Fees are aligned with the financial benefit delivered (up to ₹70L total support: ₹20L grant + ₹50L investment).  
2. **Risk Sharing**: Success fees reflect the scheme’s moderate risk profile — processing time of ~45 days for selection (per ISMC evaluation) and disbursement in milestone-based installments (first grant installment within 60 days), reducing upfront risk but requiring diligent fund utilization tracking.  
3. **Market Alignment**: Retainer and success fee ranges are benchmarked against standard consulting rates for government grant schemes in India, adjusted for SISFS’s sector-agnostic, pan-India reach and high application volume (2,974+ startups funded as per portfolio data).  
4. **Scheme-Specific Note**: The scheme’s 45-day selection timeline and mandatory legal agreement with incubators create a predictable but structured engagement — success fees are contingent on fund disbursement milestones, not just approval, due to tranche-based release (grant) or sanction (debt).
