### Startup India Seed Fund Scheme (SISFS)
- **Scheme ID:** SCH-001
- **Category:** Grant
- **Geography:** Pan-India
- **Administering Body:** Department for Promotion of Industry and Internal Trade (DPIIT), Ministry of Commerce and Industry, Government of India
- **Portal / Application URL:** https://seedfund.startupindia.gov.in/
- **Current Status:** Rolling
- **Window:** Year-round ‘Call for Applications’ for Incubators and Startups. Incubators to complete 100% selection by – 30 June 2026.

#### Eligibility Criteria
- Business type: DPIIT-recognized startup (Private Limited Company, Registered Partnership Firm, or Limited Liability Partnership)
- Age of business: Incorporated not more than 2 years ago at the time of application
- Sector: Sector-agnostic with preference for social impact, waste management, water management, financial inclusion, education, agriculture, food processing, biotechnology, healthcare, energy, mobility, defence, space, railways, oil and gas, textiles, etc.
- Revenue cap: Not explicitly mentioned in evidence
- Employee count: Not mentioned in evidence
- Other: 
  - Startup must have a business idea to develop a product or service with market fit, viable commercialization, and scope of scaling
  - Startup must use technology in its core product/service, business model, distribution model, or methodology
  - Startup should not have received more than Rs 10 lakh monetary support under any other Central or State Government scheme (excluding prize money, subsidized working space, founder monthly allowance, access to labs, prototyping facility)
  - Shareholding by Indian promoters in the startup must be at least 51% at time of application
  - Startup can avail seed support in the form of grant and debt/convertible debentures each once only

#### What It Offers
- Amount / Benefit: 
  - Up to Rs. 20 Lakhs as grant for validation of Proof of Concept, prototype development, or product trials
  - Up to Rs. 50 Lakhs as investment for market entry, commercialization, or scaling up through convertible debentures, debt, or debt-linked instruments
- Disbursement mode: 
  - Grant: Milestone-based installments
  - Debt/Convertible debentures: Direct credit at interest rate not more than prevailing repo rate, tenure ≤ 60 months, moratorium up to 12 months
- Duration of benefit: One-time (grant and debt/convertible debentures each available once per startup)

#### Documents Required
1. Certificate of Incorporation / Registration
2. PAN of the entity
3. Pitch deck or business description
4. Authorization letter from authorized signatory
5. Details of funding received (if any)
6. Board Resolution/ Authorization Letter/ POA (as per sample document)

#### Application Process (numbered steps)
1. Register on startupindia.gov.in if not already registered as a DPIIT-recognized startup
2. Login to the Startup India Seed Fund Portal using your startup credentials
3. Click on 'Apply Now'
4. Fill/upload necessary details as per the fields, including entity details, team profile, problem statement, product/service overview, business model, customer profile, market size, quantum of funds needed, projected utilization plan for funds, etc.
5. Select incubator preferences (up to 3) and funding instrument (Grant/Debt/Debenture) under funding details
6. Check the self-declaration box and click on Submit
7. Your application is submitted successfully
8. Track the progress of your application on the Startup India portal dashboard using your login credentials
