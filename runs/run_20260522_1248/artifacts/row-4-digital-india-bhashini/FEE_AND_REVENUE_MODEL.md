## Pricing Principles
1. **Value-Based Alignment**: Fees are calibrated to the scheme’s non-monetary benefit value (multilingual digital access, linguistic inclusivity) rather than direct financial outlays, as the scheme provides no quantifiable financial support to beneficiaries.
2. **Risk-Adjusted Pricing**: Success fees reflect low financial risk but moderate implementation risk due to the scheme’s reliance on technological integration and stakeholder coordination; processing time is not applicable as there is no formal application window or approval lag.
3. **Tiered Accessibility**: The Starter tier is excluded as the scheme targets startups and government agencies, not micro/proprietorship entities lacking scalable digital integration capacity.
4. **Scheme-Specific Note**: Given the absence of financial disbursements and the focus on platform enablement (e.g., API access, linguistic models), revenue is derived from advisory services for participation readiness, not grant acquisition. Success fees are contingent on measurable outcomes like API integration or multilingual deployment, not fund receipt.
