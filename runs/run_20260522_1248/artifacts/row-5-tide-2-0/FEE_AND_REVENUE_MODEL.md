## Pricing Principles
1. **Value-Based Alignment**: Fees are calibrated to the actual financial benefit delivered—TIDE 2.0 provides up to ₹40 Lakh per startup for product development and market outreach, making the Growth tier (₹10L–₹1Cr scheme value) the primary applicability band.
2. **Risk-Adjusted Pricing**: Success fees reflect the scheme’s moderate risk profile—while TIDE 2.0 has a high confidence rating and clear disbursement stages, the 5-year scheme period (ended Jan 2024) and multi-stage approval process (MoU, utilization certificates, technical reports) introduce timing and compliance risks that justify the 6–8% success fee range for Growth-tier clients.
3. **Market Competitiveness**: The retainer-success fee hybrid model aligns with incubator consulting norms, where retainers cover baseline advisory (eligibility mapping, document prep) and success fees incentivize approval outcomes—consistent with MSH’s observed support for 1400+ incubated startups under TIDE 2.0.
4. **Scheme-Specific Note**: TIDE 2.0’s financial support is structured as grants (not loans) with defined caps (EiR: ₹4L, prototype: ₹7L, scaleup: ₹40L), reducing repayment risk but increasing documentation burden—hence the emphasis on retainer for document readiness and success fee tied to grant release milestones.
