### TIDE 2.0
- **Scheme ID:** SCH-001
- **Category:** Incubation
- **Geography:** Pan-India
- **Administering Body:** Innovation and IPR Division, MeitY
- **Portal / Application URL:** https://msh.meity.gov.in/schemes/tide
- **Current Status:** Closed
- **Window:** 01 February, 2019 to 31st January, 2024

#### Eligibility Criteria
- Business type: Incubation centres (institutions of higher learning and R&D organizations having incubation facilities and established linkages with large companies, industry bodies, industry clusters)
- Age of business: Not specified
- Sector: ICT startups using emerging technologies such as IoT, AI, Block-chain, Robotics etc. in seven pre-identified areas of societal relevance (Healthcare, Education, Agriculture, Financial inclusion including digital payments, Infrastructure and transportation, Environment and clean tech, Clean energy solutions)
- Revenue cap: Not specified
- Employee count: Not specified
- Other: Proposals to be obtained from 51 TIDE 2.0 Incubation Centres; incubators must have established linkages with large companies, industry bodies, industry clusters to provide mentorship and possible infusion of innovative businesses

#### What It Offers
- Amount / Benefit: 
  - Entrepreneurs-in-residence (EiR): up to Rs. 4 Lakh
  - Grants for prototype development: up to Rs. 7 Lakh
  - Investment for product development and market outreach: up to Rs. 40 Lakh
- Disbursement mode: Direct credit (grant-in-aid released in stages as per budgetary support FY-wise)
- Duration of benefit: One-time (per tranche, with staged releases over 5 years)

#### Documents Required
1. MoU to be executed by Implementing Agencies/Partnering Institutes
2. Terms & Conditions governing release of Grant-in-Aid (Annexure II)
3. Surety Bond by Implementing Agencies
4. Utilization Certificate of Previous release
5. Technical & Financial Progress Report
6. Audited statement of accounts
7. Recommendations of the PRSG

#### Application Process (numbered steps)
1. Proposals from 51 TIDE 2.0 Incubation Centres are to be obtained from institutions of higher learning and R&D organizations having incubation facilities and established linkages with large companies, industry bodies, industry clusters.
2. The scheme will be implemented through empowering 51 incubators in India and handholding close to 2000 tech start-ups over a period of 5 years.
3. Each of the 51 TIDE 2.0 Centres will be categorized into three distinct groups as summarized below: Group 1 Centers, Group 2 Centers, Group 3 Centers.
4. Release of payment will be in stages as per the budgetary support FY-wise: FY 2018-19: 05.00 Cr, FY 2019-20: 35.94 Cr, FY 2020-21: 52.72 Cr, FY 2021-22: 67.78 Cr, FY 2022-23: 73.13 Cr, FY 2023-24: 30.05 Cr.
5. Stages of release to Incubation Centres: 1st Year: 40% of the amount earmarked for First year on Initiation of the project (MoU to be executed by Implementing Agencies/Partnering Institutes, Terms & Conditions governing release of Grant-in-Aid, Submission of Surety Bond by Implementing Agencies).
6. 6 Months: 60% of the amount earmarked for First year (Submission of Utilization Certificate of Previous release, Technical & Financial Progress Report, Audited statement of accounts, Recommendations of the PRSG).
7. 2nd Year: 50% of the amount earmarked for second year every 6 Months (Submission of Utilization Certificate of Previous release, Technical & Financial Progress Report, Audited statement of accounts, Recommendations of the PRSG).
8. 3rd Year: 50% of the amount earmarked for third year every 6 Months (Submission of Utilization Certificate of Previous release, Technical & Financial Progress Report, Audited statement of accounts, Recommendations of the PRSG).
9. 4th Year: 50% of the amount earmarked for fourth year every 6 Months (Submission of Utilization Certificate of Previous release, Technical & Financial Progress Report, Audited statement of accounts, Recommendations of the PRSG).
10. 5th Year: 50% of the amount earmarked for fifth year every 6 Months (Submission of Utilization Certificate of Previous release, Technical & Financial Progress Report, Audited statement of accounts, Recommendations of the PRSG).
