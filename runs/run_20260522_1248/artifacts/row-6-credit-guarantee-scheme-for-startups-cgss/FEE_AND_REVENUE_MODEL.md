## Pricing Principles
1. Value-based pricing aligned with the ₹20 crore maximum guarantee cover per borrower under CGSS.
2. Tiered success fees reflect decreasing marginal effort as client size and loan complexity increase.
3. Retainers cover initial eligibility assessment, DPIIT recognition support, and documentation preparation.
4. Scheme-specific note: CGSS has a moderate risk profile due to its reliance on Member Institutions for loan disbursement and guarantee issuance; processing time is typically 2–4 weeks post-application via Jan Samarth Portal, creating a predictable revenue lag for success fees.
