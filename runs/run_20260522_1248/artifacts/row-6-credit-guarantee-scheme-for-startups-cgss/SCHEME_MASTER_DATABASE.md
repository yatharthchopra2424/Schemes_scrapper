### Credit Guarantee Scheme for Startups (CGSS)
- **Scheme ID:** SCH-001
- **Category:** Loan
- **Geography:** Pan-India
- **Administering Body:** National Credit Guarantee Trust Company (NCGTC)
- **Portal / Application URL:** https://www.jansamarth.gov.in/
- **Current Status:** Open
- **Window:** Always Open

#### Eligibility Criteria
- Business type: Startup as recognized by DPIIT as per Gazette Notifications issued from time to time
- Age of business: Not specified in evidence
- Sector: Not specified in evidence
- Revenue cap: Not specified in evidence
- Employee count: Not specified in evidence
- Other: Startup not in default to any lending/investing institution and not classified as Non-Performing Asset as per RBI guidelines; Startup whose eligibility is certified by the Member Institution for the purpose of guarantee cover

#### What It Offers
- Amount / Benefit: ₹20 crore per borrower (maximum amount of debt eligible for guarantee cover)
- Disbursement mode: Guarantee cover provided to Member Institutions (MIs) who extend loans to startups; not direct credit to startups
- Duration of benefit: For transaction-based guarantee cover: tied to the loan; for umbrella-based guarantee cover: runs through the life of the venture debt fund

#### Documents Required
1. Signed undertaking (format given on website)
2. Board Resolution
3. Application for guarantee cover on NCGTC’s portal (after registration as Member Institution)
4. DPIIT recognition certificate (for startups to approach Member Institutions)
5. Eligibility certification from Member Institution

#### Application Process (numbered steps)
1. Online Application: Visit the Jan Samarth Portal using the link below and complete the online application: Apply on Jan Samarth Portal. For step-by-step guidance, please refer to the Jan Samarth Portal instructional video: Watch Instructional Video.
2. Offline Application: Approach the nearest branch of any Member Lending Institution (MLI) directly and enquire about the required process. The list of eligible MLIs is available in the designated reference link/document: View Eligible MLIs.
3. Registration Process: All eligible institutions may register itself under the said scheme by submitting a signed undertaking (format given on website) and Board Resolution. Upon successful registration of the Member Institution (MI), login credentials of the MI shall be created whereafter it can apply for guarantee cover on NCGTC’s portal. To know more and register as an MI, visit the NCGTC's portal.
