## Pricing Principles
1. Value-based pricing aligned with the non-dilutive grant nature of NIDHI Prayas, ensuring fees reflect the ₹10 lakhs prototype development support without equity taken.
2. Tiered success fees calibrated to scheme value brackets, with lower percentages for higher-value engagements due to reduced relative effort per lakh.
3. Retainer covers application preparation, centre liaison, and milestone tracking, reflecting the rolling basis and centre-specific application process.
4. Scheme-specific note: NIDHI Prayas has a medium confidence level in evidence and rolling basis disbursement tied to centre capacity, introducing moderate execution risk; success fees are contingent on milestone-based fund release, not just approval.
