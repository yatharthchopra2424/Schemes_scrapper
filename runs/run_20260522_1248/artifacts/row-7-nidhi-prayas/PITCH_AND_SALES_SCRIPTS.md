# Pitch & Sales Scripts

## Philosophy
We are advisors, not salespeople. We diagnose first. We only pitch a scheme if it fits the client. We never oversell success rates.

---

## 1. Discovery Call Script (First Contact — 20 min)

### Opening (2 min)
Good morning/afternoon! I specialize in helping innovators and early-stage startups access government grants for prototype development. The NIDHI Prayas scheme, run by the Department of Science and Technology, provides up to INR 10 lakhs in non-repayable grant funding specifically for turning ideas into working prototypes—perfect if you're at the idea or early prototype stage and looking to de-risk your innovation before seeking further investment.

### Discovery Questions
1. What stage is your innovation currently at—idea, early prototype, or more advanced?
2. Are you an individual innovator, part of a startup team, or a student innovator from an academic institution?
3. Are you currently associated with or planning to apply through a recognized NIDHI Prayas Centre (incubator)?
4. Is your innovation focused on solving a problem with potential for commercialization and social impact?
5. Are you an Indian citizen, and is your innovation at the idea or prototype stage (not yet a full product)?
6. Have you begun documenting your innovation, such as a project proposal, team details, or budget estimate?
7. Are you seeking funding that does not require equity dilution or repayment?
8. What is the primary goal you hope to achieve with prototype development funding?

### Qualification Checklist (internal — don't read aloud)
- [ ] Applicant is an individual innovator, startup, or student innovator from an academic institution  
- [ ] Applicant is an Indian citizen  
- [ ] Innovation is at the idea or prototype stage  
- [ ] Innovation has potential for commercialization and social impact  
- [ ] Applicant is associated with a recognized NIDHI Prayas Centre (incubator)  
- [ ] Grant will be used solely for prototype development as per approved plan  
- [ ] Applicant agrees to submit periodic progress reports and financial statements  
- [ ] Innovation does not infringe on existing intellectual property  

### Transition to Pitch
Based on your responses, it sounds like you’re working on an early-stage innovation with real potential—and the NIDHI Prayas grant could be an ideal fit. It offers up to INR 10 lakhs in non-repayable funding specifically for prototype development through a NIDHI Prayas Centre, with no equity dilution. Let me walk you through how it works and whether you qualify.

## 2. Scheme Pitch (5 min)

### Structure: Problem → Scheme → Value → Proof → Next Step

**Problem framing:**
Many innovators struggle to move beyond the idea stage due to lack of funds for prototyping, limited access to labs and mentorship, and difficulty validating their concepts—especially when they want to avoid giving up equity early on.

**Scheme intro:**
The NIDHI Prayas scheme, implemented by the Department of Science and Technology (DST), Government of India, provides grant-in-aid support of up to INR 10 lakhs per innovator or startup for prototype development. The grant is non-repayable and does not require equity dilution, with funds disbursed in milestones based on project progress.

**Why they qualify (personalised):**
- You are an Indian citizen innovator or startup team  
- Your innovation is at the idea or prototype stage  
- You aim to develop a prototype with potential for commercialization and social impact  
- You are or plan to be associated with a recognized NIDHI Prayas Centre (incubator)
