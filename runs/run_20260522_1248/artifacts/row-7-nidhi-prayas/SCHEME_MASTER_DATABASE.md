### NIDHI Prayas
- **Scheme ID:** SCH-001
- **Category:** Grant
- **Geography:** Pan-India
- **Administering Body:** Department of Science and Technology (DST), Government of India
- **Portal / Application URL:** https://startupindia.gov.in/content/sih/en/government-schemes.html
- **Current Status:** Rolling
- **Window:** Always Open

#### Eligibility Criteria
- Business type: Individual innovators, startups, and student innovators from academic institutions
- Age of business: Not specified
- Sector: Not specified
- Revenue cap: Not specified
- Employee count: Not specified
- Other: Applicant must be an Indian citizen; innovation must be at idea or prototype stage; innovation must have potential for commercialization and social impact; applicant must be associated with a recognized NIDHI Prayas Centre (incubator) for implementation

#### What It Offers
- Amount / Benefit: Up to INR 10 lakhs
- Disbursement mode: Direct credit (in milestones based on project progress)
- Duration of benefit: One-time (disbursed in milestones)

#### Documents Required
1. Proof of identity (Aadhaar, PAN, etc.)
2. Proof of address
3. Detailed project proposal including innovation description, objectives, and methodology
4. Team details and resumes
5. Budget estimate for prototype development
6. Any prior work or IP related to the innovation
7. Letter of support from academic institution (if applicable)
8. Declaration of originality and ownership of the idea

#### Application Process (numbered steps)
1. Identify and apply to a recognized NIDHI Prayas Centre (incubator)
2. Submit the application with details of the innovation, team, and development plan
3. Present the idea to the selection committee at the Centre
4. If selected, sign an agreement with the Centre
5. Receive the first tranche of funds upon approval
6. Develop the prototype as per the agreed milestones
7. Submit progress reports and utilization certificates
8. Receive subsequent tranches based on milestone completion
9. Final submission of prototype and project report
10. Receive final payment upon successful completion
