The potential benefit is indirect financial support to startups through SEBI-registered AIFs that receive contributions from FFS.

## 3. Our Scope of Work
1. Initial eligibility assessment and scheme suitability analysis  
2. Documentation preparation and review as per scheme requirements  
3. Application submission and liaison with the Implementation Agency (SIDBI)  
4. Coordination with Venture Capital Investment Committee (VCIC) for due diligence  
5. Assistance in meeting the 50%+ matching fund requirement from other investors  
6. Portal registration on [https://startupindia.gov.in/content/sih/en/government-schemes.html](https://startupindia.gov.in/content/sih/en/government-schemes.html)  
7. Post-approval support for fund drawdown and compliance monitoring  

*Note: A DPR/project report is not required as FFS contributes to SEBI-registered AIFs, not direct startup investments.*
