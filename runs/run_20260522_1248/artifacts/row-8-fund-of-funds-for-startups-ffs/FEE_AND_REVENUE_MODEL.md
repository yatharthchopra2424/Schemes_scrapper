## Pricing Principles
1. Value-based pricing aligned with the indirect financial support mechanism of the Fund of Funds for Startups (FFS), where benefits accrue through SEBI-registered AIFs investing in startups.
2. Tiered success fees reflect the scheme’s focus on early-stage and growth-oriented startups, with higher fees for clients accessing smaller AIFs and lower fees for larger fund commitments.
3. Retainer fees cover due diligence, proposal preparation, and coordination with SIDBI and VCIC, reflecting the multi-stage application process involving IA review and VCIC evaluation.
4. Scheme-specific note: The FFS has a moderate risk profile due to its structured tranche-based disbursement over the 16th and 17th Finance Commission Cycles and dependency on AIFs meeting 50%+ third-party funding thresholds; however, the scheme’s high confidence rating and SIDBI’s role as Implementation Agency reduce execution risk for consultants.

## Invoice Templates

### Retainer Invoice
**Description:** Retainer for consulting services related to the Fund of Funds for Startups (FFS) scheme, including AIF proposal preparation, due diligence support, and coordination with SIDBI as Implementation Agency.

### Success Fee Invoice
**Description:** Success fee for securing commitments under the Fund of Funds for Startups (FFS) scheme, implemented by the Small Industries Development Bank of India (SIDBI), upon approval of AIF proposals by the Venture Capital Investment Committee (VCIC) constituted by DPIIT.
