## Pricing Principles
1. Value-based pricing aligned with the scheme’s benefit of enabling access to government subsidies, priority sector lending, and government procurement via GeM integration.
2. Transparent fee structure with no hidden charges; retainer covers advisory and documentation support.
3. Success fee contingent on successful Udyam Registration and subsequent benefit realization.
4. Scheme-specific note: Udyam Registration itself provides no direct financial support but is mandatory to access most MSME schemes and credit benefits. Processing is immediate (same-day certificate issuance upon successful self-declaration), resulting in minimal revenue lag and low execution risk.
