# Pitch & Sales Scripts

We are advisors, not salespeople. We diagnose first. We only pitch a scheme if it fits the client. We never oversell success rates.

## 1. Discovery Call Script (First Contact — 20 min)

### Opening (2 min)
Good day! I specialize in helping micro, small, and medium enterprises (MSMEs) across India access government benefits through the Udyam Registration Portal—a free, paperless recognition system from the Ministry of MSME that unlocks priority sector lending, subsidies, and government procurement opportunities. Since this is foundational for most MSME schemes, I’d like to understand your business to see if Udyam Registration is the right first step for you.

### Discovery Questions
1. What is the date of incorporation or planned launch of your enterprise?  
2. Do you have an Aadhaar number for the proprietor, managing partner, or karta (if HUF)?  
3. Do you have a PAN for your enterprise (required for all entity types except proprietorships not registered under any Act)?  
4. What is your current investment in plant and machinery or equipment?  
5. What is your annual turnover (excluding exports)?  
6. Are all your business units with GSTIN registered under the same PAN?  
7. Are you planning to apply for any Ministry of MSME schemes or bank loans under priority sector lending?  
8. Have you previously registered under Udyog Aadhaar or EM Part-II?

### Qualification Checklist (internal — don't read aloud)
- [ ] Intends to establish or is operating a micro, small, or medium enterprise  
- [ ] Has Aadhaar number (proprietor/managing partner/karta)  
- [ ] Has PAN (mandatory for all except proprietorships not registered under any Act)  
- [ ] Investment in plant and machinery ≤ ₹50 crore and turnover ≤ ₹250 crore (medium enterprise thresholds)  
- [ ] All units with GSTIN are listed under the same PAN (treated as one enterprise)  
- [ ] Not already registered under Udyam (to avoid duplicate filing)  
- [ ] Willing to self-declare investment and turnover (no document upload required)  
- [ ] Understands registration is mandatory for most MSME schemes and bank credit access  

### Transition to Pitch
Based on your responses, it looks like your enterprise aligns with the Udyam Registration criteria. Let me walk you through how this free registration from the Ministry of MSME can unlock access to collateral-free loans, government subsidies, and GeM procurement—without any cost or renewal.

## 2. Scheme Pitch (5 min)

### Structure: Problem → Scheme → Value → Proof → Next Step

**Problem framing:**
Many MSMEs struggle to access formal credit, government subsidies, or participate in government tenders because they lack official recognition. Banks and government departments often require proof of MSME status, which informal businesses cannot provide, limiting growth opportunities.

**Scheme intro:**
The Udyam Registration Portal, launched by the Ministry of Micro, Small and Medium Enterprises, provides free, lifetime recognition for MSMEs based on self-declaration of investment and turnover. It issues a dynamic QR-coded certificate with no renewal required and is mandatory to avail benefits under most Ministry of MSME schemes and to access priority sector lending from banks as per RBI notification RBI/2020-2021/26 dated August 21, 2020.

**Why they qualify (personalised):**
- You intend to establish or are operating an enterprise (eligible for self-declaration)  
- You have an Aadhaar number (required for registration)  
- You have a PAN (mandatory for your entity type)  
- Your investment and turnover fall within the MSME thresholds (micro: ≤₹1 crore investment and ≤₹5 crore turnover; small: ≤₹10 crore and ≤₹50 crore; medium: ≤₹50 crore and ≤₹250 crore)
