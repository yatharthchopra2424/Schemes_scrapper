### Udyam Registration Portal
- **Scheme ID:** SCH-001
- **Category:** Recognition
- **Geography:** Pan-India
- **Administering Body:** Ministry of Micro, Small and Medium Enterprises
- **Portal / Application URL:** https://udyamregistration.gov.in
- **Current Status:** Rolling
- **Window:** Always Open

#### Eligibility Criteria
- Business type: Any person who intends to establish a micro, small or medium enterprise
- Age of business: Not specified
- Sector: All sectors (manufacturing and services)
- Revenue cap: Micro enterprise (turnover ≤ ₹5 crore), Small enterprise (turnover ≤ ₹50 crore), Medium enterprise (turnover ≤ ₹250 crore)
- Employee count: Not specified
- Other: Based on self-declaration; no requirement to upload documents, papers, certificates or proof; classification based on investment in plant and machinery or equipment and turnover; all units with GSTIN listed against the same PAN are collectively treated as one enterprise

#### What It Offers
- Amount / Benefit: Udyam Registration Certificate with dynamic QR code for verification; no renewal required; access to Priority Sector Lending from banks; eligibility for government subsidies and schemes; facilitation in government procurement via GeM integration; access to credit from financial institutions as per RBI notification; availing of concessions and rebates under various central and state government schemes
- Disbursement mode: Direct credit (certificate issued electronically)
- Duration of benefit: One-time (no renewal required)

#### Documents Required
1. Aadhaar number
2. PAN number

#### Application Process (numbered steps)
1. Visit the Udyam Registration Portal at https://udyamregistration.gov.in
2. Click on 'For New Enterprise who are not Registered yet as MSME'
3. Enter Aadhaar number and validate
4. Enter PAN details
5. Fill in enterprise details including name, type, address, bank details
6. Provide investment and turnover details (auto-fetched from IT/GST if available)
7. Submit self-declaration
8. Receive Udyam Registration Certificate with dynamic QR code
