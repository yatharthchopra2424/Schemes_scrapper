## Pricing Principles
1. Retainer covers initial eligibility assessment, document preparation, and application submission support.
2. Success fee is contingent upon successful DPIIT recognition and access to benefits under the Startup India Initiative.
3. Fees are structured to align with the scheme’s rolling application basis and non-competitive selection process.
4. Scheme-specific note: The Startup India Initiative offers non-financial benefits (tax exemptions, compliance ease, IPR support) with high success rates for eligible entities; processing time is typically 2–4 weeks for recognition, reducing revenue lag risk.
