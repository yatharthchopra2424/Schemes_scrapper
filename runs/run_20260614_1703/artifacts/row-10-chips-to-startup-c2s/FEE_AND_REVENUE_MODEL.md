## Pricing Principles
1. Value-based pricing aligned with the financial benefit delivered to the client (e.g., up to ₹10 Lacs per startup under the C2S scheme via Micron-backed CREST accelerator).
2. Success fees contingent on measurable outcomes such as grant disbursement or equity-linked funding secured through the scheme.
3. Retainer covers initial eligibility assessment, program selection, documentation preparation, and application submission.
4. Scheme-specific note: The C2S scheme has a moderate risk profile due to variable processing times across sub-programs (e.g., fixed deadlines for MedTech OCP 4.0 vs. rolling basis for others) and dependency on incubator-led disbursement, which may extend the approval-to-funding timeline.
