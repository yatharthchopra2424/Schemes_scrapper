# Comprehensive Scheme Masterclass & File Guide

## Scheme Deep Dive

### Overview
The Chips to Startup (C2S) scheme, operating under the MeitY Startup Hub (MSH), is a pan-India incubation initiative led by the Ministry of Electronics and Information Technology (MeitY), Government of India. It serves as a national coordination, facilitation, and monitoring centre integrating incubation centres, startups, and innovation-related activities. The scheme promotes technology innovation, startup creation, and deep-tech development through structured programs like TIDE 2.0, SAMRIDH, GENESIS, and corporate partnerships with entities such as Micron, MathWorks, Razorpay, Samsung, and others. Financial support is disbursed through incubators and accelerators, with a total fund size of ₹49.21 crore allocated to 51 TIDE 2.0 Centers, 23 SAMRIDH Accelerators, and 63 GENESIS Implementing Agencies. The application portal is https://msh.meity.gov.in/, with deadlines varying by program (e.g., MedTech OCP 4.0 deadline: Mar 29, 2023) or operating on a rolling basis.

### Objectives
- Promote technology innovation and startup creation  
- Integrate incubation centres, startups, and innovation-related activities  
- Facilitate knowledge transactions and expert leveraging  
- Promote investments in deep-tech startups  
- Support inclusive and sustainable techno-socio-economic development  
- Enable international expansion programs for startups  
- Strengthen the startup ecosystem through resource integration and upskilling

**Required Documents**:
1. Certificate of Incorporation / Registration  
2. PAN of the entity  
3. Pitch deck or business description  
4. Authorization letter from authorized signatory  
5. Details of funding received (if any)  
6. Bank account details  
7. GST registration (if applicable)  
8. UDYAM certificate (for MSMEs)  

**Contact Details**:  
Email: meity-sthub@gov.in | Contact: +91-11-24301244  

**Application Portal**: https://msh.meity.gov.in/  

**Key Caveats**:  
- Global programs (e.g., Brussels delegation) subject to travel and participation constraints  
- Logo usage requires prior written consent; carries no financial/legal obligations on MSH  
- Status/deadlines vary by program (fixed or rolling)
