**Data Classification:** All client data related to scheme applications is classified as
Confidential and subject to strict handling procedures.

**Storage Standards:**
- All data stored in encrypted cloud storage (AES-256 encryption at rest and in transit)
- Multi-factor authentication (2FA) required for all system access
- Role-based access control with principle of least privilege
- No personal devices permitted for accessing or storing client data
- Regular security audits and penetration testing conducted quarterly

**Access Controls:**
- Access limited to authorized consultants directly engaged in the client's case
- Access logs maintained and reviewed monthly
- Immediate revocation of access upon case closure or consultant departure

**Retention Policy:**
- Active case data retained for duration of engagement plus 2 years post case closure
- Scheme-specific requirement: All documents submitted to Anusandhan National Research Foundation (ANRF) under Anusandhan National Research Foundation (ANRF)
  must be retained for a minimum of 3 years for audit purposes
- Secure deletion of data after retention period using certified data destruction methods

**Breach Response:**
- 24-hour breach notification protocol to affected clients and relevant authorities
- Designated Data Protection Officer responsible for breach coordination
- Forensic investigation initiated within 4 hours of breach detection
- Mitigation measures implemented immediately to prevent further data exposure

**Compliance:**
- Policy aligned with IT Act 2000 and relevant data protection regulations
- Annual staff training on data handling and security protocols
- Policy reviewed and updated bi-annually or upon regulatory changes
- All third-party vendors assessed for data protection compliance prior to engagement
