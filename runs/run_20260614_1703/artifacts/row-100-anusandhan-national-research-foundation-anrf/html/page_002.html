Registration Guidelines

  • Step 1. Visit "www.anrfonline.in". You can see "SIGN IN / REGISTER" section as shown in image below. Click on the Sign in button.
  • Step 2. If you are not a registered user (PI/Co-Pi/Mentor/Referee), then click on "NEW USER". A registration form will open up as shown in image below, kindly fill all the required details keenly in the form.
  • Step 3. After filling all the details, click on the "Submit" button which is in the end of the form.
  • Step 4. After successful submission, a "Verification mail" will be sent to the given mail id of the user.
    • NOTE: User need to click on that link for completing the registration process.
