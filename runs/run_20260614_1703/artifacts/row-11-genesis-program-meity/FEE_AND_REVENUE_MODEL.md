## Pricing Principles
1. **Value-Based Alignment**: Fees are calibrated to the actual financial benefits delivered under GENESIS, which provides up to ₹1 Crore for deep-tech startups in Tier-II/III cities, ensuring consultant compensation reflects client outcomes.
2. **Risk Mitigation**: Success fees are tiered to reflect scheme risk; GENESIS has a 5-year duration and supports ~1,600 startups, indicating stable government backing but moderate competition for slots, justifying mid-range success fees.
3. **Accessibility for Target Segment**: Retainer structures are designed to be accessible to early-stage startups in Tier-II/III cities, aligning with GENESIS’s focus on grassroots innovation and early-stage support.
4. **Transparency & Trust**: All fees are disclosed upfront, with clear linkage to scheme-specific milestones (e.g., approval of Pilot Funding or Deep-Tech Funding components), building confidence in the consulting engagement.
