# Pitch & Sales Scripts

## Philosophy
We are advisors, not salespeople. We diagnose first. We only pitch a scheme if it fits the client. We never oversell success rates.

---

## 1. Discovery Call Script (First Contact — 20 min)

### Opening (2 min)
Good morning/afternoon! I’m reaching out because I specialize in helping technology startups in Tier-II and Tier-III cities access government funding designed specifically for their growth stage. The GENESIS Program by MeitY is built to bridge the funding and mentorship gap for startups outside metro hubs—so I’d love to understand where you are in your journey and see if this could be a fit.

### Discovery Questions
1. Where is your startup incorporated and currently operating? (To confirm Tier-II/Tier-III city eligibility)
2. What stage is your startup at—idea validation, product piloting, market fundraising, or deep-tech development?
3. Have you already validated your startup idea with early users or prototypes?
4. Are you seeking funding to pilot your product, raise market investment, or scale a deep-tech solution?
5. What is your approximate annual revenue or current funding stage (pre-revenue, seed, etc.)?
6. How many employees are on your team currently?
7. Are you looking for non-dilutive grant support, or are you open to equity-linked funding as well?
8. Have you previously applied for any MeitY or central government startup schemes?

### Qualification Checklist (internal — don't read aloud)
- [ ] Startup is incorporated and operating in a Tier-II or Tier-III city in India
- [ ] Startup is in the technology, electronics, IT, or deep-tech sector
- [ ] Startup is at one of these stages: idea validation (EiR), product piloting (Pilot Funding), market fundraising (Investment Support), or deep-tech solution development (Deep-Tech Funding)
- [ ] Startup has not exceeded the scheme’s 5-year duration limit or approximate 1600 startup cap (internal tracking)
- [ ] Founder(s) are Indian nationals or the startup is registered in India

### Transition to Pitch
Based on what you’ve shared, it sounds like you’re working on [mention stage: e.g., piloting your product in a Tier-II city], which aligns closely with the GENESIS Program’s Pilot Funding Support—offering up to ₹40 lakhs for startups like yours. Let me walk you through how it works.

## 2. Scheme Pitch (5 min)

### Structure: Problem → Scheme → Value → Proof → Next Step

**Problem framing:**
Startups in Tier-II and Tier-III cities often struggle to access early-stage funding, mentorship, and innovation infrastructure compared to their metro counterparts. This limits their ability to validate ideas, pilot products, or raise follow-on investment—despite having strong potential.

**Scheme intro:**
The Gen-Next Support for Innovative Startups (GENESIS) Scheme, launched by the Ministry of Electronics and Information Technology (MeitY), Government of India, is a grant-based initiative with a ₹490 crore outlay over 5 years. It provides financial support through four components: Entrepreneur in Residence (EiR) Support (up to ₹10 lakhs), Pilot Funding Support (up to ₹40 lakhs), Investment Support (up to ₹50 lakhs), and Deep-Tech Funding Support (up to ₹1 crore)—along with mentorship, capacity building, and ecosystem strengthening in Tier-II/III cities.

**Why they qualify (personalised):**
- Your startup is incorporated and operating in a Tier-II or Tier-III city (confirmed in discovery)
- You are at the [product piloting/market fundraising/deep-tech/idea validation] stage, matching one of GENESIS’s four funding components
- You are developing a technology-driven solution in electronics, IT, or deep-tech sectors
- You are seeking non-dilutive grant support to scale without equity dilution
