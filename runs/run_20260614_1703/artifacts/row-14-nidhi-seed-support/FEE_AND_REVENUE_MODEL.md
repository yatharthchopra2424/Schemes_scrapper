## Pricing Principles
1. Value-based pricing aligned with the scheme’s maximum benefit of up to ₹100 lakh per startup.
2. Success fees scaled to reflect effort and risk, with lower percentages for higher-value engagements.
3. Retainers cover initial eligibility assessment, documentation, and application preparation.
4. Scheme-specific note: The NIDHI Seed Support program has an annual application cycle with phased disbursement, introducing moderate revenue lag risk; however, the high success rate of funded startups (as evidenced by multiple incubator lists and compendiums) supports predictable success fee realization.
