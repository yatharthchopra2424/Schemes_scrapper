## Pricing Principles
1. Value-based pricing aligned with the quantum of SERB grants, which typically cover project costs for 3 years and include fellowships with stipends and research grants.
2. Tiered success fees reflect decreasing percentage as scheme value increases, appropriate for high-value, competitive research grants.
3. Retainers cover initial proposal development, document preparation, and portal registration efforts.
4. Scheme-specific note: SERB grants involve rigorous peer review and mid-term/final evaluations, leading to moderate risk; processing times vary by scheme but approvals typically occur within 6–12 months, supporting a 3-month lag in revenue realization.
