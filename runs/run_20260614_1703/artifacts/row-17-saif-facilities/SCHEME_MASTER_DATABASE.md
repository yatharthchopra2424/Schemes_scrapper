### SAIF Facilities
- **Scheme ID:** SCH-001
- **Category:** Subsidy
- **Geography:** Pan-India
- **Administering Body:** Department of Science & Technology (DST)
- **Portal / Application URL:** https://dst.gov.in/
- **Current Status:** Rolling
- **Window:** Always Open

#### Eligibility Criteria
- Business type: Academic institutions, national laboratories, and industry entities engaged in scientific research and development activities
- Age of business: Not specified
- Sector: Scientific research and development
- Revenue cap: Not specified
- Employee count: Not specified
- Other: Access is typically granted based on project proposals and institutional requirements

#### What It Offers
- Amount / Benefit: Access to sophisticated analytical instruments, shared use of high-end equipment, reduced capital expenditure for individual institutions, enhanced research capabilities, and promotion of collaborative R&D activities
- Disbursement mode: Direct credit (facility access)
- Duration of benefit: One-time (per approved project/slot)

#### Documents Required
1. Project proposal
2. Institutional authorization letter
3. Details of intended use of facility
4. User charges payment proof (if applicable)

#### Application Process (numbered steps)
1. Identify the required facility or instrument from the SAIF network.
2. Submit an application through the respective SAIF center.
3. Provide project details and justification for facility use.
4. Obtain approval from the facility in-charge.
5. Schedule access and utilize the facility as per allocated time slots.
6. Submit utilization report and settle any applicable user charges.
