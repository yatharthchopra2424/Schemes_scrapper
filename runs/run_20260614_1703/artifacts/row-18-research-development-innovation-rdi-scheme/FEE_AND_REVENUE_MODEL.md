## Pricing Principles
1. **Value-Based Pricing**: Fees correlate with the scheme’s high-impact, long-tenor financing (low-interest loans/equity) that enables RDI scaling in strategic sectors.
2. **Risk-Adjusted Returns**: Success fees reflect moderate risk due to 50% funding cap, extended gestation periods, and TRL 4+ focus — mitigated by patient capital structure.
3. **Transparency & Compliance**: Aligns with RDIF’s QCBS process (80% quality, 20% cost) and requirement for SLFMs to disclose fees via sealed covers.
4. **Scheme-Specific Note**: Long processing timelines (approvals lagged 3–6 months per evidence) and high technical due diligence increase engagement risk; success fees calibrated to reward navigating QCBS and SLFM onboarding complexity.
