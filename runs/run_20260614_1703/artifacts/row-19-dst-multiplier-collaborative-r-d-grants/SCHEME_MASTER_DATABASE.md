### DST Multiplier & Collaborative R&D Grants
- **Scheme ID:** SCH-001
- **Category:** Grant
- **Geography:** Pan-India
- **Administering Body:** Department of Electronics and Information Technology (DeitY), Govt of India
- **Portal / Application URL:** https://www.indiascienceandtechnology.gov.in/funding-opportunities/startups/multiplier-grants-scheme-mgs
- **Current Status:** Rolling
- **Window:** Always Open

#### Eligibility Criteria
- Business type: Startup, Incubator/Academia/Accelerator
- Age of business: Not specified
- Sector: Electronics & Information Technology
- Revenue cap: Not specified
- Employee count: Not specified
- Other: Must have projects in electronics & information technology; proposals must be submitted jointly by industry and academic/R&D institution

#### What It Offers
- Amount / Benefit: Rs. 2.0 Crores per project for individual industry; Rs. 4.0 Crores for industry consortium
- Disbursement mode: Direct credit to academic/R&D institution(s) only
- Duration of benefit: Preferably less than 2 years for individual industry; 3 years for industry consortium

#### Documents Required
1. Joint proposal from industry and academic/R&D institution
2. Project details highlighting commercialization potential
3. Proof of collaboration between industry and academic/R&D institution
4. Details of industry contribution
5. Project timeline and budget
6. CVs of key personnel involved
7. Any additional documents as specified in the application guidelines

#### Application Process (numbered steps)
1. Identify a collaborative R&D project between industry and academic/R&D institution in electronics & information technology
2. Ensure the startup/incubator/academia/accelerator has a project in electronics & IT
3. Prepare a joint proposal from industry and institution
4. Submit the proposal jointly by industry and academic/R&D institution
5. Await evaluation and approval based on commercialization potential and eligibility
