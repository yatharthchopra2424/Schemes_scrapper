## 3. Our Scope of Work
1. Initial eligibility assessment against NDTSP deep tech startup criteria  
2. Document preparation and review as per scheme requirements  
3. Application submission via Startup India portal  
4. Coordination with Department for Promotion of Industry and Internal Trade (DPIIT) for recognition  
5. Liaison with Office of the Principal Scientific Adviser (PSA) for policy alignment
