## Pricing Principles
1. **Value-Based Alignment**: Fees are structured to reflect the substantial non-dilutive support accessed via the NDTSP, including patient capital, regulatory sandboxes, and IP protection mechanisms.
2. **Risk-Adjusted Pricing**: Success fees reflect the medium-to-high risk profile of deep tech ventures, given extended development timelines, high technical uncertainty, and long gestation periods — though mitigated by NDTSP’s de-risking instruments like the FoF and regulatory sandboxes.
3. **Transparency & Predictability**: Retainer covers advisory workload (eligibility mapping, documentation, FoF application support), while success fee is contingent on fund allocation or benefit realization.
4. **Scheme-Specific Note**: The NDTSP offers high-value support (up to ₹10,000 Cr FoF corpus) but involves complex application and long approval cycles (typically 6–12 months). Success fees are structured to align with milestone-based disbursements from AIFs under the FoF.
