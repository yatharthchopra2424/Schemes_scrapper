# Pitch & Sales Scripts

## Philosophy
We are advisors, not salespeople. We diagnose first. We only pitch a scheme if it fits the client. We never oversell success rates.

## 1. Discovery Call Script (First Contact — 20 min)

### Opening (2 min)
Good morning/afternoon. I specialize in helping deep tech startups access government support tailored to their unique R&D-intensive, long-gestation models. The National Deep Tech Startup Policy (NDTSP) is designed specifically for ventures like yours—working on frontier science or engineering solutions with high technical uncertainty and extended development timelines. I’d like to understand your current stage and see if this policy’s benefits—such as access to the ₹10,000 crore Deep Tech Fund of Funds, regulatory sandboxes, and IP support—could accelerate your path to commercialization.

### Discovery Questions
1. What scientific or engineering discipline is your solution based on, and is it still in development or yet to be developed?  
2. What percentage of your total expenditure or funding is allocated to research and development (R&D) activities?  
3. Are you currently creating or own significant novel intellectual property (IP), and are you taking steps to commercialize it?  
4. What is your expected development timeline, and do you face high capital or infrastructure requirements?  
5. When was your company incorporated or registered?  
6. What is your current annual turnover (revenue) from operations?  
7. Are you working on innovation, development, or improvement of products, processes, or services with potential for employment generation or wealth creation?  
8. Have you been formed by splitting up or reconstructing an existing business?

### Qualification Checklist (internal — don't read aloud)
- [ ] Working on a solution based on new knowledge/advancements in a scientific or engineering discipline (single or multiple), yet to be developed or in development  
- [ ] High percentage of expenditure on R&D activities as a percentage of revenue/funding  
- [ ] Owning or in the process of creating significant novel IP and taking steps to commercialize it  
- [ ] Facing extended development timelines, long gestation periods, high capital/infrastructure requirements, and large technical/scientific uncertainty  
- [ ] Incorporated or registered in India as a private limited company, partnership firm, LLP, or cooperative society  
- [ ] Date of incorporation/registration is within 20 years (for Deep Tech Startup recognition under NDTSP)  
- [ ] Turnover for any financial year since incorporation does not exceed ₹300 crore  
- [ ] Not formed by splitting up or reconstruction of an existing business  
- [ ] Engaged in innovation, development, or improvement of products/processes/services, or has a scalable business model with high potential for employment/wealth creation  

### Transition to Pitch
Based on your responses, it appears your venture aligns with the NDTSP’s definition of a Deep Tech Startup—particularly your extended R&D timeline, IP focus, and incorporation within the last 20 years. The NDTSP offers access to the ₹10,000 crore Deep Tech Fund of Funds, regulatory sandboxes, and IP regime support—benefits that could significantly derisk and accelerate your journey. May I share how this works?
