## Pricing Principles
1. Value-based pricing aligned with the strategic impact of AI adoption enabled by the IndiaAI Mission.
2. Tiered retainer structure reflecting client complexity and resource requirements for navigating national AI initiatives.
3. Success fees calibrated to the non-financial, ecosystem-level benefits of the scheme (e.g., access to AI R&D, skilling, data infrastructure).
4. **Scheme-specific note**: The IndiaAI Mission operates as an ongoing national program with no fixed application deadlines or direct grant disbursements to individual beneficiaries. Revenue realization depends on long-term engagement (typically 6–12 months) to support clients in accessing AI research, skill development, and governance frameworks, resulting in a moderate risk profile due to delayed but high-impact outcomes.
