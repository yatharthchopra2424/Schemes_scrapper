## Pricing Principles
1. Value-based pricing aligned with the financial benefit delivered to the client (e.g., up to ₹1 crore under ANIC, up to ₹2.5 crores for ACIC with matching, or seed grants of ₹2.2–3.5 lakhs in Youth Co:Lab).
2. Success fees are contingent on successful grant approval and disbursement, reducing upfront financial burden on clients.
3. Retainers cover initial eligibility assessment, application preparation, documentation, and submission efforts.
4. Scheme-specific note: The risk profile is moderate due to rolling basis applications for some programs (ATL, AIC, ACIC) but fixed deadlines for others (e.g., Youth Co:Lab). Processing times vary: ANIC supports over 12–18 months, ACIC requires matching funding, and Youth Co:Lab has a defined timeline. Success is evidenced by 3500+ startups supported, 72 AICs operationalized, and 10,000 ATLs established, indicating a strong track record for approved applications.
