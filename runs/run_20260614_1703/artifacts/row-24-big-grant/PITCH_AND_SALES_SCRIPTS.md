# Pitch & Sales Scripts

## Philosophy
We are advisors, not salespeople. We diagnose first. We only pitch a scheme if it fits the client. We never oversell success rates.

---

## 1. Discovery Call Script (First Contact — 20 min)

### Opening (2 min)
Good morning/afternoon! I specialize in helping biotech startups access non-dilutive government grants like the Biotechnology Ignition Grant (BIG) from BIRAC, which provides up to INR 50 lakhs in grant funding to translate innovative ideas into Proof of Concept. I’d love to learn about your venture and see if this could be a fit.

### Discovery Questions
1. When was your company/LLP incorporated under the Indian Companies Act, 1956/2013?
2. What is your current shareholding pattern, and is at least 51% beneficially owned by resident Indian citizens or Indian companies?
3. Do you have an in-house R&D facility that is functional and adequate to execute your project, or are you currently incubated at an incubator?
4. Are any of your promoters holding 20% or more shares also involved as co-promoters in another company or LLP that is ineligible for BIG (unless operating in a non-biotech domain)?
5. Has your company or its promoters ever defaulted on loan repayment to BIRAC?
6. Is your project focused on ideation to Proof of Concept (PoC) in biotech, with clear commercialization potential?
7. Have you or any other agency already received funding for the exact same objectives and deliverables you’re proposing?
8. Are you prepared to provide documentary evidence for verification by BIG Partners, including undertakings from scientific advisors and key technical team members?

### Qualification Checklist (internal — don't read aloud)
- [ ] Company/LLP registered under Indian Companies Act, 1956/2013  
- [ ] Incorporation date not earlier than 5 years from the date of launch of the BIG call  
- [ ] Minimum 51% capital beneficially owned by resident Indian citizen(s) or Indian companies ultimately owned/controlled by resident Indians  
- [ ] Application represented by a qualified Project Leader who is a shareholder  
- [ ] Own functional and adequate in-house R&D facility OR incubated at an incubator  
- [ ] No promoter holding ≥20% shares is a co-promoter in another ineligible company/LLP (unless that entity operates in non-biotech domain)  
- [ ] Not in default of loan repayment to BIRAC (self or promoters)  
- [ ] Project has strong biotech component, novelty, and scientific foundation  
- [ ] Not a late-stage project involving systematic clinical/field trials or late-stage validation  
- [ ] BIG grant not previously received by this startup  
- [ ] No prior funding received for same objectives/deliverables from any other agency  
- [ ] Willing to provide undertaking that project has not received funding from any other agency  

### Transition to Pitch
Based on your responses, it sounds like you’re working on an early-stage biotech innovation with strong commercial potential—and if you meet the eligibility criteria we just discussed, the **BIG Grant from BIRAC** could provide up to **INR 50 lakhs in grant funding** over 18 months to help you reach Proof of Concept without diluting equity.

## 2. Scheme Pitch (5 min)

### Structure: Problem → Scheme → Value → Proof → Next Step

**Problem framing:**
Early-stage biotech startups often struggle to translate innovative ideas into Proof of Concept due to lack of non-dilutive funding. Traditional sources like angel or VC investment are hard to access at the ideation stage, and taking on debt can be risky when revenue is uncertain. This gap causes many promising biotech innovations to stall before they can demonstrate viability.

**Scheme intro:**
The **Biotechnology Ignition Grant (BIG)** is a flagship grant-in-aid scheme of the **Biotechnology Industry Research Assistance Council (BIRAC)**, a Public Sector Enterprise set up by the Department of Biotechnology (DBT), Government of India. It provides early-stage funding support of **up to INR 50 lakhs** for a period of **up to 18 months** to help startups translate innovative ideas into Proof of Concept. The grant is released in milestone-based installments: INR 20 lakhs upon signing the Grant Agreement, followed by one or two installments of up to INR 20 lakhs each based on progress, and up to INR 5 lakhs upon successful completion and submission of final reports.

**Why they qualify (personalised):**
- Your company is incorporated within the last 5 years from the BIG call launch date  
- At least 51% of your capital is beneficially owned by resident Indian citizens or Indian companies  
- You have (or will arrange via incubation) access to a functional in-house R&D facility adequate for your project  
- Your project focuses on ideation to Proof of Concept with clear commercialization potential in biotech
