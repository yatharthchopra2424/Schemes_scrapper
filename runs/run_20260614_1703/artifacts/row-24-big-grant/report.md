# Comprehensive Scheme Masterclass & File Guide

## Scheme Deep Dive

### Overview
The **Biotechnology Ignition Grant (BIG)** is the flagship programme of the Biotechnology Industry Research Assistance Council (BIRAC), a Public Sector Enterprise set up by the Department of Biotechnology (DBT), Government of India. It supports young startups by providing early-stage funding to translate innovative ideas into Proof of Concept (PoC). The scheme is designed to foster ideation, upscale and validate proof of concept, encourage researchers to take technology closer to market through startups, and stimulate enterprise formation.

**Scheme Name:** BIG Grant  
**Scheme ID:** row-24  
**Ministry / Category:** Biotechnology  
**Scheme Type:** grant  
**Geographic Scope:** Pan-India  
**Implementing Agency:** Biotechnology Industry Research Assistance Council (BIRAC)  
**Application Portal:** [https://birac.nic.in/big.php](https://birac.nic.in/big.php)  
**Status / Deadlines:** Call for proposals announced twice a year (1st January and 1st July); typically open for about 45 days  
**Last Updated:** 2026  
**Confidence:** high  

### Objectives
- Foster generation of ideas with commercialisation potential  
- Upscale and validate proof of concept  
- Encourage researchers to take technology closer to market through a startup  
- Stimulate enterprise formation  

### Eligibility Matrix
| Criteria | Requirement | Source |
|---------|-------------|--------|
| Entity Type | Company/LLP registered under Indian Companies Act, 1956/2013 | Key Facts, Guidelines |
| Incorporation Date | Not earlier than 5 years from the date of launch of a particular BIG call | Key Facts, Guidelines |
| Ownership | Minimum 51% of capital beneficially owned by resident Indian citizen(s) and/or Indian companies ultimately owned/controlled by resident Indian citizens | Key Facts, Guidelines |
| Project Leader | Application must be represented by a Project Leader who is a shareholder in the applicant company, technically qualified, and has completed basic undergraduate training | Key Facts, Guidelines |
| R&D Facility | Must have own in-house R&D facility that is functional and adequate; if not, must be incubated at an incubator | Key Facts, Guidelines |
| Promoter Restrictions | If any promoter holding ≥20% shares is a co-promoter of another ineligible company or partner in another ineligible LLP, applicant is ineligible (exception: latter entity operates in non-biotech domain) | Key Facts, Guidelines |
| Loan Default | Companies in default of loan repayment to BIRAC, and their promoters, are ineligible | Key Facts, Guidelines |
| Target Beneficiaries | Startups | Key Facts |

> **> WARNING:** BIG grant is **not** a research fellowship and **cannot** be used to support PhD or any other academic research. Projects with low novelty, lacking significant biotech component or robust scientific foundation, or involving late-stage validation (systematic clinical trials, field trials) are **not supported**. BIG support can be provided **only once** to any startup. Proposed objectives/deliverables must **not** have received funding from any other agency, and applicant must provide an undertaking to this effect.

### Application Process
```mermaid
flowchart TD
    A[BIRAC issues national call for BIG proposals<br>(twice yearly: 1st Jan & 1st Jul)] --> B[Call advertised via website, newspapers, journals]
    B --> C[Call open for ~45 days]
    C --> D[Applicants submit online ONLY via<br>https://birac.nic.in/big.php]
    D --> E[BIG Partners screen for eligibility & suitability]
    E --> F[Online review by 3-5 reviewers + Review Committee]
    F --> G[Presentation before Technical Expert Panel (TEP)<br>(conducted centrally by BIRAC)]
    G --> H[TEP-recommended applicants undergo financial & technical due diligence by BIG Partner]
    H --> I[Applicant has 1-45 days to accept grant offer & complete due diligence<br>(offer withdrawn after 45 days)]
    I --> J[BIRAC releases funds to BIG Partners<br>who disburse to grantees]
    J --> K[Grantees sign Grant Agreement<br>receive 1st instalment (INR 20 lakhs)]
    K --> L[Milestone-based monitoring & mentoring by BIG Partner]
    L --> M[Subsequent instalments released<br>based on progress & funding needs]
    M --> N[Final tranche (up to INR 5 lakhs)<br>released as reimbursement<br>upon UC, SoE, and Project Completion Report]
    N --> O[Grantee interacts with ≥2 investors during 18 months<br>includes feedback in final report]
    O --> P[Project completion & closure]
```

> **> KEY TAKEAWAY:** Applicants are advised to submit early to avoid last-minute rush; the online system auto-closes at the specified date/time. Requests for changes post-submission are **not entertained**. Providing incorrect information intentionally may lead to disqualification.
