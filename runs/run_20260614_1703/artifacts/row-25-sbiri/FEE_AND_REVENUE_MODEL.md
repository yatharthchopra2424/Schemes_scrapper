## Pricing Principles
1. Value-based pricing aligned with the grant amount (up to ₹50 lakhs) and non-dilutive nature of SBIRI funding.
2. Tiered success fees reflect effort intensity: higher for early-stage validation (proof-of-concept/prototype) due to technical and regulatory complexity.
3. Retainer covers proposal development, documentation, and BIRAC portal navigation — critical given the 18-month project timeline and milestone-based disbursement.
4. Scheme-specific note: SBIRI has a moderate risk profile due to its 18-month project completion requirement and periodic progress reporting; success is evidenced by BIRAC’s structured milestones and DBT/BIRAC oversight, reducing execution risk for consultants familiar with biotech validation frameworks.

## Invoice Templates

### Retainer Invoice
**Description:** Retainer fee for SBIRI (Small Business Innovation Research Initiative) grant consulting services under the Department of Biotechnology (DBT), Government of India.  
This includes project proposal preparation, documentation support, BIRAC portal registration, and ongoing compliance guidance for proof-of-concept and prototype development in biotechnology.

### Success Fee Invoice
**Description:** Success fee for securing SBIRI grant approval from the Biotechnology Industry Research Assistance Council (BIRAC), under the Department of Biotechnology (DBT), Ministry of Science & Technology, Government of India.  
Fee is contingent upon award letter issuance and agreement signing with BIRAC for grant-based funding up to INR 50 lakhs for biotech innovation validation.
