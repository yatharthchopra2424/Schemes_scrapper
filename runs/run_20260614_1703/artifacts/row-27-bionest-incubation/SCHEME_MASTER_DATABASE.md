### BioNEST Incubation
- **Scheme ID:** SCH-001
- **Category:** Subsidy
- **Geography:** Pan-India
- **Administering Body:** Biotechnology Industry Research Assistance Council (BIRAC), a Public Sector Enterprise, set up by Department of Biotechnology (DBT), Government of India
- **Portal / Application URL:** https://birac.nic.in/bionest.php
- **Current Status:** Open
- **Window:** Always Open

#### Eligibility Criteria
- Business type: Indian institutions including Universities/ Research Institutes/ Colleges/ Management Institutes/ Business Schools/ Business Incubators/Biotech-Parks/State government S & T bodies/ Biotech State Councils/Research Hospitals
- Age of business: Not specified
- Sector: Biotechnology
- Revenue cap: Not specified
- Employee count: Not specified
- Other: Incorporation of an Indian legal entity* is recommended for Bioincubators, however it is not a must for the new applicants especially from Indian Institutions including Universities/ Research Institutes/Colleges/ Management Institutes/ Business Schools etc. *Private Company/ Not for profit Section 8 company/Trust/Society - The legal entities under the relevant Law of India having at least 51% Indian (Citizen) stakeholders (owners/partners/trustees/members/associates/directors etc. as applicable).

#### What It Offers
- Amount / Benefit: Grant in aid for both Capital and Operational expenses based on the need and scope of the project as adjudged by the BioNEST committee; Private entities/Institutions - Minimum contribution of 30% will be required for the total project cost by the applicant; Funding to Government entities shall be done as per the following categories: Government Academic Institutions (Centre/State -owned): Support shall be provided as Grant-in-aid; Central Government owned Section -8 company (ies): Support shall be provided as Grant-in-aid; State Government -Owned Section -8 company(ies) /Councils: Minimum contribution of 50% from the applicant is required (for applications under Category 4).
- Disbursement mode: Grant in aid
- Duration of benefit: Initial funding period will be up to three years, with the possibility of a two-year extension based on performance evaluation and the availability of funds

#### Documents Required
1. Proposal form (downloaded from the portal after clicking 'Click here to download format')
2. Filled proposal form converted to PDF and uploaded
3. Details of Project coordinator/lead

#### Application Process (numbered steps)
1. Go to home page of BIRAC and click on the provided link
2. Click on 'Click here to Register'
3. Click on 'Click here to register/apply for BioNEST'
4. The below shown page will open
5. Read the instructions carefully and click on 'next' at the bottom of the page
6. Register by filling in the details
7. After registration, link for password reset will be sent over email. Reset the password to get the login credentials
8. Click on to 'Login/Register'
9. Login using the credentials
10. After login, below shown page will open. Click on 'BioNEST'
11. Click on 'Click here to submit new proposal'
12. The below shown page will open. Kindly fill in details required
13. Kindly fill in 'Basic information'
14. Click on 'Proposal Details'
15. After clicking on 'Proposal Details', below page will open. Download the proposal form by clicking on 'Click here to download format'. Fill the form and convert into PDF and upload
16. Click on 'Project coordinator/lead' and fill in the details
17. Click on Final submit
