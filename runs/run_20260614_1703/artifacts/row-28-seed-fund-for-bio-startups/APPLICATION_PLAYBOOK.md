# Application Playbook

## How to Use This File
For each new client case, assign it a Case ID, pick the target scheme, and follow the steps below.

---

## Stage 1: Eligibility Verification (Day 1–3)

### Checklist
- [ ] Incubator has been supported through BIRAC’s BioNEST programme  
- [ ] Incubator has been operational for the last three years  
- [ ] Incubator has in-house capacity in incubating & mentoring early stage Biotech/Life Sciences startups  
- [ ] Incubator has established IP&TT facilitation services for startups  
- [ ] Incubator has prior experience in management of early-stage funding schemes or other grants  
- [ ] Startup is registered under Companies Act, 2013  
- [ ] Startup has at least 51% shareholders as Indian citizens (excluding OCI/PIO)  
- [ ] Startup is a post-PoC biotech/early-stage life sciences startup  

---

## Stage 2: Document Collection (Day 3–10)

### Universal Documents (always required)
- Certificate of Incorporation / Registration  
- PAN of the entity  
- ITR (last 2–3 years)  
- GST registration certificate  
- Bank statements (last 6 months)  
- Udyam Registration (if applicable)  

### Scheme-Specific Documents
- Shareholding pattern certified by CA/CS (minimum 51% Indian citizen holding)  
- Business description or pitch deck  
- Details of funding received (if any)  
- Authorization letter from authorized signatory  
- Project progress reports and utilization certificates (for half-yearly reporting)  
- Exit report with disinvestment summary (at exit)  

### Document Quality Check
- All documents must be legible scans or certified copies  
- File names must match the document type (e.g., "PAN_Card.pdf", "Shareholding_Pattern_CA_Certified.pdf")  
- File size should not exceed 5 MB per document  
- All certificates must be issued on official letterhead with signature and seal  
- Documents in regional languages must be accompanied by certified English translations  
- Shareholding certificate must be issued by a practicing CA or CS

## Stage 3: Application Drafting (Day 10–18)

### Project Report / DPR (if required)
Not explicitly required for the Seed Fund application by the startup. However, incubators must submit half-yearly implementation reports to BIRAC including Utilization Certificates (UC), Statement of Accounts (SOA), and project progress. **Verify with nodal officer if a DPR is needed for internal incubator processes.**

### Application Form Filling Protocol
1. Ensure all fields in the incubator’s application to BIRAC are complete and accurate  
2. Attach all mandatory documents as per the checklist  
3. Confirm shareholding pattern shows ≥51% Indian citizen shareholders (excluding OCI/PIO)  
4. Validate that the incubator meets all eligibility criteria (BioNEST-supported, 3+ years operational, IP&TT setup, prior grant management experience)  
5. Confirm startup is registered under Companies Act, 2013  
6. Ensure authorization letter is signed by an authorized signatory  
7. Double-check that no startup receives Seed Fund support more than once  
8. Confirm SEED and LEAP fund support will not be concurrent for any startup  

### Internal Review Gate
- [ ] All application fields completed  
- [ ] All universal and scheme-specific documents attached  
- [ ] Shareholding pattern certified by CA/CS confirms ≥51% Indian citizen holding  
- [ ] Authorization letter signed and dated  
- [ ] Incubator eligibility verified (BioNEST-supported, 3+ years operational, IP&TT, prior grant experience)  
- [ ] Startup eligibility verified (Companies Act, 2013 registration, ≥51% Indian shareholders)  
- [ ] Declarations signed (if applicable)  
- [ ] No duplicate Seed Fund support for the startup  
- [ ] SEED and LEAP fund non-concurrency confirmed

## Stage 4: Submission & Follow-Up (Day 18–30)

### Submission Protocol
- Submit the application through the selected BioNEST incubator to BIRAC  
- Obtain and screenshot the submission confirmation or receipt  
- Store the application reference number provided by the incubator or BIRAC  
- Set follow-up reminders for Day 30 and Day 45

## Stage 5: Post-Approval (Day 30–90+)

### Approval Actions
- [ ] Obtain and verify the approval letter from BIRAC  
- [ ] Confirm sanctioned amount (up to INR 30 lakhs per startup as equity/equity-linked instruments)  
- [ ] Ensure grant is parked in the incubator’s savings account under a separate account head  
- [ ] Monitor utilization: funds must be used within 36 months from signing the SEED Fund Implementation Agreement (SIFA)  
- [ ] Prepare for half-yearly reporting: Utilization Certificates (UC), Statement of Accounts (SOA), and project progress reports  
- [ ] At exit: Ensure incubator retains 50% of gross return and restitutes 50% to BIRAC within 60 days  
- [ ] Obtain exit report with disinvestment summary  
- [ ] Coordinate with client on success fee invoicing as per consultancy agreement
