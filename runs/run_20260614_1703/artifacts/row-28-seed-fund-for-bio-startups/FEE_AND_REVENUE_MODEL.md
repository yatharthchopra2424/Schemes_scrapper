## Pricing Principles
1. **Value-Based Alignment**: Fees are structured to reflect the substantial non-dilutive support provided (up to ₹30 lakhs in equity-linked funding) and the scheme’s role in de-risking early-stage biotech ventures for follow-on investment.
2. **Risk Mitigation**: Success fees are capped within standard ranges due to the scheme’s structured implementation via BIRAC-nominated governance and milestone-based disbursement, reducing execution risk despite a 36-month utilization window.
3. **Market Competitiveness**: Pricing mirrors industry norms for grant consulting in deep-tech sectors, where success fees of 4–10% are typical for equity-linked public grants with high technical validation requirements.
4. **Scheme-Specific Note**: The Seed Fund for Bio Startups has a moderate risk profile — while processing involves incubator-level due diligence and half-yearly reporting to BIRAC, the rolling application cycle and high success rate (evidenced by 112 startups supported across partners) support predictable revenue recognition, with a typical 3–6 month lag from application to fund disbursement affecting cash flow timing.
