    OBJECTIVE OF THE FUND

    LEAP Fund support is positioned to act as a catalyst to raise follow on private funding enabling potential startups to pilot/commercialize their products and technologies.

    LEAP support is positioned for Statups to attract Angel/Venture investments. The scheme is implemented through selected BioNEST Incubators who as BIRAC’s LEAP Fund partners receive grant–in-aid support of INR 500 lakhs/ cycle for deployment in Biotech Startups.

    Eligibility to become LEAP Fund Partner
    • Incubator should be BIRAC’s SEED fund partner.
    • Incubator Should be Operational for last 5 years.
    • Incubator should have in-house capacity in incubating & mentoring early stage Biotech / Life Sciences Startups.
    • Incubator should have established IP&TT facilitation services for startups.
    • Incubator must have prior experience of 4 years in management of early stage funding schemes or other grants.
