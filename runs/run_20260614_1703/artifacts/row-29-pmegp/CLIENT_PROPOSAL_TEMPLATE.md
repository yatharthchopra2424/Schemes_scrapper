## 1. Executive Summary
Based on our initial review of [Client Legal Name]'s business profile, we have identified PMEGP — a subsidy scheme administered by the Khadi and Village Industries Commission (KVIC) — for which your business appears eligible. The potential benefit is a subsidy ranging from 15% to 35% of the project cost, with a maximum project cost of ₹50 lakh in the manufacturing sector and ₹20 lakh in the business/service sector.

## 2. Recommended Schemes

| Scheme | Potential Benefit | Est. Timeline | Confidence Level |
|---|---|---|---|
| PMEGP | Up to 35% of project cost (max ₹17.5 lakh for manufacturing, ₹7 lakh for service) | Rolling basis — applications accepted throughout the year | Medium |

## 3. Our Scope of Work
1. Initial eligibility assessment and scheme suitability confirmation  
2. Document collection guidance and verification against PMEGP requirements  
3. Project report preparation and technical feasibility review  
4. Online application submission via the PMEGP e-portal (https://www.kviconline.gov.in/pmegpeportal/pmegphome/index.jsp)  
5. Liaison with District Industries Centre (DIC)/KVIC/KVIB for application scrutiny  
6. Coordination with the selected bank for loan sanction and margin money disbursement  
7. Post-sanction compliance monitoring and lock-in period advisory

3. Response to document requests or queries within 2 business days to avoid delays
