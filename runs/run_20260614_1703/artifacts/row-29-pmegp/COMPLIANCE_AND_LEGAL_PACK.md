**Data Handling Policy:**

1. **Storage:** All client data is stored in encrypted cloud storage with AES-256 encryption at rest
   and TLS 1.2+ in transit.
2. **Access Control:** Access is limited to authorized personnel only, role-based permissions enforced.
3. **Authentication:** Two-factor authentication (2FA) is mandatory for all system access.
4. **Device Policy:** No personal devices permitted for accessing or processing client data.
5. **Retention:** Client data is retained for 2 years post case closure, unless longer retention is
   required by law or scheme guidelines.
6. **Breach Notification:** In the event of a data breach, affected parties and regulators will be
   notified within 24 hours of confirmation.
7. **Scheme-specific note:** All documents submitted to Khadi and Village Industries Commission (KVIC)
   under PMEGP must be retained for a minimum of 3 years for audit purposes.
8. **Disposal:** After retention period, data is securely deleted using cryptographic erasure or
   physical destruction of storage media.
9. **Compliance:** This policy aligns with IT Act 2000, SPDI Rules, and ISO 27001 standards where
   applicable.
