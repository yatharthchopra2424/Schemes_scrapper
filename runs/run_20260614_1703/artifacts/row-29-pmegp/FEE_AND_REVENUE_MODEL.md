## Pricing Principles
1. **Value-Aligned Fees**: Success fees are tied to the actual subsidy disbursed (15–35% of project cost), ensuring clients pay only for realized benefits.
2. **Risk-Adjusted Pricing**: Given the 3–6 month processing time for bank loan sanction and subsidy release (per evidence on instalment release and lock-in period), retainers cover upfront project report preparation and application support.
3. **Tiered Accessibility**: The Growth tier aligns with PMEGP’s focus on MSMEs/startups with project costs between ₹10 lakh (manufacturing) and ₹5 lakh (service), and maximum project costs of ₹50 lakh (manufacturing) and ₹20 lakh (service).
4. **Scheme-Specific Note**: PMEGP has a medium confidence rating due to variability in subsidy rates (15–35%) and dependency on bank loan approval; success fees are structured at the lower end of typical ranges (6–8% for Growth tier) to reflect moderate execution risk and longer revenue lag.

## Invoice Templates

### Retainer Invoice
**Description**: Retainer fee for PMEGP (Prime Minister’s Employment Generation Programme) application support, including project report preparation, eligibility assessment, and submission via the KVIC portal (https://www.kviconline.gov.in/pmegpeportal/pmegphome/index.jsp).

### Success Fee Invoice
**Description**: Success fee for PMEGP subsidy disbursement, calculated as a percentage of the margin money subsidy released by the Khadi and Village Industries Commission (KVIC) through nodal banks, per government norms.
