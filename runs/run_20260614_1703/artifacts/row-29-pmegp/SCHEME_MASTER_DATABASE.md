### PMEGP
- **Scheme ID:** SCH-001
- **Category:** Subsidy
- **Geography:** Pan-India
- **Administering Body:** Khadi and Village Industries Commission (KVIC)
- **Portal / Application URL:** https://www.kviconline.gov.in/pmegpeportal/pmegphome/index.jsp
- **Current Status:** Rolling
- **Window:** Always Open

#### Eligibility Criteria
- Business type: Individuals, self-help groups, institutions, MSME, entrepreneurs
- Age of business: New ventures only (existing units under PMRY, REGP or any other government scheme are not eligible)
- Sector: Manufacturing, business/service
- Revenue cap: Not specified
- Employee count: Not specified
- Other: 
  - Applicant must be above 18 years of age
  - For project cost > Rs. 10 lakh (manufacturing) or > Rs. 5 lakh (business/service): minimum VIII standard pass educational qualification
  - Must not have availed subsidy under any other central or state government scheme
  - Must not be an existing unit under PMRY, REGP or any other government scheme

#### What It Offers
- Amount / Benefit: 
  - Maximum project cost: Rs. 50 lakh (manufacturing), Rs. 20 lakh (business/service)
  - Subsidy (margin money): 15% to 35% of project cost (based on beneficiary category and location)
  - Balance project cost: Provided as loan by banks
- Disbursement mode: Direct credit to special term deposit account (non-withdrawable until lock-in period)
- Duration of benefit: One-time subsidy credit after project verification and viability check

#### Documents Required
1. Project Report
2. Proof of Age (Birth Certificate, School Leaving Certificate, etc.)
3. Educational Qualification Certificate (if applicable)
4. Caste Certificate (for SC/ST/OBC beneficiaries)
5. Special Category Certificate (if applicable, e.g., ex-servicemen, physically handicapped)
6. Rural Area Certificate (if applicable)
7. Bank Account Details
8. Identity Proof (Aadhaar, Voter ID, PAN, etc.)
9. Address Proof
10. Two Passport Size Photographs

#### Application Process (numbered steps)
1. The applicant must prepare a project report for the proposed micro-enterprise.
2. The applicant applies online through the PMEGP e-portal (https://www.kviconline.gov.in/pmegpeportal/pmegphome/index.jsp) or submits the application form offline to the concerned District Industries Centre (DIC) or KVIC/KVIB office.
3. The application is scrutinized by the concerned agency (DIC/KVIC/KVIB) for eligibility and viability.
4. Upon approval, the application is forwarded to the selected bank for sanction of loan.
5. The bank sanctions the loan and disburses the margin money subsidy (as per government norms) into a special term deposit account.
6. The beneficiary sets up the enterprise and starts operations.
7. The bank monitors the account and ensures compliance with scheme guidelines.
