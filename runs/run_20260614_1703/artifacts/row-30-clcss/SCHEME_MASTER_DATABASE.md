### CLCSS
- **Scheme ID:** SCH-001
- **Category:** Subsidy
- **Geography:** Pan-India
- **Administering Body:** Ministry of Micro, Small and Medium Enterprises (MSME), Government of India
- **Portal / Application URL:** https://nimsme.gov.in/about-scheme/credit-linked-capital-subsidy-scheme-for-technology-upgradation
- **Current Status:** Rolling
- **Window:** Always Open

#### Eligibility Criteria
- Business type: Micro and Small Enterprises (MSEs)
- Age of business: Not specified
- Sector: 51 approved sub-sectors/products for technology upgradation
- Revenue cap: Not specified
- Employee count: Not specified
- Other: Must have availed institutional finance of up to Rs 1 crore for technology upgradation; technology must be well-established and improved and duly approved under scheme guidelines; includes new MSEs that have set up facilities with appropriate eligible and proven technology

#### What It Offers
- Amount / Benefit: Upfront capital subsidy of 15% on institutional finance availed, with a maximum eligible loan amount of Rs 1 crore, resulting in a maximum subsidy of Rs 15 lakh per unit
- Disbursement mode: Direct credit to the lending institution, which adjusts it against the borrower's loan account
- Duration of benefit: One-time

#### Documents Required
1. Loan sanction letter from bank/financial institution
2. Proof of loan disbursement
3. Invoice and payment proof for machinery/technology purchased
4. Certificate of installation and commissioning
5. Details of technology adopted (specification, approval under 51 sub-sectors)
6. UDYAM Registration Certificate
7. Bank account details
8. Authorization letter for subsidy claim

#### Application Process (numbered steps)
1. Avail institutional finance (term loan) from a bank or financial institution for technology upgradation.
2. Ensure the technology is among the 51 approved sub-sectors/products under CLCSS.
3. Submit application through the lending institution with required documents.
4. Lending institution forwards the claim to the nodal agency.
5. Nodal agency verifies eligibility and approves subsidy.
6. Subsidy amount (15% of loan, up to Rs 15 lakh) is released to the lending institution, which adjusts it against the borrower's loan account.
