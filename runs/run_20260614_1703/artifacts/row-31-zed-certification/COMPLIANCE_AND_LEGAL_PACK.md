## 8D. Internal Data Handling Policy

(Standard data handling policy — encrypted cloud storage, 2FA, limited access, no personal devices,
2-year retention post case closure, 24-hour breach notification.)

**Purpose:** To ensure the secure handling, storage, and disposal of client data in compliance with applicable data protection laws and scheme-specific requirements.

**Scope:** This policy applies to all consultants, employees, and contractors of [Firm Name] who handle client data in relation to the ZED Certification scheme.

**Policy Statements:**
1. **Data Storage:** All client data shall be stored in encrypted cloud storage environments with access controls.
2. **Access Control:** Access to client data is limited to authorized personnel only, based on role and necessity.
3. **Authentication:** Multi-factor authentication (2FA) is required for all access to systems containing client data.
4. **Device Usage:** No personal devices shall be used to store, process, or transmit client data.
5. **Retention Period:** Client data shall be retained for a period of two (2) years after case closure, unless otherwise required by law or scheme guidelines.
6. **Scheme-Specific Retention:** All documents submitted to Quality Council of India (QCI) under ZED Certification must be retained for a minimum of 3 years for audit purposes.
7. **Breach Notification:** In the event of a data breach, the firm shall notify affected clients and relevant authorities within 24 hours of discovery.
8. **Data Disposal:** Upon expiration of the retention period, client data shall be securely deleted or destroyed using approved methods.
9. **Training:** All personnel handling client data shall receive annual training on data protection and privacy obligations.
10. **Audit Compliance:** The firm shall maintain logs of data access and processing for audit and compliance purposes.

**Enforcement:** Violation of this policy may result in disciplinary action, up to and including termination of employment or contract.
