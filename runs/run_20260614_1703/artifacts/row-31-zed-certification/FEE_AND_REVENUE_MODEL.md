## Pricing Principles
1. **Value-Based Pricing**: Fees are aligned with the financial benefit delivered to the client, leveraging the scheme’s structured subsidies (up to 100% for women-owned MSMEs on certification cost, plus handholding support up to ₹5 Lakhs with 80% subsidy for micro enterprises).
2. **Risk Mitigation**: Success fees are contingent on successful certification approval, reducing client financial risk; the scheme’s rolling basis and high approval rates (evidenced by 647,274 Bronze, 6,138 Silver, and 4,645 Gold certifications) support predictable outcomes.
3. **Transparency**: All fees are disclosed upfront; the scheme mandates that consultancy fees are prescribed in guidelines and paid only via the ZED portal, ensuring no hidden charges.
4. **Scalability**: Tiered retainer and success fee structures accommodate varying MSME sizes and certification levels (Bronze/Silver/Gold), with applicability focused on Starter and Growth tiers due to the scheme’s MSME-specific eligibility and benefit caps.
