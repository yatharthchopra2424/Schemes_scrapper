# Pitch & Sales Scripts

## Philosophy
We are advisors, not salespeople. We diagnose first. We only pitch a scheme if it fits the client. We never oversell success rates.

---

## 1. Discovery Call Script (First Contact — 20 min)

### Opening (2 min)
Good morning/afternoon! I specialize in helping MSMEs access government incentives for quality improvement and sustainability. The ZED Certification scheme from the Ministry of MSME, implemented by the Quality Council of India, offers substantial financial support for certification and handholding—perfect for manufacturers looking to enhance competitiveness, reduce waste, and access new markets. I’d like to understand your current operations and see if this aligns with your growth goals.

### Discovery Questions
1. What is your Udyam Registration Number, and when was your enterprise registered?  
2. Are you a manufacturing MSME, or do you plan to include service activities under ZED in the future?  
3. What is your current employee count and annual turnover to determine if you qualify as Micro, Small, or Medium?  
4. Are you owned by a Woman, SC/ST entrepreneur, or located in NER/Himalayan/LWE/Island/aspirational districts?  
5. Are you part of any SFURTI or MSE-CDP cluster programme?  
6. Have you already taken the ZED Pledge on the portal https://zed.msme.gov.in/?  
7. What quality or environmental certifications (e.g., ISO 9001, ISO 14001) do you currently hold, if any?  
8. Are you interested in financial support for technology upgradation (up to Rs. 3 Lakhs) or handholding (up to Rs. 2 Lakhs)?

### Qualification Checklist (internal — don't read aloud)
- [ ] Udyam Registered MSME (manufacturing or service)  
- [ ] Valid Udyam Number and associated mobile number  
- [ ] Enterprise falls under Micro, Small, or Medium classification  
- [ ] Willing to take ZED Pledge online (free)  
- [ ] Able to provide GST/TAN (if applicable) and representative selfie/site photos  
- [ ] Not currently debarred or ineligible due to prior scheme misuse  

### Transition to Pitch
Based on your responses, you appear eligible for the ZED Certification scheme, which offers up to 90% subsidy on certification costs and financial assistance of up to Rs. 5 Lakhs for handholding and technology upgrades. Let me walk you through how this works and the real benefits it can deliver for your business.

## 2. Scheme Pitch (5 min)

### Structure: Problem → Scheme → Value → Proof → Next Step

**Problem framing:**
Many MSMEs struggle with inconsistent quality, high rejection rates, limited market access, and difficulty adopting sustainable practices due to lack of resources and guidance—hindering competitiveness in domestic and export markets.

**Scheme intro:**
The MSME Sustainable (ZED) Certification Scheme, administered by the Quality Council of India (QCI) under the Ministry of MSME, provides financial assistance for Zero Defect Zero Effect (ZED) Certification at Bronze, Silver, and Gold levels. The cost of certification is Rs. 10,000 (Bronze), Rs. 40,000 (Silver), and Rs. 90,000 (Gold), with subsidies based on enterprise size and additional incentives for special categories.

**Why they qualify (personalised):**
- You are a Udyam-registered MSME, making you eligible to apply  
- Your enterprise classification (Micro/Small/Medium) determines your base subsidy (80%/60%/50%)  
- If you are a women-owned, SC/ST, or located in NER/Himalayan/LWE/Island/aspirational districts, you qualify for an additional 10% subsidy  
- If part of SFURTI or MSE-CDP, you qualify for an extra 5% subsidy
