### ZED Certification
- **Scheme ID:** SCH-001
- **Category:** Subsidy
- **Geography:** Pan-India
- **Administering Body:** Quality Council of India (QCI)
- **Portal / Application URL:** https://zed.msme.gov.in/
- **Current Status:** Rolling
- **Window:** Always Open

#### Eligibility Criteria
- Business type: All MSMEs registered with the UDYAM registration portal (of the MoMSME)
- Age of business: Not specified
- Sector: Manufacturing and Service Sector (Phase 1: Manufacturing MSMEs with UDYAM Registration; Phase 2: Inclusion of Service Sector MSMEs)
- Revenue cap: Not specified
- Employee count: Not specified
- Other: Any number of Units registered under one Udyam Registration can apply for subsidy under this Scheme. Each Unit (under one Udyam Registration) will need to apply for Certification separately to avail subsidy/benefits/incentives.

#### What It Offers
- Amount / Benefit: 
  - Certification cost: Bronze: Rs. 10,000, Silver: Rs. 40,000, Gold: Rs. 90,000 (as per Amendment_ZED_guideline_2.0_17.9.2024.pdf)
  - Subsidy on cost of certification: Micro Enterprises: 80%, Small Enterprises: 60%, Medium Enterprises: 50%
  - Additional subsidy: 10% for MSMEs owned by Women/SC/ST Entrepreneurs OR MSMEs in NER/Himalayan/LWE/Island territories/aspirational districts
  - Additional subsidy: 5% for MSMEs which are also a part of the SFURTI OR Micro & Small Enterprises - Cluster Development Programme (MSE-CDP) of the Ministry
  - Limited-purpose joining reward of Rs. 10,000/- offered to each MSME once they take the ZED Pledge, to be utilized within 1 year for ZED Certification (Bronze, Silver, Gold)
  - For handholding support: up to Rs. 5 Lakhs per MSME (up to Rs. 2 Lakhs for handholding support and up to Rs. 3 Lakhs for technology upgradation)
  - For surveillance assessment: Silver Level: Rs. 10,000/-, Gold Level: Rs. 20,000/-
  - Women-owned MSMEs are eligible for 100% subsidy on cost of certification (effective from 11.11.2023)
  - Free MSME KAWACH (WASH Certification) for ZED registered MSMEs
  - Assistance for obtaining international certifications like CE for international market access
- Disbursement mode: Direct credit (subsidy applied to reduce MSME contribution; payment made via payment gateway for MSME's share)
- Duration of benefit: One-time per certification level (certificate valid for 3 years; surveillance assessments required for Silver and Gold levels)

#### Documents Required
1. Udyam Registration Number
2. Mobile Number associated with Udyam Registration
3. Valid Email ID
4. GST Number (if applicable)
5. TAN Number (if applicable)
6. MSME Representative Selfie
7. Site Exterior Photographs
8. Basic Information and Documents (as per certification level requirements)

#### Application Process
1. Register on the ZED portal (https://zed.msme.gov.in/) by providing Udyam Registration Number and associated mobile number.
2. Validate Udyam registration through API.
3. Provide valid Email ID for receiving ZED ID, Password, and Pledge Certificate.
4. Take the ZED Pledge online.
5. After registration, login with ZED ID and password.
6. Apply for desired ZED Certification Level (Bronze, Silver, or Gold) through the dashboard.
7. Download the ZED MSME mobile application from Play Store (https://play.google.com/store/apps/details?id=com.qci.zed.msme) or App Store.
8. Provide GST Number and TAN Number as applicable.
9. Opt to avail the Joining Reward of Rs. 10,000/- (if applicable), which will be deducted from the total cost of certification.
10. Proceed to payment of the MSME contribution (certification fee minus any joining reward applied).
11. Select payment option from the list and make payment via payment gateway.
12. After successful payment, submit Basic Information and Documents via mobile application: capture MSME Representative Selfie and Site Exterior Photographs, and upload required documents.
13. Assessor will verify the information and documents submitted.
14. In case of non-conformity, the applicant MSME will be given an opportunity to close the non-conformity(ies) within a defined time period (30 days for Bronze, 60 days for Silver/Gold surveillance, etc.).
15. After successful verification, the ZED Certificate and Assessment Report will be issued and sent to the applicant's email ID and made available on the applicant dashboard.
