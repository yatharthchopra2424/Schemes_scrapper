# Comprehensive Scheme Masterclass & File Guide

## Scheme Deep Dive

### Overview
The **ZED Certification** (Zero Defect Zero Effect) is a subsidy-based scheme implemented by the **Quality Council of India (QCI)** under the **Ministry of MSME**. It aims to enhance the competitiveness, sustainability, and quality of Micro, Small, and Medium Enterprises (MSMEs) across India through a structured certification process at **Bronze, Silver, and Gold levels**. The scheme operates on a **rolling basis** with no fixed deadline, accepting applications year-round via the portal: **https://zed.msme.gov.in/**.

### Objectives
- Encourage and enable MSMEs to manufacture quality products using latest technology, tools, and process upgrades for high quality and productivity with minimal environmental impact.
- Develop an ecosystem for ZED manufacturing in MSMEs to enhance competitiveness and enable exports.
- Promote adoption of ZED practices and recognize successful MSMEs.
- Encourage MSMEs to achieve higher ZED Certification levels through graded incentives.
- Increase public awareness on demanding Zero Defect and Zero Effect products.
- Identify areas for improvement to assist government in policy decisions and investment prioritization.

### Eligibility Matrix
All MSMEs registered with the **UDYAM registration portal** (MoMSME) are eligible. Each unit under one Udyam Registration must apply separately for certification to avail benefits.

| Criteria | Details |
|---------|---------|
| **Target Beneficiaries** | MSME (Micro, Small, Medium Enterprises) |
| **Registration Requirement** | Valid Udyam Registration Number |
| **Geographic Scope** | Pan-India |
| **Implementing Agency** | Quality Council of India (QCI) |
| **Application Portal** | https://zed.msme.gov.in/ |
| **Status / Deadlines** | Rolling basis — no fixed deadline. Applications accepted year-round. |
| **Last Updated** | 2024 |
| **Scheme Validity** | Financial support, incentives, benefits valid till **March 31, 2026** (as per Guidelines 2022) |
| **Special Notes** | Any number of units under one Udyam Registration can apply. Each unit must apply separately for subsidy/benefits. |

### Benefits & Financial Support

#### Cost of Certification (as per Amendment_ZED_guideline_2.0_17.9.2024.pdf)
| Certification Level | Original Cost | Amended Cost (Effective 17.09.2024) |
|---------------------|---------------|--------------------------------------|
| Bronze              | Rs. 8,000     | **Rs. 10,000**                       |
| Silver              | Rs. 32,000    | **Rs. 40,000**                       |
| Gold                | Rs. 72,000    | **Rs. 90,000**                       |
*Note: Includes verification, assessment, certification, monitoring, and taxes.*

#### Subsidy on Cost of Certification
| Enterprise Type | Base Subsidy | Additional Subsidy (Women/SC/ST or Special Area) | Additional Subsidy (SFURTI/MSE-CDP) | Max Total Subsidy |
|-----------------|--------------|--------------------------------------------------|-------------------------------------|-------------------|
| Micro Enterprises | 80%          | +10%                                             | +5%                                 | **95%**           |
| Small Enterprises | 60%          | +10%                                             | +5%                                 | **75%**           |
| Medium Enterprises | 50%         | +10%                                             | +5%                                 | **65%**           |
| **Women-owned MSMEs** | — | **100%** (effective 11.11.2023) | — | **100%** |

> **> 💡 Key Takeaway**: Women-owned MSMEs receive **100% subsidy** on certification cost (effective 11.11.2023), making certification free for them.

#### Joining Reward
- **Rs. 10,000/-** offered once per MSME after taking the **ZED Pledge**.
- Must be utilized **within 1 year** of taking the pledge.
- Can be applied **only once** toward Bronze, Silver, or Gold certification.
- **If used for Bronze**: Effective cost to MSME becomes **zero** (after subsidy).
- **If used for Silver/Gold**: Reward deducted from certification cost; subsidy applies on the balance.

#### Handholding & Technology Upgradation Support
- **Up to Rs. 5 Lakhs per MSME**:
  - **Up to Rs. 2 Lakhs** for handholding support.
  - **Up to Rs. 3 Lakhs** for technology upgradation (zero effect solutions/pollution control/cleaner tech).
- **Subsidy Structure**:
  - Micro Enterprises: 80%
  - Small Enterprises: 60%
  - Medium Enterprises: 50%
  - **+10%** for Women/SC/ST or special area MSMEs (NER/Himalayan/LWE/Island/aspirational districts)
  - **+5%** for SFURTI/MSE-CDP participants

#### Surveillance Assessment (Mandatory for Silver & Gold)
| Level | Assessment Cost | Subsidy (Micro/Small/Medium) | Notes |
|-------|------------------|-------------------------------|-------|
| **Silver** | Rs. 10,000 | 80% / 60% / 50% | Conducted between 12–18 months post-certification |
| **Gold**   | Rs. 20,000 | 80% / 60% / 50% | Conducted between 12–18 months post-certification |
> **> ⚠️ Caveat**: Two surveillance assessments required within 3-year cycle: <br>
> - **Surveillance 1**: 12–18 months after certification <br>
> - **Surveillance 2**: 24–30 months after certification <br>
> Failure to complete results in **certification suspension/withdrawal**; reapplication requires full fee payment.

#### Additional Incentives
- **Free MSME KAWACH (WASH Certification)** for ZED-registered MSMEs.
- Assistance for obtaining **international certifications (e.g., CE)** for global market access.
- Financial assistance for **Testing/System/Product Certification** (75% subsidy, max Rs. 50,000) post-ZED certification.

### Application Process (Mermaid Flowchart)
```mermaid
flowchart TD
    A[Start: Visit https://zed.msme.gov.in/] --> B[Register: Enter Udyam No. + Linked Mobile No.]
    B --> C[Validate Udyam via API]
    C --> D[Provide Valid Email ID → Receive ZED ID, Password, Pledge Certificate]
    D --> E[Take ZED Pledge Online]
    E --> F[Login with ZED ID & Password]
    F --> G[Apply for Desired Certification Level: Bronze/Silver/Gold]
    G --> H[Download ZED MSME Mobile App (Play Store/App Store)]
    H --> I[Provide GST No. & TAN No. (if applicable)]
    I --> J[Opt for Joining Reward of Rs. 10,000 (if eligible)]
    J --> K[Pay MSME Contribution: Certification Fee − Joining Reward (if used)]
    K --> L[Select Payment Gateway → Complete Online Payment]
    L --> M[Submit Basic Info & Documents via Mobile App:<br/>• MSME Representative Selfie<br/>• Site Exterior Photographs<br/>• Required Docs (per level)]
    M --> N[Assessor Verifies Info & Docs]
    N --> O{Non-Conformity?}
    O -->|Yes| P[Given Defined Time to Close NC:<br/>• Bronze: 30 days<br/>• Silver/Gold: 60 days]
    P --> Q[Close NC via Mobile App → Resubmit]
    Q --> N
    O -->|No| R[Issue ZED Certificate & Assessment Report]
    R --> S[Send via Email + Available on Dashboard]
    S --> T[End: Certification Valid for 3 Years]
    T --> U[For Silver/Gold: Schedule Surveillance Assessments<br/>• 1st: 12–18 months<br/>• 2nd: 24–30 months]
```

> **> 🔗 Source**: Application process detailed in [ZED Portal](https://zed.msme.gov.in/) and user manuals (Bronze/Silver/Gold Certification).
