## Pricing Principles
1. Value-based pricing aligned with the scheme’s facilitation of access to finance, marketing, technology, and capacity building support.
2. Tiered retainer structure reflecting client scale and complexity of handholding required via the Champions portal.
3. Success fee capped at 8% for MSME clients, reflecting the non-financial, facilitative nature of the scheme (no direct grants).
4. Scheme-specific note: The MSME Champions Scheme has a low financial risk profile as it provides guidance and handholding rather than direct funding; success depends on client uptake of facilitated services, with processing time being immediate (rolling basis), enabling faster revenue recognition.
