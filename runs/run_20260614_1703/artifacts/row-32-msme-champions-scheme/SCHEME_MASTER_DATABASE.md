### MSME Champions Scheme
- **Scheme ID:** SCH-001
- **Category:** Subsidy
- **Geography:** Pan-India
- **Administering Body:** Ministry of Micro, Small & Medium Enterprises
- **Portal / Application URL:** https://msme.gov.in
- **Current Status:** Rolling
- **Window:** Always Open

#### Eligibility Criteria
- Business type: MSMEs
- Age of business: Not specified
- Sector: Not specified
- Revenue cap: Not specified
- Employee count: Not specified
- Other: MSMEs are eligible to benefit from the Champions portal as part of the Ministry's initiatives for redressal, guidance, and handholding.

#### What It Offers
- Amount / Benefit: Support in finance, marketing, technology, and capacity building; information dissemination in eleven regional languages through 69 State control rooms nationwide; redressal, guidance, and handholding for MSMEs.
- Disbursement mode: Not applicable (non-financial support)
- Duration of benefit: Ongoing

#### Documents Required
1. No specific documents required for access to the Champions portal (self-service platform)

#### Application Process
1. Access the Champions portal through the Ministry's website.
2. Utilize the platform for redressal, guidance, and handholding.
3. Access information in eleven regional languages via State control rooms.
4. Seek support in finance, marketing, technology, and capacity building.
