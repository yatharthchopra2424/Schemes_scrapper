## Pricing Principles
1. Value-based pricing aligned with the financial benefit delivered to the client.
2. Transparent fee structure with no hidden charges.
3. Retainer covers initial assessment, documentation, and application submission.
4. Success fee is contingent upon approval of financial support under the scheme.
   - **Scheme-specific note**: The MSME Incubation Scheme has a medium processing time and moderate success rate based on incubator availability and fund allocation, resulting in a medium risk profile for consultants.
