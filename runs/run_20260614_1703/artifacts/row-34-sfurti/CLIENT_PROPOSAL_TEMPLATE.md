## 3. Our Scope of Work
1. Initial eligibility assessment and scheme suitability analysis  
2. Documentation review and gap analysis against scheme requirements  
3. Preparation and submission of concept note and supporting documents via the SFURTI portal  
4. Coordination with Nodal Agency for concept validation and scorecard submission  
5. Preparation of Detailed Project Report (DPR) as per SFURTI guidelines  
6. Facilitation of land verification and IA/SPV share confirmation (10% or 5% for NER/Hilly areas)  
7. Portal registration and application submission on [https://sfurti.msme.gov.in](https://sfurti.msme.gov.in)  
8. Liaison with Technical Agency for DPR preparation and validation  
9. Support in obtaining statutory clearances and approvals  
10. Monitoring of application status and response to queries from Nodal Agency/MSME  
11. Assistance in fund utilization and submission of progress reports and utilization certificates  
12. Handholding till project sanction and disbursement of funds  
13. Post-sanction compliance support for 10 years as per scheme requirements
