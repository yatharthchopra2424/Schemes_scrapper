### SFURTI
- **Scheme ID:** SCH-001
- **Category:** Grant
- **Geography:** Pan-India
- **Administering Body:** Ministry of Micro, Small and Medium Enterprises
- **Portal / Application URL:** https://sfurti.msme.gov.in
- **Current Status:** Open
- **Window:** Always Open

#### Eligibility Criteria
- Business type: Implementing Agency (IA) registered as NGO, Government Organization/PRI, or Special Purpose Vehicle (SPV)
- Age of business: Minimum 1 year of existence (for IA/SPV)
- Sector: Traditional Industries (Khadi, Village Industries, Coir Industries)
- Revenue cap: Not explicitly mentioned
- Employee count: Not explicitly mentioned
- Other: IA must authenticate list of artisans, form project-specific SPV as 'for profit' entity, ensure land availability for CFC construction (owned or leased for 15+ years), confirm IA/SPV's share of 10% (5% for NER and Hilly areas)

#### What It Offers
- Amount / Benefit: Financial support up to Rs. 5 crore per cluster (Regular Clusters: up to Rs. 2.50 crore; Major Clusters: up to Rs. 5.00 crore); Working Capital up to 20% of project cost as revolving fund for initial production cycle
- Disbursement mode: Disbursed to Implementing Agencies (IAs) through Nodal Agencies (NAs) on recommendation of Technical Agencies (TAs)
- Duration of benefit: One-time grant for project implementation (Regular Clusters: 12 months; Major Clusters: 18 months)

#### Documents Required
1. Project Initiation Reports
2. Project Completion Reports
3. Project Evaluation/Monitoring reports
4. Certificate of Incorporation (with renewal if applicable)
5. Self-attested year-wise project summary report
6. Self-attested village-wise number of beneficiary summary report
7. Self-attested summary report indicating types of interventions undertaken
8. Audited Statement of last three years (Income & Expenditure Account)
9. Audited Statement of last three years (Income & Expenditure Account and Balance Sheet)
10. Utilisation Certificate (for Completed Projects)
11. Grant Letter (for Ongoing Projects)
12. Self-attested summary report mentioning interventions carried out in the area/for artisans
13. Self-attested comparative table indicating present income of artisans and break-up/justification of income increase
14. Registered lease deed (if land is leased by SPV)
15. Registered sale or lease deed (long term of 15 years or more) in IA/SPV's name for land availability

#### Application Process
1. Register as an Implementing Agency on the SFURTI portal to create user ID and password.
2. Log in to the portal and download the Instruction/Guidelines for filling Concept Application and Scorecard.
3. Select 'Scorecard' from the dashboard.
4. Fill the basic details form and save.
5. Select the 'Edit' option to proceed after saving Cluster Details.
6. Upload the duly filled Concept Application Form (download format from dashboard if new applicant).
7. Fill details against each indicator, selecting options and uploading supporting evidence simultaneously before moving to the next indicator.
8. Upload supporting evidence for each indicator by choosing and uploading files (PDF, max 5 MB per indicator).
9. For indicators requiring multiple options, select multiple checkboxes as per instructions.
10. View the scorecard from the 'View Scorecard' tab.
11. Select the preferred Nodal Agency from the drop-down list and click the forward button to submit the scorecard with application and uploaded documents to the concerned Nodal Agency.
12. Upon forwarding, a submission intimation message is displayed; the scorecard can be printed if required.
