## Pricing Principles
1. Retainer covers initial eligibility assessment, documentation review, and application preparation.
2. Success fee is contingent on grant approval and disbursement, aligning consultant incentives with client outcomes.
3. Tiered pricing reflects scheme accessibility: TREAD for Women targets micro and small enterprises (up to ₹10 lakhs grant), making Starter and Growth tiers most relevant.
4. Scheme-specific note: Processing involves installment-based disbursement tied to project milestones, introducing moderate risk due to beneficiary’s 70% cost contribution requirement and potential delays in utilization reporting.
