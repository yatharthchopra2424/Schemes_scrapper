# Pitch & Sales Scripts

## Philosophy
We are advisors, not salespeople. We diagnose first. We only pitch a scheme if it fits the client. We never oversell success rates.

---

## 1. Discovery Call Script (First Contact — 20 min)

### Opening (2 min)
Good morning/afternoon! I specialize in helping women entrepreneurs access government grants for setting up or expanding their micro and small enterprises in the non-farm sector. The TREAD for Women scheme from the Ministry of MSME offers financial support, training, and market linkages specifically designed for women like you. I’d love to understand your business goals and see if this scheme aligns with your needs.

### Discovery Questions
1. Are you a woman entrepreneur above 18 years of age?
2. Are you planning to set up or already running a micro or small enterprise in the non-farm sector (e.g., manufacturing, services, trading)?
3. What is your current stage—idea, planning, or operational?
4. Do you belong to any of the preferred categories: SC/ST, OBC, minority, or differently abled communities?
5. Have you prepared a detailed project report for your business idea or expansion plan?
6. What is the estimated total project cost for your venture?
7. Are you able to contribute at least 70% of the project cost from your own funds or through a loan?
8. Do you have documents like identity proof, address proof, bank details, and quotations for machinery/equipment ready?

### Qualification Checklist (internal — don't read aloud)
- [ ] Woman entrepreneur above 18 years of age
- [ ] Engaged in or planning to set up a micro or small enterprise
- [ ] Enterprise is in the non-farm sector
- [ ] Belongs to SC/ST/OBC/minority/differently abled community (preference given)
- [ ] Project report prepared
- [ ] Able to contribute ≥70% of project cost from own sources or loan
- [ ] Grant sought is for non-farm sector activities only
- [ ] Not seeking grant for land acquisition or working capital

### Transition to Pitch
Based on your responses, it looks like you may be a strong fit for the TREAD for Women scheme, which provides grants of up to INR 10 lakhs (30% of project cost) along with training and market linkage support. Let me share how this scheme can specifically help you.

## 2. Scheme Pitch (5 min)

### Structure: Problem → Scheme → Value → Proof → Next Step

**Problem framing:**
Many women entrepreneurs struggle to access sufficient funding to start or grow their businesses due to lack of collateral, limited credit history, or societal barriers. They also often need guidance on business planning, skills, and market access to succeed.

**Scheme intro:**
TREAD for Women (Trade Related Entrepreneurship Assistance and Development) is a grant scheme by the Ministry of Micro, Small and Medium Enterprises (MSME), Government of India. It provides a grant of up to 30% of the project cost, with a maximum of INR 10 lakhs per enterprise. The remaining 70% must be contributed by the beneficiary or arranged via loans.

**Why they qualify (personalised):**
- You are a woman entrepreneur above 18 years of age ✅  
- You are planning or operating a micro/small enterprise in the non-farm sector ✅  
- You belong to a preferred category (SC/ST/OBC/minority/differently abled) — giving you priority consideration ✅  
- You have or can prepare a project report and contribute at least 70% of project cost ✅

We offer a free, no-obligation eligibility check. If you qualify, we assist with project report preparation, application submission, and end-to-end handholding—charging only a success fee upon grant approval.
