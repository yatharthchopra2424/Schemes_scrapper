# Comprehensive Scheme Masterclass & File Guide

## Scheme Deep Dive

### Scheme Overview
**Scheme Name:** TREAD for Women  
**Scheme ID:** row-35  
**Ministry / Category:** MSME  
**Scheme Type:** grant  
**Geographic Scope:** Pan-India  
**Implementing Agency:** Ministry of Micro, Small and Medium Enterprises  
**Status / Deadlines:** Rolling basis  
**Max Per Entity:** Up to INR 10 Lakhs  
**Application Portal URL:** https://msme.gov.in/tread *(Note: Currently returns "Page Not Found", but scheme details are confirmed via key facts)*  

### Objectives
- To promote entrepreneurship among women  
- To provide financial assistance for setting up and expanding women-led enterprises  
- To enhance skills and capabilities of women entrepreneurs through training  
- To facilitate access to credit and market linkages  
- To support women in non-farm sectors  
- To encourage innovation and technology adoption by women entrepreneurs  

### Eligibility Matrix
| Criteria | Details |
|---------|---------|
| **Beneficiary Type** | Women entrepreneurs |
| **Age Requirement** | Above 18 years of age |
| **Enterprise Status** | Engaged in or planning to set up micro and small enterprises |
| **Sector** | Non-farm sector only |
| **Preference Groups** | SC/ST, OBC, minority, and differently abled communities |
| **Geographic Scope** | Pan-India |

### Benefits & Financial Support
| Support Type | Details |
|--------------|---------|
| **Financial Assistance** | Grant of up to 30% of project cost, with a maximum ceiling of Rs. 10 lakhs per enterprise |
| **Beneficiary Contribution** | Remaining 70% of project cost to be borne by beneficiary or arranged via loans from financial institutions |
| **Non-Financial Support** | Training and skill development support, market linkage assistance, handholding for enterprise setup and expansion |
| **Grant Usage Restrictions** | Cannot be used for land acquisition or working capital |
| **Disbursement Mode** | Installments based on project progress and utilization |

### Required Documents
1. Project report  
2. Proof of identity (Aadhaar, PAN, etc.)  
3. Address proof  
4. Bank account details  
5. Educational qualification certificates (if applicable)  
6. Caste certificate (for SC/ST/OBC beneficiaries)  
7. Disability certificate (if applicable)  
8. Quotations for machinery and equipment  
9. Land/building ownership or lease documents  
10. No objection certificate from pollution control board (if applicable)  

### Application Process Flowchart
```mermaid
flowchart TD
    A[Identify viable business idea and prepare project report] --> B[Approach designated implementing agency (KVIC, Coir Board, or MSME-DFO)]
    B --> C[Submit application form with project report and required documents]
    C --> D[Undergo evaluation and screening by agency]
    D --> E{Approval?}
    E -->|Yes| F[Receive sanction letter]
    F --> G[Disbursement of grant in installments]
    G --> H[Utilize funds for setting up/expanding enterprise per approved plan]
    H --> I[Submit utilization reports and progress updates]
    E -->|No| J[Application rejected; feedback provided for resubmission]
```

### Key Caveats
> **Warning:** Grant is disbursed in installments based on project progress and utilization. Beneficiary must contribute at least 70% of project cost from own sources or loan. Scheme applicable only for non-farm sector activities. Preference given to women from SC/ST, OBC, minority, and differently abled communities. Grant cannot be used for land acquisition or working capital.
