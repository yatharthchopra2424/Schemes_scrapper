## 8D. Internal Data Handling Policy

All client data and scheme application materials shall be handled in accordance with the following internal data handling policy:

- **Storage:** All documents are stored in encrypted cloud storage with access controls.
- **Access:** Access is limited to authorized consultants only; role-based permissions enforced.
- **Authentication:** Two-factor authentication (2FA) is mandatory for all system access.
- **Device Policy:** No personal devices may be used to store or process client data.
- **Retention:** Client data is retained for a maximum of two (2) years post case closure, after which it is securely deleted.
- **Breach Notification:** In the event of a data breach, affected parties and relevant authorities will be notified within 24 hours.
- **Audit Logs:** All access to client data is logged and monitored for suspicious activity.

**Scheme-Specific Note:** All documents submitted to Ministry of Agriculture & Farmers Welfare under Agri Clinics & Agribusiness Centres must be retained for a minimum of 3 years for audit purposes.
