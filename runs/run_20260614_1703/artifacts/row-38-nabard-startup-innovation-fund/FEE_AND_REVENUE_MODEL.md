## Pricing Principles
1. **Value-Aligned Fees**: Success fees are calibrated to the actual financial benefit delivered under the NABARD Startup & Innovation Fund, ensuring clients pay only for realized outcomes.
2. **Risk-Adjusted Pricing**: Given the scheme’s low confidence rating and limited publicly available evidence on approval rates and processing times, success fees reflect the higher uncertainty in securing funding.
3. **Accessibility for Early-Stage Ventures**: The Growth tier pricing is structured to support startups and MSMEs in the agriculture and rural innovation space, aligning with the scheme’s target beneficiaries.
4. **Transparency and Predictability**: Retainer fees cover initial eligibility assessment and application preparation, while success fees are contingent on fund disbursement, with clear milestones defined in engagement letters.

*Note: The scheme’s processing time and approval likelihood are not well-documented in public sources, increasing revenue lag risk; consultants should adjust cash flow projections accordingly.*
