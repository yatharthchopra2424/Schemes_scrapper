# Pitch & Sales Scripts

## Philosophy
We are advisors, not salespeople. We diagnose first. We only pitch a scheme if it fits the client. We never oversell success rates.

---

## 1. Discovery Call Script (First Contact — 20 min)

### Opening (2 min)
Good morning/afternoon. I specialise in helping agri-startups and rural innovation ventures access government funding designed specifically for early-stage enterprises in the agriculture and allied sectors. The NABARD Startup & Innovation Fund supports exactly this — providing critical capital to transform rural ideas into scalable businesses. I’d like to understand if your venture aligns with this opportunity.

### Discovery Questions
- When was your agri-startup or rural innovation venture incorporated or registered?
- Is your primary focus on agriculture, allied activities, or rural livelihood innovation?
- Are you currently seeking funding for prototype development, pilot scaling, or technology adoption in rural contexts?
- Is your venture registered as a private limited company, LLP, or recognised startup under DPIIT?
- Do you have a working prototype or MVP related to farm productivity, supply chain, or rural enterprise?
- Are you based in or primarily operating from a rural or semi-urban area?
- Are you looking for non-dilutive support such as grants, seed funding, or soft loans?
- Have you previously applied for any NABARD or ministry of agriculture-linked innovation schemes?

### Qualification Checklist (internal — don't read aloud)
- [ ] Venture incorporated/registered less than 10 years ago  
- [ ] Focused on agriculture, allied sectors, or rural innovation  
- [ ] Registered as private limited company, LLP, or DPIIT-recognised startup  
- [ ] Has a working prototype, MVP, or pilot-ready innovation  
- [ ] Operating primarily in rural or semi-urban regions  
- [ ] Seeking early-stage funding for innovation validation or scaling  
- [ ] Not a large corporate or public sector undertaking  
- [ ] Engaged in activities aligned with NABARD’s rural development mandate  

### Transition to Pitch
Based on your responses, your venture appears to align well with the NABARD Startup & Innovation Fund — which provides seed support and innovation grants to early-stage agri-startups. Let me share how this scheme works and whether it could be a fit for your next growth phase.

## 2. Scheme Pitch (5 min)

### Structure: Problem → Scheme → Value → Proof → Next Step

**Problem framing:**
Agri-startups often struggle to access early-stage capital due to perceived high risk, long gestation periods, and lack of collateral — especially when working on innovations in rural supply chains, farm tech, or sustainable agriculture. Traditional investors may hesitate, leaving promising ventures underfunded during critical validation phases.

**Scheme intro:**
The NABARD Startup & Innovation Fund is a government-backed initiative managed by the National Bank for Agriculture and Rural Development (NABARD) to support early-stage startups and innovations in agriculture and allied sectors. It offers financial assistance in the form of seed funding, grants, or soft loans — typically up to ₹25 lakh per venture — to validate, prototype, or scale rural-focused innovations.

**Why they qualify (personalised):**
- Your venture is focused on agriculture or rural innovation — a core eligibility criterion  
- Incorporated within the last 10 years — meeting the startup age threshold  
- Has a working prototype or MVP — demonstrating readiness for validation support  
- Operating or targeting rural/ semi-urban markets — aligning with NABARD’s developmental mandate
