**Policy Statement:**  
[Firm Name] is committed to protecting the confidentiality, integrity, and availability of client
data. This policy outlines the minimum standards for handling sensitive information throughout
the engagement lifecycle.

**Data Storage:**  
All client data shall be stored in encrypted cloud storage environments with access controls
enforced via role-based permissions. Storage providers must be ISO 27001 certified or equivalent.

**Access Control:**  
Access to client data is restricted to authorized personnel only. Multi-factor authentication (2FA)
is mandatory for all system access. No personal devices may be used to access or store client
data without explicit encryption and MDM compliance.

**Data Sharing:**  
Client data shall not be shared with third parties except as strictly necessary for the preparation
and submission of PM-PRANAM applications to Ministry of Agriculture and Farmers Welfare, or as
required by law.

**Retention:**  
Client data shall be retained for a period of two (2) years post case closure, after which it shall
be securely deleted unless longer retention is required for legal or regulatory compliance.

**Scheme-specific note:**  
All documents submitted to Ministry of Agriculture and Farmers Welfare under PM-PRANAM
must be retained for a minimum of [3 years / as per scheme guidelines from evidence] for audit purposes.

**Breach Notification:**  
In the event of a data breach, [Firm Name] shall notify affected clients and relevant authorities
within 24 hours of confirmation, in accordance with applicable data protection laws.

**Policy Review:**  
This policy shall be reviewed annually or upon significant changes to regulatory requirements
or business operations.

**Compliance:**  
All employees and contractors are required to comply with this policy. Violations may result in
disciplinary action, up to and including termination.
