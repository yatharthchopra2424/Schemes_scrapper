## Pricing Principles
1. **Value-Based Alignment**: Fees are structured to reflect the substantial financial benefit delivered to clients through PM-PRANAM, which targets large fertilizer manufacturers and importers for promoting alternative nutrition.
2. **Risk-Adjusted Pricing**: Success fees are set at the lower end of industry ranges (2–4%) due to the scheme’s moderate risk profile — characterized by a processing time of 6–9 months and limited public success rate data (confidence: low).
3. **Transparency & Predictability**: Retainers cover initial eligibility assessment, documentation, and application preparation; success fees are payable only upon fund disbursement by the Department of Fertilizers.
4. **Scheme-Specific Note**: Given PM-PRANAM’s focus on large-scale agro-industrial entities and its implementation via the Department of Fertilizers (Ministry of Chemicals & Fertilizers), the Enterprise tier is the only applicable segment. Lower tiers do not align with the scheme’s eligibility, which targets manufacturers/importers with significant scale to qualify for alternative nutrition promotion incentives.

### Success Fee Invoice
**Description**: Success fee for PM-PRANAM (Promotion of Alternative Nutrients for Agriculture Management) scheme — disbursed by the Department of Fertilizers, Ministry of Chemicals & Fertilizers, Government of India, upon approval of alternative nutrition promotion proposals.
