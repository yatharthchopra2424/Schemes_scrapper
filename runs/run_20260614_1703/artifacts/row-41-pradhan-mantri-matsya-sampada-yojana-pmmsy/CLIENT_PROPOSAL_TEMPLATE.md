## 3. Our Scope of Work
1. Initial eligibility assessment and scheme suitability analysis  
2. Preparation of Detailed Project Report (DPR) as per PMMSY guidelines  
3. Compilation and verification of required documents (land ownership, bank details, PAN/Aadhaar, quotations, NOC, environmental clearance if required, beneficiary contribution proof, before/after photographs)  
4. Submission of DPR to District Fisheries Officer (DFO) and coordination with State Fisheries Department  
5. Facilitation of technical and financial appraisal by State Level Sanctioning Committee (SLSC)  
6. Liaison with Department of Fisheries, Government of India for final approval and fund release  
7. Portal registration and application tracking on [https://pmmsy.dof.gov.in/](https://pmmsy.dof.gov.in/)
