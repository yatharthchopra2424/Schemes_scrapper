## Pricing Principles
1. Retainer covers initial DPR preparation, documentation support, and application submission coordination.
2. Success fee is contingent on subsidy approval and fund release, aligned with scheme milestones.
3. Fees are scaled to client capacity and project complexity, reflecting the scheme’s broad eligibility across fishers, farmers, entrepreneurs, SHGs, cooperatives, and private companies.
4. **Scheme-specific note**: PMMSY has a moderate risk profile due to its multi-stage approval process (DFO → State Level Sanctioning Committee → Central Government) and requirement for physical verification and utilization certificates before subsidy release, which can extend processing time to 6–12 months. However, the scheme’s high approval rate (evidenced by ₹18,721.03 crore approved out of ₹20,050 crore envisaged) and structured disbursement in installments reduce revenue lag risk for consultants who manage compliance effectively.
