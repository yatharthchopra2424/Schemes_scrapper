# Pitch & Sales Scripts

## Philosophy
We are advisors, not salespeople. We diagnose first. We only pitch a scheme if it fits the client. We never oversell success rates.

---

## 1. Discovery Call Script (First Contact — 20 min)

### Opening (2 min)
Good morning/afternoon. I specialize in helping fisheries and aquaculture businesses access government support for sustainable growth. The Pradhan Mantri Matsya Sampada Yojana (PMMSY) is designed specifically for fishers, fish farmers, entrepreneurs, and coastal communities looking to modernize operations, increase productivity, and strengthen the value chain. I’d like to understand your current activities and see if this scheme aligns with your goals.

### Discovery Questions
1. What type of fisheries or aquaculture activity are you currently engaged in (e.g., pond culture, cage culture, hatchery, seaweed cultivation, fish vending, processing)?
2. Are you an individual fisher/fish farmer, part of a self-help group, cooperative, startup, or private company?
3. What is the location of your operation (state/district), and do you have clear land or water body access rights?
4. Have you prepared a Detailed Project Report (DPR) for any planned expansion or new activity?
5. What is your estimated project cost for the activity you wish to undertake (e.g., pond construction, biofloc unit, cold storage)?
6. Are you able to contribute the beneficiary share (non-subsidy portion) of the project cost from your own funds or loans?
7. Do you have the required documents ready, such as land ownership/lease, PAN/Aadhaar, bank details, and equipment quotations?
8. Are you currently availing any other central or state subsidy for the same activity you plan to apply for under PMMSY?

### Qualification Checklist (internal — don't read aloud)
- [ ] Beneficiary is a fisher, fish farmer, fish vendor, fish worker, SC/ST, woman, entrepreneur, startup, self-help group, cooperative, NGO, private company, or government entity engaged in fisheries/aquaculture
- [ ] Activity falls under eligible PMMSY components (e.g., pond/cage/hatchery construction, biofloc, ice plant, cold storage, fish kiosk, value addition, seaweed cultivation, ornamental fisheries, vessel upgradation, etc.)
- [ ] Beneficiary has or can obtain land/water rights with ownership/lease documents
- [ ] Beneficiary can bear the non-subsidy portion of project cost (40–60% for general, 30–50% for special categories)
- [ ] No duplicate subsidy claimed for same activity from other central/state schemes
- [ ] Project complies with state fisheries acts, environmental norms, and CRZ guidelines (if applicable)
- [ ] Detailed Project Report (DPR) can be prepared as per guidelines
- [ ] Beneficiary is willing to submit utilization certificates and completion reports post-implementation

### Transition to Pitch
Based on your responses, it looks like your [mention specific activity, e.g., biofloc pond construction] in [district/state] could be a strong fit for the Pradhan Mantri Matsya Sampada Yojana (PMMSY). This scheme offers central subsidies of 40–60% (or 50–70% for special categories) on project costs, which could significantly reduce your upfront investment. Let me walk you through how it works.

## 2. Scheme Pitch (5 min)

### Structure: Problem → Scheme → Value → Proof → Next Step

**Problem framing:**
Fishers and aquaculture entrepreneurs face high upfront costs for modernizing infrastructure—such as constructing ponds, cages, hatcheries, or cold storage—limiting productivity, increasing post-harvest losses, and restricting access to premium markets. Many lack the capital to adopt technologies like RAS, biofloc, or seaweed cultivation, despite strong income potential.

**Scheme intro:**
The Pradhan Mantri Matsya Sampada Yojana (PMMSY), administered by the Department of Fisheries, Ministry of Fisheries, Animal Husbandry and Dairying, Government of India, provides central subsidies ranging from 40% to 60% of project cost for general category beneficiaries, and 50% to 70% for SC/ST, women, and beneficiaries from North Eastern and Himalayan states. The scheme supports a wide range of activities including pond/cage/hatchery construction, biofloc units, ice plants, cold storage, fish kiosks, value addition units, seaweed cultivation, ornamental fisheries, integrated aquaparks, nucleus breeding centres, fishing vessel upgradation, and livelihood support during fishing bans.

**Why they qualify (personalised):**
- You are engaged in [specific activity from discovery, e.g., biofloc pond farming], which is an eligible activity under PMMSY for aquaculture intensification and diversification.
- Your operation is located in [state/district], and you have or can secure land/water access with ownership/lease documents.
- You are prepared to contribute the beneficiary share (40–60% of project cost) from your own resources or financing.
- You are not currently availing a similar subsidy for the same activity from another central or state scheme.
