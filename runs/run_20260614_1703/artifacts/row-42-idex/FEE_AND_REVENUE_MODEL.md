## Pricing Principles
1. **Value-Based Alignment**: Fees are structured to reflect the substantial non-dilutive grant value (up to ₹1.5 Cr) and strategic defence sector access provided by iDEX.
2. **Risk-Adjusted Pricing**: Success fees account for technical and milestone-based risks; iDEX’s rigorous evaluation and progress-review system implies moderate execution risk despite high reward potential.
3. **Transparency & Trust**: Clear milestone-linked billing aligns with iDEX’s own disbursement structure, ensuring clients understand fee triggers.
4. **Scheme-Specific Note**: Given iDEX’s rolling application cycle and typical 3–6 month review-to-approval timeline (based on evaluation committee reviews, presentations, and milestone sanctions), revenue recognition may lag. Success is contingent on technical merit and defence priority alignment, not just documentation — increasing consultant workload but also justifying premium fees for expertise in defence innovation articulation.

## Invoice Templates

### Retainer Invoice
**Description**: Retainer fee for iDEX scheme consulting services, including portal registration, problem statement analysis, proposal drafting, technical documentation support, and submission guidance under the Innovation for Defence Excellence (iDEX) initiative by the Ministry of Defence, Government of India.

### Success Fee Invoice
**Description**: Success fee for securing grant approval under the Innovation for Defence Excellence (iDEX) scheme. Amount calculated as a percentage of the sanctioned grant (up to INR 1.5 Crore per project) awarded by the Defence Innovation Organisation (DIO) under the Ministry of Defence, Government of India, following successful proposal evaluation, presentation, and milestone-based sanction.
