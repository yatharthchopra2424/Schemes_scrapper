# Pitch & Sales Scripts

## Philosophy
We are advisors, not salespeople. We diagnose first. We only pitch a scheme if it fits the client. We never oversell success rates.

---

## 1. Discovery Call Script (First Contact — 20 min)

### Opening (2 min)
Good [morning/afternoon], I'm calling from [Your Firm Name]. We specialise in helping defence and aerospace innovators access government funding to develop technologies that address real challenges faced by the armed forces. I understand you're working on innovative solutions in this space — could I take just two minutes to see if the iDEX grant scheme might be relevant for your current projects?

### Discovery Questions
1. When was your entity incorporated or registered in India? (iDEX requires Indian registration)
2. Are you currently developing a technology or solution specifically for defence or aerospace applications?
3. Have you reviewed any of the problem statements released by the armed forces, DPSUs, or OFB on the iDEX portal?
4. What stage is your project at — idea, proof of concept, prototype, or ready for trials?
5. Do you have a clear budget and financial plan for your defence innovation project?
6. Are you seeking funding to support prototype development, trials, or evaluation by the armed forces?
7. Is your team equipped with the technical expertise to execute a defence-focused innovation project?
8. Have you previously worked with defence organisations or received government grants for R&D?

### Qualification Checklist (internal — don't read aloud)
- [ ] Entity is registered in India (startup, MSME, innovator, or R&D institute)
- [ ] Working on innovative defence and aerospace technology
- [ ] Addressing a challenge articulated by armed forces, DPSUs, or OFB
- [ ] Seeking funding for proof of concept, prototype development, or trials
- [ ] Has required documents: Incorporation certificate, PAN, GST (if applicable), project proposal, technical specs, budget, team credentials
- [ ] Prepared to align with defence priorities and undergo milestone-based reviews
- [ ] Accepts that IP may be subject to government use for defence purposes
- [ ] Located anywhere in India (Pan-India scope)

### Transition to Pitch
Based on your responses, it sounds like you're developing a defence-relevant innovation and could benefit from non-dilutive funding to advance your project. The iDEX scheme, run by the Ministry of Defence, offers grants of up to INR 1.5 Crore specifically for proof of concept, prototype development, and trials — exactly the stage you're at. Let me share how it works and whether you qualify.

## 2. Scheme Pitch (5 min)

### Problem framing:
Defence and aerospace innovators often struggle to access early-stage funding for high-risk, high-impact projects that require specialised testing, prototyping, and validation by the armed forces. Traditional funding sources may not understand defence requirements, and self-funding trials is often prohibitive, slowing down indigenous technology development.

### Scheme intro:
The iDEX (Innovations for Defence Excellence) scheme is a grant programme launched by the Ministry of Defence, Government of India, implemented through the Defence Innovation Organisation (DIO). It provides grant funding of up to INR 1.5 Crore per project for proof of concept, prototype development, and trials, disbursed in milestones based on progress.

### Why they qualify (personalised):
- You are a startup/MSME/innovator/R&D institute registered in India  
- Your project focuses on innovative defence or aerospace technology  
- You are addressing or can align with a problem statement from the armed forces, DPSUs, or OFB
