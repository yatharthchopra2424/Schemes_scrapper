### iDEX
- **Scheme ID:** SCH-001
- **Category:** Grant
- **Geography:** Pan-India
- **Administering Body:** Ministry of Defence, Government of India
- **Portal / Application URL:** https://www.idex.gov.in/
- **Current Status:** Rolling
- **Window:** Always Open

#### Eligibility Criteria
- Business type: Startups, MSMEs, innovators, and R&D institutes
- Age of business: Not specified
- Sector: Defence and aerospace technologies
- Revenue cap: Not specified
- Employee count: Not specified
- Other: Entities must be registered in India and working on innovative defence and aerospace technologies that address challenges articulated by the armed forces, Defence Public Sector Undertakings (DPSUs), and Ordnance Factories Board (OFB)

#### What It Offers
- Amount / Benefit: Up to INR 1.5 Crore per project
- Disbursement mode: Milestone-based grant funding
- Duration of benefit: Project-based (disbursed in milestones based on project progress)

#### Documents Required
1. Certificate of Incorporation/Registration
2. PAN of the entity
3. GST registration (if applicable)
4. Detailed project proposal
5. Technical specifications and innovation details
6. Budget and financial plan
7. Team credentials and expertise
8. Any prior work or prototypes related to the proposal

#### Application Process (numbered steps)
1. Visit the iDEX portal and register as an innovator.
2. Review the problem statements released by the armed forces, DPSUs, or OFBs.
3. Submit a proposal addressing a specific problem statement.
4. Proposals are evaluated by the iDEX Evaluation Committee.
5. Shortlisted applicants are invited for presentations.
6. Selected projects are sanctioned funding and enter the development phase.
7. Progress is monitored through periodic reviews and milestone-based disbursement.
