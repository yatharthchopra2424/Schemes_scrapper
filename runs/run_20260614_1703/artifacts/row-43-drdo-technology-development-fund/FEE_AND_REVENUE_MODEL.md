## Pricing Principles
1. Value-based pricing aligned with the grant amount (up to ₹10 Cr per project, 90% funding).
2. Tiered retainer reflects client capacity to engage in lengthy proposal development (technical scope, budget, milestones, compliance docs).
3. Success fee scaled to risk: moderate due to 90% funding coverage but offset by technical/financial evaluation by DRDO and alignment with DRDO priorities.
4. Scheme-specific note: Processing involves technical and financial evaluation by DRDO experts; success is not guaranteed despite high funding potential, warranting success fees in the 4–8% range for most applicants.
