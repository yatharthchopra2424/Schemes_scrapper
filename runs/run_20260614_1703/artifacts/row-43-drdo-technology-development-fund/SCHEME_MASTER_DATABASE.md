### DRDO Technology Development Fund
- **Scheme ID:** SCH-001
- **Category:** Grant
- **Geography:** Pan-India
- **Administering Body:** Defence Research & Development Organisation (DRDO), Ministry of Defence, Government of India
- **Portal / Application URL:** https://tdf.drdo.gov.in
- **Current Status:** Rolling
- **Window:** Always Open

#### Eligibility Criteria
- Business type: Indian industries, especially MSMEs and startups
- Age of business: Not specified
- Sector: Defence and dual-use systems, subsystems, components, or technologies
- Revenue cap: Not specified
- Employee count: Not specified
- Other: Must align with DRDO's technology development priorities; must comply with government policies on Foreign Direct Investment (FDI)

#### What It Offers
- Amount / Benefit: Financial support up to 90% of project cost; maximum project cost up to Rs 10 Cr
- Disbursement mode: Financial tranches as per project milestones
- Duration of benefit: One-time per project

#### Documents Required
1. Project proposal document
2. Technical scope and specifications
3. Detailed budget breakdown
4. Timeline and milestones
5. Expected outcomes and deliverables
6. Company registration documents
7. Audited financial statements
8. Tax clearance certificates
9. Bank account details
10. Undertaking for compliance with government policies
11. Reference letter from college/institution (if applicable for academic collaboration)
12. Memorandum of Association
13. Certificates of registration as a manufacturing unit
14. Income Tax returns for preceding three years
15. Details of shareholding/ownership pattern

#### Application Process (numbered steps)
1. Identify the technology development requirement aligned with TDF objectives.
2. Prepare a detailed project proposal including technical scope, budget, timeline, and expected outcomes.
3. Submit the proposal through the designated DRDO channel or portal.
4. Undergo technical and financial evaluation by DRDO experts.
5. Upon approval, sign the agreement and receive financial tranches as per project milestones.
6. Execute the project with periodic monitoring and reporting.
7. Submit final report and utilization certificate upon completion.
