## Pricing Principles
1. **Value-Based Alignment**: Fees are structured to reflect the grant amount secured, ensuring consultant compensation scales with client benefit.
2. **Risk Mitigation**: Retainer covers initial proposal development and documentation effort; success fee only payable upon grant approval, aligning incentives.
3. **Scheme-Specific Note**: The IN-SPACe Seed Fund has a rolling application basis with no fixed deadlines, but grant disbursement is milestone-based and subject to fund availability. Processing time from application to approval typically ranges from 3–6 months based on committee screening cycles, introducing moderate revenue lag risk. Success fees are therefore structured to be paid only upon formal grant sanction, not disbursement, to reduce client risk while ensuring consultant compensation for successful outcomes.
4. **Market Competitiveness**: Pricing tiers are benchmarked against standard grant consulting rates in India’s deep-tech and innovation sectors, adjusted for the niche space technology focus and non-dilutive nature of the grant.

## Invoice Templates

### Retainer Invoice
**Description**: Retainer fee for IN-SPACe Seed Fund grant consulting services, including eligibility assessment, project proposal development, technical feasibility report preparation, budget breakdown, and application submission support via the IN-SPACe portal (https://www.inspaceindia.org/).

### Success Fee Invoice
**Description**: Success fee for securing grant approval under the IN-SPACe Seed Fund, implemented by the Indian National Space Promotion and Authorization Centre (IN-SPACe), payable upon formal sanction of the grant award letter.
