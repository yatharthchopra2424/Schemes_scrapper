### IN-SPACe Seed Fund
- **Scheme ID:** SCH-001
- **Category:** Grant
- **Geography:** Pan-India
- **Administering Body:** Indian National Space Promotion and Authorization Centre (IN-SPACe)
- **Portal / Application URL:** https://www.inspaceindia.org/
- **Current Status:** Rolling
- **Window:** Always Open (subject to fund availability)

#### Eligibility Criteria
- Business type: Indian-incorporated startups
- Age of business: Not specified
- Sector: Space technologies, applications, or services
- Revenue cap: Not specified
- Employee count: Not specified
- Other: Must be working on innovative space technologies with potential for commercialization and scalability; preference given to entities demonstrating technical feasibility, innovation, and alignment with national space goals

#### What It Offers
- Amount / Benefit: Grant-based financial support; exact quantum determined by project scope and milestones, subject to fund availability and evaluation by screening committee
- Disbursement mode: Milestone-based grant disbursement
- Duration of benefit: One-time grant (subject to milestone completion)

#### Documents Required
1. Certificate of Incorporation
2. PAN of the entity
3. Detailed project proposal
4. Technical feasibility report
5. Budget breakdown and utilization plan
6. Team credentials and experience
7. Any existing IP or prior work documentation

#### Application Process (numbered steps)
1. Visit the official IN-SPACe website and navigate to the Seed Fund section.
2. Review eligibility criteria and guidelines.
3. Prepare a detailed project proposal including technical approach, innovation, commercialization plan, and budget.
4. Submit the application through the designated online portal or email as specified.
5. Applications are screened by an expert committee for technical merit, innovation, and feasibility.
6. Shortlisted candidates may be invited for presentations or interviews.
7. Final selection is based on overall evaluation and fund availability.
8. Selected startups receive grant approval and are onboarded for implementation.
