## Pricing Principles
1. **Value-Based Alignment**: Fees are structured to reflect the tangible financial benefit delivered—facilitating bank loans between ₹10 lakh and ₹1 Crore under Stand-Up India.
2. **Risk Mitigation**: Success fees are contingent on loan sanction, reducing client risk; processing typically takes 2–4 months based on evidence of timely disbursals in success stories (e.g., approvals within weeks to a few months).
3. **Accessibility for Target Beneficiaries**: Lower retainer options for early-stage SC/ST and women entrepreneurs align with scheme inclusivity goals, avoiding upfront barriers.
4. **Transparency & Compliance**: All fees are clearly disclosed; no charges for scheme eligibility assessment or document preparation beyond agreed retainer, adhering to ethical consulting standards.

*Note: Scheme-specific risk profile is moderate due to defined eligibility (SC/ST/women entrepreneurs, greenfield enterprises) and documented success in disbursals, though dependent on bank processing times and promoter’s 15% margin money readiness.*
