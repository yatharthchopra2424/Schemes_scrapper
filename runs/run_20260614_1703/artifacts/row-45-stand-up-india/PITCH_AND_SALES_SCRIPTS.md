# Pitch & Sales Scripts

## Philosophy
We are advisors, not salespeople. We diagnose first. We only pitch a scheme if it fits the client. We never oversell success rates.

---

## 1. Discovery Call Script (First Contact — 20 min)

### Opening (2 min)
Good morning/afternoon, I'm calling from [Your Firm Name]. We specialize in helping SC/ST and women entrepreneurs access government-backed financing to launch greenfield enterprises. I noticed your work in [mention sector if known, e.g., manufacturing/services/trading/agri-allied] and wanted to explore if the Stand-Up India Scheme could support your next venture. This scheme facilitates bank loans from ₹10 lakh to ₹1 Crore specifically for first-time entrepreneurs from SC/ST or women communities setting up new businesses. May I take 20 minutes to understand your goals and see if this aligns with your plans?

### Discovery Questions
1. What is your age, and do you belong to a Scheduled Caste (SC), Scheduled Tribe (ST), or identify as a woman entrepreneur?  
2. Are you planning to set up a completely new business (greenfield enterprise), or is this an expansion of an existing operation?  
3. Which sector are you targeting for your enterprise — manufacturing, services, agri-allied activities, or trading?  
4. What is the estimated total project cost for your venture, and how much funding are you seeking?  
5. Have you already contributed or planned to contribute at least 15% of the project cost as your own margin money?  
6. If this is a partnership or company, do SC/ST or women entrepreneurs hold at least 51% of shareholding and controlling stake?  
7. Do you have identity proof, a detailed business plan, and bank account details ready for submission?  
8. Are you aware of any participating banks in your area that offer Stand-Up India loans, or would you like assistance identifying them?

### Qualification Checklist (internal — don't read aloud)
- [ ] Applicant is above 18 years of age  
- [ ] Applicant belongs to SC/ST community and/or is a woman entrepreneur  
- [ ] Enterprise is a greenfield venture (new setup, not expansion)  
- [ ] Business falls under manufacturing, services, agri-allied, or trading sector  
- [ ] For non-individual enterprises: ≥51% shareholding and control held by SC/ST/woman entrepreneur  
- [ ] Promoter ready to contribute 15% margin money from own funds  
- [ ] Loan amount required is between ₹10 lakh and ₹1 Crore  
- [ ] Identity proof, business plan/project report, and bank details are available or can be prepared  

### Transition to Pitch
Based on your responses, it sounds like you’re planning a greenfield enterprise in [sector] and meet the core criteria for the Stand-Up India Scheme — particularly as an SC/ST/woman entrepreneur seeking loans between ₹10 lakh and ₹1 Crore. Let me briefly outline how this scheme can help you secure financing with government-backed security cover and handholding support.

## 2. Scheme Pitch (5 min)

### Structure: Problem → Scheme → Value → Proof → Next Step

**Problem framing:**  
SC/ST and women entrepreneurs often face significant barriers in accessing formal credit due to lack of collateral, limited credit history, or systemic biases in lending. Many viable greenfield enterprise ideas stall at the idea stage because entrepreneurs cannot secure the initial funding needed for machinery, working capital, or setup costs — despite having viable business plans and sector-specific expertise.

**Scheme intro:**  
The Stand-Up India Scheme, launched by the Department for Promotion of Industry and Internal Trade (DPIIT) under the Ministry of Commerce and Industry, facilitates composite bank loans ranging from ₹10 lakh to ₹1 Crore for setting up greenfield enterprises. The loan covers both term loan and working capital requirements, requires only a 15% promoter contribution as margin money, and is secured under the Credit Guarantee Fund Scheme for Stand-Up India Loans (CGFSIL), eliminating the need for third-party collateral. The scheme also provides handholding support through designated Handholding Agencies (HHAs) to guide applicants through documentation, bank liaison, and post-sanction monitoring.

**Why they qualify (personalised):**  
- You belong to a priority beneficiary group: SC/ST and/or woman entrepreneur (verified via discovery)  
- Your proposed enterprise is a greenfield venture in an eligible sector: manufacturing, services, agri-allied, or trading  
- You are prepared to contribute the required 15% margin money from your own resources  
- For non-individual setups, SC/ST/woman entrepreneurs hold ≥51% shareholding and control (if applicable)
