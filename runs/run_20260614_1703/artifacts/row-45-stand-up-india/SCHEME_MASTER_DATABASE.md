### Stand-Up India
- **Scheme ID:** SCH-001
- **Category:** Loan
- **Geography:** Pan-India
- **Administering Body:** Department for Promotion of Industry and Internal Trade (DPIIT), Ministry of Commerce and Industry
- **Portal / Application URL:** https://standupmitra.in/
- **Current Status:** Rolling
- **Window:** Always Open

#### Eligibility Criteria
- Business type: Greenfield enterprises (new ventures)
- Age of business: Not applicable (must be new venture)
- Sector: Manufacturing, services, agri-allied activities, or trading
- Revenue cap: Not mentioned
- Employee count: Not mentioned
- Other: 
  - Borrowers must be SC/ST and/or women entrepreneurs above 18 years of age
  - For non-individual enterprises, at least 51% of shareholding and controlling stake must be held by either an SC/ST or woman entrepreneur

#### What It Offers
- Amount / Benefit: Bank loans ranging from ₹10 lakh to ₹1 Crore per borrower
- Disbursement mode: Direct credit (loan sanctioned and disbursed by bank)
- Duration of benefit: One-time loan facility (composite loan inclusive of term loan and working capital)

#### Documents Required
1. Identity proof
2. Business plan or project report
3. Details of the greenfield enterprise
4. Proof of SC/ST or woman entrepreneur status (if applicable)
5. Bank account details
6. Any additional documents as required by the lending bank

#### Application Process (numbered steps)
1. Visit the Stand-Up India portal at https://standupmitra.in/
2. Click on 'Apply Here' to begin the application process
3. Approach a participating bank branch with your business proposal
4. Ensure eligibility criteria are met (SC/ST/woman entrepreneur above 18 years, greenfield enterprise)
5. Submit required documents including identity proof, business plan, and project details
6. Bank processes the loan application under the Stand-Up India Scheme
7. Upon approval, loan is sanctioned and disbursed
8. Utilize handholding support from designated Handholding Agencies (HHAs) if needed
