# Comprehensive Scheme Masterclass & File Guide

## Scheme Deep Dive

### Overview
The Stand-Up India Scheme is a flagship initiative launched by the Government of India to promote entrepreneurship among socially disadvantaged groups, specifically Scheduled Castes (SC), Scheduled Tribes (ST), and women entrepreneurs. Implemented by the Department for Promotion of Industry and Internal Trade (DPIIT) under the Ministry of Commerce and Industry, the scheme facilitates access to institutional credit for setting up greenfield enterprises across manufacturing, services, agri-allied, and trading sectors. The scheme operates on a rolling basis with no fixed deadline, allowing applications throughout the year via the official portal: [https://standupmitra.in/](https://standupmitra.in/).

### Objectives
The scheme aims to achieve the following objectives:
- Facilitate bank loans ranging from ₹10 lakh to ₹1 Crore per borrower to SC/ST and women entrepreneurs.
- Support the establishment of greenfield enterprises in manufacturing, services, agri-allied, or trading sectors.
- Ensure that at least one SC/ST borrower and one woman borrower receive support per bank branch under the scheme.
- Promote entrepreneurship and financial inclusion among socially disadvantaged groups.
- Provide composite loans inclusive of term loan and working capital components.
- Offer security cover under the Credit Guarantee Fund Scheme for Stand-Up India Loans (CGFSIL).
- Encourage handholding and support through designated Handholding Agencies (HHAs).
- Monitor and report on sanctioned applications and disbursed amounts for transparency and accountability.

### Eligibility Matrix
The eligibility criteria are strictly defined to target the intended beneficiaries and ensure compliance with scheme guidelines.

| **Criteria** | **Requirement** | **Details** |
|--------------|-----------------|-----------|
| **Borrower Identity** | SC/ST and/or women entrepreneurs | Must be above 18 years of age. |
| **Enterprise Type** | Greenfield enterprise only | Loans are exclusively for setting up new ventures; existing businesses are not eligible. |
| **Non-Individual Enterprises** | ≥51% shareholding & control | For partnership firms, companies, or other non-individual entities, at least 51% of shareholding and controlling stake must be held by SC/ST and/or woman entrepreneur(s). |
| **Permitted Sectors** | Manufacturing, Services, Agri-allied, Trading | Enterprises must operate in one of these four sectors. |
| **Age Limit** | Above 18 years | Applicants must be adults; no upper age limit specified. |
| **Residency** | Pan-India | Available across all states and union territories of India. |

> **Key Caveats on Eligibility**  
> - Loans are **only** for greenfield enterprises (new ventures); expansion or working capital for existing units is not permitted.  
> - In non-individual enterprises, **both** 51% shareholding **and** controlling stake must be held by SC/ST/woman entrepreneur.  
> - The promoter must contribute **15% margin money** from own funds; this cannot be borrowed.  
> - Security is covered under CGFSIL, but final terms are subject to the lending bank and guarantee scheme guidelines.  
> - Maximum loan amount is capped at **₹1 Crore per borrower**, regardless of project cost.

### Application Process
The application process is bank-led but initiated through the Stand-Up India portal. The flowchart below illustrates the end-to-end journey from inquiry to disbursement and post-sanction support.

```mermaid
flowchart TD
    A[Start: Visit https://standupmitra.in/] --> B[Click 'Apply Here']
    B --> C[Approach Participating Bank Branch with Business Proposal]
    C --> D{Check Eligibility}
    D -->|SC/ST/Woman Entrepreneur<br>Above 18 Years<br>Greenfield Enterprise<br>≥51% SC/ST/Woman in Non-Individual| E[Submit Required Documents]
    D -->|Not Eligible| Z[Application Rejected - Do Not Proceed]
    E --> F[Bank Processes Loan Application<br>Under Stand-Up India Scheme]
    F --> G{Bank Approval?}
    G -->|Yes| H[Loan Sanctioned & Disbursed]
    G -->|No| I[Application Rejected<br>Review Feedback & Reapply if Eligible]
    H --> J[Utilize Handholding Support<br>From Designated HHAs (Optional)]
    J --> K[Monitor Repayment & Compliance]
    K --> L[End: Successful Loan Utilization]
```

#### Step-by-Step Breakdown:
1. **Portal Access**: Visit the official Stand-Up India portal at [https://standupmitra.in/](https://standupmitra.in/).
2. **Initiate Application**: Click on the 'Apply Here' button to begin the process.
3. **Bank Engagement**: Approach a participating bank branch (list available on portal) with a detailed business proposal.
4. **Eligibility Verification**: Confirm that:
   - Applicant is SC/ST and/or woman entrepreneur above 18 years.
   - Enterprise is a greenfield venture.
   - For non-individual entities, ≥51% shareholding and control is held by eligible promoter(s).
   - Business falls under manufacturing, services, agri-allied, or trading.
5. **Document Submission**: Submit:
   - Identity proof (Aadhaar, PAN, Voter ID, etc.)
   - Comprehensive business plan or project report
   - Details of the greenfield enterprise (location, sector, investment plan)
   - Proof of SC/ST/woman entrepreneur status (caste certificate, gender proof)
   - Bank account details (cancelled cheque, passbook)
   - Any additional documents as required by the lending bank
6. **Bank Processing**: The bank evaluates the application under the Stand-Up India Scheme guidelines.
7. **Sanction & Disbursement**: Upon approval, the loan is sanctioned and disbursed as a composite loan (term loan + working capital).
8. **Handholding Support**: Applicants may opt for support from designated Handholding Agencies (HHAs) for business setup, compliance, and operational guidance.
