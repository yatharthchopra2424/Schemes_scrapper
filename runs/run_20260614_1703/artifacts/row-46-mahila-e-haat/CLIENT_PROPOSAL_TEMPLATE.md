## 1. Executive Summary
Based on our initial review of [Client Legal Name]'s business profile, we have identified **Mahila E-Haat** — a free online marketplace scheme administered by the **Ministry of Women and Child Development** — for which your business appears eligible. The scheme provides access to a national market for women entrepreneurs, self-help groups, and NGOs to sell products directly to buyers without any listing fees or commissions.

## 3. Our Scope of Work
1. Initial eligibility assessment against Mahila E-Haat requirements for women entrepreneurs, self-help groups, or NGOs  
2. Guidance on preparing identity proof, address proof, bank account details, product images, and descriptions  
3. Portal registration assistance at https://www.mahilahaat.gov.in/ including form filling and document upload  
4. Support in creating effective product listings with optimized descriptions and images  
5. Guidance on navigating the seller dashboard and managing online orders  
6. Training on receiving online payments and handling buyer interactions  
7. Advisory on leveraging promotional support features within the platform for increased visibility  

*Note: No DPR or project report is required for this scheme as it is a marketplace platform, not a grant or loan program.*
