## Pricing Principles
1. **Value-based alignment**: Fees correlate with the tangible benefit delivered — in this case, enabling market access and sales generation rather than direct financial disbursement.
2. **Risk-adjusted pricing**: Success fees reflect the effort required to optimize product listings, drive visibility, and support conversion on a commission-free platform where sales outcomes depend on seller competitiveness and market demand.
3. **Transparency**: All fees are disclosed upfront; no hidden charges, mirroring the scheme’s zero-commission, zero-fee seller policy.
4. **Scalability**: Tiered retainers allow firms to support clients at different stages of digital marketplace readiness, from onboarding to sustained sales growth.

> *Scheme-specific note: Mahila E-Haat involves no direct financial grant or subsidy; therefore, traditional "grant value" metrics do not apply. Revenue potential for consultants stems from enabling sales conversion, not accessing public funds. Processing time is minimal (registration typically approved within days), but revenue realization depends on post-listing sales performance, introducing a lag between service delivery and measurable client outcomes.*
