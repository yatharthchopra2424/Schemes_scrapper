### Mahila E-Haat
- **Scheme ID:** SCH-001
- **Category:** Subsidy
- **Geography:** Pan-India
- **Administering Body:** Ministry of Women and Child Development
- **Portal / Application URL:** https://www.mahilahaat.gov.in/
- **Current Status:** Open
- **Window:** Always Open

#### Eligibility Criteria
- Business type: Women entrepreneurs, self-help groups, and NGOs
- Age of business: Not specified
- Sector: Manufacturing, production, or sale of products and services
- Revenue cap: Not specified
- Employee count: Not specified
- Other: Must be Indian women entrepreneurs, self-help groups, or NGOs; no registration or listing fees

#### What It Offers
- Amount / Benefit: Free online marketplace access; no commission or fees on sales; direct buyer-seller interaction; online payment processing; promotional support
- Disbursement mode: Direct credit (payments received directly from buyers into seller's bank account)
- Duration of benefit: Ongoing (as long as registered and active on platform)

#### Documents Required
1. Identity proof (Aadhaar/PAN/Voter ID)
2. Address proof
3. Bank account details
4. Product images
5. Product descriptions

#### Application Process (numbered steps)
1. Visit the Mahila E-Haat portal at https://www.mahilahaat.gov.in/
2. Click on 'Register as a Seller'
3. Fill in the required details including name, contact information, address, and bank account details
4. Upload product images and descriptions
5. Submit the registration form for verification
6. Once approved, list products and start selling to buyers across India.
