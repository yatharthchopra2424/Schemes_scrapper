## 3. Our Scope of Work
1. Initial eligibility assessment against DAY-NRLM Startup Grants criteria  
2. Document preparation and verification as per scheme requirements  
3. Business plan development and activity report preparation  
4. Portal registration and login support on https://svep.nrlm.gov.in/landing  
5. Liaison with Community Resource Persons–Enterprise Promotion (CRP-EP) for profile creation  
6. Coordination with Block Resource Centre (BRC) for business plan review and approval  
7. Post-sanction hand-holding support for enterprise stabilization and Performance Tracking System (PTS) compliance
