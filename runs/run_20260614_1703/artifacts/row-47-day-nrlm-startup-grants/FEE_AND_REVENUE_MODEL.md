## Pricing Principles
1. Value-based pricing aligned with the actual grant/benefit amount received by the client under DAY-NRLM Startup Grants.
2. Transparent fee structure with retainer covering advisory workload and success fee tied to fund disbursement.
3. Tiered pricing reflects client capacity, with Growth tier (MSME/Startup) being the primary applicability for this scheme targeting rural poor, youth, and women in SHGs/VOs.
4. Scheme-specific note: Moderate risk profile due to 60-day average CEF loan disbursement time (per mid-term evaluation) and community-based approval process, but high success likelihood (99% profitability rate of supported enterprises per evidence).
