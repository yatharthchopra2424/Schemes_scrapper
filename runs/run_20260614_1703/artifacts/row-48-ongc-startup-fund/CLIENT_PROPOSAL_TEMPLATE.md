## 1. Executive Summary
Based on our initial review of [Client]'s business profile, we have identified ONGC Startup Fund — a grant scheme administered by Corporate CSR Group, New Delhi and the CSR Departments at work centres across India — for which your business appears eligible. The potential benefit is financial support for CSR projects, capacity building of CSR personnel, transparent and efficient process via the ONGC CSR portal, monitoring and evaluation of projects, impact assessment for projects with outlay of Rs. 1 Cr. or more, and reporting through annual CSR report included in Board’s Report.

## 3. Our Scope of Work
1. Initial eligibility assessment against ONGC Startup Fund criteria  
2. Project concept note development aligned with Schedule VII of Companies Act, 2013  
3. Detailed Project Report (DPR) preparation with need-based assessment, objectives, beneficiary details, timelines, budget break-up  
4. Documentation compilation as per the 15-point checklist (Organization Details, Project Details, Budget, etc.)  
5. Portal registration on www.ongccsr.co.in and proposal submission  
6. Liaison with ONGC CSR Committee for review and recommendation  
7. Agreement signing and fund disbursement coordination upon Board approval  

## 4. What We Need From You

Documents as per the attached checklist (Organization Details, Details of similar projects ongoing/completed in previous three years, Impact assessment of previous projects in similar focus areas, Details of previous support received from ONGC, Details of largest project undertaken by agency in recent past with financial implication, Details of proposed CSR project, Brief about goals and objectives of the project, Report of Need Assessment/Baseline Survey (if any), Brief about targeted beneficiaries, Methodology of beneficiary selection and number, Details about physical and social milestones/deliverables, Detail timeline/duration of the project, Budget estimate with detailed break-up and documents proving rate reasonability, Monitoring mechanism for project activities and benefits, Documents depicting funding sources other than ONGC and amounts funded by each)
