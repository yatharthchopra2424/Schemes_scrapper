## Pricing Principles
- Fees are structured to align with the client’s scale and the scheme’s financial benefit range, ensuring accessibility for startups and MSMEs while maintaining profitability for the firm.
- Success fees are contingent on approved grant disbursement, reducing upfront financial risk for clients.
- Retainers cover initial proposal development, documentation, and submission efforts, which are critical given the scheme’s detailed application requirements.
- **Scheme-specific note**: The ONGC Startup Fund has a moderate risk profile due to its rolling application basis and reliance on CSR Committee and Board approval timelines. While there is no fixed deadline, the multi-stage review process (portal submission → CSR Committee recommendation → Board approval) introduces potential delays. However, the scheme’s structured digital portal (www.ongccsr.co.in) and clear eligibility criteria for startups improve predictability of success for well-prepared applications, supporting a mid-range success fee assumption in projections.
