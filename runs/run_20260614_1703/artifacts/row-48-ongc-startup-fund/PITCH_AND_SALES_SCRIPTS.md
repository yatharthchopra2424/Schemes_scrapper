# Pitch & Sales Scripts

## Philosophy
We are advisors, not salespeople. We diagnose first. We only pitch a scheme if it fits the client. We never oversell success rates.

---

## 1. Discovery Call Script (First Contact — 20 min)

### Opening (2 min)
Good morning/afternoon. I specialize in helping startups and social enterprises access CSR funding from India’s leading PSUs. ONGC’s Startup Fund supports innovative projects that align with sustainable development and benefit marginalized communities. I’d like to understand your mission and current initiatives to see if this opportunity fits your goals.

### Discovery Questions
1. What is the primary social or environmental problem your startup aims to solve?
2. When was your organization incorporated or registered?
3. Are you registered as a Section 8 company, trust, or society under Indian law?
4. Do you have a valid CSR-1 registration number from the MCA portal?
5. Have you implemented similar projects in the last three years, and can you share outcomes?
6. What is your proposed project’s budget, and how much funding are you seeking from ONGC?
7. Who are your target beneficiaries, and do they include marginalized or deprived sections of society?
8. Do you have a need assessment or baseline survey for your proposed project?

### Qualification Checklist (internal — don't read aloud)
- [ ] Organization is a Section 8 company, registered trust, society, or statutory body
- [ ] Valid CSR-1 registration number obtained
- [ ] At least three years of track record in similar activities (if applicable)
- [ ] Project aligned with Schedule VII of Companies Act, 2013
- [ ] Project to be undertaken within geographical limits of India
- [ ] Preference given to projects benefiting marginalized, disadvantaged, poor, or deprived sections
- [ ] No direct contribution to Government initiatives unless specified by Act/Rules
- [ ] Administrative overheads not exceeding 5% of total CSR expenditure
- [ ] Surplus from CSR project to be ploughed back or transferred per Schedule VII
- [ ] Impact assessment expenditure capped at 2% of total CSR expenditure or ₹50 lakh, whichever is higher

### Transition to Pitch
Based on your responses, your work in [specific area from discovery] appears strongly aligned with ONGC’s CSR priorities. The ONGC Startup Fund offers financial support for qualifying CSR projects through a transparent portal, and I believe we can help you build a compelling application.

## 2. Scheme Pitch (5 min)

### Structure: Problem → Scheme → Value → Proof → Next Step

**Problem framing:**
Startups working on social impact often struggle to access patient capital and technical support for scaling CSR initiatives. Many lack the guidance to navigate complex CSR compliance, impact assessment requirements, and funding procedures mandated by the Companies Act, 2013.

**Scheme intro:**
The ONGC Startup Fund, administered by the Corporate CSR Group, New Delhi and CSR Departments across ONGC’s work centres, provides financial support for CSR projects aligned with Schedule VII of the Companies Act, 2013. Funding is disbursed to implementing agencies with valid CSR-1 registration, and projects are selected based on ONGC’s annual CSR action plan approved by the Board. The fund supports capacity building, monitoring, evaluation, and impact assessment for projects with an outlay of ₹1 Crore or more.

**Why they qualify (personalised):**
- Your organization holds a valid CSR-1 registration number (verified in discovery)
- Your project targets marginalized or deprived communities, aligning with ONGC’s preference
- Your proposed activities fall under Schedule VII sectors such as education, healthcare, or environmental sustainability
- You have a track record of similar projects over the past three years
