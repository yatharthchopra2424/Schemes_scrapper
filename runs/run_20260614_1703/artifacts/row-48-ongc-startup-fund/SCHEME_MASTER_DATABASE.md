### ONGC Startup Fund
- **Scheme ID:** SCH-001
- **Category:** Grant
- **Geography:** Pan-India
- **Administering Body:** Corporate CSR Group, New Delhi and the CSR Departments at work centres across India
- **Portal / Application URL:** www.ongccsr.co.in
- **Current Status:** Rolling
- **Window:** Always Open (proposals can be submitted throughout the year as per the annual action plan)

#### Eligibility Criteria
- Business type: Startups
- Age of business: Not specified
- Sector: Not specified (aligned with Schedule VII of Companies Act, 2013)
- Revenue cap: Not specified
- Employee count: Not specified
- Other: Projects must be undertaken within geographical limits of India alone; preference given to marginalized, disadvantaged, poor and deprived sections; administrative overheads shall not exceed 5% of total CSR expenditure; surplus from CSR projects shall not form part of business profit and must be ploughed back or transferred per Schedule VII

#### What It Offers
- Amount / Benefit: Financial support for CSR projects (amount not specified; based on 2% of average net profit of preceding three financial years)
- Disbursement mode: Direct credit to implementing agency
- Duration of benefit: One-time per project (project duration up to 3 years, excluding year of commencement)

#### Documents Required
1. Organization Details (brief description, goals & objectives)
2. Details of similar projects ongoing/completed in previous three years
3. Impact assessment of previous projects in similar focus areas (if any)
4. Details of previous support received from ONGC
5. Details of largest project undertaken by agency in recent past with financial implication
6. Details of proposed CSR project
7. Brief about goals and objectives of the project
8. Report of Need Assessment/Baseline Survey (if any)
9. Brief about targeted beneficiaries
10. Methodology of beneficiary selection and number
11. Details about physical and social milestones/deliverables
12. Detail timeline/duration of the project
13. Budget estimate with detailed break-up and documents proving rate reasonability
14. Monitoring mechanism for project activities and benefits
15. Documents depicting funding sources other than ONGC and amounts funded by each

#### Application Process (numbered steps)
1. Identify CSR project aligned with Schedule VII of Companies Act, 2013
2. Prepare Detailed Project Report (DPR) with need-based assessment, objectives, beneficiary details, timelines, budget break-up
3. Submit proposal through ONGC CSR portal (www.ongccsr.co.in) with required documents
4. ONGC CSR Committee reviews and recommends to Board for approval
5. Upon approval, sign agreement with Implementing Agency
6. Disburse funds as per implementation schedule
7. Monitor project progress through field visits, documentation, beneficiary feedback
8. Conduct impact assessment for projects ≥ Rs. 1 Cr.
9. Submit utilization report and fund utilization certificate
10. Report outcomes in annual CSR report
