ONGC At A Glance

explore us

A team comprising more than 24,000 dedicated professionals toil round the clock to amplify the performance figures of ONGC’s exploration and production activities of oil & gas and related oil-field services. Needless to emphasize, this has been made possible by the men & women behind the machines who have built & nurtured a world-class human capital poised for leadership in the energy business.

24,000+ Total Workforce

Amidst a rapidly transforming global energy landscape, ONGC demonstrated resilience and operational strength by posting a Profit After Tax (PAT) of Rs. 356,103 million in FY’25. Oil & gas production (inclusive of Joint Ventures) stood at 41.08 MMTOE.

356,103 million PAT

Being an integrated Oil & Gas Corporate displaying a dynamic in-house capability in all aspects of exploration & production business, ONGC contributes to around 72.8% of India's crude oil and around 55.8% of its natural gas production. Its structured & holistic approach is assured to play a major role in transforming the Maharatna into India's energy anchor.

63.3 % Total Production

Our wholly-owned subsidiary & overseas arm, ONGC Videsh owns Participating Interests in 32 oil and gas assets across 15 countries. Being the second largest petroleum company of India in terms of reserves and production, next only to its parent company, ONGC Videsh registered production worth 10.28 MMTOE from overseas assets in FY 2024-25.
