# Comprehensive Scheme Masterclass & File Guide

## Scheme Deep Dive

### Scheme Overview
The **ONGC Startup Fund** (Scheme ID: row-48) is a **grant-type** scheme under **PSU Support**, implemented by the **Corporate CSR Group, New Delhi** and the **CSR Departments at work centres across India**. It operates on a **Pan-India** geographic scope with a **rolling basis** application status — proposals can be submitted throughout the year as per the annual action plan formulated by the CSR Committee and submitted to the Board for approval. The scheme was last updated in **2025**.

### Objectives
The scheme aims to:
- Support responsible and sustainable initiatives while addressing People, Planet and Profit
- Enhance triple bottom line benchmarks of economic, environmental and social performance
- Develop social wealth for communities engaged with ONGC
- Promote efficient usage of scarce resources and encourage green energy initiatives
- Develop innovative solutions to fulfill vision by stepping beyond mandatory CSR provisions

### Benefits & Financial Support
| Benefit/Financial Aspect | Details | Source |

### Application Process
The application process consists of the following steps:

```mermaid
flowchart TD
    A[Identify CSR project aligned with Schedule VII of Companies Act, 2013] --> B[Prepare Detailed Project Report (DPR) with need-based assessment, objectives, beneficiary details, timelines, budget break-up]
    B --> C[Submit proposal through ONGC CSR portal (www.ongccsr.co.in) with required documents]
    C --> D[ONGC CSR Committee reviews and recommends to Board for approval]
    D --> E[Upon approval, sign agreement with Implementing Agency]
    E --> F[Disburse funds as per implementation schedule]
    F --> G[Monitor project progress through field visits, documentation, beneficiary feedback]
    G --> H{Project outlay ≥ Rs. 1 Cr.?}
    H -->|Yes| I[Conduct impact assessment]
    H -->|No| J[Skip impact assessment]
    I --> K[Submit utilization report and fund utilization certificate]
    J --> K
    K --> L[Report outcomes in annual CSR report]
```

### Required Documents
Applicants must submit the following 15 documents:

1. Organization Details (brief description, goals & objectives)
2. Details of similar projects ongoing/completed in previous three years
3. Impact assessment of previous projects in similar focus areas (if any)
4. Details of previous support received from ONGC
5. Details of largest project undertaken by agency in recent past with financial implication
6. Details of proposed CSR project
7. Brief about goals and objectives of the project
8. Report of Need Assessment/Baseline Survey (if any)
9. Brief about targeted beneficiaries
10. Methodology of beneficiary selection and number
11. Details about physical and social milestones/deliverables
12. Detail timeline/duration of the project
13. Budget estimate with detailed break-up and documents proving rate reasonability
14. Monitoring mechanism for project activities and benefits
15. Documents depicting funding sources other than ONGC and amounts funded by each

### Key Caveats
- Administrative overheads shall not exceed 5% of total CSR expenditure
- No direct contribution to Government initiatives unless specified by Act/Rules
- Projects must be undertaken within geographical limits of India alone
- Preference given to marginalized, disadvantaged, poor and deprived sections
- Surplus from CSR projects shall not form part of business profit and must be ploughed back or transferred per Schedule VII
- Impact assessment expenditure capped at 2% of total CSR expenditure or ₹50 lakh, whichever is higher
- In jointly funded projects, CSR expenditure claimed only for funds disbursed and utilized by ONGC
