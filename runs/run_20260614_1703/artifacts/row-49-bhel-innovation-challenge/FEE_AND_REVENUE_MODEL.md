## Pricing Principles
1. **Value-Based Pricing**: Fees are aligned with the non-financial benefits of the BHEL Innovation Challenge, such as mentorship, technical collaboration, and access to BHEL’s facilities, which can lead to pilot projects or commercialization opportunities.
2. **Risk-Adjusted Fees**: Success fees reflect the moderate risk profile of the scheme, given its rolling basis and lack of guaranteed financial grants; success depends on post-selection agreements for pilot projects or commercialization.
3. **Transparency**: Clients are informed that the scheme offers primarily non-financial support, with any financial support contingent on post-selection agreements, ensuring clear expectations.
4. **Scalability**: The tiered structure accommodates the scheme’s eligibility for startups, MSMEs, individual innovators, and entities registered in India, allowing consultants to tailor services based on client size and innovation stage.
