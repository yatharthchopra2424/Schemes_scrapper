# Pitch & Sales Scripts

## Philosophy
We are advisors, not salespeople. We diagnose first. We only pitch a scheme if it fits the client. We never oversell success rates.

---

## 1. Discovery Call Script (First Contact — 20 min)

### Opening (2 min)
Good morning/afternoon. I'm reaching out because I specialize in helping startups, MSMEs, and innovators in the energy, industry, and infrastructure sectors connect with opportunities like the BHEL Innovation Challenge—a recognition-based program from Bharat Heavy Electricals Limited that offers mentorship, technical collaboration, and access to BHEL's facilities for solutions relevant to their operational domains. Before we explore whether this is a fit, I’d like to understand your current innovation stage and goals to see if we can add value.

### Discovery Questions
- What stage of development is your innovation currently at—prototype, pilot-tested, or ready for demonstration?  
- Which of BHEL’s operational domains does your solution address: power generation, transmission, transportation, defence, aerospace, renewable energy, or industrial systems?  
- Are you registered as a startup, MSME, or individual innovator in India?  
- Do you have a detailed proposal document outlining your innovation’s relevance, feasibility, and potential impact?  
- Have you prepared technical specifications, schematics, or existing test results/pilot data for your solution?  
- Are you seeking collaboration opportunities with a major PSU for scaling or commercialization?  
- What is your primary goal in applying—recognition, technical guidance, access to facilities, or potential pilot projects?  
- Are you clear on the IP terms and willing to enter into formal agreements post-selection?

### Qualification Checklist (internal — don't read aloud)
- [ ] Startup, MSME, individual innovator, or entity registered in India  
- [ ] Innovation relevant to BHEL’s operational domains (power, transmission, transportation, defence, aerospace, renewable energy, industrial systems)  
- [ ] Solution at demonstration, pilot testing, or integration stage  
- [ ] Proposal document prepared  
- [ ] Proof of entity registration available (if startup/MSME)  
- [ ] Technical specifications/schematics ready  
- [ ] Prototype, test results, or pilot data available  
- [ ] Declaration of originality and IP rights prepared  

### Transition to Pitch
Based on what you’ve shared, your innovation in [specific domain] at the [stage] stage appears aligned with what BHEL is seeking through their Innovation Challenge. This scheme offers recognition, technical collaboration, and access to BHEL’s expertise—without requiring financial grants—making it a strategic fit for validation and scaling. Let me walk you through how it works.
