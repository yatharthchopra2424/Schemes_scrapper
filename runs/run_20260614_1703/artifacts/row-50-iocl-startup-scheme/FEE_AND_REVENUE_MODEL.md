## Pricing Principles
1. **Value-Aligned Fees**: Success fees are calibrated to the actual financial benefit delivered under the IOCL Startup Scheme, which provides case-to-case grant funding, seed investment, or milestone-based disbursements for joint development projects, with quantum depending on scope, technology readiness, and commercial potential.
2. **Risk-Adjusted Pricing**: The scheme’s rolling application basis and post-selection negotiation of financial terms introduce moderate uncertainty in timing and amount; success fees reflect this via tiered ranges (6–8% for Growth tier) rather than fixed percentages.
3. **Accessibility for Early-Stage Firms**: Retainer structures are designed to be accessible to startups with MVPs or prototypes, aligning with the scheme’s preference for applicants with working prototypes and under 10 years of incorporation.
4. **Transparency in Negotiation**: Fees are structured to mirror the scheme’s negotiated, milestone-based disbursement model, ensuring consultants are compensated only upon tangible progress toward funding or commercialization outcomes.
