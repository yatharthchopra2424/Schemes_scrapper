# Pitch & Sales Scripts

## Philosophy
We are advisors, not salespeople. We diagnose first. We only pitch a scheme if it fits the client. We never oversell success rates.

---

## 1. Discovery Call Script (First Contact — 20 min)

### Opening (2 min)
Good morning/afternoon, I’m [Your Name] from [Your Firm]. We specialize in helping innovative startups in the energy and allied sectors access strategic support from Indian Oil Corporation Limited (IOCL). I’d love to learn about your startup’s work in areas like renewable energy, green hydrogen, biofuels, or sustainable mobility to see if the IOCL Startup Scheme could be a fit for your next growth phase.

### Discovery Questions
1. When was your startup incorporated? (To assess if under 10 years old)
2. Under which act is your entity registered? (Companies Act, 2013 / LLP Act, 2008 / partnership firm)
3. What innovative product, service, or process are you developing? (Focus domains: renewable energy, green hydrogen, biofuels, carbon capture, AI/ML for energy optimization, sustainable mobility)
4. Do you have a working prototype or minimum viable product (MVP)? (Preference given to startups with MVP)
5. What is the technology readiness level of your solution?
6. Are you seeking support for proof-of-concept (PoC) development, joint development projects, or potential commercialization with IOCL?
7. What are your key milestones for the next 6–12 months?
8. How do you envision collaborating with a PSU like IOCL to scale your solution?

### Qualification Checklist (internal — don't read aloud)
- [ ] Startup registered under Companies Act, 2013, LLP Act, 2008, or as a partnership firm
- [ ] Working on innovative products/services/processes in eligible domains (renewable energy, green hydrogen, biofuels, carbon capture, AI/ML for energy optimization, sustainable mobility)
- [ ] Startup age ≤ 10 years from date of incorporation
- [ ] Preference: Has a working prototype or MVP
- [ ] Seeking support for PoC, joint development, or commercialization aligned with IOCL’s strategic goals
- [ ] Prepared to comply with IOCL’s safety, security, and confidentiality protocols
- [ ] Willing to enter into an MoU or project agreement post-selection

### Transition to Pitch
Based on your responses, it sounds like your work in [specific domain from answer] with a prototype aligns well with what IOCL is seeking through their Startup Scheme. Let me share how this scheme provides access to IOCL’s infrastructure, expertise, and potential project-based support—without requiring equity dilution.
