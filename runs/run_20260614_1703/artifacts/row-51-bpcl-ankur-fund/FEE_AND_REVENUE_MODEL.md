## Pricing Principles
1. **Value-Aligned Fees**: Success fees are calibrated to the actual financial benefit delivered under the BPCL Ankur Fund scheme, ensuring client interests are aligned with consultant performance.
2. **Risk-Adjusted Pricing**: Given the scheme’s low confidence rating and PSU-specific nature (Bharat Petroleum Corporation Limited), success fees reflect the moderate risk of application rejection or delays in fund disbursement.
3. **Transparency**: All fee structures are disclosed upfront, with retainers covering initial eligibility assessment and application preparation, and success fees payable only upon fund sanction.
4. **Scheme-Specific Note**: The BPCL Ankur Fund is a PSU-driven initiative with limited public detail on eligibility and benefit amounts (confidence: low). Processing timelines are not specified in available evidence, introducing potential revenue lag; therefore, success fee recognition assumes a 3-month approval lag unless otherwise confirmed by client-specific tracking.
