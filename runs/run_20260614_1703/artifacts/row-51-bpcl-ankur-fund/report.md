# Comprehensive Scheme Masterclass & File Guide

## Scheme Deep Dive

### Overview
The BPCL Ankur Fund is a scheme administered by Bharat Petroleum Corporation Limited (BPCL), a Maharatna Public Sector Undertaking (PSU) under the Ministry of Petroleum and Natural Gas, Government of India. It is designed to support early-stage startups and innovative ventures aligned with BPCL's strategic interests in the energy sector.

### Objectives
- To foster innovation and entrepreneurship in sectors relevant to BPCL's business, including but not limited to alternative fuels, renewable energy, energy efficiency, and digital transformation in oil & gas.
- To identify and nurture startups with high growth potential that can become strategic partners or vendors for BPCL.
- To contribute to the Government of India's startup ecosystem initiatives under schemes like Startup India.
- To provide non-dilutive early-stage funding to de-risk innovation and accelerate product development.
