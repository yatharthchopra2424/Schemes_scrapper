## Pricing Principles
1. Value-based pricing aligned with the financial benefit delivered (e.g., term loan amounts up to ₹1000 Crore).
2. Tiered success fees reflect decreasing marginal effort as deal size increases.
3. Retainers cover initial due diligence, bid preparation, and regulatory liaison.
4. Scheme-specific note: This scheme involves financial term loans (e.g., ₹500 Crore by NTPC Mining Limited, ₹1000 Crore by NTPC) with processing times tied to bid evaluation and sanction (typically 3–6 months post-submission), resulting in moderate risk due to competitive bidding and financial covenant compliance (e.g., 3:1 total liabilities to net worth ratio, EBITDA to interest expense ≥1.75:1).
