# Pitch & Sales Scripts

## Philosophy
We are advisors, not salespeople. We diagnose first. We only pitch a scheme if it fits the client. We never oversell success rates.

---

## 1. Discovery Call Script (First Contact — 20 min)

### Opening (2 min)
Good morning/afternoon. I’m reaching out because NTPC Limited, India’s largest integrated power utility and a Maharatna CPSE under the Ministry of Power, Government of India, supports stakeholders across India—including communities near project sites, employees, investors, and partners in joint ventures and subsidiaries—through reliable power, community development, and sustainable energy initiatives. I’d like to understand if your organisation or community might benefit from engaging with NTPC’s ongoing operations and initiatives.

### Discovery Questions
1. What is your organisation’s or community’s primary engagement with the power or energy sector?  
2. Are you located near any NTPC project sites or interested in community development initiatives in health, education, or livelihood?  
3. Are you an employee, investor, or stakeholder seeking to engage with NTPC through equity, debt, procurement, or partnership opportunities?  
4. What is your timeline for exploring partnerships or investments with PSUs like NTPC?  
5. Do you have experience working with government companies or Maharatna CPSEs on joint ventures or CSR initiatives?  
6. Are you looking for opportunities in renewable energy, green hydrogen, battery storage, or nuclear energy as part of NTPC’s energy transition goals?  
7. What is your annual turnover or investment capacity to assess eligibility for financial instruments like term loans or equity participation?  
8. How do you currently approach sustainability or ESG compliance in your operations?

### Qualification Checklist (internal — don't read aloud)
- [ ] Engages with or seeks to engage with communities near project sites, employees, investors, or stakeholders  
- [ ] Interested in reliable and affordable power, national energy security, or clean technology innovation  
- [ ] Seeks employment opportunities, community development through CSR, or investment in NTPC  
- [ ] Open to partnerships in joint ventures, subsidiaries, or community initiatives  
- [ ] Aligns with NTPC’s objectives: 60 GW renewable energy by 2032, ESG integration, innovation in green hydrogen/battery storage/nuclear, operational excellence, community empowerment via CSR  
- [ ] Not restricted by government policy, budgetary allocations, or regulatory approvals (as benefits are subject to these)  

### Transition to Pitch
Based on your responses, it appears your organisation/community aligns with NTPC Startup Incubation’s focus on supporting stakeholders through reliable power, community development, and sustainable energy initiatives. NTPC provides reliable and affordable power to millions, contributes to national energy security, drives innovation in clean technologies, creates employment opportunities, supports community development through CSR spending (₹363 Cr in FY 2024-25), and delivers strong financial returns to shareholders. Would you like me to outline how you can engage with NTPC’s ongoing operations and initiatives?

## 2. Scheme Pitch (5 min)

### Structure: Problem → Scheme → Value → Proof → Next Step

**Problem framing:**
Many organisations and communities struggle to access reliable and affordable power, participate in national energy security efforts, benefit from clean technology innovation, or secure employment and community development opportunities linked to large-scale power projects. Additionally, investors and partners often lack clear pathways to engage with Maharatna CPSEs like NTPC through equity, debt, procurement, or joint ventures.

**Scheme intro:**
NTPC Startup Incubation is not a traditional scheme with a fixed application process; rather, it refers to NTPC Limited’s ongoing operations and initiatives as a Maharatna Central Public Sector Enterprise under the Ministry of Power, Government of India. NTPC provides reliable and affordable power to millions, contributes to national energy security, drives innovation in clean technologies, creates employment opportunities, supports community development through CSR spending (₹363 Cr in FY 2024-25), ensures environmental sustainability via 100% ash utilisation and renewable energy expansion, and delivers strong financial returns to shareholders. The company also offers a progressive work culture, recognized as a 'Great Place to Work' for 19 consecutive years and a Top Employer 2025. Financial support is mobilised through equity, debt, and internal accruals, with access to domestic and international markets for loans (e.g., ₹500 Cr by NTPC Mining Limited and ₹1000 Cr by NTPC) and external commercial borrowings, subject to financial covenants like a 3:1 total liabilities to net worth ratio and EBITDA to interest expense ratio of at least 1.75:1.

**Why they qualify (personalised):**
- You are seeking engagement as a community near project sites, employee, investor, or stakeholder — all explicitly listed as target beneficiaries  
- You are interested in reliable power, community development (health, education, livelihood), or sustainable energy — aligned with NTPC’s CSR spending of ₹363 Cr in FY 2024-25  
- You are open to partnerships in joint ventures, subsidiaries, or community initiatives — consistent with NTPC’s operational model  
- You support or benefit from innovation in green hydrogen, battery storage, or nuclear energy — key objectives of NTPC’s energy transition
