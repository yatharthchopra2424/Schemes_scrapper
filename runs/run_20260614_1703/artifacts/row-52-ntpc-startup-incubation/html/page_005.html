Leading India's Power Sector

Energy is indispensable and is one of the essential pillars on which our economy thrives. NTPC is committed to provide uninterrupted and affordable power to fulfill this requirement, thereby bringing cheers to the farthest reaches of the country. Today, with an installed capacity of over 90 GW, NTPC is steering ahead to be India’s largest integrated power company and targets to become a 149 GW firm with diversified fuel mix and a 650+ BU company in terms of generation by 2032.

Read more

POWERING ENERGY TRANSITION

First Energy company to declare its Energy Compact Goals as part of UN High-level Dialogue on Energy (HLDE), NTPC has been spearheading India's Renewable Energy ecosystem by strengthening its RE footprint, expanding to new areas like Green Hydrogen, Waste to Energy, Battery Storage and Nuclear Energy, with an aim to have 50% non-fossil based capacity by 2032. more than 8 GW  of renewable energy under operation and more than13 GW under implementation, the company aims to have 60 GW of RE capacity under its belt by 2032. It has teamed with NITI Aayog for the Net Energy Zero effort.

Read more

Connecting with Communities

Building sustainable communities not only forms the foundation of CSR and Sustainability policies for NTPC, it is essential to the company's core business in terms of generating power and illuminating millions of lives. The company believes in offering reliable power, related products and services at affordable cost, integrating different energy sources with innovative and eco-friendly technology and thereby contributing to the society.

Nurturing Leaders of Tomorrow

Our people practices aim at achieving excellence in development and management of Human Resources through continuous process improvement, increasing employee engagement, focussed learning and development, providing career and growth opportunity and employee welfare. It is an outcome of NTPC’s “People Before PLF” approach which is the guiding philosophy behind the entire gamut of HR policies thus building an enabling and engaging progressive work culture. Today, NTPC is recognised as one of the finest employers and workplaces in the country.

Driving Sustainable Growth

The foundation of NTPC’s long-term value creation lies in integrating sustainability into our strategy and operations, delivering both economic growth and societal value. By embedding ESG principles across decision-making, we ensure resilient performance while supporting India’s clean energy transition.
