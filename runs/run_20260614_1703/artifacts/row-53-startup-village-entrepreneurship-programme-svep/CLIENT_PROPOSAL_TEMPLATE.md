## 3. Our Scope of Work
- Initial eligibility assessment and scheme suitability confirmation  
- Guidance on preparing and submitting the business idea and personal details through CRP-EPs  
- Support in feasibility assessment and business plan development with Block Resource Centres (BRCs)  
- Assistance in compiling and submitting the Detailed Project Report (DPR) for appraisal  
- Coordination with Block Level Committee for proposal approval  
- Facilitation of grant disbursement and bank linkage for loan component  
- Portal registration and application tracking on the [SVEP application portal](https://nrlm.gov.in/svep/)  
- Post-sanction handholding support for enterprise establishment and growth
