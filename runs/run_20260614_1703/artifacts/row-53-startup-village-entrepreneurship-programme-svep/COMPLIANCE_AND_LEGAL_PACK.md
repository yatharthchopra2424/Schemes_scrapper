- Not disclose any Confidential Information to any third party without the Client’s prior written consent.

  confidential information, but in no event less than reasonable care.

**Remedies:** The Client may seek injunctive relief for any breach of this NDA, in addition to
any other rights or remedies available at law or in equity.

**Governing Law:** This NDA shall be governed by and construed in accordance with Indian law.
