## Pricing Principles
1. **Value-aligned pricing**: Fees are structured as a percentage of the financial benefit secured (grant + loan), ensuring client success drives consultant revenue.
2. **Risk-adjusted retainer**: Lower retainers reflect the scheme’s rolling application basis and high volume of micro-enterprise clients, reducing upfront risk for clients.
3. **Transparency in success fees**: Success fees are calculated only on the disbursed grant amount (up to ₹1.5 lakh), as the loan component is repayable and not a direct benefit to the client.
4. **Scheme-specific note**: SVEP has a moderate risk profile due to its multi-step approval process involving CRP-EPs, BRCs, and Block Level Committees, with disbursement tied to project milestones. Evidence indicates successful approvals for eligible rural entrepreneurs, but processing time varies based on fund availability and verification, necessitating a 3-month lag in revenue recognition for success fees.
