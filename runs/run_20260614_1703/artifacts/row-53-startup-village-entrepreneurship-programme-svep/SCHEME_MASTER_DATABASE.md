### Startup Village Entrepreneurship Programme (SVEP)
- **Scheme ID:** SCH-001
- **Category:** Grant
- **Geography:** Rural areas across India
- **Administering Body:** Ministry of Rural Development, Government of India
- **Portal / Application URL:** [https://nrlm.gov.in/svep](https://nrlm.gov.in/svep)
- **Current Status:** Rolling
- **Window:** Always Open

#### Eligibility Criteria
- Business type: Non-farm economic activities (manufacturing, trading, or services)
- Age of business: New enterprises (first-time entrepreneurs)
- Sector: Non-farm sector (manufacturing, trading, services)
- Revenue cap: Not specified in evidence
- Employee count: Not specified in evidence
- Other: Must be located in rural blocks; priority to DAY-NRLM Self-Help Group (SHG) members and their families; preference for SC/ST, women, and youth; no prior support under similar central or state schemes

#### What It Offers
- Amount / Benefit: Up to INR 1.5 lakh grant + up to INR 1.0 lakh loan (total up to INR 2.5 lakh per enterprise)
- Disbursement mode: Grant provided directly to beneficiary; loan facilitated through banking linkages
- Duration of benefit: Disbursed in stages based on project milestones (one-time financial support with ongoing handholding)

#### Documents Required
1. Identity proof (Aadhaar/Voter ID)
2. Address proof
3. Bank account details
4. Detailed Project Report (DPR)
5. Quotations for equipment/machinery (if applicable)
6. Proof of enterprise location (rural setting)
7. Consent letter from family (if required)
8. Self-declaration of no prior enterprise under similar scheme

#### Application Process (numbered steps)
1. Identification of potential entrepreneurs by Community Resource Persons - Enterprise Promotion (CRP-EPs) at the village level
2. Submission of a basic business idea and personal details by the entrepreneur
3. Feasibility assessment and business plan development with support from CRP-EPs and Block Resource Centres (BRCs)
4. Submission of the Detailed Project Report (DPR) for appraisal
5. Approval of the proposal by the Block Level Committee
6. Disbursement of grant and facilitation of loan through bank linkage
7. Post-sanction handholding support for enterprise establishment and growth
