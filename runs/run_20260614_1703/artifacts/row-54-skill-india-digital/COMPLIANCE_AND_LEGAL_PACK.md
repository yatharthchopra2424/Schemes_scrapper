## 8D. Internal Data Handling Policy

All client data related to scheme applications shall be handled in accordance with the following standards:

- **Storage:** Encrypted cloud storage (AES-256 encryption at rest and in transit).
- **Access Control:** Role-based access with multi-factor authentication (2FA) required for all personnel accessing client data.
- **Device Policy:** No personal devices permitted for accessing or storing client data; all work must be conducted on firm-issued, secured devices.
- **Retention:** Client data shall be retained for a minimum of two (2) years post case closure, after which it shall be securely deleted unless longer retention is required by law or scheme-specific guidelines.
- **Breach Notification:** In the event of a data breach, the firm shall notify affected clients and relevant authorities within 24 hours of discovery.

**Scheme-specific note:** All documents submitted to Ministry of Skill Development and Entrepreneurship (MSDE) under Skill India Digital must be retained for a minimum of 3 years for audit purposes, as per scheme guidelines from evidence. Therefore, client data related to Skill India Digital applications shall be retained for three (3) years post case closure to comply with audit requirements, overriding the standard two-year retention period.
