# Pitch & Sales Scripts

## Philosophy
We are advisors, not salespeople. We diagnose first. We only pitch a scheme if it fits the client. We never oversell success rates.

---

## 1. Discovery Call Script (First Contact — 20 min)

### Opening (2 min)
Good morning/afternoon. I specialise in helping businesses and individuals in the skill development and vocational training sector access government support for upskilling and certification. The Skill India Digital initiative is designed to make quality skill training more accessible and recognised across India — I’d like to understand if this could support your goals in workforce development or personal upskilling.

### Discovery Questions
- Are you an individual seeking certification in a vocational or technical skill, or an organisation looking to train employees or beneficiaries?
- What is your current involvement in skill development — are you a learner, training provider, employer, or assessing body?
- Have you previously accessed any government skill development schemes or digital platforms for training or certification?
- Is your organisation or training centre registered with any government body (e.g., NCVET, MSDE, or State Skill Development Mission)?
- Are you aiming to get your skills certified under the National Skills Qualification Framework (NSQF)?
- Do you have access to digital devices and internet connectivity to participate in online learning or assessments?
- Are you looking for recognition of prior learning (RPL) or fresh skill training through accredited courses?
- If you're an organisation, do you train individuals from disadvantaged backgrounds or underprivileged communities?

### Qualification Checklist (internal — don't read aloud)
- [ ] Individual is an Indian citizen seeking skill training or certification  
- [ ] Organisation is a registered training provider, employer, or assessing body in India  
- [ ] Training programme aligns with National Skills Qualification Framework (NSQF) standards  
- [ ] Learner or beneficiary is above 15 years of age (varies by course)  
- [ ] Organisation has necessary infrastructure for training delivery (physical or digital)  
- [ ] Individual or organisation is willing to undergo assessment and certification process  
- [ ] For RPL: Candidate has prior work experience or informal learning in the skill domain  
- [ ] Training provider is willing to upload data to Skill India Digital Hub (SIDH) portal  

### Transition to Pitch
Based on what you’ve shared, it sounds like you’re looking to either gain recognised certification for your skills or deliver quality training that leads to verifiable outcomes — exactly what the **Skill India Digital** initiative supports. This platform, managed by the Ministry of Skill Development and Entrepreneurship (MSDE), enables access to NSQF-aligned courses, digital certification, and recognition of prior learning — all at no cost to eligible candidates. Let me walk you through how it works.

## 2. Scheme Pitch (5 min)

### Structure: Problem → Scheme → Value → Proof → Next Step

**Problem framing:**
Many individuals gain valuable skills through work or informal training but lack formal certification, limiting their job prospects and mobility. Similarly, training providers struggle to deliver standardised, recognised programmes and issue verifiable credentials at scale — especially in underserved areas.

**Scheme intro:**
The **Skill India Digital** initiative, administered by the **Ministry of Skill Development and Entrepreneurship (MSDE)**, is a national digital platform that provides free access to skill training courses, assessment, and certification aligned with the National Skills Qualification Framework (NSQF). It supports both learners seeking certification and organisations delivering training, with benefits including free course access, digital certificates, and recognition of prior learning (RPL) — all aimed at enhancing employability and workforce quality.

**Why they qualify (personalised):**
- You are an Indian citizen or organisation operating in India seeking skill development support  
- Your training or learning goals align with NSQF-compliant job roles or sectors  
- You are willing to participate in online or blended learning and assessment via the SIDH portal  
- For organisations: You have the capacity to train and assess candidates as per scheme guidelines  

**Proof:**
"We've helped [N] similar businesses and training centres get their learners certified through Skill India Digital. [TO BE FILLED BY CONSULTANT]"

**Next step:**
We offer a free, no-obligation eligibility check — we’ll review your profile or training programme against the scheme requirements and let you know if you qualify. If you do, we can support you with enrolment, documentation, and application — charging only a success fee upon successful certification or approval.

| "We're too small / too big" | There is no minimum or maximum size limit for individuals or organisations — the scheme supports everyone from solo trainers to large vocational institutes, as long as training meets NSQF standards and is delivered through recognised channels. |
