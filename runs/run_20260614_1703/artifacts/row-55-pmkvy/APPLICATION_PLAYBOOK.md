# Application Playbook

## How to Use This File
For each new client case, assign it a Case ID, pick the target scheme, and follow the steps below.

---

## Stage 1: Eligibility Verification (Day 1–3)

### Checklist
- [ ] Candidate is an Indian national  
- [ ] Candidate is unemployed, a school/college dropout, or seeking to upgrade skills  
- [ ] Candidate possesses valid identity proof (Aadhaar, Voter ID, or PAN)  
- [ ] For Recognition of Prior Learning (RPL): candidate has prior work experience or informal learning in a specific skill area  
- [ ] No minimum or maximum age limit (preference given to youth aged 15–45 years)  
- [ ] Candidate belongs to target beneficiary group: youth, unemployed, school/college dropouts, women, SC/ST, or persons with disability (if applicable)  
- [ ] Candidate is willing to enroll through a PMKVY-affiliated and NSDC-approved training center  

> **Sources**: Application Portal: https://pmkvyofficial.org/; Eligibility criteria from Scheme Key Facts  

---

## Stage 2: Document Collection (Day 3–10)

### Universal Documents (always required)
- [ ] PAN Card  
- [ ] Aadhaar Card  
- [ ] Bank account details (passbook or cancelled cheque)  
- [ ] Recent passport-sized photographs  
- [ ] Educational qualification certificates (if any)  
- [ ] Domicile certificate (if required by training center)  
- [ ] Category certificate (SC/ST/OBC, if applicable)  
- [ ] Disability certificate (if applicable)  
- [ ] Udyam Registration (if applicable for self-employment/entrepreneurship track)  

### Scheme-Specific Documents
- [ ] Aadhaar Card (mandatory for identity verification and bank linkage)  
- [ ] Bank account details (for direct transfer of monetary reward post-certification)  
- [ ] Educational qualification certificates (if any — to determine eligibility for specific QPs)  
- [ ] Passport-sized photographs (for training center records and certification)  
- [ ] Domicile certificate (if required by the training center for state-specific allocations)  
- [ ] Category certificate (SC/ST/OBC) — for tracking beneficiary coverage under inclusive growth objectives  
- [ ] Disability certificate (if applicable) — for accessibility and special support under scheme  

### Document Quality Check
- [ ] All documents are clear, legible scans or high-quality photographs  
- [ ] File size does not exceed 2 MB per document  
- [ ] Name on all documents matches exactly as per Aadhaar (or other primary ID)  
- [ ] Aadhaar number is visible and not masked (where required for authentication)  
- [ ] Bank account details include account holder name, account number, and IFSC code  
- [ ] Certificates are self-attested or gazetted officer-attested if required by training center  
- [ ] Documents are in English or Hindi; if in regional language, accompanied by notarized translation  
- [ ] Photographs are recent (within last 6 months), with light background and full face visibility  

### Client Communication Template
**Subject**: Enrollment Opportunity under PMKVY – Free Skill Training & Monetary Reward (INR 8,000–10,000)  

Dear [Client Name],  

We are pleased to inform you that you may be eligible for the **Pradhan Mantri Kaushal Vikas Yojana (PMKVY)** — a flagship skill development scheme by the Ministry of Skill Development and Entrepreneurship (MSDE), Government of India.  

**Scheme Highlights**:  
- 100% funded training in industry-aligned job roles (Qualification Packs under NSQF)  
- Monetary reward of **INR 8,000–10,000** upon successful certification, transferred directly to your bank account  
- Free training and assessment — no cost to you  
- Placement assistance through Rozgar Melas and industry linkages  
- Open to unemployed individuals, school/college dropouts, and those seeking skill upgradation  
- No age bar (preference for 15–45 years); special focus on women, SC/ST, and persons with disability  

**How to Apply**:  
1. Visit the official portal: **https://pmkvyofficial.org/**  
2. Or contact a nearby PMKVY-affiliated training center (list available on the portal)  
3. Select a job role (QP) and submit required documents: Aadhaar, bank details, educational certificates (if any), photos, and category/domicile/disability certificates (if applicable)  

**Important Notes**:  
- Training must be conducted only through **PMKVY-affiliated and NSDC-approved training partners**  
- Monetary reward is disbursed **only after** successful completion of training and passing the assessment  
- Placement is facilitated but not guaranteed  
- Admissions are on a **rolling basis** throughout the year, subject to training center availability

## Stage 3: Application Drafting (Day 10–18)

### Project Report / DPR (if required)
- **Not required**. PMKVY is a candidate-centric skill certification and reward scheme; no Detailed Project Report (DPR) is needed for individual enrollment.  
- Training providers (TPs) may require internal planning documents, but these are not submitted by candidates.  
- **Verify with nodal officer** only if applying as a training provider seeking accreditation (outside scope of candidate application playbook).  

### Application Form Filling Protocol
1. Access the official portal: **https://pmkvyofficial.org/** or visit a PMKVY-affiliated training center in person  
2. Select “Candidate Registration” or “Enroll Now” option on the portal  
3. Enter Aadhaar number and validate via OTP sent to linked mobile number  
4. Fill in personal details exactly as per Aadhaar (name, date of birth, gender, address)  
5. Provide accurate bank account information (account number, IFSC code, bank name, branch) for reward transfer  
6. Upload clear, legible copies of required documents (Aadhaar, bank proof, photos, educational/category certificates if applicable)  
7. Select a job role (Qualification Pack - QP) from the available list based on interest and eligibility  
8. Confirm enrollment and submit; note down the application/reference number for tracking  
9. Attend orientation session as scheduled by the training center  
10. Ensure all declarations are signed (digitally or physically) regarding authenticity of information and consent for data use  

> **Sources**: Application Process from Scheme Key Facts; Portal: https://pmkvyofficial.org/  

### Internal Review Gate
- [ ] Candidate’s name matches exactly across all documents (especially Aadhaar and bank proof)  
- [ ] Aadhaar number is correctly entered and validated via OTP  
- [ ] Bank account details are complete and accurate (name, number, IFSC)  
- [ ] All uploaded documents are clear, valid, and within size limits  
- [ ] Selected job role (QP) is available and appropriate for candidate’s background  
- [ ] Declarations and consent forms are completed and signed  
- [ ] Application/reference number is recorded and shared with candidate  
- [ ] Candidate has been informed of training schedule, assessment process, and reward disbursement timeline

## Stage 4: Submission & Follow-Up (Day 18–30)

### Submission Protocol
- Submit application either online via **https://pmkvyofficial.org/** or in person at a PMKVY-affiliated training center  
- Take a screenshot of the confirmation page showing application/reference number and timestamp  
- Store the reference number securely in the case file  
- Save copies of all submitted documents and confirmation emails/SMS  
- Set a follow-up reminder for 7 days post-submission to confirm enrollment status  
- Mark calendar for training start date and assessment date as communicated by the training center

## Stage 5: Post-Approval (Day 30–90+)

### Approval Actions
- [ ] Confirm receipt of provisional admission letter or enrollment confirmation from training center  
- [ ] Verify that training has commenced as per schedule  
- [ ] Monitor attendance and progress through updates from training center or candidate  
- [ ] Ensure candidate undergoes final assessment by affiliated assessment agency  
- [ ] Upon passing, confirm receipt of **certification** and **monetary reward (INR 8,000–10,000)** directly into the candidate’s bank account  
- [ ] Collect copy of skill certificate and bank transaction proof (screenshot or statement) for case closure  
- [ ] Assist candidate in accessing placement support (Rozgar Melas, job fairs, industry linkages) if desired  
- [ ] Document outcome: certification status, reward received, placement status (if any)  
- [ ] Calculate and invoice success fee as per firm’s retention agreement  
- [ ] Archive all documents, correspondence, and proof of outcome in case file  

> **Sources**: Financial Support and Benefits from Scheme Key Facts; Application Process: reward disbursed post-assessment
