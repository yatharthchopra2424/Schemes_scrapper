## Pricing Principles
1. **Value-Based Alignment**: Fees are structured to reflect the actual financial benefit delivered to candidates (average INR 8,000–10,000 per certified candidate), ensuring consultancy compensation correlates with measurable outcomes.
2. **Risk Sharing**: Success fees are contingent on successful candidate certification and reward disbursement, aligning consultant incentives with scheme completion rates.
3. **Transparency**: All fee structures are disclosed upfront, with retainers covering initial eligibility assessment, documentation support, and enrollment facilitation.
4. **Scheme-Specific Note**: Given the rolling admissions basis and typical processing time for training completion and assessment (evidence indicates batches run throughout the year based on center availability), revenue recognition may experience a 2–4 month lag from enrollment to reward disbursement. The scheme exhibits moderate operational risk due to dependency on training center scheduling and assessment agency availability, but high success potential when candidates complete training, as monetary rewards are directly transferred upon certification.
