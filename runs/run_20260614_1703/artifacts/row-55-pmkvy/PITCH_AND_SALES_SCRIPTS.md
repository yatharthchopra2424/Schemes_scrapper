# Pitch & Sales Scripts

## Philosophy
We are advisors, not salespeople. We diagnose first. We only pitch a scheme if it fits the client. We never oversell success rates.

---

## 1. Discovery Call Script (First Contact — 20 min)

### Opening (2 min)
Good morning/afternoon. I specialize in helping individuals and organizations access government skill development initiatives like the Pradhan Mantri Kaushal Vikas Yojana (PMKVY), which provides 100% funded training and a monetary reward of INR 8,000–10,000 upon certification. I’d like to understand your background and goals to see if this scheme could support your skill development or workforce training needs.

### Discovery Questions
- Are you an Indian national seeking to gain new skills, upgrade existing ones, or get certified for prior work experience?
- What is your current employment status — are you unemployed, a school/college dropout, or looking to upskill while employed?
- Do you fall within the age group of 15–45 years, or are you seeking certification under Recognition of Prior Learning (RPL) regardless of age?
- Do you possess valid identity proof such as Aadhaar, Voter ID, or PAN card?
- Are you interested in training for a specific job role or sector aligned with industry demand (e.g., retail, healthcare, IT, manufacturing)?
- Do you have access to a bank account for direct transfer of the monetary reward upon certification?
- If applying under RPL, do you have prior work experience or informal learning in a specific skill area you’d like certified?
- Are you affiliated with or able to access a PMKVY-affiliated training center or Training Partner (TP)?

### Qualification Checklist (internal — don't read aloud)
- [ ] Candidate is an Indian national  
- [ ] Candidate is unemployed, a school/college dropout, or seeking skill upgradation  
- [ ] Candidate possesses valid identity proof (Aadhaar/Voter ID/PAN)  
- [ ] Candidate is within preferred age group (15–45 years) or applying under RPL with prior experience  
- [ ] Candidate has access to a bank account for direct benefit transfer  
- [ ] Candidate is willing to enroll through a PMKVY-affiliated and NSDC-approved training provider  
- [ ] Candidate understands that placement assistance is facilitated but not guaranteed  
- [ ] Candidate agrees to undergo training and assessment as per NSQF-aligned Qualification Pack (QP)

### Transition to Pitch
Based on your responses, it appears you may be eligible for the Pradhan Mantri Kaushal Vikas Yojana (PMKVY), which offers fully funded skill training and a monetary reward of INR 8,000–10,000 upon successful certification. Let me share how this scheme works and how we can support your application.

## 2. Scheme Pitch (5 min)

### Structure: Problem → Scheme → Value → Proof → Next Step

**Problem framing:**
Many individuals face barriers to skill development due to high training costs, lack of certification for prior experience, or difficulty accessing job-relevant training. This limits employability, especially among youth, school dropouts, and marginalized groups seeking to enter or advance in the workforce.

**Scheme intro:**
The Pradhan Mantri Kaushal Vikas Yojana (PMKVY), implemented by the Ministry of Skill Development and Entrepreneurship (MSDE), Government of India, is a pan-India subsidy scheme that provides 100% funding for training and assessment through NSDC-approved partners. Upon successful completion and certification, candidates receive a direct monetary reward averaging INR 8,000–10,000, transferred to their bank account. The scheme supports free training, placement assistance via Rozgar Melas, and Recognition of Prior Learning (RPL) for informal skills.

**Why they qualify (personalised):**
- You are an Indian national with valid identity proof (Aadhaar/Voter ID/PAN)  
- You are either unemployed, a school/college dropout, or seeking to upgrade your skills  
- You fall within the target age group (15–45 years) or have prior work experience eligible for RPL assessment  
- You have a bank account for direct transfer of the monetary reward upon certification
