### PMKVY
- **Scheme ID:** SCH-001
- **Category:** Subsidy
- **Geography:** Pan-India
- **Administering Body:** Ministry of Skill Development and Entrepreneurship (MSDE), Government of India
- **Portal / Application URL:** https://pmkvyofficial.org/
- **Current Status:** Rolling
- **Window:** Always Open (admissions and training batches conducted throughout the year based on training center availability)

#### Eligibility Criteria
- Business type: Not applicable (individual beneficiary scheme)
- Age of business: Not applicable
- Sector: Open to all sectors (training aligned with industry standards across job roles)
- Revenue cap: Not applicable
- Employee count: Not applicable
- Other: Indian nationals who are unemployed, school/college dropouts, or seeking to upgrade their skills; valid identity proof (Aadhaar, Voter ID, or PAN); for Recognition of Prior Learning (RPL), prior work experience or informal learning in a specific skill area

#### What It Offers
- Amount / Benefit: Average monetary reward of INR 8,000–10,000 per successfully certified candidate; 100% funding for training and assessment costs paid directly to training providers
- Disbursement mode: Direct credit (monetary reward transferred directly to candidate's bank account; training costs paid to providers)
- Duration of benefit: One-time (upon successful completion and certification)

#### Documents Required
1. Aadhaar Card
2. Bank account details (for monetary reward transfer)
3. Educational qualification certificates (if any)
4. Passport-sized photographs
5. Domicile certificate (if required by the training center)
6. Category certificate (SC/ST/OBC, if applicable)
7. Disability certificate (if applicable)

#### Application Process (numbered steps)
1. Visit the official PMKVY website or contact a nearby PMKVY-affiliated training center.
2. Select a job role from the available list of Qualification Packs (QPs) under the National Skills Qualification Framework (NSQF).
3. Enroll in the training program by submitting required documents.
4. Attend the training sessions as per the schedule.
5. Undergo assessment by an affiliated assessment agency.
6. Upon passing, receive certification and monetary reward directly into the bank account.
