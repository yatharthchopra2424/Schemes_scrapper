# Comprehensive Scheme Masterclass & File Guide

## Scheme Deep Dive

### Overview
The Pradhan Mantri Kaushal Vikas Yojana (PMKVY) is a flagship skill development scheme launched by the Ministry of Skill Development and Entrepreneurship (MSDE), Government of India. It operates on a pan-India basis with a total fund size of INR 12,000 crore. The scheme follows a subsidy model where the government bears 100% of training and assessment costs, and provides direct monetary rewards to certified candidates. Admissions and training batches are conducted on a rolling basis throughout the year, depending on training center availability.

### Objectives
- Enable and mobilize a large number of Indian youth to take up outcome-based skill training  
- Increase productivity of the existing workforce and align training with actual industry needs  
- Encourage standardization in certification and initiate a registry of skills  
- Provide monetary awards for skill certification to boost employability and productivity  
- Focus on underprivileged and marginalized sections (youth, unemployed, school/college dropouts, women, SC/ST, persons with disability) to promote inclusive growth  
- Leverage existing infrastructure in government institutions, colleges, and schools  
- Encourage public-private partnerships (PPP) for greater reach and effectiveness  

### Eligibility Matrix
| **Criteria**               | **Details**                                                                 | **Notes**                                                                 |
|----------------------------|-----------------------------------------------------------------------------|---------------------------------------------------------------------------|
| **Nationality**            | Indian nationals                                                            | Mandatory                                                                 |
| **Employment Status**      | Unemployed, school/college dropouts, or seeking skill upgradation           | Open to all                                                               |
| **Age Limit**              | No minimum or maximum age limit                                             | Preference given to youth aged 15–45 years                                |
| **Identity Proof**         | Valid Aadhaar, Voter ID, or PAN                                             | Mandatory for enrollment                                                  |
| **Target Beneficiaries**   | Youth, unemployed, school/college dropouts, women, SC/ST, persons with disability | Priority groups under inclusive growth objective                          |
| **Recognition of Prior Learning (RPL)** | Individuals with prior work experience or informal learning in a specific skill area | Eligible for assessment and certification without undergoing training     |

### Benefits & Financial Support
| **Benefit Type**           | **Details**                                                                 | **Amount / Coverage**                                                     |
|----------------------------|-----------------------------------------------------------------------------|---------------------------------------------------------------------------|
| **Training Cost**          | 100% funding for training and assessment                                    | Borne entirely by Government of India; paid directly to training providers |
| **Assessment Cost**        | 100% funding                                                                | Included in training cost coverage                                        |
| **Monetary Reward**        | Direct transfer to candidate's bank account post-certification              | Average INR 8,000–10,000 per successfully certified candidate             |
| **Placement Assistance**   | Facilitated through Rozgar Melas and industry linkages                      | Not guaranteed; subject to demand and performance                         |
| **Additional Support**     | Access to entrepreneurship development modules, post-placement tracking     | Provided through accredited training centers (TCs) and training providers (TPs) |
| **Certification**          | NSQF-aligned certification under Qualification Packs (QPs)                  | Nationally recognized and industry-validated                              |

> **Key Financial Notes**:  
> - The entire cost of training and assessment is subsidized; there is **no loan component**.  
> - Monetary reward is **disbursed only after** successful completion of training and passing the assessment.  
> - Funds are allocated annually and subject to availability under budgetary provisions.  
> - Total scheme outlay: **INR 12,000 crore**.

### Required Documents
1. Aadhaar Card  
2. Bank account details (for monetary reward transfer)  
3. Educational qualification certificates (if any)  
4. Passport-sized photographs  
5. Domicile certificate (if required by the training center)  
6. Category certificate (SC/ST/OBC, if applicable)  
7. Disability certificate (if applicable)  

### Contact Details

### Key Caveats
> - Monetary reward is disbursed **only after** successful completion of training and passing the assessment.  
> - Training must be conducted through **PMKVY-affiliated and NSDC-approved training partners only**.  
> - Placement is **not guaranteed**; it is facilitated based on industry demand and candidate performance.  
> - The scheme is subject to **periodic revisions and updates** based on mid-term evaluations.  
> - Funds are allocated annually and subject to availability under budgetary provisions.  
> - Last updated: **2024**.
