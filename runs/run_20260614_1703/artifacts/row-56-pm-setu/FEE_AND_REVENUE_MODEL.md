## Pricing Principles
1. Retainer fees cover initial assessment, documentation preparation, and submission support for PM-SETU proposals, reflecting the scheme’s complex, multi-stage application process involving State/UT proposals, hub-and-spoke consortia formation, and DGT evaluation.
2. Success fees are contingent on verified achievement of disbursement-linked indicators (DLIs), aligning with the Program-for-Results (PforR) financing mechanism where funds are released only upon result verification.
3. Pricing reflects the scheme’s high administrative burden: proposals require detailed Strategic Investment Plans (SIPs), MoUs with industry partners, baseline data, and monitoring plans, justifying tiered retainer structures.
4. Scheme-specific note: PM-SETU involves significant risk due to its reliance on DLI achievement and multi-agency coordination (State/UT, DGT, World Bank), with processing times extending over multiple years; success fees are structured to mitigate client risk while ensuring consultant compensation for long-term engagement.
