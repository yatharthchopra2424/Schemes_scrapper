## 1. Executive Summary
Based on our initial review of [Client]'s business profile, we have identified PM-DAKSH — a skill development scheme administered by the Ministry of Social Justice and Empowerment, Government of India — for which your business appears eligible. The potential benefit is 100% financial support for training costs, including course fees, training material, and stipends (where applicable), directly paid to empaneled training providers.

## 2. Recommended Schemes

| Scheme | Potential Benefit | Est. Timeline | Confidence Level |
|---|---|---|---|
| PM-DAKSH | 100% financial support for training costs (course fees, material, stipends where applicable) | Rolling basis — applications accepted throughout the year subject to training slot availability | Medium |

## 3. Our Scope of Work
1. Initial eligibility assessment against PM-DAKSH criteria (caste, age 18-45, economic status)  
2. Document collection and verification as per scheme requirements  
3. Online application submission via the official portal: https://pmdaksh.dosje.gov.in  
4. Coordination with empaneled training providers for course selection and enrollment  
5. Monitoring of application status and liaison with Ministry of Social Justice and Empowerment officials  
6. Support during training completion and certification process  
7. Assistance with placement support or self-employment availing (as per scheme provisions)  

*Note: No detailed project report (DPR) is required for PM-DAKSH; the scheme focuses on direct beneficiary registration and training enrollment.*

## 4. What We Need From You
1. Documents as per the PM-DAKSH checklist: Caste Certificate (SC/OBC/DNT), Identity Proof, Address Proof, Age Proof, Passport-sized Photographs, and Mobile Number/Email ID  
2. Designated point of contact from your organization for coordination and updates  
3. Timely responses within a 2-day SLA to avoid delays in application processing
