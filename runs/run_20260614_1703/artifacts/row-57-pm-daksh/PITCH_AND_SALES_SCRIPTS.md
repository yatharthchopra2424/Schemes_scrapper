# Pitch & Sales Scripts

## Philosophy
We are advisors, not salespeople. We diagnose first. We only pitch a scheme if it fits the client. We never oversell success rates.

---

## 1. Discovery Call Script (First Contact — 20 min)

### Opening (2 min)
Good morning/afternoon. I’m calling from [Your Firm Name], and we specialize in helping individuals from marginalized communities access government skill development schemes like PM-DAKSH, which provides 100% funded training in high-demand sectors such as IT, healthcare, and construction. I’d like to take just a few minutes to understand your background and see if this scheme could support your career or livelihood goals — no obligation, just a conversation.

### Discovery Questions
1. To confirm, are you an Indian citizen belonging to one of the following communities: Scheduled Caste (SC), Other Backward Class (OBC), Safai Karamchari (including waste picker), Denotified Tribe (DNT), Sem Nomadic Tribe, or Manual Scavenger?
2. What is your current age? (The scheme supports applicants aged 18–45, with relaxations possible for certain categories.)
3. Are you currently employed, unemployed, or looking to upgrade your skills for better job opportunities or self-employment?
4. Have you previously undertaken any formal skill training or vocational education?
5. Which sectors are you interested in for training — such as IT, healthcare, hospitality, construction, or textiles?
6. Do you have access to your caste certificate, identity proof (like Aadhaar), and address proof for documentation?
7. Are you able to commit to attending a full-time training program for several weeks or months, as required by the course?
8. Would you be open to receiving placement support or self-employment assistance after completing training?

### Qualification Checklist (internal — don't read aloud)
- [ ] Belongs to SC, OBC, Safai Karamchari, DNT, Sem Nomadic Tribe, or Manual Scavenger community  
- [ ] Indian citizen  
- [ ] Age between 18 and 45 years (relaxation possible for eligible categories)  
- [ ] Seeking skill development for employability or self-employment  
- [ ] Interested in NSQF-aligned training in sectors like IT, healthcare, hospitality, construction, or textiles  
- [ ] Able to provide caste certificate, identity proof, address proof, age proof, and photographs  
- [ ] Willing to attend training through empaneled providers and comply with attendance requirements  
- [ ] Not currently enrolled in another government-funded skill training scheme (if applicable per guidelines)

### Transition to Pitch
Based on your responses, it looks like you may be eligible for the **PM-DAKSH scheme**, which offers **100% free skill development training** — including course fees, materials, and stipends where applicable — directly paid to accredited training providers. This could be a strong fit for your goals. May I share how the scheme works and how we’ve helped others like you access it?

## 2. Scheme Pitch (5 min)

### Structure: Problem → Scheme → Value → Proof → Next Step

**Problem framing:**
Many individuals from SC, OBC, and other marginalized communities face barriers to accessing quality skill training due to high costs, lack of information, or limited access to accredited providers. This limits their employability and income growth, especially in competitive job markets requiring certified skills.

**Scheme intro:**
The **PM-DAKSH scheme** (Pradhan Mantri Dakshta Aur Kushalta Sampann Hitgrahi), administered by the **Ministry of Social Justice and Empowerment, Government of India**, provides **100% financial support** for skill development training in sectors aligned with the National Skills Qualification Framework (NSQF), including IT, healthcare, hospitality, construction, and textiles. Benefits include free course material, stipends (where applicable), and post-training placement or self-employment support. There is **no direct cash transfer** — the government pays empaneled training providers upon successful completion and placement outcomes.

**Why they qualify (personalised):**
- You belong to one of the priority target groups: SC, OBC, Safai Karamchari, DNT, Sem Nomadic Tribe, or Manual Scavenger  
- You are within the eligible age range of 18–45 years (with possible relaxations)  
- You are seeking industry-relevant training to improve employability or pursue self-employment  
- You are able to provide the required documents: caste certificate, identity proof, address proof, age proof, and photographs

If eligible, we’ll guide you through the application, training center selection, and post-training support. Our fee is **success-based only** — we charge nothing unless you secure admission and begin training.
