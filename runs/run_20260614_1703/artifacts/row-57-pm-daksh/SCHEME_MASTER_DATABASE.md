### PM-DAKSH
- **Scheme ID:** SCH-001
- **Category:** Employment schemes
- **Geography:** Pan-India
- **Administering Body:** Ministry of Social Justice and Empowerment, Government of India
- **Portal / Application URL:** https://pmdaksh.dosje.gov.in
- **Current Status:** Rolling
- **Window:** Always Open

#### Eligibility Criteria
- Business type: Individuals (not businesses)
- Age of business: Not applicable (individual-focused scheme)
- Sector: IT, healthcare, hospitality, construction, textiles (aligned with NSQF)
- Revenue cap: Not applicable (individual-focused scheme)
- Employee count: Not applicable (individual-focused scheme)
- Other: Must belong to Scheduled Castes (SC), Other Backward Classes (OBC), Safai Karamcharis (including waste pickers), Denotified Tribes (DNT), Sem Nomadic Tribes, or Manual Scavengers; Indian citizen; age 18-45 years (relaxable for certain categories); priority to economically weaker sections within these communities

#### What It Offers
- Amount / Benefit: 100% financial support for training costs (course fees, training material, stipends where applicable); free skill development training; nationally recognized certificate upon completion; placement assistance or self-employment support
- Disbursement mode: Direct payment to empaneled training providers (reimbursement basis)
- Duration of benefit: One-time (covers full training duration until certification)

#### Documents Required
1. Caste Certificate (SC/OBC/DNT as applicable)
2. Identity Proof (Aadhaar Card, Voter ID, PAN, etc.)
3. Address Proof (Aadhaar, utility bill, ration card)
4. Age Proof (Birth Certificate, School Leaving Certificate)
5. Passport-sized Photographs
6. Mobile Number and Email ID for communication

#### Application Process (numbered steps)
1. Visit the official PM-DAKSH portal at https://pmdaksh.dosje.gov.in
2. Click on the 'Beneficiary Registration' or 'Apply Now' section
3. Fill in the online application form with personal, educational, and category details
4. Upload required documents such as caste certificate, identity proof, and address proof
5. Submit the application and await verification
6. Upon approval, select an approved training center and course
7. Attend training as per schedule
8. Complete assessment and receive certification
9. Avail placement support or self-employment assistance
