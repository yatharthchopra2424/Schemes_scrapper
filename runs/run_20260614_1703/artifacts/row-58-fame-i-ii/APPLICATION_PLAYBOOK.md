# Application Playbook

## How to Use This File
For each new client case, assign it a Case ID, pick the target scheme, and follow the steps below.

---

## Stage 1: Eligibility Verification (Day 1–3)

### Checklist
- [ ] Buyer type: Individual, institutional buyer, fleet operator, or service provider (as per FAME-II guidelines)
- [ ] Vehicle type: Electric two-wheeler, three-wheeler, four-wheeler (passenger car or LCV), or bus approved under FAME-II
- [ ] Vehicle model: Must be tested and approved by designated agencies (ARAI, ICAT, etc.) and registered on the FAME portal
- [ ] Purchase channel: Vehicle must be purchased from a dealer registered on the FAME portal
- [ ] Geographic scope: Pan-India (no state-specific restrictions)
- [ ] Technical compliance: Vehicle must meet specified technical standards under FAME-II
- [ ] Beneficiary identification: Aadhaar card required for individual beneficiaries
- [ ] Payment proof: Valid invoice and proof of payment must be available
- [ ] Bank details: Beneficiary’s bank account must be provided for DBT (if applicable)
- [ ] Manufacturer compliance: FAME compliance certificate from manufacturer must be obtainable
- [ ] Application timing: Apply within stipulated time from purchase (as per portal guidelines; verify current SLA)
- [ ] Exclusions: Hybrid vehicles only eligible if specifically approved under FAME-II (verify model listing)

> **Sources**: Application Portal: https://fame.gov.in/ | Key Facts: Eligibility, Target Beneficiaries, Required Documents, Application Process

## Stage 2: Document Collection (Day 3–10)

### Universal Documents (always required)
- [ ] PAN card of beneficiary/applicant
- [ ] Aadhaar card of beneficiary (mandatory for individual buyers)
- [ ] Bank account details (cancelled cheque or bank passbook) for DBT
- [ ] Udyam Registration (if applicant is a manufacturer/dealer/MSME)
- [ ] GSTIN certificate (if applicable)
- [ ] Latest ITR (for institutional buyers/fleet operators, if required for verification)
- [ ] Business registration proof (Partnership deed, COI, LLP agreement, etc., for non-individual applicants)
- [ ] Address proof (utility bill, rental agreement, etc.)

### Scheme-Specific Documents
- [ ] Vehicle invoice showing chassis number, model, and ex-showroom price
- [ ] Registration Certificate (RC) of the vehicle
- [ ] Proof of purchase and payment (payment receipt, bank transaction proof)
- [ ] FAME compliance certificate from the manufacturer (mandatory)
- [ ] Copy of vehicle’s approval/test report from designated agency (ARAI/ICAT/etc.)
- [ ] Dealer registration proof on FAME portal (to be verified by consultant; dealer must be FAME-registered)

### Document Quality Check
- [ ] All documents are legible, scanned in color (if original is color), and in PDF format
- [ ] File size does not exceed 2 MB per document; compress if necessary without losing clarity
- [ ] File names follow format: `CaseID_DocumentType_VehicleChassisNumber.pdf` (e.g., `CASE001_Invoice_MA1XYZ123.pdf`)
- [ ] Names on all documents (especially Aadhaar, PAN, bank) match exactly as per government records
- [ ] Non-English documents accompanied by certified Hindi/English translation
- [ ] Bank account details include IFSC, account number, and beneficiary name matching PAN/Aadhaar
- [ ] Vehicle invoice and RC show matching chassis number, engine number (if applicable), and model
- [ ] FAME compliance certificate is issued by manufacturer and references the specific vehicle model approved on FAME portal

## Stage 3: Application Drafting (Day 10–18)

### Project Report / DPR (if required)
Not required for end-user benefit under FAME-II. The incentive is processed at the point of sale based on vehicle sale upload by dealer/manufacturer.  
**However**, if the client is a manufacturer or dealer seeking model approval or charging infrastructure support, a Detailed Project Report (DPR) may be needed — **verify with nodal officer** for such cases.

### Application Form Filling Protocol
1. **Verify dealer/manufacturer is FAME-registered**: Confirm the selling dealer has uploaded the vehicle model to the FAME portal before sale.
2. **Match chassis number exactly**: Ensure the chassis number on invoice, RC, and portal entry is identical (no variations).
3. **Select correct vehicle category**: Choose the right segment (2W, 3W, 4W-car, 4W-LCV, bus) as incentive rates differ (₹15k/kWh for 2W, ₹10k/kWh for 3W/4W-car/LCV, ₹20k/kWh for bus).
4. **Enter battery capacity accurately**: Incentive is per kWh; use certified battery capacity from test report (ARAI/ICAT).
5. **Apply cost caps**: For 4W cars, incentive capped at ₹1.5 lakh; for LCVs, ₹1 lakh; for 2W, max 40% of vehicle cost.
6. **Beneficiary details**: Enter Aadhaar number (individuals) or official ID (institutions) exactly as per documents.
7. **Bank details for DBT**: If applicable, enter correct account number and IFSC; mismatch leads to rejection.
8. **Declaration**: Ensure dealer/beneficiary declares that benefit is passed to end consumer and no double claiming occurs.

> **Portal Note**: Application is primarily dealer/manufacturer-driven via https://fame.gov.in/. End beneficiaries typically do not fill forms directly; ensure dealer uploads sale with correct beneficiary and vehicle details.

### Internal Review Gate
- [ ] All vehicle details (model, chassis, battery kW) match across invoice, RC, test report, and FAME portal listing
- [ ] Incentive amount calculated correctly per kWh and category-specific caps applied
- [ ] Beneficiary name and ID (Aadhaar/PAN) consistent on all documents
- [ ] Bank account details verified (name, number, IFSC) and match beneficiary
- [ ] FAME compliance certificate is valid and issued by manufacturer
- [ ] Dealer is confirmed registered on FAME portal (check portal dealer list)
- [ ] Proof of payment shows transaction to dealer for the exact vehicle
- [ ] Declarations signed (if form requires) by authorized signatory
- [ ] No duplicate application suspected (check chassis number not used before)

## Stage 4: Submission & Follow-Up (Day 18–30)

### Submission Protocol
- [ ] Confirm dealer/manufacturer has uploaded sale details on FAME portal (https://fame.gov.in/) with correct beneficiary and vehicle data
- [ ] Obtain and screenshot the submission acknowledgment/reference number from the portal
- [ ] Store reference number in case file with format: `FAME-[VehicleChassisNumber]-[YYYYMMDD]`
- [ ] Set automated follow-up reminders: Day 3, Day 7, Day 15, Day 30 post-submission
- [ ] Monitor portal status regularly for "Under Verification", "Approved", or "Rejected" flags
- [ ] Maintain log of all communications with dealer/portal/helpdesk

## Stage 5: Post-Approval (Day 30–90+)

### Approval Actions
- [ ] Obtain and verify approval letter from FAME portal or dealer (showing sanctioned incentive amount)
- [ ] Confirm incentive amount matches eligibility (kWh rate × battery capacity, subject to category caps)
- [ ] Verify that benefit has been passed to end consumer via reduced invoice price (if client is buyer)
- [ ] If client is manufacturer/dealer: confirm receipt of incentive amount in bank account
- [ ] Monitor utilization: Ensure no resale or misuse of vehicle within lock-in period (if any; verify current guidelines)
- [ ] Prepare for potential audit: Retain all documents (invoice, RC, approval, bank credit) for minimum 3 years
- [ ] If applicable, assist client in claiming any charging infrastructure subsidy under FAME-II (separate process)
- [ ] Close case only after successful disbursement and client confirmation of benefit realization

> **Sources**: Application Portal: https://fame.gov.in/ | Key Facts: Benefits, Financial Support, Application Process, Contact Details, Caveats
