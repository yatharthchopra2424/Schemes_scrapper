## 1. Executive Summary
Based on our initial review of [Client]'s business profile, we have identified FAME I & II — a subsidy scheme administered by the Department of Heavy Industries, Ministry of Heavy Industries and Public Enterprises — for which your business appears eligible. The potential benefit is up to ₹20,000 per kWh for buses, ₹10,000 per kWh for four-wheelers (capped at ₹1.5 lakh for cars and ₹1 lakh for LCVs), and up to ₹15,000 per kWh for two-wheelers (capped at 40% of vehicle cost).

## 3. Our Scope of Work
1. Initial eligibility assessment of your business and vehicle models against FAME I & II requirements  
2. Guidance on obtaining necessary testing and approvals from designated agencies (e.g., ARAI, ICAT)  
3. Support with registration of approved vehicle models on the FAME portal (https://fame.gov.in/)  
4. Assistance in compiling and verifying required documents per scheme checklist  
5. Liaison with dealers/manufacturers for accurate upload of sale details on the FAME portal  
6. Monitoring of claim verification and incentive disbursement by the Department of Heavy Industries  
7. Ensuring end-consumer benefit realization through reduced purchase price at point of sale
