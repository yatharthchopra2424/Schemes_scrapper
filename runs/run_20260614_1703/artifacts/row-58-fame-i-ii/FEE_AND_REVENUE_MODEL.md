## Pricing Principles
1. **Value-Aligned Fees**: Success fees are calibrated to the actual incentive value received by clients under FAME II, ensuring our compensation scales with the financial benefit delivered (e.g., up to ₹1.5 lakh for electric cars, ₹10,000/kWh for buses).
2. **Risk-Adjusted Pricing**: Given the 3–6 month processing lag for incentive disbursement (evidenced by the multi-step verification via FAME portal and DHI), retainers cover initial effort while success fees are paid only upon confirmed fund transfer to manufacturer/dealer.
3. **Tiered Accessibility**: The scheme’s applicability across vehicle segments (two-wheelers to buses) and buyer types (individuals, fleets, institutions) allows our pricing tiers to serve MSMEs (e.g., EV dealers, fleet operators) up to large OEMs and charging infrastructure providers.
4. **Outcome-Focused Engagement**: Fees are structured to incentivize successful claim submission and tracking, with no success fee payable if incentives are not disbursed due to eligibility gaps or portal rejections — aligning our interests with client outcomes.
