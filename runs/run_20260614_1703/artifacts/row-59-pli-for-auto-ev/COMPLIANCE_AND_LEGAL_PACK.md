## 8D. Internal Data Handling Policy

All client data related to scheme consulting engagements shall be handled in accordance with the following standards:

- **Storage:** Encrypted cloud storage (AES-256 encryption at rest, TLS 1.2+ in transit).
- **Access Control:** Role-based access; limited to assigned consultants and supervisors only.
- **Authentication:** Mandatory two-factor authentication (2FA) for all system access.
- **Device Policy:** No personal devices permitted for accessing or storing client data; all work must be conducted on firm-issued, managed devices.
- **Retention:** Client data retained for two (2) years post case closure, after which it is securely deleted.
- **Breach Notification:** In the event of a data breach, affected clients and relevant authorities will be notified within 24 hours of confirmation.

**Scheme-Specific Note:** All documents submitted to Ministry of Heavy Industries under PLI for Auto & EV must be retained for a minimum of 3 years for audit purposes.
