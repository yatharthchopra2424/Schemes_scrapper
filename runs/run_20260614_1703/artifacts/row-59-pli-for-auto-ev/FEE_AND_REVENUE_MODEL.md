## Pricing Principles
1. **Value-aligned pricing**: Fees are structured to reflect the substantial financial benefits available under the PLI for Auto & EV scheme, which offers significant incentives tied to incremental sales and investment.
2. **Risk-adjusted terms**: Success fees reflect the scheme’s moderate risk profile — while the PLI for Auto & EV has demonstrated success in attracting large investments, processing times can be lengthy due to rigorous verification of sales and capital expenditure claims, requiring diligent documentation and follow-up.
3. **Transparency**: All fees and payment terms are clearly communicated upfront, with retainers covering initial assessment and application preparation, and success fees contingent only on actual approval of incentives.
4. **Scalability**: The model accommodates firms across the eligibility spectrum, with tailored tiers for mid-market and large enterprises that are the primary beneficiaries of this scheme, as evidenced by its focus on boosting manufacturing in automobiles and electric vehicles.
