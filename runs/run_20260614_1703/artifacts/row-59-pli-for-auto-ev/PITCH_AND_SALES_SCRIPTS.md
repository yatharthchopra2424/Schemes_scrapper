# Pitch & Sales Scripts

## Philosophy
We are advisors, not salespeople. We diagnose first. We only pitch a scheme if it fits the client. We never oversell success rates.

---

## 1. Discovery Call Script (First Contact — 20 min)

### Opening (2 min)
Good morning/afternoon. I specialise in helping auto and electric vehicle manufacturers access government incentives designed to boost domestic production and innovation in the sector. The PLI for Auto & EV scheme is specifically structured to support companies investing in advanced automotive and EV manufacturing — so I’d like to understand if this could be relevant for your operations.

### Discovery Questions
- When was your company incorporated, and what is your primary manufacturing focus — auto components, vehicles, or EV-specific products?
- Are you currently undertaking or planning new investments in manufacturing facilities for automobiles or electric vehicles?
- What is your annual turnover from auto or EV manufacturing activities?
- Do you have plans to expand production capacity or localise supply chains for auto/EV components?
- Are you seeking financial support to offset capital expenditure for setting up or upgrading manufacturing units?
- Is your business engaged in the production of goods covered under the PLI for Auto & EV scheme’s target sectors?
- Have you previously applied for any central government production-linked incentive schemes?
- What is your timeline for making investment decisions related to manufacturing expansion or technology upgradation?

### Qualification Checklist (internal — don't read aloud)
- [ ] Company engaged in manufacturing of automobiles or electric vehicles
- [ ] Investment in eligible auto/EV manufacturing activities (as per scheme guidelines)
- [ ] Willingness to commit to incremental sales and investment thresholds
- [ ] Not availing benefits under any other central government PLI scheme for the same product
- [ ] Capable of meeting documentation and reporting requirements under the scheme
- [ ] Operating within the validity period of the scheme (as per notified timelines)

### Transition to Pitch
Based on what you’ve shared, it sounds like your auto/EV manufacturing expansion plans could align well with the PLI for Auto & EV scheme — which offers financial incentives on incremental sales from eligible investments. Let me walk you through how it works and whether you’d qualify.

## 2. Scheme Pitch (5 min)

### Structure: Problem → Scheme → Value → Proof → Next Step

**Problem framing:**
Auto and EV manufacturers face high upfront capital costs when scaling production or adopting new technologies, which can delay expansion and reduce competitiveness against imported alternatives. Many struggle to access timely financial support that offsets these risks without diluting equity.

**Scheme intro:**
The Production Linked Incentive (PLI) Scheme for Auto & EV, administered by the Ministry of Heavy Industries, provides financial incentives of up to 18% on incremental sales of eligible automobiles and electric vehicles over a five-year period, based on qualifying investments in manufacturing.

**Why they qualify (personalised):**
- Engaged in manufacturing of automobiles or electric vehicles as defined under the scheme
- Planning or undertaking new investment in eligible manufacturing facilities
- Committed to achieving incremental sales growth from the invested capacity
- Not currently receiving benefits under another central government PLI scheme for the same product
