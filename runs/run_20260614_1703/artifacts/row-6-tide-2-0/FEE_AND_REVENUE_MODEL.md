## Pricing Principles
1. **Value-Based Alignment**: Fees are structured to reflect the actual financial benefit delivered to clients under TIDE 2.0, which provides grants up to ₹7 Lakh for prototype development and investment support up to ₹40 Lakh for product development and market outreach, ensuring consulting fees remain proportional to the scheme’s impact.
2. **Risk-Adjusted Pricing**: Success fees account for the scheme’s processing time and documentation intensity — requiring Utilization Certificates, Technical & Financial Progress Reports, Audited Statements, and PRSG Recommendations — which typically extend approval timelines to 3–6 months, justifying staggered fee realization.
3. **Tiered Accessibility**: The retainer-success fee hybrid model lowers barriers for early-stage startups (eligible under EiR and Grant tiers) while ensuring sustainable revenue for consultants supporting scaling ventures accessing the ₹40 Lakh investment component.
4. **Scheme-Specific Note**: TIDE 2.0 has a moderate risk profile due to its structured, multi-stage disbursement process and reliance on incubator intermediation; however, evidence shows consistent fund disbursement (₹34.04 crore to 51 TIDE 2.0 Centers) and strong startup outcomes (e.g., BrainSightAI raising $5M, PierSight Space securing $6M), supporting predictable success fee realization upon milestone completion.
