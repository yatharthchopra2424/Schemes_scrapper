# Pitch & Sales Scripts

## Philosophy
We are advisors, not salespeople. We diagnose first. We only pitch a scheme if it fits the client. We never oversell success rates.

---

## 1. Discovery Call Script (First Contact — 20 min)

### Opening (2 min)
Good morning/afternoon. I’m calling from MeitY Startup Hub, the government’s dedicated platform for nurturing deep-tech startups in India. We work specifically with incubators and startups focused on emerging technologies like IoT, AI, blockchain, and robotics to solve societal challenges in healthcare, education, agriculture, and more. I’d love to understand your current work and see if TIDE 2.0 could support your mission.

### Discovery Questions
1. What is the incorporation date of your institution or incubation centre?  
2. Do you have established linkages with large companies, industry bodies, or industry clusters for mentorship and business infusion?  
3. Are you currently supporting ICT startups using emerging technologies such as IoT, AI, blockchain, or robotics?  
4. Which of the seven pre-identified societal relevance areas (healthcare, education, agriculture, financial inclusion, infrastructure, environment, clean energy) do your incubated startups focus on?  
5. Do you have the infrastructure and capacity to host Entrepreneurs-in-Residence (EiR), provide grants for prototype development, or support investment for product development and market outreach?  
6. Are you able to submit Utilization Certificates, Technical & Financial Progress Reports, audited accounts, and PRSG recommendations as part of the funding process?  
7. Have you executed an MoU with partnering institutes or have a Surety Bond in place for grant utilization?  
8. What is your current capacity to handhold tech startups — are you aiming to support approximately 2000 over five years as per the scheme’s target?

### Qualification Checklist (internal — don't read aloud)
- [ ] Institution is an institute of higher learning or R&D organisation  
- [ ] Has incubation facilities  
- [ ] Has established linkages with large companies, industry bodies, or industry clusters  
- [ ] Supports ICT startups using emerging technologies (IoT, AI, blockchain, robotics, etc.)  
- [ ] Focuses on at least one of the seven pre-identified societal relevance areas  
- [ ] Capable of provisioning EiRs (up to ₹4 Lakh), grants (up to ₹7 Lakh), or investments (up to ₹40 Lakh)  
- [ ] Able to submit Utilization Certificate, Technical & Financial Progress Report, audited accounts, and PRSG recommendations  
- [ ] Willing to execute MoU and submit Surety Bond  
- [ ] Aligns with the goal of handholding approximately 2000 tech startups over five years  

### Transition to Pitch
Based on your responses, it sounds like your centre is well-positioned to benefit from TIDE 2.0 — the MeitY scheme that provides grants of up to ₹7 Lakh for prototype development and investment support of up to ₹40 Lakh for market readiness through 51 approved incubation centres across India.

**Problem framing:**
Many incubation centres struggle to provide adequate financial and technical support to deep-tech startups working on societal challenges in emerging technologies, limiting their ability to scale innovations from idea to market.

**Scheme intro:**
Technology Incubation and Development of Entrepreneurs (TIDE 2.0), administered by the Innovation and IPR Division of MeitY in association with 51 incubation centres, is a grant-based scheme offering financial support to incubators: Entrepreneurs-in-Residence (EiR) up to ₹4 Lakh for idea to PoC, Grants up to ₹7 Lakh for prototype development, and Investment support up to ₹40 Lakh for product development and market outreach.
