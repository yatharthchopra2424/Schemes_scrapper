### TIDE 2.0
- **Scheme ID:** SCH-001
- **Category:** Grant
- **Geography:** Pan-India
- **Administering Body:** Innovation and IPR Division, MeitY in association with 51 incubation centres from institutes of higher learning and R&D organisations
- **Portal / Application URL:** https://msh.meity.gov.in/schemes/tide
- **Current Status:** Closed
- **Window:** 01 February, 2019 to 31st January, 2024

#### Eligibility Criteria
- Business type: Incubation Centres (institutions of higher learning and R&D organisations having incubation facilities and established linkages with large companies, industry bodies, industry clusters)
- Age of business: Not specified
- Sector: ICT startups using emerging technologies such as IoT, AI, Block-chain, Robotics etc. in seven pre-identified areas of societal relevance (Healthcare, Education, Agriculture, Financial inclusion including digital payments, Infrastructure and transportation, Environment and clean tech, Clean energy solutions)
- Revenue cap: Not specified
- Employee count: Not specified
- Other: Proposals to be obtained from institutions of higher learning and R&D organisations having incubation facilities and established linkages with large companies, industry bodies, industry clusters so as to provide necessary mentorship and possible infusion of innovative businesses into their larger business operations

#### What It Offers
- Amount / Benefit: Entrepreneurs-in-residence (EiR): upto Rs. 4 Lakh, Grants: for prototype development upto Rs. 7 Lakh, Investment: for product development and market outreach upto Rs. 40 Lakh
- Disbursement mode: Direct credit (based on submission of Utilization Certificate, Technical & Financial Progress Report, Audited statement of accounts, and Recommendations of the PRSG)
- Duration of benefit: One-time (per stage: EiR, Grant, Investment)

#### Documents Required
1. MoU executed by Implementing Agencies/Partnering Institutes
2. Surety Bond by Implementing Agencies
3. Utilization Certificate of Previous release
4. Technical & Financial Progress Report
5. Audited statement of accounts
6. Recommendations of the PRSG

#### Application Process (numbered steps)
1. Proposals from 51 TIDE 2.0 Incubation Centres are to be obtained from institutions of higher learning and R&D organisations having incubation facilities and established linkages with large companies, industry bodies, industry clusters.
2. MoU to be executed by Implementing Agencies/Partnering Institutes.
3. Submission of Surety Bond by Implementing Agencies.
4. Submission of Utilization Certificate of Previous release.
5. Technical & Financial Progress Report.
6. Audited statement of accounts.
7. Recommendations of the PRSG.
