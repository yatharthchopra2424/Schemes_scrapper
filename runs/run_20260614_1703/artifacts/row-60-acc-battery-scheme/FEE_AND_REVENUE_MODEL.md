## Pricing Principles
1. **Value-Aligned Fees**: Success fees are calibrated to the actual financial benefit delivered under the ACC Battery Scheme, ensuring clients pay only for realized outcomes.
2. **Risk-Adjusted Pricing**: Given the scheme’s low confidence rating and processing uncertainties observed in similar PLI-linked incentives, retainer fees cover preliminary eligibility and documentation work, while success fees are contingent on final approval.
3. **Tiered Accessibility**: The scheme targets mid-to-large scale battery manufacturing projects (typically > ₹1Cr investment), making the Scale and Enterprise tiers most relevant; Starter and Growth tiers are generally ineligible due to high capital requirements.
4. **Transparency & Predictability**: All fee structures are disclosed upfront, with retainers billed monthly and success fees invoiced only upon formal approval from the Ministry of Heavy Industries, minimizing client financial risk.
