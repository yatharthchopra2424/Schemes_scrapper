# Pitch & Sales Scripts

## Philosophy
We are advisors, not salespeople. We diagnose first. We only pitch a scheme if it fits the client. We never oversell success rates.

---

## 1. Discovery Call Script (First Contact — 20 min)

### Opening (2 min)
Good morning/afternoon. I specialise in helping battery manufacturing and energy storage companies access government incentives for capital investment. The ACC Battery Scheme is designed specifically to support domestic production of advanced chemistry cells, so I wanted to understand if your operations might align with its goals.

### Discovery Questions
1. When was your battery manufacturing or energy storage business incorporated or commenced commercial operations?
2. Are you currently engaged in the manufacturing of advanced chemistry cells (such as lithium-ion) for electric vehicles or energy storage systems?
3. What is your current annual turnover from battery-related activities?
4. Have you made or are you planning capital investments in plant and machinery for battery production?
5. Is your facility located in India, and do you have plans to expand or set up new production capacity?
6. Are you seeking financial support to offset the cost of setting up or expanding your battery manufacturing unit?
7. Do you have the necessary clearances and approvals for your battery manufacturing project?
8. Are you currently availing of any other central or state government subsidies for the same investment?

### Qualification Checklist (internal — don't read aloud)
- [ ] Entity engaged in manufacturing of advanced chemistry cells (ACC)
- [ ] Project involves setting up of ACC manufacturing facility in India
- [ ] Minimum investment threshold met (as per scheme guidelines)
- [ ] Entity is not availing of similar subsidy for the same investment
- [ ] Commitment to domestic value addition and technology deployment
- [ ] Compliance with performance benchmarks and localisation requirements
- [ ] Valid clearances and approvals for the project in place
- [ ] Incorporated under Companies Act, 2013 or relevant statute

### Transition to Pitch
Based on your responses, it appears your battery manufacturing initiative may align with the ACC Battery Scheme’s objective of boosting domestic ACC production. The scheme offers financial incentives to offset capital expenditure, and I’d like to outline how it could support your expansion plans.

## 2. Scheme Pitch (5 min)

### Structure: Problem → Scheme → Value → Proof → Next Step

**Problem framing:**
Battery manufacturers face high upfront capital costs for setting up advanced chemistry cell production facilities, which can hinder scaling and domestic value addition in the energy storage sector.

**Scheme intro:**
The ACC Battery Scheme, administered by the Ministry of Heavy Industries, provides financial support in the form of a production-linked incentive to eligible manufacturers of advanced chemistry cells (ACC) for setting up manufacturing facilities in India.

**Why they qualify (personalised):**
- Engaged in manufacturing of advanced chemistry cells (e.g., lithium-ion) for EVs or energy storage
- Setting up or expanding ACC manufacturing facility in India with minimum threshold investment
- Not availing of similar central/state subsidy for the same investment
- Committed to meeting localisation and domestic value addition requirements under the scheme
