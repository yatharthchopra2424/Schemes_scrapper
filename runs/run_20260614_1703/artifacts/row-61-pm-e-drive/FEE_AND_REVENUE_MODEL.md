## Pricing Principles
1. Retainer covers initial eligibility assessment, documentation preparation, and application submission support.
2. Success fee is contingent on successful claim approval and disbursement from MHI.
3. Fees are structured to align with client outcomes and reduce upfront financial burden.
4. Scheme-specific note: PM E-DRIVE has a moderate risk profile due to its 120-day claim submission window and high success rate for compliant OEMs, as evidenced by the operational guidelines requiring undertakings and PMP compliance for disbursement.
