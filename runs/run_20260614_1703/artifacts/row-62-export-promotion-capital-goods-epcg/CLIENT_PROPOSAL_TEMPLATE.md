## 1. Executive Summary
Based on our initial review of [Client]'s business profile, we have identified Export Promotion Capital Goods (EPCG) — a subsidy scheme administered by Directorate General of Foreign Trade (DGFT), Ministry of Commerce and Industry — for which your business appears eligible. The potential benefit is zero customs duty on import of capital goods for pre-production, production, and post-production, along with IGST and Compensation Cess exemption up to 31.03.2020.

## 3. Our Scope of Work
1. Initial eligibility assessment and scheme suitability analysis  
2. Document preparation and compilation as per Chapter 5 of Foreign Trade Policy and Handbook of Procedures  
3. IEC validation and application submission via ANF-5A form  
4. Portal registration and application tracking on [https://dgft.gov.in/CP/?opt=epcg](https://dgft.gov.in/CP/?opt=epcg)  
5. Coordination with DGFT for amendments, revalidation, or closure if required  
6. Assistance in obtaining installation certificate and license status verification
