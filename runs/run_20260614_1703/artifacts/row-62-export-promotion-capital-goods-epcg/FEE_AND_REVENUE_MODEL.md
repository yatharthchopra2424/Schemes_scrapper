## Pricing Principles
1. Value-based pricing aligned with the duty savings and customs duty exemption benefits under the EPCG scheme.
2. Transparent fee structure with no hidden charges; retainer covers advisory and documentation efforts.
3. Success fee contingent upon successful authorization and realization of export obligation benefits.
4. Scheme-specific note: The EPCG scheme involves a moderate risk profile due to the export obligation (EO) compliance requirement and processing time typically ranging from 3–6 months for authorization, as evidenced by the rolling application basis and procedural steps outlined in the DGFT portal. Success is highly probable with proper documentation, but delays in EO fulfilment can affect fee realization.
