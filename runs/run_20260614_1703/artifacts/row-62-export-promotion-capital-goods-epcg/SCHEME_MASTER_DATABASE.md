### Export Promotion Capital Goods (EPCG)
- **Scheme ID:** SCH-001
- **Category:** Subsidy
- **Geography:** Pan-India
- **Administering Body:** Directorate General of Foreign Trade (DGFT), Ministry of Commerce and Industry
- **Portal / Application URL:** https://dgft.gov.in/CP/?opt=epcg
- **Current Status:** Rolling
- **Window:** Always Open

#### Eligibility Criteria
- Business type: Manufacturer exporters; merchant exporters; service providers
- Age of business: Not specified
- Sector: Not specified
- Revenue cap: Not specified
- Employee count: Not specified
- Other: IEC (Importer Exporter Code) is required; other pre-requisites as mentioned in Chapter 5 of Foreign Trade Policy and Handbook of Procedures

#### What It Offers
- Amount / Benefit: Zero customs duty on import of capital goods for pre-production, production, and post-production; exemption from IGST and Compensation Cess up to 31.03.2020
- Disbursement mode: Duty exemption (customs duty waiver)
- Duration of benefit: One-time per authorization; IGST and Compensation Cess exemption available only up to 31.03.2020

#### Documents Required
1. IEC (Importer Exporter Code)
2. ANF-5A application form
3. Documents as per Chapter 5 of Foreign Trade Policy and Handbook of Procedures

#### Application Process (numbered steps)
1. Ensure you have an IEC (Importer Exporter Code).
2. Refer to Chapter 5 of Foreign Trade Policy and Handbook of Procedures for pre-requisites.
3. Apply for EPCG via ANF-5A form.
4. Access services such as amendment, invalidation, revalidation, clubbing, closure, installation certificate, license status, EO/block extension, authorisation transmission, manual EODC status update, EPCG committee request, review application, transfer of authorisation, annual reporting of EO fulfilment, and help/FAQs through the DGFT portal.
