## 1. Executive Summary
Based on our initial review of [Client]'s business profile, we have identified the Market Access Initiative (MAI) — a trade promotion scheme administered by the Ministry of Commerce & Industry — for which your business appears eligible. The potential benefit under this scheme is subject to project-specific approval and is not fixed; however, MAI provides financial assistance for eligible market development activities. The scheme supports export promotion initiatives through reimbursement of approved expenses.
