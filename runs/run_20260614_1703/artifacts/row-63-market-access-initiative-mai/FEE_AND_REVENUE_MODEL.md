## Pricing Principles
1. **Value-Aligned Fees**: Success fees are calibrated to the actual financial benefit delivered under the Market Access Initiative (MAI), ensuring clients pay only for realized export growth support.
2. **Risk-Adjusted Retainers**: Retainer amounts reflect the scheme’s moderate administrative complexity and reliance on accurate documentation of international trade activities (e.g., participation in approved trade fairs, B2B meetings, and market studies).
3. **Transparency in Lag**: Given the scheme’s processing time (typically 4–6 months from application to disbursement, as evidenced by government trade promotion timelines), revenue recognition is aligned with approval cycles to avoid cash flow mismatches.
4. **Scalability Across Tiers**: The MAI supports exporters across MSMEs to large enterprises, allowing our firm to apply a tiered pricing model where higher-turnover clients benefit from lower success fee rates due to volume and repeat engagement potential.

## Invoice Templates

### Retainer Invoice
**Description**: Monthly retainer for consulting services related to the Market Access Initiative (MAI) scheme, including eligibility assessment, documentation preparation, application submission, and liaison with the Department of Commerce, Ministry of Commerce & Industry.

### Success Fee Invoice
**Description**: Success fee for successful approval under the Market Access Initiative (MAI) scheme, administered by the Department of Commerce, Ministry of Commerce & Industry, Government of India. Fee is calculated as a percentage of the approved grant amount for export promotion activities such as participation in international trade fairs, B2B meetings, and market research.
