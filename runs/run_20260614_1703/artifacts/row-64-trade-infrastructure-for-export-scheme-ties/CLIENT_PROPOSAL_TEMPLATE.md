## 3. Our Scope of Work
1. Initial eligibility assessment for TIES based on business profile and export infrastructure plans  
2. Document preparation and review as per scheme requirements  
3. Portal registration and application submission on [https://commerce.gov.in/ties](https://commerce.gov.in/ties)  
4. Liaison with the Ministry of Commerce & Industry for application tracking and query resolution  
5. Support in obtaining and verifying required certifications and approvals  
6. Assistance in preparing and submitting the Detailed Project Report (DPR), which is required for TIES  
7. Post-submission follow-up until sanction or final outcome  

## 4. What We Need From You
1. Documents as per the TIES checklist, including but not limited to: incorporation documents, export performance details, land ownership/lease documents, project cost estimates, and financing arrangements  
2. Designated point of contact from your team for coordination and document sharing  
3. Response to queries within 2 business days to ensure timely progress
