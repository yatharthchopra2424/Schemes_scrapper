## 8D. Internal Data Handling Policy

(Standard data handling policy — encrypted cloud storage, 2FA, limited access, no personal devices,
2-year retention post case closure, 24-hour breach notification.)

All client data shall be handled in accordance with the following security protocols:

- **Storage:** All files stored in encrypted cloud storage (AES-256 encryption at rest, TLS 1.3 in transit).
- **Access Control:** Role-based access enforced; multi-factor authentication (2FA) required for all
  system access.
- **Device Policy:** No personal devices permitted for accessing or storing client data.
- **Access Logging:** All access to client data is logged and monitored for anomalous activity.
- **Breach Response:** In the event of a data breach, affected parties and relevant authorities will be
  notified within 24 hours of confirmation.
- **Retention:** Client data retained for two (2) years post case closure, after which it is securely
  deleted unless legal hold applies.
- **Third-Party Sharing:** No sharing with third parties except as required for scheme applications
  submitted to Ministry of Commerce & Industry.

**Scheme-Specific Note:** All documents submitted to Ministry of Commerce & Industry under Trade Infrastructure for Export Scheme (TIES)
must be retained for a minimum of 3 years for audit purposes. (Note: As scheme-specific retention
guidelines were not explicitly detailed in the evidence, the default audit retention period of 3 years
is applied per standard government audit practices in India.)
