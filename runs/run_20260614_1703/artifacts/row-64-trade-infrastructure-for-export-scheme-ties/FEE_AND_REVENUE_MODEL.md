## Pricing Principles
1. **Value Alignment**: Fees are structured to reflect the substantial financial benefit delivered under TIES, which supports export-linked infrastructure projects with significant capital outlay.
2. **Risk Sharing**: Success fees are contingent on successful project approval and disbursement, aligning consultant incentives with client outcomes.
3. **Transparency**: All fee components are clearly communicated upfront, with retainers covering initial feasibility and application preparation.
4. **Scheme-Specific Note**: Given the scheme’s focus on large-scale export infrastructure (e.g., ports, railways, warehousing), processing times are typically long (6–18 months), and success depends on technical viability and inter-ministerial coordination. Evidence indicates low confidence in automated data extraction, suggesting complex eligibility and documentation requirements, which increases engagement risk but also justifies premium fees for expert navigation.
