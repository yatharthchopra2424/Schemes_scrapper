## 8D. Internal Data Handling Policy

All client data related to scheme consulting engagements shall be handled in accordance with the following standards:

- **Storage:** Encrypted cloud storage (AES-256 encryption) with access restricted to authorized personnel only.
- **Access Control:** Role-based access controls enforced; multi-factor authentication (2FA) mandatory for all system access.
- **Device Policy:** No personal devices permitted for accessing or storing client data; all work must be conducted on firm-issued, secured devices.
- **Retention Period:** Client data retained for a minimum of two (2) years post case closure, after which it is securely deleted unless longer retention is required by law or scheme-specific guidelines.
- **Breach Notification:** In the event of a data breach, affected clients and relevant authorities will be notified within 24 hours of discovery.
- **Audit Logs:** Comprehensive access and modification logs maintained for all client data files.

**Scheme-specific note:** All documents submitted to Directorate General of Foreign Trade (DGFT) under Remission of Duties and Taxes on Exported Products (RoDTEP) must be retained for a minimum of 3 years for audit purposes, as per scheme guidelines from evidence.
