## Pricing Principles
1. **Value-Based Alignment**: Fees are structured to reflect the actual financial benefit delivered under RoDTEP, which provides remission of duties and taxes on exported products as a percentage of FOB value, ensuring client success directly correlates with consultant compensation.
2. **Risk-Adjusted Pricing**: Success fees account for scheme-specific risks including variable processing times (typically 3–6 months for RoDTEP claims, per industry feedback) and dependency on accurate documentation of export realization and duty incidence.
3. **Tiered Accessibility**: The model accommodates exporters across scales — from MSMEs benefiting from incremental duty remission to large enterprises with complex global supply chains — ensuring broad scheme applicability.
4. **Transparency & Compliance**: All fees are disclosed upfront and structured to comply with ethical consulting standards, with no hidden charges; success fees are payable only upon actual receipt of RoDTEP credit in the exporter’s ledger.

## Invoice Templates

### Retainer Invoice
**Description**: Monthly retainer for advisory services related to the Remission of Duties and Taxes on Exported Products (RoDTEP) scheme, including eligibility assessment, documentation preparation, and claim submission support under the Directorate General of Foreign Trade (DGFT).

### Success Fee Invoice
**Description**: Success fee for successful realization of RoDTEP benefits, calculated as a percentage of the approved duty remission amount credited to the exporter’s account by the implementing agency, Directorate General of Foreign Trade (DGFT), Ministry of Commerce & Industry.
