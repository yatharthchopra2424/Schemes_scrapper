1. **Storage:** All client data shall be stored in encrypted cloud storage with access controls.
2. **Authentication:** Two-factor authentication (2FA) is required for all system access.
3. **Access Control:** Access to client data is limited to authorized personnel only on a need-to-know basis.
4. **Device Policy:** No personal devices may be used to access or store client data.
5. **Retention:** Client data shall be retained for a period of two (2) years post case closure, except as noted below.
6. **Breach Notification:** In the event of a data breach, notification shall be made to affected clients within 24 hours.
7. **Scheme-specific note:** All documents submitted to Department of Social Justice and Empowerment, Ministry of Social Justice and Empowerment, Government of India under Pradhan Mantri Anusuchit Jaati Abhyuday Yojana (PM-AJAY) must be retained for a minimum of 3 years for audit purposes.
8. **Disposal:** After the retention period, data shall be securely deleted or destroyed in accordance with data protection standards.
