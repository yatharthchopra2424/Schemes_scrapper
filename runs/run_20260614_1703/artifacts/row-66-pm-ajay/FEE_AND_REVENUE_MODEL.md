## Pricing Principles
1. **Value-Based Fees**: Fees are aligned with the financial benefit delivered to the client, leveraging the scheme’s substantial per-beneficiary grant of ₹10,000 or 50% of loan amount under Income Generating Schemes.
2. **Risk-Adjusted Pricing**: Success fees reflect the scheme’s moderate implementation risk due to annual Action Plan cycles, state-level fund disbursement delays (evidenced in states like Karnataka), and PACC approval dependencies.
3. **Transparency**: All fees and payment terms are disclosed upfront, with retainers covering initial plan preparation and success fees tied to verified fund disbursement.
4. **Scalability**: Tiered pricing accommodates clients ranging from district-level administrations (Scale tier) to state governments and large implementing agencies (Enterprise tier).
