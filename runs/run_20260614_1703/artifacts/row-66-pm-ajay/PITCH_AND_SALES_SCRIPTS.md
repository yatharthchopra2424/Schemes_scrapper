# Pitch & Sales Scripts

## Philosophy
We are advisors, not salespeople. We diagnose first. We only pitch a scheme if it fits the client. We never oversell success rates.

---

## 1. Discovery Call Script (First Contact — 20 min)

### Opening (2 min)
Good morning/afternoon. I specialize in helping Scheduled Caste (SC) communities and SC-dominated villages access government grants for socio-economic development. The Pradhan Mantri Anusuchit Jaati Abhyuday Yojana (PM-AJAY) supports infrastructure, livelihoods, and education in SC-majority areas. I’d like to understand your current initiatives and see if this scheme aligns with your goals.

### Discovery Questions
1. What is the SC population percentage in your target village or district?
2. Are you currently working on any income-generating or skill development projects for SC beneficiaries?
3. Do you have a Village Development Plan (VDP) approved by the Gram Sabha and DL-PACC?
4. Are you preparing an Annual Action Plan (AAP) for the upcoming financial year?
5. What infrastructure gaps (e.g., drinking water, sanitation, roads) exist in your SC-dominated areas?
6. Are you seeking financial assistance for hostel construction/repair in educational institutions?
7. Do you have login credentials for the PM-AJAY portal (https://pmajay.dosje.gov.in)?
8. Have you submitted Utilization Certificates (UCs) in GFR 12C format for previous funds?

### Qualification Checklist (internal — don't read aloud)
- [ ] SC population ≥ 40% and total population ≥ 500 (for village selection)
- [ ] Village has SC population ≥ 50% (for infrastructure grants)
- [ ] Target beneficiaries are SC persons below poverty line (for income generation/skill development)
- [ ] Annual Action Plan (AAP) prepared for current financial year
- [ ] Perspective Plan submitted for Grants-in-Aid component (if applicable)
- [ ] Utilization Certificate (UC) in GFR 12C format ready for prior funds
- [ ] Letter of Credentials for State/District Level Officers obtained
- [ ] Project proposals ready under Adarsh Gram, Grants-in-Aid, or Administrative Expenses components
- [ ] VDP finalized and approved by Gram Sabha and DL-PACC
- [ ] Institute/University registration details available (for hostel component)

### Transition to Pitch
Based on your responses, it appears your initiative aligns well with PM-AJAY’s focus on SC-dominated villages and income generation. The scheme offers up to Rs 10,000 per beneficiary or 50% of loan for livelihood projects—let me share how we can support your application.

## 2. Scheme Pitch (5 min)

### Structure: Problem → Scheme → Value → Proof → Next Step

**Problem framing:**
SC communities face persistent poverty due to limited access to income-generating opportunities, inadequate infrastructure in SC-dominated villages, and insufficient residential facilities for SC students pursuing higher education.

**Scheme intro:**
Pradhan Mantri Anusuchit Jaati Abhyuday Yojana (PM-AJAY), a Centrally Sponsored Scheme administered by the Department of Social Justice and Empowerment, Government of India, provides 100% central funding. Under the Grants-in-Aid component, it offers a subsidy of Rs 10,000 per beneficiary or 50% of the loan amount for income-generating schemes. The application portal is https://pmajay.dosje.gov.in.

**Why they qualify (personalised):**
- Your area has SC population ≥ 50%, qualifying for infrastructure development grants
- You target SC persons below poverty line, eligible for income generation and skill development benefits
- You are preparing an Annual Action Plan (AAP), a mandatory requirement since 2019-20
- You have or can obtain login credentials for the PM-AJAY portal for online submission
