## Pricing Principles
1. **Value-Aligned Fees**: Success fees are tied to the actual financial benefit disbursed under the SMILE Scheme, ensuring clients pay only for realized outcomes.
2. **Risk-Adjusted Pricing**: Given the scheme’s rolling basis, dependency on State-level fund allocation, and requirement for viable business plans and training completion, success fees reflect moderate implementation risk.
3. **Transparency & Simplicity**: Retainer covers eligibility assessment, documentation support, and application submission; success fee applies only upon verified disbursement of seed capital or subsidy.
4. **Scheme-Specific Note**: The SMILE Scheme has a medium confidence rating due to variability in State-level implementation and fund availability. Processing time is not fixed but involves multiple stages (survey, training, business plan approval), typically spanning 3–6 months, which affects revenue recognition lag.

## Invoice Templates

### Retainer Invoice
**Description**: Retainer fee for eligibility assessment, documentation support (identity proof, residence proof, transgender/begging certificate, bank details, photographs), and application submission assistance under the SMILE Scheme administered by the Ministry of Social Justice and Empowerment, Government of India.

### Success Fee Invoice
**Description**: Success fee for facilitating disbursement of seed capital or subsidy for self-employment or enterprise development under the SMILE Scheme, implemented by the Ministry of Social Justice and Empowerment, Government of India, upon verified approval and release of financial assistance to the beneficiary.
