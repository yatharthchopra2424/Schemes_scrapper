#### Eligibility Criteria
- Business type: Self-employment ventures, income-generating activities, enterprise development
- Age of business: Not applicable (scheme supports setup of new ventures)
- Sector: Not specified (open to any viable income-generating activity)
- Revenue cap: No income ceiling specified
- Employee count: Not mentioned
- Other: Must be Indian citizen; must belong to target groups (transgender persons or persons engaged in begging) as identified through State Government/UT Administration surveys or implementing agencies; priority given to those living in extreme poverty or without sustainable livelihood

#### What It Offers
- Amount / Benefit: One-time financial assistance for income-generating activities; specific amounts vary based on nature of venture and State-level implementation guidelines; includes seed capital and margin money assistance for entrepreneurial activities
- Disbursement mode: Disbursed through implementing agencies after verification of business plans and training completion
- Duration of benefit: One-time

#### Documents Required
1. Proof of identity (Aadhaar card, Voter ID, etc.)
2. Proof of residence
3. Certificate of transgender identity (if applicable)
4. Proof of engagement in begging (as per survey or affidavit)
5. Bank account details
6. Passport-sized photographs
7. Business plan or project proposal (for self-employment assistance)

#### Application Process (numbered steps)
1. Identification of beneficiaries through surveys conducted by State Governments/UT Administrations or NGOs
2. Verification of eligibility and enrollment in the scheme
3. Provision of temporary shelter and basic amenities in shelter homes
4. Enrollment in skill development and vocational training programs
5. Submission of business plan for self-employment or enterprise
6. Assessment and approval of financial assistance by the implementing agency
7. Disbursement of seed capital or subsidy for setting up the venture
8. Post-disbursement monitoring and mentorship support
