## 8D. Internal Data Handling Policy

All client data related to scheme applications shall be handled in accordance with the following standards:

- **Encrypted Cloud Storage:** All files are stored in encrypted cloud storage with access controls.
- **Two-Factor Authentication (2FA):** Mandatory for all system access involving client data.
- **Limited Access:** Access to client data is restricted to authorized personnel only on a need-to-know basis.
- **No Personal Devices:** Consultants must use firm-issued devices only for accessing or processing client data.
- **Retention Period:** Client data is retained for a minimum of 2 years post case closure.
- **Breach Notification:** In the event of a data breach, affected clients will be notified within 24 hours.

**Scheme-specific note:** All documents submitted to Department of Social Justice and Empowerment, Ministry of Social Justice and Empowerment, Government of India under Atal Vayo Abhyuday Yojana (AVYAY) must be retained for a minimum of 3 years for audit purposes.
