## Pricing Principles
1. **Value-Based Alignment**: Fees are calibrated to the actual grant quantum available under AVYAY for senior citizen care facilities (SrCH-25, SrCH-50, CCH), ensuring client returns justify consulting costs.
2. **Risk-Adjusted Pricing**: Success fees reflect moderate risk due to defined eligibility (2+ years registration, 2+ years social sector experience) and structured e-Anudaan workflow, though processing involves multiple stages (verification, inspection, scrutiny, recommendation, financial approval).
3. **Transparency & Predictability**: Retainer covers document preparation, portal navigation, and GAP district verification; success fee is contingent on GIA sanction via e-Anudaan portal workflow.
4. **Scheme-Specific Note**: AVYAY has a predictable processing timeline (applications open July–August 2024 for 2024-25 cycle) with evidence of timely disbursement via e-Anudaan, reducing revenue lag risk compared to schemes with opaque timelines.
