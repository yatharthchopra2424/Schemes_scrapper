# Pitch & Sales Scripts

## Philosophy
We are advisors, not salespeople. We diagnose first. We only pitch a scheme if it fits the client. We never oversell success rates.

---

## 1. Discovery Call Script (First Contact — 20 min)

### Opening (2 min)
Good morning/afternoon. I specialise in helping NGOs and charitable institutions working in senior citizen welfare access government grants like the Atal Vayo Abhyuday Yojana (AVYAY). Before we explore whether this scheme fits your organisation’s goals, I’d like to understand your current work in elder care—particularly if you’re operating or planning Senior Citizen Homes or Continuous Care Homes for Alzheimer’s/Dementia patients.

### Discovery Questions
1. What type of senior citizen care facility are you currently operating or planning to set up (e.g., Senior Citizen Home for 25/50 beneficiaries, Continuous Care Home for Alzheimer’s/Dementia patients)?
2. How long has your organisation been registered, and what is your experience in the social sector?
3. Are you registered on the NGO-DARPAN portal and do you have your NGO Unique ID ready?
4. Have you verified whether your project location falls under a designated GAP district (if applying for IPSrC projects)?
5. What kind of financial support are you seeking—for infrastructure, staffing, operational costs, or program implementation?
6. Do you have audited financial reports from the previous years available for submission?
7. Is your organisation prepared to submit a project proposal as per the prescribed proforma through the e-Anudaan portal?
8. Are you targeting beneficiaries such as senior citizens, including those with Alzheimer’s or dementia, in underserved or Gap districts?

### Qualification Checklist (internal — don't read aloud)
- [ ] Organisation registered for at least two years
- [ ] Minimum two years’ experience in the social sector
- [ ] Registered on NGO-DARPAN portal (NGO Unique ID available)
- [ ] Applying through e-Anudaan portal only (https://grants-msje.gov.in/ngo-login)
- [ ] For IPSrC projects in Gap districts: GAP status verified with District Administration
- [ ] Target beneficiaries: senior citizens (including Alzheimer’s/Dementia patients)
- [ ] Organisation type: NGO, Trust, or Charitable institution
- [ ] Prepared to submit: project proposal, infrastructure details, bank account, audit reports, PAN, UDID (if applicable)

### Transition to Pitch
Based on your responses, it appears your organisation’s work in senior citizen care aligns well with the Atal Vayo Abhyuday Yojana (AVYAY), which provides Grant-in-Aid for setting up and running Senior Citizen Homes and Continuous Care Homes. Let me walk you through how this scheme can support your mission.

## 2. Scheme Pitch (5 min)

### Structure: Problem → Scheme → Value → Proof → Next Step

**Problem framing:**
Many NGOs dedicated to elder care struggle to secure sustainable funding for infrastructure, staffing, and operational costs—especially when establishing Senior Citizen Homes or Continuous Care Homes for vulnerable elderly, including those with Alzheimer’s or dementia. This limits their ability to scale services in underserved or Gap districts where support is most needed.

**Scheme intro:**
The Atal Vayo Abhyuday Yojana (AVYAY), implemented by the Department of Social Justice and Empowerment, Ministry of Social Justice and Empowerment, Government of India, provides Grant-in-Aid through the e-Anudaan portal for setting up and running Senior Citizen Homes (SrCH-25, SrCH-50) and Continuous Care Homes (CCH) for Alzheimer’s/Dementia patients. Financial assistance covers both recurring and non-recurring expenses, with cost norms detailed on pages 45–47 of the scheme guidelines.

**Why they qualify (personalised):**
- Your organisation has been registered for at least two years and has two years’ experience in the social sector  
- You are targeting senior citizens, including those with Alzheimer’s/Dementia, as beneficiaries  
- You intend to apply exclusively through the e-Anudaan portal (https://grants-msje.gov.in/ngo-login)  
- For IPSrC projects, you have or will verify GAP district status with District Administration
