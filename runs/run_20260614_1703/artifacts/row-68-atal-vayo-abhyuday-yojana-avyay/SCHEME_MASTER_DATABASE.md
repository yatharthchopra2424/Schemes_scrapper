### Atal Vayo Abhyuday Yojana (AVYAY)
- **Scheme ID:** SCH-001
- **Category:** Grant
- **Geography:** Pan-India (with focus on Gap districts)
- **Administering Body:** Department of Social Justice and Empowerment, Ministry of Social Justice and Empowerment, Government of India
- **Portal / Application URL:** https://grants-msje.gov.in/ngo-login
- **Current Status:** Closed (for 2024-25 window; check portal for current notices)
- **Window:** 01st July 2024 till 15th August, 2024 (for new projects under IPSrC/AVYAY for 2024-25); deadlines vary by sub-scheme and year

#### Eligibility Criteria
- Business type: NGO/Trust/Charitable institution
- Age of business: Registered for at least two years
- Sector: Social sector (with at least two years' experience in the social sector at the time of applying)
- Revenue cap: Not specified in evidence
- Employee count: Not specified in evidence
- Other: Must apply through the e-Anudaan portal only; for IPSrC projects in Gap districts, organisations must verify with District Administration whether the proposed district is a GAP district; NGOs must register first at NGO-DARPAN portal before registering on e-Anudaan

#### What It Offers
- Amount / Benefit: Financial assistance as Grant-in-Aid; quantum and conditions detailed in AVYAY scheme guidelines; cost norms for projects specified on pages 45 to 47 of the Scheme Guideline; covers both recurring and non-recurring expenses for setting up and running senior citizen care facilities
- Disbursement mode: Grant-in-Aid through the e-Anudaan portal
- Duration of benefit: One-time grant for project setup and recurring support for operational costs (as per project approval and guidelines)

#### Documents Required
1. NGO Unique ID from NGO-DARPAN portal
2. PAN of the organisation
3. Certificate of Registration
4. Project proposal as per prescribed proforma
5. Details of infrastructure and facilities
6. Bank account details
7. Audit reports of previous years
8. UDID number or UDID enrollment ID with disability certificate (if applicable under related schemes)

#### Application Process (numbered steps)
1. Visit the e-Anudaan portal at https://grants-msje.gov.in/ngo-login
2. Ensure NGO is registered for at least two years with two years' experience in the social sector
3. Verify if the district is a GAP district (for IPSrC projects) with District Administration
4. Login to the portal and submit the online application
5. Upload required documents including NGO Unique ID, PAN, and project details
6. Submit application before the deadline
7. Await verification, inspection, scrutiny, recommendation, financial approval, and GIA sanction through the portal workflow
