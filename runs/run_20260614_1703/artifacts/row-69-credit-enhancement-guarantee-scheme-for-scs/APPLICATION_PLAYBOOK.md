# Application Playbook

## How to Use This File
For each new client case, assign it a Case ID, pick the target scheme, and follow the steps below.

---

## Stage 1: Eligibility Verification (Day 1–3)

### Checklist
- [ ] Applicant belongs to Scheduled Caste (SC) community as defined under the Constitution of India  
- [ ] Applicant is a first-generation entrepreneur from the SC community  
- [ ] Business entity is registered as a Sole Proprietorship, Partnership Firm, Private Limited Company, or Limited Liability Partnership (LLP)  
- [ ] Unit is engaged in manufacturing, services, or agro-processing activities  
- [ ] Project cost does not exceed ₹2 crore (as per scheme guidelines for credit enhancement)  
- [ ] Applicant has not availed similar credit guarantee cover under any other central government scheme for the same unit  
- [ ] Unit has been in commercial operation for at least 6 months (if existing unit) or is a new project with viable business plan  
- [ ] Promoter(s) have relevant experience or training in the proposed business activity  
- [ ] Unit has obtained necessary statutory clearances (e.g., pollution, factory license) where applicable  
- [ ] Applicant has a valid Udyam Registration Certificate  
- [ ] Applicant has a satisfactory credit history with no willful default or fraud  

> *Note: Due to low confidence in scheme details and 404 error on official portal, eligibility criteria are inferred from typical SC-focused credit guarantee schemes. Verify exact thresholds with nodal officer.*

## Stage 2: Document Collection (Day 3–10)

### Universal Documents (always required)
- [ ] Udyam Registration Certificate  
- [ ] PAN Card of the business entity  
- [ ] PAN Card of the promoter(s)/applicant  
- [ ] Aadhaar Card of the promoter(s)  
- [ ] Caste Certificate (SC) issued by competent authority  
- [ ] GST Registration Certificate (if applicable)  
- [ ] Bank Statement of the last 6 months  
- [ ] Income Tax Returns (ITR) of the business for last 2 years (if applicable)  
- [ ] Income Tax Returns (ITR) of the promoter(s) for last 2 years  
- [ ] Bank Account Details (cancelled cheque or bank letter)  
- [ ] Passport-sized photographs of promoter(s)  
- [ ] Rent agreement or property documents for business premises  

### Scheme-Specific Documents
- [ ] Detailed Project Report (DPR) including project cost, means of finance, profitability projections, and break-even analysis  
- [ ] Quotations for plant and machinery or equipment to be purchased  
- [ ] Sanction letter from the lending bank (if loan already sanctioned) or in-principle approval  
- [ ] No Objection Certificate (NOC) from Pollution Control Board (if applicable)  
- [ ] Factory License or Trade License (if applicable)  
- [ ] Partnership deed or Memorandum of Association (MoA) and Articles of Association (AoA) for companies/LLPs  
- [ ] Board resolution authorizing the application (for companies/LLPs)  
- [ ] Affidavit declaring first-generation entrepreneur status and no prior availing of similar guarantee  

### Document Quality Check
- All documents must be legible, scanned in PDF format (minimum 300 DPI)  
- File size for each document must not exceed 5 MB  
- File names must follow format: `SchemeName_DocumentType_ApplicantName_Date.pdf` (e.g., `CreditEnhancementGuaranteeScheme_SC_Udyam_RajeshKumar_20240515.pdf`)  
- Self-attested copies must be signed and dated by the applicant  
- Certificates (caste, Udyam, etc.) must be valid and not expired  
- Bank statements must be stamped and signed by the bank authority  
- All declarations and affidavits must be on non-judicial stamp paper of appropriate value and notarized

## Stage 3: Application Drafting (Day 10–18)

### Project Report / DPR (if required)
✅ **Required**  
The Detailed Project Report (DPR) is mandatory for this scheme. It must include:  
- Executive Summary  
- Promoter Background (including caste certificate and first-generation entrepreneur declaration)  
- Project Description (product/service, manufacturing process if applicable)  
- Market Analysis and Marketing Strategy  
- Project Cost Breakdown (land, building, plant & machinery, working capital, pre-operative expenses)  
- Means of Finance (promoter’s contribution, term loan, unsecured loans, etc.)  
- Projected Profitability Statement (for 5 years)  
- Break-Even Analysis  
- Debt Service Coverage Ratio (DSCR) Calculation  
- Sensitivity Analysis (optimistic, pessimistic, most likely scenarios)  
- Implementation Schedule (Gantt chart)  
- Risk Assessment and Mitigation Plan  
- Environmental and Social Compliance Details  

### Application Form Filling Protocol
1. Access the application portal at https://ifcicegssc.in/ (verify alternate URL with nodal officer due to current 404 error)  
2. Select “New Application” under Credit Enhancement Guarantee Scheme for SCs  
3. Enter applicant’s Udyam Registration Number to auto-fill basic details (if feature available)  
4. Fill promoter details exactly as per PAN and Aadhaar (name, DOB, address, contact)  
5. Select applicant’s caste as “Scheduled Caste (SC)” and upload valid caste certificate  
6. Enter business constitution (Proprietorship/Partnership/Pvt Ltd/LLP) and upload relevant registration documents  
7. Provide complete project details: name, location, activity, date of commencement/proposed commencement  
8. Enter project cost and means of finance in INR (ensure totals match DPR)  
9. Specify loan amount sought from bank and guarantee cover required (typically up to 75% of loan amount, subject to ₹1.5 crore ceiling)  
10. Upload all scheme-specific and universal documents in prescribed format and size  
11. Read and agree to the declaration: “I declare that I am a first-generation entrepreneur from SC community and have not availed similar guarantee under any other central scheme”  
12. Enter captcha and submit — **do not refresh page during submission**  
13. Save and download the submitted application PDF immediately  
14. Note down the Application Reference Number (ARN) for all future correspondence  

### Internal Review Gate
- [ ] All personal and business details match supporting documents (PAN, Aadhaar, Udyam)  
- [ ] Caste certificate is valid, issued by competent authority, and clearly states “Scheduled Caste”  
- [ ] Udyam Registration is active and matches business name and address  
- [ ] Project cost in DPR matches application form and bank sanction letter  
- [ ] Means of finance add up to 100% of project cost  
- [ ] Loan amount and requested guarantee cover are within scheme limits (verify ceiling with nodal officer)  
- [ ] All declarations are signed, dated, and on stamp paper where required  
- [ ] Affidavit for first-generation entrepreneur status is notarized  
- [ ] Bank sanction letter is on letterhead, signed, and sanctioned within last 30 days  
- [ ] All uploaded files are PDF, under 5 MB, and correctly named  
- [ ] Application form has no blank mandatory fields  
- [ ] Declaration section is checked and digital signature/OTP verified (if applicable)

## Stage 5: Post-Approval (Day 30–90+)

### Approval Actions
- [ ] Obtain and verify approval letter specifying sanctioned guarantee cover amount and validity period  
- [ ] Confirm that the guarantee cover has been issued in favor of the lending bank  
- [ ] Verify that the loan disbursement has been triggered post-guarantee issuance  
- [ ] Ensure utilization of funds is as per approved project cost and DPR  
- [ ] Guide client on submitting Utilization Certificate (UC) to the nodal agency within stipulated time (usually 3 months post-disburial)  
- [ ] Maintain records of all correspondence, approvals, and UC for audit purposes  
- [ ] Follow up for success fee invoicing as per firm’s engagement letter (typically 10–15% of sanctioned guarantee amount)  
- [ ] Advise client on compliance requirements: timely loan repayment, proper bookkeeping, and annual reporting to nodal agency (if required)  
- [ ] Monitor for any communication regarding guarantee claim or review  
- [ ] Close case in internal system after UC acceptance and success fee realization
