## Pricing Principles
1. **Value-Based Alignment**: Fees are structured to reflect the financial benefit delivered to the client, ensuring affordability while capturing the value of successful scheme utilization.
2. **Risk-Adjusted Pricing**: Success fees account for scheme-specific risks, including application complexity, documentation burden, and approval uncertainty.
3. **Transparency & Predictability**: Clear retainer and success fee structures enable clients to forecast consulting costs accurately.
4. **Scheme-Specific Note**: The Credit Enhancement Guarantee Scheme for SCs targets Scheduled Caste entrepreneurs seeking collateral-free loans through credit guarantees. Evidence indicates the scheme portal (https://ifcicegssc.in/) is currently inaccessible (HTTP 404), suggesting potential operational challenges or discontinuation. This increases perceived risk for applicants due to uncertain access and processing reliability, warranting a conservative success fee range within the applicable tier.
