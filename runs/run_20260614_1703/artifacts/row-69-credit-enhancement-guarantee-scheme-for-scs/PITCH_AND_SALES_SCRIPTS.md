# Pitch & Sales Scripts

## Philosophy
We are advisors, not salespeople. We diagnose first. We only pitch a scheme if it fits the client. We never oversell success rates.

---

## 1. Discovery Call Script (First Contact — 20 min)

### Opening (2 min)
Good morning/afternoon. I specialise in helping Scheduled Caste entrepreneurs access government-backed financial support designed to strengthen their creditworthiness and expand business opportunities. The Credit Enhancement Guarantee Scheme for SCs is specifically created to assist SC-owned enterprises in securing loans by providing government guarantees to lenders. I’d like to understand your current business financing needs to see if this scheme could be a fit for you.

### Discovery Questions
- When was your business incorporated or registered?
- Are you or the majority of owners from a Scheduled Caste community as defined by the Government of India?
- What type of business activity are you engaged in (e.g., manufacturing, services, trading)?
- What is your current annual turnover or projected revenue?
- Are you currently seeking a term loan or working capital loan from a bank or financial institution?
- Have you faced challenges in getting loan approvals due to lack of collateral or credit history?
- Are you looking for financial support to scale operations, purchase equipment, or meet working capital needs?
- Do you have audited financial statements or business registration documents available?

### Qualification Checklist (internal — don't read aloud)
- [ ] Business is at least 51% owned and controlled by Scheduled Caste individual(s)
- [ ] Enterprise is engaged in permissible economic activities (manufacturing, services, or trading)
- [ ] Business is registered as a proprietorship, partnership, LLP, or private limited company
- [ ] Unit is classified as a micro or small enterprise (as per MSME criteria)
- [ ] Loan proposal is for a term loan or working capital loan from a scheduled bank or financial institution
- [ ] Borrower has not defaulted on any previous government-sponsored loan
- [ ] Project is technically feasible and economically viable
- [ ] Unit has not received guarantee cover under any other government guarantee scheme for the same loan

### Transition to Pitch
Based on what you’ve shared, it sounds like your business may meet the core criteria for the Credit Enhancement Guarantee Scheme for SCs — particularly around ownership, business type, and financing needs. This scheme can help you secure bank loans by providing a government guarantee of up to 75% of the loan amount, reducing the collateral burden. Let me explain how it works.

## 2. Scheme Pitch (5 min)

### Problem framing:
Many SC entrepreneurs struggle to access formal credit due to limited collateral, weak credit history, or perceived higher risk by lenders. This often forces them to rely on expensive informal financing or limits their ability to scale operations, invest in equipment, or meet working capital demands.

### Scheme intro:
The Credit Enhancement Guarantee Scheme for SCs, administered by the Ministry of Social Justice and Empowerment through the IFCI Venture Capital Funds Ltd., provides a credit guarantee cover to banks and financial institutions for loans extended to SC-owned enterprises. It offers a guarantee of up to 75% of the loan amount, subject to a maximum of ₹1.50 crore per borrower, thereby enhancing credit access and reducing lending risk for financial institutions.

### Why they qualify (personalised):
- Your business is majority-owned and controlled by a Scheduled Caste individual(s)
- You are engaged in an eligible sector such as manufacturing, services, or trading
- Your enterprise qualifies as a micro or small unit under MSME classification
- You are seeking a term or working capital loan from a scheduled bank or financial institution
