## Scheme Deep Dive

### Basic Overview
The **Credit Enhancement Guarantee Scheme for SCs** (Scheme ID: row-69) is a government initiative administered under the **Ministry of Social Justice & Economic Development**. Classified as an "other" type scheme, it aims to facilitate access to credit for Scheduled Caste (SC) entrepreneurs and businesses by providing credit enhancement guarantees to lending institutions. Despite the scheme's intended purpose, the official application portal at `https://ifcicegssc.in/` currently returns a **404 Not Found error**, indicating potential issues with accessibility, maintenance, or active operation.

### Objectives
Based on the scheme name and ministry classification, the primary objectives include:
- Enhancing creditworthiness of SC-owned enterprises to secure loans from formal financial institutions.
- Reducing perceived lending risk for banks and NBFCs when extending credit to SC borrowers.
- Promoting entrepreneurship and economic empowerment within Scheduled Caste communities.
- Facilitating access to formal credit for income-generating activities, micro-enterprises, and small businesses.
- Supporting inclusive financial growth under the broader social justice and economic development agenda.
