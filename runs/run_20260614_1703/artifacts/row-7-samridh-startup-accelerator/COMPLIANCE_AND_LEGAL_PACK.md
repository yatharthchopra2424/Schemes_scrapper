## 8D. Internal Data Handling Policy

(Standard data handling policy — encrypted cloud storage, 2FA, limited access, no personal devices,
2-year retention post case closure, 24-hour breach notification.)

**Data Classification:** All client data is classified as Confidential and handled according to strict security protocols.

**Storage:** All data is stored in encrypted cloud storage with access controls. Encryption at rest and in transit using industry-standard algorithms (AES-256).

**Access Control:** Role-based access control (RBAC) implemented. Access granted on a least-privilege basis. Multi-factor authentication (MFA/2FA) required for all access.

**Device Policy:** No personal devices permitted for accessing or processing client data. All work must be conducted on firm-issued, encrypted devices with endpoint protection.

**Network Security:** Secure VPN required for remote access. Firewalls and intrusion detection/prevention systems (IDS/IPS) in place.

**Backup & Recovery:** Encrypted backups maintained with geo-redundancy. Recovery time objective (RTO) and recovery point objective (RPO) defined and tested regularly.

**Retention:** Client data retained for 2 years post case closure for operational and legal purposes, unless otherwise specified by scheme or regulatory requirements.

**Scheme-specific note:** All documents submitted to Ministry of Electronics and Information Technology (MeitY), Government of India under SAMRIDH Startup Accelerator must be retained for a minimum of 3 years for audit purposes.

**Breach Notification:** In the event of a data breach, affected parties and relevant authorities will be notified within 24 hours of breach confirmation, in accordance with applicable data protection laws.

**Disposal:** Secure deletion or destruction of data upon expiration of retention period, using methods that render data unrecoverable.

**Training:** Annual mandatory data protection and security training for all personnel handling client data.

**Audit & Monitoring:** Regular security audits, access log monitoring, and vulnerability assessments conducted. Audit trails maintained for all data access and modifications.
