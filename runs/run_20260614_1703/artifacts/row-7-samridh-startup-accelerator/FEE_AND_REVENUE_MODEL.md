## Pricing Principles
1. **Value-Aligned Pricing**: Fees are structured to reflect the tangible outcomes delivered, such as securing up to ₹40 lakh in matched funding per accelerator under SAMRIDH.
2. **Risk-Sharing Model**: Success fees are contingent on successful accelerator selection and funding disbursement, aligning consultant incentives with client outcomes.
3. **Transparency & Predictability**: Clear retainer and success fee structures ensure clients understand costs upfront, with no hidden charges.
4. **Scheme-Specific Note**: The SAMRIDH scheme has a moderate risk profile due to its multilevel screening process and reliance on accelerator performance; however, evidence shows strong traction (100+ startups funded, ₹45.55 Cr released in first cohort), supporting a realistic expectation of success with proper accelerator selection and application quality.
