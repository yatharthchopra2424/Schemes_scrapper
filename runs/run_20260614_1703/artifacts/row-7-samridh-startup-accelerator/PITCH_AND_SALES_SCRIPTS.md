# Pitch & Sales Scripts

## Philosophy
We are advisors, not salespeople. We diagnose first. We only pitch a scheme if it fits the client. We never oversell success rates.

---

## 1. Discovery Call Script (First Contact — 20 min)

### Opening (2 min)
Good morning/afternoon! I specialize in helping IT-based startups and accelerators access government funding to scale their impact. The SAMRIDH Startup Accelerator program, run by MeitY Startup Hub, is designed specifically to support accelerators in extending their services to high-potential tech startups through matched funding and market access. I’d love to understand your accelerator’s current work and see if this aligns with your goals.

### Discovery Questions
1. What stage is your accelerator currently at—are you already operational, or are you in the setup phase?
2. How many startups are you currently supporting, and what is your target cohort size per batch?
3. Are you focused on IT-based or product-led startups, particularly in areas like AI, IoT, or deep tech?
4. Do you operate across multiple states, or are you concentrated in a specific region?
5. What kind of support do you currently offer startups—mentorship, infrastructure, investor connects, or market access?
6. Have you previously applied for government accelerator funding, or is this your first time exploring such schemes?
7. Are you looking to scale your accelerator’s reach and deepen your impact on startup outcomes?
8. What are your main challenges in sustaining or expanding your accelerator program financially?

### Qualification Checklist (internal — don't read aloud)
- [ ] Applicant is an accelerator (existing or upcoming)
- [ ] Focuses on IT-based or product-led startups
- [ ] Aims to scale startups through customer, investor, and international market connects
- [ ] Willing to undergo multilevel screening process for selection
- [ ] Capable of extending acceleration services to startups (mentorship, infrastructure, etc.)
- [ ] Based in India (Pan-India eligibility)
- [ ] Seeking matched funding support of up to ₹40 lakh
- [ ] Committed to delivering measurable startup outcomes (revenue, employment, funding raised)

### Transition to Pitch
Based on what you’ve shared, it sounds like your accelerator is actively supporting IT-based startups and looking to scale impact—exactly what the SAMRIDH Startup Accelerator scheme is built for. It provides one-to-one matching funding of up to ₹40 lakh to accelerators like yours, enabling you to extend your services and drive real outcomes for startups.
