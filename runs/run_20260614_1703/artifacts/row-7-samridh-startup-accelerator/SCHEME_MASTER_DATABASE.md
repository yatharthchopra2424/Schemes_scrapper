### SAMRIDH Startup Accelerator
- **Scheme ID:** SCH-001
- **Category:** Incubation
- **Geography:** Pan-India
- **Administering Body:** Ministry of Electronics and Information Technology (MeitY), Government of India
- **Portal / Application URL:** https://msh.meity.gov.in/schemes/samridh
- **Current Status:** Rolling
- **Window:** Always Open

#### Eligibility Criteria
- Business type: Accelerators (existing and upcoming)
- Age of business: Not specified
- Sector: IT-based startups (product-based)
- Revenue cap: Not specified
- Employee count: Not specified
- Other: Selected through a multilevel screening process; must support acceleration services to start-ups

#### What It Offers
- Amount / Benefit: Up to ₹40 lakh (one-to-one matching funding support)
- Disbursement mode: Direct credit (to accelerators for extending services)
- Duration of benefit: One-time (per selected accelerator cohort)

#### Documents Required
1. Application form (as per SAMRIDH scheme guidelines)
2. Proof of accelerator status/registration
3. Details of cohort startups to be supported
4. Business plan and acceleration strategy
5. Financial statements and audit reports
6. Team credentials and mentor network details
7. Infrastructure and facility details
8. Past performance and impact metrics
9. Compliance certificates (if applicable)
10. Any other documents as specified in the official scheme guidelines

#### Application Process (numbered steps)
1. Visit the official SAMRIDH scheme portal at https://msh.meity.gov.in/schemes/samridh
2. Review eligibility criteria and guidelines
3. Prepare required documentation as per scheme requirements
4. Submit application through the MeitY Startup Hub portal
5. Await multilevel screening process evaluation
6. If selected, enter into agreement with MeitY Startup Hub
7. Receive matching funding support of up to ₹40 lakh
8. Extend acceleration services to selected start-ups
9. Utilize funds for customer connect, investor connect, and international market access
10. Submit progress reports and utilization certificates as required
