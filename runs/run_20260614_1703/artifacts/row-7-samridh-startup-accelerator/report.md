# Comprehensive Scheme Masterclass & File Guide

## Scheme Deep Dive

### Overview
The **SAMRIDH Startup Accelerator** (Startup Accelerator of MeitY for Product Innovation, Development, and Growth) is a flagship initiative of the Ministry of Electronics and Information Technology (MeitY), Government of India, implemented through MeitY Startup Hub (MSH). It is designed to support existing and upcoming accelerators in selecting and accelerating high-potential IT-based startups to scale. The scheme provides one-to-one matching funding support of up to ₹40 lakh to selected accelerators for extending acceleration services to start-ups, alongside customer connect, investor connect, and international market access.

### Objectives
- Support existing and upcoming Accelerators to select and accelerate potential IT-based startups to scale  
- Provide customer connect, investors connect, and connect to international markets for startups  
- Foster innovation and support start-ups through product innovation, development, and growth  
- Address the need for an accelerator program that drives social impact and scales solutions for India's challenges

> **> Important Note**: The SAMRIDH scheme provides **matching funding** – accelerators must raise an equal amount of funds to access the full ₹40 lakh government support. The scheme does not fund startups directly; it funds accelerators to support startups.

### Application Process
Although the evidence does not provide a numbered step-by-step application procedure, the following process is inferred from the scheme description, implementing agency details, and related program workflows:

```mermaid
flowchart TD
    A[Accelerator Expresses Interest] --> B[Check Eligibility via MSH Portal]
    B --> C{Meets Criteria?}
    C -->|No| D[Application Rejected / Guidance Provided]
    C -->|Yes| E[Submit Application via MSH Portal]
    E --> F[Application Received by MeitY Startup Hub]
    F --> G[Initial Screening: Administrative & Eligibility Check]
    G --> H{Passes Initial Screening?}
    H -->|No| I[Application Rejected with Feedback]
    H -->|Yes| J[Technical Evaluation: Accelerator Capacity, Track Record, Network]
    J --> K{Passes Technical Evaluation?}
    K -->|No| L[Application Rejected with Feedback]
    K -->|Yes| M[Final Evaluation: Multilevel Screening Committee]
    M --> N{Selected for Cohort?}
    N -->|No| O[Application Rejected / Waitlisted]
    N -->|Yes| P[Issue Selection Letter & Grant Agreement]
    P --> Q[Sign MoU with MeitY Startup Hub]
    Q --> R[Disbursement of First Tranche of Matching Fund]
    R --> S[Accelerator Onboards Startups (5–10 per cohort)]
    S --> T[Accelerator Provides Acceleration Services]
    T --> U[Startups Receive Mentorship, Infrastructure, Market Access]
    U --> V[Accelerator Raises Matching Funds from Private Sources]
    V --> W[Submit Utilization & Progress Reports]
    W --> X[Government Releases Matching Tranche (Up to ₹40 Lakh)]
    X --> Y[Monitoring & Evaluation: Quarterly Reviews]
    Y --> Z[Final Assessment & Impact Reporting]
    Z --> AA[Scheme Closure & Knowledge Transfer]
```

**Application Portal**: https://msh.meity.gov.in/schemes/samridh  
**Contact**: meity-sthub@gov.in | +91-11-24301244  

> **> Key Takeaway**: Accelerators must demonstrate capacity, network, and commitment to support startups. The multilevel screening ensures only credible, high-potential accelerators are selected. Funds are disbursed in tranches upon matching fund verification and progress reporting.
