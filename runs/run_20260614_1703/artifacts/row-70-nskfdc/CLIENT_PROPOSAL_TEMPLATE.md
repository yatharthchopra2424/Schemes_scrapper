## 3. Our Scope of Work
1. Initial eligibility assessment against NSKFDC criteria for Safai Karamcharis, Manual Scavengers, dependents, or promoted entities  
2. Document procurement guidance for certificates from Revenue Officer, Municipal Officer, Gazetted Officer, etc.  
3. Project proposal and income-generating activity detailing support  
4. Portal registration assistance on https://nskfdc.nic.in/en/content/how-apply/loan-application-form  
5. Application form completion and submission through State Channelising Agency (SCA), RRB, or Nationalised Bank  
6. Liaison with implementing agency for evaluation based on financial viability and priority criteria  
7. Post-approval disbursement coordination and utilization monitoring
