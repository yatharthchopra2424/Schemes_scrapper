## 8D. Internal Data Handling Policy

(Standard data handling policy — encrypted cloud storage, 2FA, limited access, no personal devices,
2-year retention post case closure, 24-hour breach notification.)

**Purpose:** This policy ensures the secure handling, storage, and disposal of client personal and financial data collected during NSKFDC scheme consulting engagements.

**Scope:** Applies to all consultants, employees, and contractors of [Firm Name] who handle client data in connection with NSKFDC scheme applications.

**Data Classification:**  
- **Restricted:** Government-issued IDs, income proofs, bank statements, eligibility certificates, project proposals.  
- **Confidential:** Client contact details, application status, internal notes.  
- **Internal:** General scheme information, templates, process guides.

**Data Handling Procedures:**  
1. **Collection:** Data collected only via secure client portal or encrypted email. No collection via unsecured channels (e.g., WhatsApp, regular email without encryption).  
2. **Storage:** All Restricted and Confidential data stored in encrypted cloud storage (AES-256 encryption) with access logs enabled.  
3. **Access Control:** Role-based access; only assigned consultants may access client data. Multi-factor authentication (2FA) required for all access.  
4. **Device Usage:** No storage of client data on personal devices (laptops, phones, USB drives). All work performed on firm-issued, encrypted devices.  
5. **Transmission:** Data shared with NSKFDC or SCAs only via encrypted upload to the official portal or password-protected email with secure file transfer (SFTP).  
6. **Retention:**  
   - Active cases: Retained for duration of engagement.  
   - Post case closure: Retained for 2 years, after which securely deleted.  
   - **Scheme-specific note:** All documents submitted to National Safai Karamcharis Finance & Development Corporation (NSKFDC), a wholly owned Govt. of India Undertaking under the Ministry of Social Justice & Empowerment (M/o SJ&E) under NSKFDC must be retained for a minimum of 3 years for audit purposes.  
7. **Breach Notification:** In the event of a data breach, affected clients and relevant authorities will be notified within 24 hours.  
8. **Training:** All staff undergo annual data protection and confidentiality training.  
9. **Audit:** Quarterly internal audits of data handling compliance.  
10. **Disposal:** Secure deletion via cryptographic erasure or physical destruction of storage media.

**Compliance:** This policy aligns with the Information Technology Act, 2000, and the SPDI Rules, 2011, and is subject to updates based on regulatory changes.
