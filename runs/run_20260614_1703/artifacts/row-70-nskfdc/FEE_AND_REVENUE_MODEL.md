## Pricing Principles
1. **Value-Based Alignment**: Fees are structured to reflect the financial benefit delivered, with success fees calibrated to the actual loan amounts disbursed under NSKFDC schemes (e.g., up to ₹15.00 Lac for General Term Loan, ₹20.00 Lac for Education Loan abroad, ₹50.00 Lac for Swachhta Udyami Yojana for groups).
2. **Risk-Adjusted Pricing**: Success fees account for processing time and approval uncertainty; NSKFDC loans involve document-intensive applications (e.g., multiple certificates from Gazetted Officers, municipal bodies) and SCA coordination, leading to moderate execution risk.
3. **Accessibility for Target Beneficiaries**: Retainer fees are kept affordable for micro and small enterprises (MSME/Startup tier) given the scheme’s focus on Safai Karamcharis, Manual Scavengers, and their dependents, many of whom operate at micro-enterprise levels.
4. **Transparency and Compliance**: All fees are disclosed upfront; the model aligns with NSKFDC’s emphasis on financial viability and income-generating capacity, avoiding hidden costs that could jeopardize project feasibility.
