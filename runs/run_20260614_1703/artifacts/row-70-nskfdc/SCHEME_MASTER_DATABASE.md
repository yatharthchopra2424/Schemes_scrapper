### NSKFDC
- **Scheme ID:** SCH-001
- **Category:** Loan
- **Geography:** Pan-India
- **Administering Body:** National Safai Karamcharis Finance & Development Corporation (NSKFDC), a wholly owned Govt. of India Undertaking under the Ministry of Social Justice & Empowerment (M/o SJ&E)
- **Portal / Application URL:** https://nskfdc.nic.in/en/content/how-apply/loan-application-form
- **Current Status:** Rolling
- **Window:** Always Open

#### Eligibility Criteria
- Business type: Safai Karamcharis (including Wastepickers), identified Manual Scavengers and their dependents; Registered co-operative societies of Safai Karamcharis; Legally constituted association/firm promoted by the target group
- Age of business: Not specified
- Sector: Income-generating activities, sanitation-related projects, education, skill development, green business, community toilets, sanitary marts
- Revenue cap: No income limit fixed for availing financial assistance
- Employee count: Not specified
- Other: Priority accorded to economic development and rehabilitation of Scavengers, those with income below double the poverty line, Women from the target group, and Disabled persons among the target group; Financial viability and income-generating capacity of the project are considered while evaluating projects for financing

#### What It Offers
- Amount / Benefit: Financial assistance through various loan schemes: General Term Loan up to Rs.15.00 Lac; Education Loan up to Rs.10.00 Lac for study in India and Rs.20.00 Lac for abroad; Mahila Samridhi Yojana up to Rs.1.00 Lac; Mahila Adhikarita Yojana up to Rs.2.00 Lac; Micro Credit Finance up to Rs.1.00 Lac; 'Pay and use' community toilets up to Rs.25.00 Lac; Sanitary Marts Scheme up to Rs.15.00 Lac; Green Business Scheme up to Rs.15.00 Lac; Swachhta Udyami Yojana up to Rs.15.00 Lac for individual/SHG/JRG/Cooperative and Rs.50.00 Lac for SHG/JRG/Cooperative
- Disbursement mode: Direct credit through State Channelising Agency (SCA), Regional Rural Bank (RRB), or Nationalised Bank
- Duration of benefit: Varies by scheme (e.g., 3 to 10 years repayment period with moratorium; Education Loan: 5 years after course completion with 1 year moratorium)

#### Documents Required
1. Certificate from local Revenue Officer
2. Certificate from local Municipal Officer
3. Certificate from Cantonment Executive Officer
4. Certificate from Railway Officer
5. Certificate from Head of Govt. Departments (Schools, Colleges, Forest, Health, Education, Animal Husbandry) with rank not less than Gazetted Officer
6. Elected Member of Municipal Body certificate
7. Pradhan of Gram Panchayat certificate
8. Regional Manager of Regional Rural Banks (RRBs) or Nationalised Banks certificate
9. Proof of identification as Safai Karamchari or Manual Scavenger
10. Proof of dependency (family member dependent on Safai Karamchari/Scavenger, age 18 years & above)
11. Project proposal/detailing income-generating activity
12. For Education Loan: admission proof in recognized institute/university for professional/technical education

#### Application Process (numbered steps)
1. Identify eligibility as Safai Karamchari, Manual Scavenger, dependent, or registered entity promoted by target group.
2. Obtain required certificate from local Revenue Officer, Municipal Officer, Cantonment Executive Officer, Railway Officer, Head of Govt. Department (Gazetted Officer rank or above), Elected Member of Municipal Body, Pradhan of Gram Panchayat, or Regional Manager of RRB/Nationalised Bank (unless identified as Manual Scavenger under MS Act, 2013 survey).
3. Approach State Channelising Agency (SCA), Regional Rural Bank (RRB), or Nationalised Bank for loan submission.
4. Submit loan application form with project details.
5. Undergo evaluation based on financial viability, income-generating capacity, and priority criteria (income below double poverty line, women, disabled).
6. Upon approval, receive disbursement through SCA/RRB/Nationalised Bank.
7. For non-loan based schemes, participate in awareness camps, workshops, job fairs, or exhibitions with free stall provision and reimbursement of expenses.
