## Pricing Principles
1. Value-based pricing aligned with the concessional financial benefit delivered to Scheduled Caste beneficiaries under NSFDC schemes.
2. Transparent fee structure with retainer for advisory workload and success fee contingent on loan sanction or fellowship award.
3. Tiered pricing reflects client capacity, with Starter and Growth tiers targeting individuals, partnership firms, and cooperative societies eligible for NSFDC credit and non-credit schemes.
4. Risk-adjusted success fees: Lower risk due to NSFDC’s high approval rate for eligible applicants (evidenced by 2000 annual NFSC fellowships and rolling loan sanctions), but moderated by 3–6 month processing lag via SCAs/CAs and PCC clearance.
