### NSFDC
- **Scheme ID:** SCH-001
- **Category:** Loan
- **Geography:** Pan-India
- **Administering Body:** National Scheduled Castes Finance and Development Corporation (NSFDC)
- **Portal / Application URL:** https://www.nsfdc.nic.in
- **Current Status:** Rolling
- **Window:** Always Open

#### Eligibility Criteria
- Business type: Individuals, Partnership Firms and Co-operative Societies
- Age of business: Not specified
- Sector: Not specified
- Revenue cap: Annual family income of each member/applicant should not exceed Rs.5.00 lakh
- Employee count: Not specified
- Other: Beneficiary should be from the Scheduled Caste Community; Partnership Firms and Co-operative Societies eligible only if all members belong to Scheduled Caste community

#### What It Offers
- Amount / Benefit: Loans up to 90% of the project cost with scheme-specific maximum limits: Term Loan (>Rs.1.25 lakh & upto Rs.45.00 lakh), Micro Finance Scheme (upto Rs.1.25 lakhs), Educational Loan Scheme (upto Rs.40.00 Lakh or 90% of course fee), Udyam Nidhi Yojana (upto Rs.4.50 lakhs), Aajeevika Microfinance Yojana (upto Rs.1.25 lakhs)
- Disbursement mode: Direct credit (funds disbursed to SCAs/CAs for onward disbursement to beneficiaries)
- Duration of benefit: Repayment periods ranging from 3 to 12 years; moratorium periods from 3 months to 1 year

#### Documents Required
1. Loan sanction letter (NSFDC schemes)
2. Aadhaar Card
3. Bank Passbook
4. Voter ID
5. PIA letter + Artisan Card (for Cluster beneficiaries)
6. Partner Agency letter + SHG name (for SEED beneficiaries)
7. Product photographs (minimum three)
8. Brief beneficiary and product description
9. Nomination form (prescribed format)

#### Application Process (numbered steps)
1. Eligible target group (Scheduled Castes persons with annual family income up to Rs.5.00 lakhs) submits loan application to District Offices of State Channelizing Agencies (SCAs).
2. District Offices of SCAs/CAs scrutinize applications and forward them to their Head Offices.
3. SCAs/CAs appraise project viability and forward viable projects to NSFDC with recommendations.
4. NSFDC's Project Desk appraises proposals and submits report to Project Clearance Committee (PCC) for concurrence.
5. PCC-recommended proposals are sanctioned.
6. Sanction Letters (Letter of Intent - LOI) with Terms & Conditions are issued to SCAs/CAs for acceptance.
7. After acceptance of terms and fulfillment of Prudential Norms, funds are disbursed to SCAs/CAs on receipt of demand.
8. SCAs/CAs disburse funds to beneficiaries.
9. Beneficiaries repay loans as per stipulated repayment schedule.
