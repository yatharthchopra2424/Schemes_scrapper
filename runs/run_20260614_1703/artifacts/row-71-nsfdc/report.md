# Comprehensive Scheme Masterclass & File Guide

## Scheme Deep Dive

### Overview
The National Scheduled Castes Finance and Development Corporation (NSFDC) is a Central Public Sector Enterprise under the Ministry of Social Justice & Empowerment, Government of India. It was incorporated on February 8, 1989, and bifurcated on April 10, 2001, to function exclusively for the development of Scheduled Castes. NSFDC provides financial assistance at concessional interest rates through various credit and non-credit schemes to empower Scheduled Caste individuals, partnership firms, and cooperative societies engaged in income-generating activities, skill development, education, and fellowships.

**Implementing Agency:** National Scheduled Castes Finance and Development Corporation (NSFDC)  
**Application Portal:** [https://www.nsfdc.nic.in](https://www.nsfdc.nic.in)  
**Geographic Scope:** Pan-India  
**Scheme Type:** Loan (with non-credit components)  
**Fund Size:** ₹1,800 crore (authorized share capital)  
**Status / Deadlines:** Rolling basis (no fixed deadline)  
**Last Updated:** 2026  
**Confidence:** High  

### Objectives
NSFDC’s objectives are explicitly defined in its mandate and include:
- Finance income-generating schemes of eligible Scheduled Caste persons through State Channelizing Agencies (SCAs) and other Channelizing Agencies (CAs)
- Improve flow of financial assistance to persons belonging to Scheduled Castes for their economic well-being
- Provide Educational Loans to target group through SCAs and/or CAs for pursuing full-time professional/technical education in India and abroad
- Provide grants for Skill Development Training Programmes of the target group
- Organize/participate in Exhibitions cum Fairs for marketing of beneficiaries' products
- Promote prosperity among Scheduled Castes through skill development and innovative initiatives
- Identify trades and economic activities important to Scheduled Castes population
- Upgrade skills and processes used by persons belonging to Scheduled Castes

### Eligibility Matrix
Eligibility varies slightly between credit-based and non-credit-based schemes. The core criteria are standardized, with specific relaxations for training programmes.

| **Criteria** | **Credit-Based Schemes** | **Non-Credit-Based Schemes (Skill Development Training)** |
|--------------|--------------------------|-----------------------------------------------------------|
| **Beneficiary Community** | Must belong to Scheduled Caste community | Must belong to Scheduled Caste community |
| **Entity Type** | Individuals, Partnership Firms, Co-operative Societies | Individuals (trainees) |
| **Partnership/Co-op Condition** | All members must be from SC community; annual family income of each member ≤ ₹5.00 lakh | Not applicable |
| **Annual Family Income Limit** | ≤ ₹5.00 lakh (for both rural and urban areas, effective 07th January 2026) | **No income criterion** |
| **Verification Responsibility** | Sole responsibility of SCAs/CAs; NSFDC may re-verify | Sole responsibility of SCAs/CAs; NSFDC may re-verify |
| **Direct Contact with NSFDC** | Not entertained under any circumstances | Not entertained under any circumstances |
| **Additional Notes** | Income limit verified as of 07th January 2026 | Applicable to all Skill Development Training Programmes |

> **> Important Caveat:** Direct contact or correspondence by Applicants/Beneficiaries with NSFDC shall not be entertained under any circumstances. All applications must be submitted through District Offices of State Channelizing Agencies (SCAs).

#### Key Financial Notes
- NSFDC provides loans up to **90% of project cost** across all credit schemes.
- Interest charged to SCAs/CAs ranges from **2.5% to 5% p.a.**, which is passed on to beneficiaries at **6.5% to 15% p.a.**
- Repayment is in **quarterly instalments** with moratorium periods ranging from **3 months to 1 year**.
- For NFSC, fellowship is disbursed **monthly via bank account** after Aadhaar seeding and verification.
- Under NFSC, contingency grants, HRA, and escort/reader assistance are additional benefits.
- Fund size: **₹1,800 crore** (authorized share capital); paid-up capital: **₹1,515 crore** as of 31st January 2026.

### Application Process
The application process is strictly channelized through SCAs/CAs. Direct application to NSFDC is not permitted.

```mermaid
flowchart TD
    A[Eligible SC Individual/Firm/Co-op] -->|Submits loan application| B[District Office of SCA/CA]
    B -->|Scrutinizes & forwards viable projects| C[Head Office of SCA/CA]
    C -->|Appraises project viability & recommends| D[NSFDC Project Desk]
    D -->|Submits appraisal report| E[Project Clearance Committee (PCC)]
    E -->|Concurrence on viable proposals| F[Sanction Letter (LOI) issued to SCA/CA]
    F -->|SCA/CA accepts terms & fulfills Prudential Norms| G[NSFDC disburses funds to SCA/CA on demand]
    G -->|SCA/CA disburses funds to beneficiary| H[Beneficiary]
    H -->|Repays loan as per SCA/CA stipulated schedule| I[Repayment to SCA/CA]
    style A fill:#e3f2fd,stroke:#1565c0
    style H fill:#fff3e0,stroke:#ef6c00
    style I fill:#e8f5e9,stroke:#2e7d32
```

> **> Critical Warning:** Loans are to be repaid by beneficiaries as per the repayment schedule stipulated by SCAs/CAs. NSFDC does not directly interact with beneficiaries for repayment.

### Key Caveats
- **Direct Contact Barrier:** Applicants/beneficiaries cannot contact NSFDC directly; all communication must route through SCAs/CAs.
- **Repayment Responsibility:** Beneficiaries repay loans per SCAs/CAs’ stipulated schedule—not NSFDC’s.
- **NFSC-Specific Restrictions:**
  - Employees of Universities/Colleges/Educational Institutions/Central/State/UT Govt. excluded (even on study/extraordinary leave).
  - Only one fellowship at a time; must declare no other monetary benefit/scholarship.
  - Scholar failing to complete research work during fellowship period ineligible to reapply.
  - Fellowship granted only up to Ph.D. thesis submission or 5 years of tenure, whichever earlier—**no extension beyond 5 years**.
  - Max gap from M.Phil. to Ph.D.: **2 years**; no fellowship paid during gap.
  - Must join fellowship within **3 months** of JRF award letter issue; candidature cancelled if not joined within 3 months.
- **Documentation:** Required documents include loan sanction letter, Aadhaar, bank passbook, voter ID, PIA letter + artisan card (cluster), partner agency letter + SHG name (SEED), product photographs (min 3), beneficiary/product description, nomination form.
