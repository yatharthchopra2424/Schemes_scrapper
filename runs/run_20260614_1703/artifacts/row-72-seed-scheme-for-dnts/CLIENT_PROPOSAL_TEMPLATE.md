## 3. Our Scope of Work
1. Initial eligibility assessment based on DNT/NT/SNT status and family income ≤ Rs. 2.5 lakh/annum.
2. Document collection and verification as per scheme requirements.
3. Online registration on the SEED portal (https://dwbdnc.dosje.gov.in/register).
4. Application submission and tracking.
5. Liaison with DWBDNC for application scrutiny.
6. Assistance in receiving 'Open Assurance' letter upon selection.
7. Post-selection support for benefit disbursement (DBT, SHG linkage, etc.).

## 4. What We Need From You
1. Documents as per the scheme's document list: Aadhaar Card, Income Certificate, Caste/Community Certificate, Bank Account details, Educational qualification marksheets/certificates, Passport size photograph, Domicile Certificate, Disability certificate (if applicable), Admission Rank Proof (for college scholarships), Proof of fee details (for college scholarships), Self-Declaration of Caste, Date of Birth, Endorsement by Local Pradhan of the Community (optional), PAN Card of Organisation (for NGO grants), PAN and Aadhar Card of Office Bearers (for NGO grants).
2. Designated point of contact for all communications.
3. Response to queries within 2 business days.
