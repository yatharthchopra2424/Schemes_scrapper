## Pricing Principles
1. **Value-Based Pricing**: Fees aligned with the tangible economic upliftment delivered (e.g., livelihood support via SHG revolving fund loans, stipends for coaching, health insurance under PMJAY, housing assistance via PMAY).
2. **Risk-Adjusted Fees**: Success fees reflect the scheme’s moderate risk profile—processing times vary by component (coaching: portal opens July/Jan for 1 month; livelihood/health/housing: rolling basis), but disbursement is DBT-linked and monitored via SHG/NGO partnerships, reducing default risk.
3. **Transparency & Compliance**: All fees disclosed upfront; retainers cover eligibility assessment, document preparation, and portal registration (Aadhaar, income/caste certificates, bank linkage) as per SEED guidelines.
4. **Scheme-Specific Note**: The SEED scheme has a low to moderate risk profile due to its rolling application basis for livelihood/health/housing components and structured DBT disbursement for coaching. Success is evidenced by documented outcomes: SHGs receiving revolving fund loans (₹5,000–₹25,000 at 1% interest), stipends (₹1,500–₹3,000/month), and Ayushman cards issued (87,745+). Processing delays are mitigated by online-only applications and merit-based selection, ensuring predictable revenue lag.
