# Pitch & Sales Scripts

## Philosophy
We are advisors, not salespeople. We diagnose first. We only pitch a scheme if it fits the client. We never oversell success rates.

---

## 1. Discovery Call Script (First Contact — 20 min)

### Opening (2 min)
Good morning/afternoon. I specialize in helping De-notified, Nomadic, and Semi-Nomadic (DNT/NT/SNT) communities access government welfare schemes that provide tangible support—like livelihood assistance, free coaching for competitive exams, health insurance, and housing aid. Many eligible families miss out simply because they don’t know these schemes exist or how to apply. Today, I’d like to understand your situation and see if the SEED Scheme for DNTs might be a fit for you or your community.

### Discovery Questions
1. To which community do you belong—De-notified Tribe (DNT), Nomadic Tribe (NT), or Semi-Nomadic Tribe (SNT)?  
2. What is your total annual family income from all sources?  
3. Are you currently receiving any similar benefits from central or state government schemes?  
4. Do you have an Aadhaar card, income certificate, and caste/community certificate?  
5. Are you interested in livelihood support (e.g., SHG loans, skill training), free coaching for exams (UPSC, SSC, NEET, JEE), health insurance under PMJAY, or housing assistance?  
6. Do you have a bank account linked to your Aadhaar for direct benefit transfers?  
7. Are you part of or interested in joining a Self-Help Group (SHG) for community-based livelihood activities?  
8. Do you possess essential documents like domicile certificate, disability certificate (if applicable), or educational certificates?

### Qualification Checklist (internal — don't read aloud)
- [ ] Belongs to DNT/NT/SNT community  
- [ ] Total family income ≤ ₹2.5 lakh per annum  
- [ ] Not availing benefits from similar central or state schemes  
- [ ] Possesses Aadhaar card  
- [ ] Has income certificate  
- [ ] Has caste/community certificate  
- [ ] Has bank account details  
- [ ] Has domicile certificate  

### Transition to Pitch
Based on your responses, it appears you may meet the core eligibility criteria for the **SEED Scheme for DNTs**—a central government initiative offering livelihood support, free coaching, health insurance, and housing assistance. Let me share how this scheme has helped others like you and how we can support your application.

## 2. Scheme Pitch (5 min)

### Structure: Problem → Scheme → Value → Proof → Next Step

**Problem framing:**
Many DNT/NT/SNT families struggle with unstable income, lack of access to formal credit, limited educational opportunities, and exclusion from mainstream welfare schemes due to documentation gaps or awareness barriers. This perpetuates cycles of poverty and financial insecurity, especially in remote or marginalized communities.

**Scheme intro:**
The **Scheme for Economic Empowerment of DNTs (SEED)**, implemented by the Development and Welfare Board for De-notified, Nomadic and Semi-Nomadic Communities (DWBDNC) under the Ministry of Social Justice & Empowerment, provides integrated support across four pillars: livelihood assistance (including revolving fund loans to SHGs), free coaching for competitive exams with stipends, health insurance coverage of ₹5 lakh annually under PMJAY, and housing assistance via PMAY. The scheme has a total outlay of ₹200 crore over five years (FY 2021-22 onwards) and accepts applications on a rolling basis via the portal: https://dwbdnc.dosje.gov.in/register.

**Why they qualify (personalised):**
- You belong to a DNT/NT/SNT community (as confirmed in discovery)  
- Your family income is ₹2.5 lakh or less per annum (self-reported)  
- You are not currently availing similar central or state scheme benefits (self-reported)  
- You possess or can obtain key documents: Aadhaar, income certificate, caste certificate, and bank details
