### SEED Scheme for DNTs
- **Scheme ID:** SCH-001
- **Category:** Grant
- **Geography:** Pan-India
- **Administering Body:** Development and Welfare Board for De-notified, Nomadic and Semi-Nomadic Communities (DWBDNC)
- **Portal / Application URL:** https://dwbdnc.dosje.gov.in/register
- **Current Status:** Rolling
- **Window:** Applications accepted throughout the year for livelihood, health, and housing components; for free coaching, portal opens in July and January each year for one month each.

#### Eligibility Criteria
- Business type: Self-employment, SHG formation, individual livelihood activities
- Age of business: Not specified
- Sector: Livelihoods, education, health, housing
- Revenue cap: Total family income from all sources of Rs. 2.50 lakh or less per annum
- Employee count: Not specified
- Other: Must belong to DNT/NT/SNT communities; must not be availing benefits from similar central or state schemes; income limit subject to change per central government policies

#### What It Offers
- Amount / Benefit: 
  - Revolving fund loans to SHGs: ₹10,000 to ₹20,000 at 1% interest
  - Stipend for coaching students: ₹1,500/month (local), ₹3,000/month (outstation)
  - Special allowance for students with disabilities: ₹2,000/month
  - Health insurance coverage: ₹5 lakh annually under PMJAY
  - Livelihood support: SHG formation, revolving fund loans, skill training, marketing support
  - Housing assistance: Via PMAY
  - Document assistance: Aadhaar, bank account, caste certificate, income certificate, etc.
- Disbursement mode: Direct credit (DBT) for stipends and loan installments; reimbursement for coaching fees after joining institute
- Duration of benefit: One-time for livelihood/housing support; annual for health insurance; stipend paid till course duration or one year, whichever is less

#### Documents Required
1. Aadhaar Card
2. Income Certificate
3. Caste/Community Certificate
4. Bank Account details
5. Educational qualification marksheets/certificates
6. Passport size photograph
7. Domicile Certificate
8. Disability certificate (if applicable)
9. Admission Rank Proof (for college scholarships)
10. Proof of fee details (for college scholarships)
11. Self-Declaration of Caste
12. Date of Birth
13. Endorsement by Local Pradhan of the Community (optional)
14. PAN Card of Organisation (for NGO grants)
15. PAN and Aadhar Card of Office Bearers (for NGO grants)

#### Application Process (numbered steps)
1. Assess eligibility based on DNT/NT/SNT status and family income ≤ Rs. 2.5 lakh/annum.
2. Apply online via the SEED portal (coaching.dosje.gov.in for education component, or https://dwbdnc.dosje.gov.in/register for livelihood/health/housing components).
3. Upload required documents (Aadhaar, income certificate, caste certificate, bank details, etc.).
4. Applications are scrutinized in order of merit.
5. Selected candidates receive an 'Open Assurance' letter.
6. For coaching: first installment (50% of course fee) released via DBT upon admission confirmation; remaining fee reimbursed after joining institute and submitting proof.
7. For livelihood: SHGs receive revolving fund support; individuals get assistance for bank account linkage, skill training, and marketing support.
8. For health: facilitate enrollment in PMJAY for Rs. 5 lakh annual coverage.
9. For housing: assist in availing benefits under PMAY-G/U.
