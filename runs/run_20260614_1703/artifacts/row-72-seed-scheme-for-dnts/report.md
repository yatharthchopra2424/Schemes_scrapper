# Comprehensive Scheme Masterclass & File Guide

## Scheme Deep Dive

### Overview
The **SEED Scheme for DNTs** (Scheme for Economic Empowerment of De-notified, Nomadic and Semi-Nomadic Tribes) is a Central Sector Scheme implemented by the **Development and Welfare Board for De-notified, Nomadic and Semi-Nomadic Communities (DWBDNC)** under the **Ministry of Social Justice and Empowerment**. Launched in Financial Year 2021-22, it has a total fund size of **₹200 crore** to be spent over 5 years. The scheme operates on a **rolling basis** for livelihood, health, and housing components, while the free coaching component has portal openings in **July and January each year for one month each**. The implementing agency is DWBDNC, and the application portal is [https://dwbdnc.dosje.gov.in/register](https://dwbdnc.dosje.gov.in/register). Contact details: Email: dwbdnc-msje@gov.in | Phone: 011-20867719.

### Objectives
The scheme has four core objectives:
- To provide coaching of good quality for DNT candidates to enable them to appear in competitive examinations
- To provide health insurance to DNT Communities
- To facilitate livelihoods initiative at community level to build and strengthen small clusters of DNT/NT/SNT Communities institutions
- To provide financial assistance for construction of houses to members of the DNT Communities

### Eligibility Matrix
| Criteria | Requirement | Source |
|---------|-------------|--------|
| **Community** | DNT (De-notified Tribes), NT (Nomadic Tribes), SNT (Semi-Nomadic Tribes) | Key Facts, Guidelines |
| **Family Income** | ≤ ₹2.50 lakh per annum from all sources | Key Facts, Guidelines (Para 3) |
| **Similar Scheme Benefits** | Must not be availing any such benefits from similar Central or State Government schemes | Key Facts, Guidelines (Para 3) |
| **Coaching Specific** | Family income ≤ ₹8 lakh per annum; must have secured minimum marks in qualifying exam; benefits can be availed not more than twice | Guidelines (Para 4.2.4) |
| **Health Insurance** | Must be of DNT community and qualify all criteria under Ayushman Bharat scheme | Central Scheme : Health page |
| **Livelihood** | All DNTs staying in villages and rural areas in India | Central Scheme : Livelihood page |
| **Housing** | All DNT persons looking for support to build housing | Central Scheme : Housing page |

> **> Important Caveats**  
> - Benefits under the scheme can be availed by a particular student not more than twice, irrespective of the number of chances in a competitive examination.  
> - Candidates must submit an affidavit that they have not availed benefits from any similar central or state scheme.  
> - Candidates must link their bank account with Aadhaar for DBT transfers.  
> - For coaching, candidates must join the selected course within 6 months of the first installment release, or the amount must be returned to the government.  
> - The income limit of ₹2.5 lakh per annum is subject to change as per central government policies.  
> - Only online applications are accepted; offline applications are summarily rejected.  
> - For coaching under SEED, the income limit is ₹8 lakh per annum (higher than the general scheme limit).

### Application Process
The application process varies by component but follows a common online-only framework.

**General Steps:**
1. Assess eligibility based on DNT/NT/SNT status and family income ≤ ₹2.5 lakh/annum (₹8 lakh for coaching).
2. Apply online via the SEED portal:
   - Livelihood/Health/Housing: [https://dwbdnc.dosje.gov.in/register](https://dwbdnc.dosje.gov.in/register)
   - Coaching: [coaching.dosje.gov.in](https://coaching.dosje.gov.in)
3. Upload required documents (Aadhaar, income certificate, caste certificate, bank details, etc.).
4. Applications are scrutinized in order of merit.
5. Selected candidates receive an 'Open Assurance' letter.
6. For coaching: First installment (50% of course fee) released via DBT upon admission confirmation; remaining fee reimbursed after joining institute and submitting proof.
7. For livelihood: SHGs receive revolving fund support; individuals get assistance for bank account linkage, skill training, and marketing support.
8. For health: Facilitate enrollment in PMJAY for ₹5 lakh annual coverage.
9. For housing: Assist in availing benefits under PMAY-G/U.

> **> Critical Warnings**  
> - No offline applications are accepted.  
> - Bank account must be linked with Aadhaar for DBT transfers.  
> - For coaching, admission must be confirmed within 6 months of first installment release, or funds must be returned.  
> - Affidavit required stating no benefits availed from similar Central/State schemes.
