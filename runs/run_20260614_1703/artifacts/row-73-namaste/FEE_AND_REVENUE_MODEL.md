## Pricing Principles
1. **Value-Based Alignment**: Fees are calibrated to the actual financial benefit delivered under the NAMASTE scheme, which provides upfront capital subsidies of up to ₹10 lakh per individual unit for Sewer and Septic Tank Workers (SSWs) and Waste Pickers, and up to ₹25 lakh for group projects.
2. **Risk-Adjusted Pricing**: Success fees reflect the scheme’s moderate risk profile—processing involves profiling drives, e-KYC validation, and project approvals, with typical approval timelines of 3–6 months based on evidence of phased implementation and annual database updates.
3. **Accessibility for Target Beneficiaries**: The Starter and Growth tiers align with the scheme’s eligibility criteria targeting individual SSWs, Waste Pickers, and small sanitation enterprises, ensuring affordability for micro and small entities.
4. **Transparency and Compliance**: All fees are structured to comply with government consulting norms, with clear delineation between retainer (for application preparation and profiling support) and success fee (tied to subsidy disbursement).
