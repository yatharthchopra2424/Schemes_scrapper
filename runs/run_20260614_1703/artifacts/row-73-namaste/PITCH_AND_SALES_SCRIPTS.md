# Pitch & Sales Scripts

## Philosophy
We are advisors, not salespeople. We diagnose first. We only pitch a scheme if it fits the client. We never oversell success rates.

---

## 1. Discovery Call Script (First Contact — 20 min)

### Opening (2 min)
Good morning/afternoon. I’m calling from [Your Firm Name], and we specialize in helping sanitation workers and waste pickers access government support schemes like the **NAMASTE Scheme**—National Action for Mechanised Sanitation Ecosystem. This scheme is designed specifically for individuals like you who are engaged in sewer and septic tank cleaning or waste picking, aiming to improve safety, dignity, and livelihood through training, PPE, health insurance, and capital subsidies. Before we explore whether this fits your situation, may I ask a few quick questions to understand your work and needs better?

### Discovery Questions
1. Are you currently engaged in cleaning sewers, septic tanks, or collecting and sorting solid waste for a living?  
2. How long have you been working in this role—have you been doing waste picking or sewer/septic tank work for at least 6 months?  
3. Are you working independently, through a contractor, or are you affiliated with a ULB, parastatal agency, or a Private Sanitation Service Organisation (PSSO)?  
4. Do you have a valid Aadhaar card, Voter ID, or any other government-issued ID for identity verification?  
5. Have you participated in any profiling camps organized by your local Urban Local Body (ULB) or Gram Panchayat to get a NAMASTE ID?  
6. Are you interested in receiving free PPE kits, health insurance under Ayushman Bharat, or training to use mechanized cleaning equipment?  
7. Would you like support to access loans or subsidies to purchase sanitation-related equipment like suction machines, E-rickshaws, or safety gear?  
8. Are you open to joining a Self-Help Group (SHG) or cooperative to access group benefits like capital subsidy for waste collection vehicles?

### Qualification Checklist (internal — don't read aloud)
- [ ] Engaged in sewer/septic tank cleaning (SSW) or waste picking (minimum 6 months)  
- [ ] Works independently, via contractor, ULB/parastatal, PSSO, or private operator  
- [ ] Possesses valid government ID (Aadhaar, Voter ID, Driving License, etc.)  
- [ ] Located in India (Pan-India coverage)  
- [ ] Willing to participate in profiling and e-KYC via NAMASTE portal/app  
- [ ] Not currently enrolled in conflicting central scheme that duplicates benefits (to be verified)  
- [ ] Interested in at least one benefit: PPE, health insurance, training, capital subsidy, or livelihood support  

### Transition to Pitch
Based on your responses, it looks like you may be eligible for the **NAMASTE Scheme**, which offers up to **Rs. 10 lakh in upfront capital subsidy** for sanitation-related equipment, along with free PPE, health insurance, and training. Let me share how this scheme works and how we’ve helped others like you access these benefits.

## 2. Scheme Pitch (5 min)

### Structure: Problem → Scheme → Value → Proof → Next Step

**Problem framing:**  
Sanitation workers face life-threatening risks daily—exposure to toxic gases, lack of protective gear, informal work without social security, and limited access to financing for safer equipment. Many want to mechanize their work or form collectives but lack the capital, training, or recognition to do so safely and sustainably.

**Scheme intro:**  
The **National Action for Mechanised Sanitation Ecosystem (NAMASTE)** is a Central Sector Scheme implemented by the **National Safai Karamcharis Finance & Development Corporation (NSKFDC)** under the Ministry of Social Justice & Empowerment. It provides an **upfront capital subsidy of 50% of project cost, up to Rs. 10 lakh per unit** for individual Sewer and Septic Tank Workers (SSWs) and **up to Rs. 5 lakh per member (max Rs. 25 lakh per group)** for group projects. It also includes free PPE kits, health insurance under Ayushman Bharat, occupational safety training, and linkage to subsidized loans.

**Why they qualify (personalised):**  
- You work as a sewer/septic tank worker or waste picker with at least 6 months of experience  
- You possess a valid government ID (Aadhaar/Voter ID) required for e-KYC and NAMASTE ID generation  
- You are open to receiving training, PPE, or financial support to improve safety and livelihood  
- You operate in India, where the scheme is active across all Urban Local Bodies and rural areas
