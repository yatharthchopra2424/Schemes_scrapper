### NAMASTE
- **Scheme ID:** SCH-001
- **Category:** Subsidy
- **Geography:** Pan-India
- **Administering Body:** National Safai Karamcharis Finance & Development Corporation (NSKFDC)
- **Portal / Application URL:** https://namastescheme.com/
- **Current Status:** Rolling
- **Window:** Always Open

#### Eligibility Criteria
- Business type: Sewer and Septic Tank Workers (SSWs), Manual Scavengers, Waste Pickers, Sanitation workers
- Age of business: Not mentioned
- Sector: Sanitation sector
- Revenue cap: Not mentioned
- Employee count: Not mentioned
- Other: Must be engaged in cleaning and maintenance of sewers including managing pumping stations, manholes/machine holes, and sewer lines; or engaged in septic tank cleaning; or informally engaged in collection and recovery of reusable and recyclable solid waste for a minimum period of 6 months

#### What It Offers
- Amount / Benefit: Upfront capital subsidy to SSWs: 50% of project cost, maximum Rs. 10.00 lakh per unit; for group projects: maximum per member subsidy Rs. 5 lakh, maximum group subsidy Rs. 25 lakh. Upfront Capital Subsidy to PSSOs and Private Contractors: 25% of project cost, maximum Rs. 10.00 lakh per unit.
- Disbursement mode: Direct credit
- Duration of benefit: One-time

#### Documents Required
1. Aadhaar Card
2. Voter ID Card
3. Driving License
4. Any other government issued valid ID proof

#### Application Process (numbered steps)
1. Identify and profile Sewer/Septic Tank Workers (SSWs) and Waste Pickers through profiling camps conducted by Urban Local Bodies (ULBs) in urban areas and Gram Panchayats in rural areas, with assistance of civil society organizations, cooperatives, self-help groups (SHGs), Common Service Centres (CSCs) and Waste Pickers collectives.
2. Use the NAMASTE mobile app or web portal for data collection during profiling.
3. Conduct e-KYC of Aadhaar IDs of waste pickers/SSWs for validation.
4. Submit survey data through the portal; it undergoes error flagging by Block Nodal Officer/Validators, with errors to be resolved within 7 days.
5. After validation, a unique NAMASTE ID is generated for each validated worker.
6. Block Nodal Officer distributes ID cards to all validated workers.
7. Access benefits such as PPE kits, health insurance under AB-PMJAY, occupational safety training, and capital subsidy for sanitation-related equipment through Swachhta Udyami Yojana (SUY) or direct subsidy for sanitation-related projects.
8. For capital subsidy, submit project proposal with required documents to the concerned authority for approval and disbursement.
