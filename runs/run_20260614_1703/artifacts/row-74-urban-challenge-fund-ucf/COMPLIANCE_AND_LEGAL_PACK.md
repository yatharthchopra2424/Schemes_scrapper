**Scheme-specific note:** This NDA covers all materials submitted as part of the Urban Challenge Fund

the termination of this NDA.

Confidential Information of the Disclosing Party.

**Data Handling Standards:**
- All client data is stored in encrypted cloud storage with AES-256 encryption.
- Two-factor authentication (2FA) is mandatory for all system access.
- Access to client data is limited to authorized personnel only on a need-to-know basis.
- Use of personal devices for accessing or storing client data is strictly prohibited.
- Data is retained for two (2) years post case closure, after which it is securely deleted.
- Any data breach must be reported to the designated compliance officer within 24 hours.
- Regular security audits and penetration testing are conducted quarterly.
- Employees receive mandatory data protection and privacy training annually.
