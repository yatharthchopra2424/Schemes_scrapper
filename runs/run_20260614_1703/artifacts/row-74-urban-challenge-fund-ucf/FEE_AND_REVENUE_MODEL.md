## Pricing Principles
1. **Value-Aligned Pricing**: Fees are structured as a percentage of the approved grant amount, ensuring consultant compensation scales with client benefit.
2. **Risk-Adjusted Margins**: Success fees reflect the scheme’s low confidence rating and extended processing timelines, which increase delivery risk and resource intensity.
3. **Transparency & Predictability**: Retainers cover initial eligibility assessment and proposal preparation; success fees are invoiced only upon formal fund disbursement.
4. **Scheme-Specific Note**: The Urban Challenge Fund (UCF) targets large-scale urban infrastructure projects typically undertaken by municipal corporations, public sector undertakings, or private consortia with significant turnover and project scale. Given its focus on high-value urban development initiatives and the evidence indicating applicability to large entities, only the Enterprise tier is relevant. The low confidence rating and lack of detailed eligibility or financial benefit data suggest higher uncertainty in outcomes, warranting careful scoping and potentially higher retainer engagement to mitigate risk.
