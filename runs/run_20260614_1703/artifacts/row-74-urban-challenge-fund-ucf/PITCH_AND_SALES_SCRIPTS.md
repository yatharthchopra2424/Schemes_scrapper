# Pitch & Sales Scripts

## Philosophy
We are advisors, not salespeople. We diagnose first. We only pitch a scheme if it fits the client. We never oversell success rates.

---

## 1. Discovery Call Script (First Contact — 20 min)

### Opening (2 min)
Good morning/afternoon, I’m reaching out because I specialise in helping urban infrastructure projects access government funding like the Urban Challenge Fund (UCF), which supports innovative solutions for city challenges such as mobility, waste management, and public space revitalisation. I’d like to understand if your initiative aligns with this opportunity — no obligation, just a quick conversation to see if we can help.

### Discovery Questions
- What specific urban challenge is your project aiming to address (e.g., traffic congestion, air quality, affordable housing, smart utilities)?
- When was your organisation or project legally incorporated or initiated?
- Are you working in partnership with a municipal corporation, urban local body, or other public sector entity?
- Is your project focused on piloting or scaling an innovative technology, service, or model in an urban setting?
- Do you have a clear implementation plan with defined milestones and outcomes over a 12–24 month period?
- What stage of development is your project at — idea, prototype, pilot, or ready for scale?
- Are you seeking financial support to cover capital expenditure, operational costs, or both for urban deployment?
- Is your project located within or directly benefiting a designated urban area or smart city mission zone?

### Qualification Checklist (internal — don't read aloud)
- [ ] Project addresses a defined urban infrastructure challenge (mobility, environment, housing, utilities, etc.)
- [ ] Applicant is a legal entity (startup, NGO, SPV, or consortium) incorporated in India
- [ ] Project involves collaboration with an Urban Local Body (ULB), municipal corporation, or parastatal agency
- [ ] Innovation is demonstrated in technology, service delivery, financing, or governance model
- [ ] Project has a clear urban focus and is located within or benefits a municipal jurisdiction
- [ ] Implementation timeline is feasible (typically 12–24 months)
- [ ] Funding request aligns with UCF’s support for piloting, scaling, or demonstration phases
- [ ] Applicant can provide necessary documentation (incorporation, MoUs, technical design, financial plan)

### Transition to Pitch
Based on what you’ve shared, it sounds like your project tackles a real urban challenge with innovative thinking — exactly the kind of initiative the Urban Challenge Fund (UCF) was created to support. UCF offers grant-based funding to help urban innovators like you de-risk and scale solutions in partnership with cities. Let me walk you through how it works.

## 2. Scheme Pitch (5 min)

### Structure: Problem → Scheme → Value → Proof → Next Step

**Problem framing:**
Urban innovators often struggle to move beyond the prototype stage due to lack of funding for real-world testing, difficulty navigating municipal procurement, and high perceived risk from investors. Many promising solutions for traffic, waste, water, or housing never get deployed at scale because they can’t secure the early-stage capital needed to pilot in live urban environments.

**Scheme intro:**
The Urban Challenge Fund (UCF), administered by the Ministry of Housing and Urban Affairs (MoHUA), provides grant-based financial support of up to ₹2 crore per project to startups, NGOs, SPVs, and consortia that are piloting or scaling innovative urban infrastructure solutions in partnership with Urban Local Bodies (ULBs). The fund focuses on de-risking innovation through co-financing and technical validation in real city contexts.

**Why they qualify (personalised):**
- Your project addresses a core urban challenge such as mobility, waste management, or public space improvement — a key UCF priority  
- You are either already collaborating with or plan to partner with a municipal corporation or ULB — a mandatory eligibility criterion  
- Your solution demonstrates innovation in technology, service model, or financing approach — aligning with UCF’s focus on novel approaches  
- You are seeking support for piloting, demonstration, or scaling phases — which UCF is specifically designed to fund

"We've helped [N] similar businesses get approved for this. [TO BE FILLED BY CONSULTANT]"

**Next step:**
We offer a free, no-obligation eligibility check — we’ll review your project details against UCF’s criteria and let you know if you’re a strong fit. If so, we can support you end-to-end: from proposal development to ULB coordination and submission. Our fee is success-based — you only pay if we help you secure the grant.
