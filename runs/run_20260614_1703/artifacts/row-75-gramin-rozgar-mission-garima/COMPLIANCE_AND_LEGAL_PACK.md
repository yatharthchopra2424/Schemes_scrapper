## 8D. Internal Data Handling Policy

(Standard data handling policy — encrypted cloud storage, 2FA, limited access, no personal devices,
2-year retention post case closure, 24-hour breach notification.)

Add a scheme-specific note:
"All documents submitted to Ministry of Rural Development under Gramin Rozgar Mission (GARIMA)
must be retained for a minimum of [3 years / as per scheme guidelines from evidence] for audit purposes."

**Purpose:** To ensure the secure, confidential, and compliant handling of client data throughout
the engagement lifecycle.

**Data Storage:** All client data shall be stored in encrypted cloud storage with access controls.
Encryption at rest and in transit is mandatory.

**Access Control:** Access to client data is limited to authorized personnel only. Role-based
access controls (RBAC) shall be enforced.

**Authentication:** Two-factor authentication (2FA) is required for all access to systems
containing client data.

**Device Policy:** No personal devices may be used to access, store, or transmit client data.
Only firm-issued, secured devices are permitted.

**Retention:** Client data shall be retained for two (2) years post case closure, after which
it shall be securely deleted unless longer retention is required by law or scheme-specific
obligations.

**Scheme-specific note:** All documents submitted to Ministry of Rural Development under
Gramin Rozgar Mission (GARIMA) must be retained for a minimum of [3 years / as per scheme
guidelines from evidence] for audit purposes.

**Breach Notification:** In the event of a data breach, the firm shall notify affected clients
and relevant authorities within 24 hours of discovery.

**Audit & Review:** This policy shall be reviewed annually or upon material change in
regulatory requirements. Internal audits shall be conducted quarterly.

**Compliance:** This policy aligns with the Information Technology Act, 2000, and the
SPD Rules, 2011, and is consistent with best practices for data protection in India.
