## Pricing Principles
1. **Value-Aligned Pricing**: Fees are structured to reflect the tangible economic benefit delivered to clients, ensuring affordability while maintaining sustainable margins for the consulting firm.
2. **Risk-Adjusted Margins**: Success fees incorporate the inherent uncertainty in scheme approval timelines and outcomes, with lower percentages for longer or less predictable processes.
3. **Transparency & Trust**: All fee components (retainer and success-based) are clearly communicated upfront via standardized invoices, eliminating hidden costs and building client confidence.
4. **Scheme-Specific Note**: The Gramin Rozgar Mission (GARIMA) lacks detailed public data on processing times and approval rates (confidence: low), indicating higher execution risk. Therefore, success fees should be set at the higher end of applicable ranges to compensate for uncertain delivery timelines and limited evidence of success from crawled sources.
