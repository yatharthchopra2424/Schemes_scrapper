## Pricing Principles
1. **Value-Aligned Fees**: Success fees are tied to the actual subsidy disbursed (capped at ₹78,000 per household), ensuring clients pay only for realized benefits.
2. **Risk-Adjusted Pricing**: Given the scheme’s 3–6 month processing time (application to subsidy disbursement) and dependency on vendor empanelment and installation verification, success fees are structured to reflect implementation risk.
3. **Volume-Based Incentives**: Retainer models are designed for high-volume residential clients, with tiered pricing to support scalability across micro and individual household segments.
4. **Scheme-Specific Note**: The PM Surya Ghar scheme has a high success rate for eligible applicants who complete installation, but delays may occur due to state-level vendor empanelment or documentation gaps. Success fees are contingent on verified subsidy disbursement, not just application submission.

## Invoice Templates

### Retainer Invoice
**Description**: Retainer fee for consulting services related to PM Surya Ghar: Muft Bijli Yojana, including eligibility assessment, document preparation, application submission guidance, and follow-up with empanelled vendors and distribution companies.

### Success Fee Invoice
**Description**: Success fee for securing subsidy disbursement under PM Surya Ghar: Muft Bijli Yojana, implemented by the Ministry of New and Renewable Energy, upon verified receipt of the central financial subsidy (capped at ₹78,000 for systems above 3 kW).
