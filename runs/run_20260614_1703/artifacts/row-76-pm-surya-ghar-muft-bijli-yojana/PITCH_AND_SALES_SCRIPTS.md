# Pitch & Sales Scripts

## Philosophy
We are advisors, not salespeople. We diagnose first. We only pitch a scheme if it fits the client. We never oversell success rates.

---

## 1. Discovery Call Script (First Contact — 20 min)

### Opening (2 min)
Good morning/afternoon! I specialize in helping residential households across India access government support for rooftop solar installations through the PM Surya Ghar: Muft Bijli Yojana. This scheme, launched by the Ministry of New and Renewable Energy, provides substantial financial subsidies and free electricity benefits to make solar adoption affordable. Before we explore whether this fits your situation, may I ask a few quick questions to understand your current energy setup and goals?

### Discovery Questions
1. What is your average monthly electricity consumption in units (as shown on your latest bill)?
2. Do you own the residential property where you wish to install solar panels, and is it located in India?
3. Have you already registered on the PM Surya Ghar portal (pmsuryaghar.gov.in) or do you have your electricity consumer account number ready?
4. What is your current rooftop availability — do you have unshaded space suitable for solar panel installation?
5. Are you aware of the subsidy structure: Rs. 30,000 per kW for first 2 kW, Rs. 18,000 per kW for next kW (up to 3 kW), and capped at Rs. 78,000 for systems above 3 kW?
6. Does your state qualify for the additional 10% subsidy per kW (special states include northeastern states, hill states, and island territories)?
7. Are you planning to install the system yourself or through a vendor, and do you know if the vendor is empanelled under the scheme?
8. What is your primary goal — reducing electricity bills, gaining energy independence, or accessing the up to 300 units of free monthly electricity?

### Qualification Checklist (internal — don't read aloud)
- [ ] Applicant is a residential household (individual homeowner)
- [ ] Property is located in India (pan-India eligibility)
- [ ] Valid electricity consumer account number available
- [ ] Mobile number, email, name, address, state, district, and PIN code provided
- [ ] Application submitted via https://pmsuryaghar.gov.in/ (directly or through empanelled vendor)
- [ ] System size appropriately matched to consumption (1-2 kW for 0-150 units, 2-3 kW for 150-300 units, >3 kW for >300 units)
- [ ] For systems >3 kW, awareness of Rs. 78,000 subsidy cap
- [ ] For special states, awareness of additional 10% per kW subsidy
- [ ] No repeated module/inverter serial numbers in application (effective post-26 May 2026)
- [ ] Commitment to post-installation verification for subsidy disbursement

### Transition to Pitch
Based on your responses, it appears your household aligns well with the PM Surya Ghar: Muft Bijli Yojana — particularly given your electricity consumption and property ownership. This scheme can provide up to Rs. 78,000 in central financial subsidy and up to 300 units of free electricity monthly after installation. Would you like me to walk you through how it works and what support we offer?

## 2. Scheme Pitch (5 min)

### Structure: Problem → Scheme → Value → Proof → Next Step

**Problem framing:**
Residential households across India face rising electricity costs and unreliable grid supply, yet many hesitate to adopt rooftop solar due to high upfront installation costs. Despite long-term savings, the initial investment remains a barrier, especially for middle-income families seeking to reduce monthly bills and gain energy independence.

**Scheme intro:**
The PM Surya Ghar: Muft Bijli Yojana, administered by the Ministry of New and Renewable Energy, is a pan-India subsidy scheme offering central financial support for rooftop solar installations. It provides Rs. 30,000 per kW for the first 2 kW, Rs. 18,000 per kW for the next kW (up to 3 kW), and a capped total subsidy of Rs. 78,000 for systems larger than 3 kW. Beneficiaries also receive up to 300 units of free electricity monthly after successful installation and verification. Applications are submitted via the national portal at https://pmsuryaghar.gov.in/.
