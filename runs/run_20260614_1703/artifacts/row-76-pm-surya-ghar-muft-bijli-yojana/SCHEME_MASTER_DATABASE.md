### PM Surya Ghar: Muft Bijli Yojana
- **Scheme ID:** SCH-001
- **Category:** Subsidy
- **Geography:** Pan-India
- **Administering Body:** Ministry of New and Renewable Energy
- **Portal / Application URL:** https://pmsuryaghar.gov.in/
- **Current Status:** Open
- **Window:** Always Open (Scheme closes on 31 March 2027)

#### Eligibility Criteria
- Business type: Residential households
- Age of business: Not applicable (residential scheme)
- Sector: Residential solar energy
- Revenue cap: Not mentioned
- Employee count: Not mentioned
- Other: Must register on the national portal and provide valid consumer details including mobile number, name, email, address, state, district, PIN code, and electricity consumer account number

#### What It Offers
- Amount / Benefit: 
  - Rs. 30,000 per kW for first 2 kW
  - Rs. 18,000 per kW for additional capacity up to 3 kW
  - Capped total subsidy of Rs. 78,000 for systems above 3 kW
  - For GHS/RWA: Rs. 18,000 per kW for common facilities up to 500 kW
  - Special states: Additional 10% subsidy per kW
  - Up to 300 units of free electricity monthly after installation
- Disbursement mode: Reimbursement (after successful installation and verification)
- Duration of benefit: One-time subsidy + ongoing free electricity benefit

#### Documents Required
1. Valid consumer mobile number
2. Email ID
3. Consumer name
4. Address
5. State
6. District
7. PIN code
8. Electricity consumer account number

#### Application Process (numbered steps)
1. Visit the website at https://pmsuryaghar.gov.in/
2. Go to consumer page and select 'Apply Now' or open the Login dropdown and select 'Consumer Login'
3. Enter valid registered consumer mobile number, enter captcha, select 'Yes, I have read all the guidelines of PM Surya Ghar Muft Bijli Yojana', then click 'Verify'
4. Enter valid mobile OTP received via SMS and click 'Login'
5. After successful login, enter profile details (consumer name, email, address, state, district, PIN code) and click 'Save'
6. Submit application either by clicking 'Apply for Solar Rooftop' or through vendor selection. Select state, district, electricity distribution company/utility, enter consumer account number, click 'Fetch Details', then click 'Next' to start filling the application
