## 1. Executive Summary
Based on our initial review of [Client]'s business profile, we have identified PM-KUSUM — a subsidy scheme administered by designated departments of the State Government — for which your business appears eligible. The potential benefit is approximately up to 50% of the total project cost for eligible components such as standalone solar pumps, solarization of grid-connected agricultural pumps, or installation of grid-connected solar power plants up to 2MW on barren/fallow land.

## 3. Our Scope of Work
1. Initial eligibility assessment based on client’s landholding, agricultural activity, and proposed solar installation type.  
2. Guidance on selecting the appropriate component (A, B, or C) of PM-KUSUM based on client objectives.  
3. Assistance in preparing and collating required documents as per state-level guidelines.  
4. Support in registering on the official PM-KUSUM portal: https://pmkusum.mnre.gov.in/  
5. Liaison with designated State Government departments for application submission.  
6. Follow-up on application status and clarification of queries from implementing authorities.  
7. Post-approval support for installation coordination and commissioning (where applicable).  

*Note: A Detailed Project Report (DPR) or project report is required for grid-connected solar power plants (Component A) and may be required for other components based on state-specific guidelines.*

## 4. What We Need From You
1. Documents as per the scheme’s document list: land ownership/lease documents, identity proof (Aadhaar), bank account details, recent electricity bill (for solarization cases), and technical details of the proposed solar system (capacity, type, vendor quotes).  
2. A designated point of contact from your side for coordination and document sharing.  
3. Timely responses within a 2-day SLA to avoid delays in application processing.
