## Pricing Principles
1. **Value-Aligned Fees**: Success fees are tied to the actual subsidy disbursed under PM-KUSUM, ensuring clients pay only for realized benefits.
2. **Risk-Adjusted Pricing**: Given the scheme’s implementation through State Government departments and variable processing times (evidenced by timelines for financial closure and completion extensions), retainer fees cover advisory work during uncertain approval cycles.
3. **Transparency**: All fees are disclosed upfront; clients receive itemized invoices referencing scheme components (A, B, C) and implementing agency.
4. **Scheme-Specific Note**: PM-KUSUM has a medium confidence rating in evidence and involves multi-stage approvals (state-level departments, DISCOM agreements). Processing delays are common, as seen in extensions for financial closure and project completion. This justifies a 3-month lag in success fee recognition and supports higher retainer engagement for ongoing compliance and liaison work.

## Invoice Templates

### Retainer Invoice
**Description**: Advisory services for eligibility assessment, document preparation, and application submission under the PM-KUSUM scheme (Pradhan Mantri Kisan Urja Suraksha evam Utthan Mahabhiyan), including liaison with designated State Government departments for Components A, B, and C.

### Success Fee Invoice
**Description**: Success fee for securing central government subsidy under PM-KUSUM, implemented by designated departments of the State Government, for installation of standalone solar pumps, solarization of grid-connected agricultural pumps, or grid-connected solar power plants up to 2MW on barren/fallow land.
