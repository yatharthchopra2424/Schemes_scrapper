## Pricing Principles
1. **Value-Based Pricing**: Fees are aligned with the financial benefit delivered (CFA subsidy per m³ for biogas plants), ensuring clients pay only for realized value.
2. **Risk-Sharing Model**: Success fees are contingent on verified installation and CFA disbursement, mitigating client risk given the scheme’s post-verification subsidy release.
3. **Tiered Accessibility**: Retainer structures accommodate varying client capacities — from MSMEs to large institutions — reflecting the scheme’s broad eligibility (individuals, farmers, institutions, MSMEs, NGOs).
4. **Scheme-Specific Note**: The National Bio Energy Programme - Biogas has a moderate risk profile due to its dependency on post-installation verification and fund availability; however, the rolling application basis and clear CFA release milestones (after inspection) support predictable revenue lag assumptions.
