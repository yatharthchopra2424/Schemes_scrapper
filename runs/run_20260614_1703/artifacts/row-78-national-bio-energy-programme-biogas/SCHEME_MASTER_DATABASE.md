### National Bio Energy Programme - Biogas
- **Scheme ID:** SCH-001
- **Category:** Subsidy
- **Geography:** Pan-India
- **Administering Body:** Ministry of New and Renewable Energy (MNRE)
- **Portal / Application URL:** https://mnre.gov.in/en/bio-gas/
- **Current Status:** Rolling
- **Window:** Always Open

#### Eligibility Criteria
- Business type: Any individual, farmers, institutions, industries, or NGOs
- Age of business: Not specified
- Sector: Not specified
- Revenue cap: Not specified
- Employee count: Not specified
- Other: Applicant must be the owner or authorized representative of the site where the biogas plant is to be installed; for community/institutional plants, the applying entity must have legal ownership or long-term usage rights of the land; the proposed biogas plant must conform to MNRE-approved designs and specifications; the beneficiary must contribute the beneficiary share as per the scheme guidelines

#### What It Offers
- Amount / Benefit: For family-size biogas plants (1 to 25 m³ capacity), CFA is ₹9,000 per m³ for general category and ₹12,000 per m³ for North Eastern States, including Sikkim; for community/institutional biogas plants (above 25 m³ capacity), CFA is 30% of the benchmark cost, with higher rates of 50% for North Eastern States, hilly areas, and islands
- Disbursement mode: Direct credit (released in instalments after verification of plant installation and functionality directly into the beneficiary's bank account)
- Duration of benefit: One-time (subsidy released after installation and verification)

#### Documents Required
1. Application form for biogas plant
2. Proof of land ownership or usage rights
3. Identity proof of applicant (Aadhaar, Voter ID, etc.)
4. Bank account details (passbook or cancelled cheque)
5. Site plan and layout of the proposed biogas plant
6. Beneficiary share contribution proof (if applicable)
7. Completion report and utilization certificate after installation
8. Photographs of the installed biogas plant (before and after)

#### Application Process (numbered steps)
1. Identify the appropriate biogas plant size and type based on requirement.
2. Contact the State Nodal Agency or Khadi and Village Industries Commission (KVIC) for guidance and application forms.
3. Submit the application along with required documents to the State Nodal Agency or implementing agency.
4. Obtain technical sanction and approval for the biogas plant design and site.
5. Install the biogas plant using an approved turnkey key worker or through self-construction with technical guidance.
6. Notify the implementing agency upon completion for inspection and verification.
7. After successful verification of plant functionality, submit the completion report and utilization certificate.
8. Receive disbursement of Central Financial Assistance (CFA) directly into the beneficiary's bank account.
