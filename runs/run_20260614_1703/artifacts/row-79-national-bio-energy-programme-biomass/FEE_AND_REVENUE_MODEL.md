## Pricing Principles
1. **Value-Based Pricing**: Fees are aligned with the financial benefit delivered—CFA under the National Bio Energy Programme - Biomass provides benchmark-cost-based support for bioenergy projects, enabling scalable revenue models.
2. **Risk Sharing**: Success fees (6–8% for typical MSME/startup clients) reflect moderate risk due to the 100-day in-principle approval timeline and requirement for performance bank guarantees (5% of approved CFA).
3. **Transparency**: All fee structures are disclosed upfront; retainers cover initial assessment and application preparation, while success fees are contingent on CFA approval.
4. **Scheme-Specific Note**: The National Bio Energy Programme - Biomass has a moderate risk profile—processing time of 100 days for in-principle approval is predictable, and evidence shows consistent CFA disbursement for eligible biomass/WTE projects, reducing uncertainty for consultants and clients.
