# Pitch & Sales Scripts

## Philosophy
We are advisors, not salespeople. We diagnose first. We only pitch a scheme if it fits the client. We never oversell success rates.

---

## 1. Discovery Call Script (First Contact — 20 min)

### Opening (2 min)
Good morning/afternoon! I specialize in helping biomass and waste-to-energy project developers access government subsidies under the National Bio Energy Programme - Biomass. Given your work in renewable energy, I’d like to understand if this scheme could support your bioenergy project goals—particularly around setting up biomass utilization, pellet/briquette manufacturing, or waste-to-energy initiatives.

### Discovery Questions
1. What type of bioenergy project are you developing—biomass, waste-to-energy (WTE), or biogas?
2. Have you incorporated your organization or registered as an individual, industry, cooperative, or government/semi-government agency?
3. Are you planning to submit your application online via the Biourja portal for WTE or Biomass programmes?
4. What is the estimated benchmark cost of your bioenergy project, as CFA is based on this?
5. Do you have plans to display signage stating 'Project is funded under National Bio Energy Programme' at the project site?
6. Are you prepared to submit a Performance Bank Guarantee of 5% of the approved CFA upon project acceptance?
7. Will all documents for CFA release be signed by you or an authorized representative?
8. Is your project located anywhere in India, as the scheme has pan-India geographic scope?

### Qualification Checklist (internal — don't read aloud)
- [ ] Applicant is an individual, organization, industry, or government/semi-government agency
- [ ] For WTE and Biomass programmes: applicant submits online application on Biourja portal
- [ ] For Biogas programme: applicant submits online application on Biogas portal and physical application to Programme Implementing Agency
- [ ] CFA is exclusively earmarked for the project and not diverted
- [ ] Prepared to submit Performance Bank Guarantee of 5% of approved CFA
- [ ] Final instalment of CFA released after comparison of approved and actual cost (lower amount considered)
- [ ] Signage to be prominently displayed: 'Project is funded under National Bio Energy Programme'
- [ ] All documents for CFA release signed by applicant or authorized representative

### Transition to Pitch
Based on your responses, it appears your [biomass/WTE/biogas] project aligns well with the National Bio Energy Programme - Biomass, which offers Central Financial Assistance (CFA) based on benchmark costs for setting up such projects. Shall I walk you through how this scheme works and the support it provides?

## 2. Scheme Pitch (5 min)

### Structure: Problem → Scheme → Value → Proof → Next Step

**Problem framing:**
Project developers in biomass, waste-to-energy, and biogas sectors face high upfront capital costs and lack access to targeted subsidies, making it difficult to set up bioenergy projects despite India’s push for renewable energy mix and pellet/briquette manufacturing support.

**Scheme intro:**
The National Bio Energy Programme - Biomass, administered by the Ministry of New and Renewable Energy (MNRE), provides Central Financial Assistance (CFA) for setting up bioenergy projects. For WTE and Biomass programmes, CFA is based on benchmark costs; for Biogas, it follows scheme guidelines. State Implementing Agencies (SIAs) are eligible for service charges of 1.33% of eligible CFA. Applications are submitted via the Biourja portal (WTE/Biomass) or Biogas portal (Biogas), with in-principle approval within 100 days.

**Why they qualify (personalised):**
- You are developing a [biomass/WTE/biogas] project, which falls under the scheme’s covered programmes
- You are an [individual/organization/industry/government agency], making you eligible to apply
- You plan to apply online via the Biourja/Biogas portal as required
- You are located in India, satisfying the pan-India geographic scope

We offer a free eligibility check with no commitment—you only pay a success fee if we help secure approval. Shall we schedule a 30-minute document review to assess your project’s fit?
