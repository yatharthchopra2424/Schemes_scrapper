### National Bio Energy Programme - Biomass
- **Scheme ID:** SCH-001
- **Category:** Subsidy
- **Geography:** Pan-India
- **Administering Body:** Ministry of New and Renewable Energy (MNRE)
- **Portal / Application URL:** https://biourja.mnre.gov.in
- **Current Status:** Rolling
- **Window:** Always Open

#### Eligibility Criteria
- Business type: Any individual, organization, industry, or government/semi-government agency
- Age of business: Not specified
- Sector: Bioenergy (Biomass, Waste to Energy, Biogas)
- Revenue cap: Not specified
- Employee count: Not specified
- Other: For WTE and Biomass programmes, project developers must submit online applications on the Biourja portal; for Biogas programme, applicants must submit online application on the Biogas portal and physical application to the Programme Implementing Agency under the Biourja portal

#### What It Offers
- Amount / Benefit: Central Financial Assistance (CFA) based on benchmark costs for WTE and Biomass programmes; CFA as per scheme guidelines for Biogas programme; State Implementing Agencies (SIAs) eligible for service charges of 1.33% of eligible CFA
- Disbursement mode: Direct credit (CFA released after comparison of approved and actual cost, with lower amount considered for re-calculation)
- Duration of benefit: One-time (for project setup)

#### Documents Required
1. Online application submitted on respective portal (Biourja for WTE/Biomass, Biogas for Biogas)
2. Performance Bank Guarantee of 5% of approved CFA (for selected applicants)
3. Project acceptance documents
4. Documents for CFA release signed by applicant or authorized representative
5. Proof of signage prominently displayed on front of project stating: 'Project is funded under National Bio Energy Programme'

#### Application Process (numbered steps)
1. Visit the respective portal: WTE (https://mnre.gov.in/en/waste-to-energy/), Biomass (https://mnre.gov.in/en/bio-mass/), or Biogas (https://mnre.gov.in/en/bio-gas/)
2. Submit online application on the Biourja portal for WTE and Biomass programmes
3. For Biogas programme, submit online application on the Biogas portal and physical application to the Programme Implementing Agency under the Biourja portal
4. Await in-principle approval of CFA within 100 days
