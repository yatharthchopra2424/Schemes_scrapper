## Pricing Principles
1. **Value-Based Alignment**: Fees are calibrated to the scheme’s actual benefit structure, which focuses on non-monetary support such as linguistic inclusivity, startup-government collaboration, and digital empowerment rather than direct financial disbursement.
2. **Risk-Adjusted Pricing**: Given the scheme’s emphasis on collaboration and innovation with no specified financial support mechanism, success fees reflect moderate risk due to uncertain timelines and outcome measurability.
3. **Tiered Accessibility**: Pricing tiers are designed to match the scheme’s target beneficiaries — primarily startups and government agencies — ensuring accessibility for early-stage entities while capturing value from scalable engagements.
4. **Transparency & Trust**: All fees are disclosed upfront, with clear linkage to deliverables such as scheme navigation, application support, and collaboration facilitation under Digital India Bhashini.
