## 8D. Internal Data Handling Policy

All client data related to scheme consulting engagements shall be handled in accordance with the following standards:

- **Storage:** Encrypted cloud storage with access controls.
- **Authentication:** Two-factor authentication (2FA) required for all system access.
- **Access Control:** Limited access on a need-to-know basis; role-based permissions enforced.
- **Device Policy:** No personal devices permitted for accessing or storing client data.
- **Retention:** Client data retained for two (2) years post case closure, unless otherwise required by law or scheme-specific obligations.
- **Breach Notification:** Any suspected or confirmed data breach must be reported internally within 24 hours; affected clients notified per applicable data protection laws.

**Scheme-specific note:** All documents submitted to Ministry of New and Renewable Energy under Grid Connected Rooftop Solar Programme Phase-II must be retained for a minimum of 3 years for audit purposes.
