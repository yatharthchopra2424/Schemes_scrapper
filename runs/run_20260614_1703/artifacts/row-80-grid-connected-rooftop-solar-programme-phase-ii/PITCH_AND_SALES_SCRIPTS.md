# Pitch & Sales Scripts

## Philosophy
We are advisors, not salespeople. We diagnose first. We only pitch a scheme if it fits the client. We never oversell success rates.

---

## 1. Discovery Call Script (First Contact — 20 min)

### Opening (2 min)
Good morning/afternoon. I specialise in helping commercial and industrial businesses in India access government incentives for renewable energy adoption, particularly the Grid Connected Rooftop Solar Programme Phase-II. Many businesses like yours are looking to reduce electricity costs and meet sustainability goals but aren’t aware of the financial support available. I’d like to understand your current energy setup and see if this programme could be a fit — no obligation, just a quick diagnostic.

### Discovery Questions
- What is your current monthly electricity expenditure for your facility?
- Do you own the rooftop space at your commercial or industrial premises, or is it leased?
- What is the approximate available shadow-free rooftop area (in square metres) suitable for solar panel installation?
- Is your facility connected to the grid, and do you have a sanctioned load from your DISCOM?
- Are you looking to install a new rooftop solar system, or expand an existing one?
- What is the primary goal for considering solar — cost reduction, carbon neutrality, or compliance with ESG requirements?
- Has your business undertaken any capital expenditure on energy efficiency or renewable energy in the past 24 months?
- Are you aware of any state-level solar policies or net metering regulations applicable in your state?

### Qualification Checklist (internal — don't read aloud)
- [ ] Applicant is a commercial, industrial, or institutional entity (not residential)
- [ ] Rooftop solar system is grid-connected
- [ ] System capacity is within eligible range (typically 1 kW to 500 kW for C&I under Phase-II)
- [ ] Beneficiary owns or has legal rights to use the rooftop
- [ ] Project is new (not already installed or commissioned)
- [ ] Located in India
- [ ] Compliant with technical standards (MNRE/BIS)
- [ ] Net metering or gross metering arrangement in place or planned

### Transition to Pitch
Based on your responses, it looks like your facility has the rooftop space and grid connection needed to benefit from the Grid Connected Rooftop Solar Programme Phase-II. This scheme offers central financial assistance to reduce the upfront cost of installation — let me walk you through how it works and what it could mean for your energy savings.

## 2. Scheme Pitch (5 min)

### Structure: Problem → Scheme → Value → Proof → Next Step

**Problem framing:**
Commercial and industrial businesses in India face rising electricity tariffs and increasing pressure to reduce carbon emissions. Upfront capital costs for rooftop solar installations often deter adoption, despite long-term savings and sustainability benefits.

**Scheme intro:**
The Grid Connected Rooftop Solar Programme Phase-II, administered by the Ministry of New and Renewable Energy (MNRE), provides Central Financial Assistance (CFA) in the form of a subsidy for grid-connected rooftop solar systems installed on commercial, industrial, and institutional buildings. The subsidy is ₹4,000 per kW for systems up to 10 kW and ₹2,500 per kW for systems between 10 kW and 100 kW, with a maximum capped amount per beneficiary.

**Why they qualify (personalised):**
- You own or control the rooftop space at your commercial/industrial facility  
- Your system will be grid-connected with net metering arrangement  
- Your proposed system size falls within the eligible range (1 kW – 100 kW for CFA applicability)  
- The installation is new and not previously commissioned
