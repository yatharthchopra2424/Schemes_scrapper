## Pricing Principles
1. Value-based pricing aligned with the financial benefit derived from ESCert trading.
2. Transparent fee structure with clear retainer and success fee components.
3. Risk-adjusted pricing reflecting the scheme’s annual processing cycle and verification lag.
4. Custom pricing for Enterprise clients due to complex PAT cycle compliance and large-scale ESCert volumes.

**Scheme-specific note:** The Carbon Credit Trading Scheme 2023 operates on an annual PAT cycle basis, with ESCert issuance and trading occurring after target period completion and verification. This creates a inherent revenue lag, increasing risk for consultants whose success fees are contingent on ESCert issuance and sale. Processing time affects cash flow timing, necessitating retainer coverage for early-stage advisory work.
