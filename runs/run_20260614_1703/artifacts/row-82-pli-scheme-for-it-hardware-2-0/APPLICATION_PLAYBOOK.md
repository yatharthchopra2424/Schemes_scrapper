# Application Playbook

## How to Use This File
For each new client case, assign it a Case ID, pick the target scheme, and follow the steps below.

---

## Stage 1: Eligibility Verification (Day 1–3)

### Checklist
- [ ] **Registration Type**: Company registered in India (CIN required)
- [ ] **Turnover Threshold**: 
  - For Global Companies: Consolidated Global Manufacturing Revenue > INR 5,000 crore for target segment OR > INR 10,000 crore for electronics hardware products/sub-assemblies/components (FY 2021-22)
  - For Hybrid (Global/Domestic) Companies: Global arm revenue > INR 5,000 crore for target segment OR > INR 10,000 crore for electronics hardware; Domestic arm revenue > INR 10 crore for target segment OR > INR 20 crore for electronics hardware
  - For Domestic Companies: Consolidated Global Manufacturing Revenue > INR 10 crore for target segment OR > INR 20 crore for electronics hardware products/sub-assemblies/components
- [ ] **Employee Count**: No specific threshold mentioned; employment generation is a benefit but not an eligibility criterion
- [ ] **Sector**: Must propose to manufacture goods covered under Target Segment: Laptops, Tablets, All-in-One PCs, Servers, Ultra Small Form Factor (USSF)
- [ ] **Age of Business**: No minimum age specified; base year for eligibility is FY 2021-22 (for PLI 2.0)
- [ ] **Other Conditions**: 
  - Only one application per applicant allowed
  - Must meet incremental investment thresholds post-March 31, 2021 (or 2023 for PLI 2.0 migrants)
  - Must submit self-certified statement of consolidated global manufacturing revenue for FY 2021-22
  - Domestic companies defined as >50% capital beneficially owned by resident Indian citizens
  - For servers, must use firmware from Indian or other trusted foreign sources certified by MeitY
  - Land and building expenditure not covered under scheme for eligibility determination

## Stage 2: Document Collection (Day 3–10)

### Universal Documents (always required)
- Certificate of Incorporation
- PAN Card
- GST Registration Certificate
- ITR (Income Tax Returns) for last 3 years (2017-18, 2018-19, 2019-20)
- Bank Statements (last 6 months)
- Udyam Registration (MSME certificate, if applicable)

### Scheme-Specific Documents
- Authorization Letter from Board of Directors or Managing Director
- Certified Shareholding Pattern (latest submitted with ROC and certified by Company Secretary)
- Profile of Directors
- Key Managerial Personnel Details
- Annual Reports (2017-18, 2018-19, 2019-20)
- Self-Certification of Consolidated Global Manufacturing Revenue (Target Segment and EHP-SA-C)
- Self-Certification of Manufacturing Revenue of Group Companies
- Certificate of Sales of Manufactured Goods (Target Segment and Eligible Products)
- Business Profile of the Applicant
- Localization Plan
- Detailed Project Report (optional but recommended)
- Regulatory Approvals Information
- Application Fee Payment Proof (NEFT/RTGS to IFCI-PMA-PLI-ITHW account)
- Integrity Undertaking (Format A)
- Baseline Certificate for Net Sales and Investment as of 31.03.2021 (or relevant date)

### Document Quality Check
- All documents must be self-certified copies unless otherwise specified
- File size: PDFs under 10 MB; scanned copies legible and clear
- File naming: Use format `[DocumentName]_[ApplicantName]_[Date].pdf`
- Name matching: Applicant name must match exactly across all documents (Certificate of Incorporation, PAN, GST, etc.)
- Language: Documents in English or Hindi; if in other language, provide certified translation
- Certification: Authorization letter, shareholding pattern, and director profiles must be certified by Company Secretary or Managing Director
- Declarations: All self-certifications must be signed and dated by authorized signatory

## Stage 3: Application Drafting (Day 10–18)

### Project Report / DPR (if required)
- **DPR Status**: Optional (not mandatory)  
- **If required, standard sections**:  
  - Executive Summary  
  - Company Background and Promoters  
  - Proposed Products and Manufacturing Process  
  - Localization Plan (PCB Assembly, Battery Packs, Power Adapters, Enclosures)  
  - Investment Plan (Capex, Funding Sources)  
  - Sales Projection (Net Incremental Sales over Base Year)  
  - Employment Generation Plan  
  - Domestic Value Addition (DVA) Projection  
  - Risk Assessment and Mitigation  
  - Implementation Timeline  
  - Financial Projections (Profitability, Payback Period)  
  - Annexures: Certificates, Bank Details, Undertakings  

### Application Form Filling Protocol
1. **Use CIN as User ID**: Login at https://pliithw.com using CIN as user ID  
2. **Password Requirements**: Minimum 8 characters, alphanumeric, with at least one lowercase, one uppercase, one number, and one special character (@$!%*?&)  
3. **Complete All Mandatory Fields**: All fields in the application form are mandatory; leave no section blank  
4. **Accurate Financial Data**: Ensure figures in self-certified statements match audited annual reports (2017-18 to 2019-20)  
5. **Group Company Disclosure**: Clearly declare relationship, registration details, and manufacturing revenue of all group companies whose revenue is being considered  
6. **Baseline Certification**: Submit self-certified certificate for net sales of manufactured goods (Target Segment) and investment as of 31.03.2021 (or relevant date)  
7. **Localization Plan Details**: Specify components to be localized (PCB Assembly mandatory in Year 1; at least one additional component/year thereafter) with timelines and investment  
8. **Declaration and Undertaking**: Sign Integrity Undertaking (Format A) and declaration on data accuracy; false information may lead to rejection and legal action

## Stage 5: Post-Approval (Day 30–90+)

### Approval Actions
- [ ] Obtain and verify approval letter from PMA/MeitY stating:  
  - Name of Applicant  
  - Category (Global/Hybrid/Domestic)  
  - Proposed Product(s)  
  - Yearly Thresholds of Incremental Investment and Net Incremental Sales  
  - Yearly Incentive Projections (annual ceilings)  
  - Plan for Domestic Value Addition/Localization  
  - Plan for Employment Generation  
- [ ] Verify sanctioned amount against eligibility thresholds and incentive projection  
- [ ] Prepare for disbursement claim submission (quarterly/half-yearly/annual)  
- [ ] Ensure compliance with localization schedule (PCB Assembly in Year 1; at least one additional component/year thereafter)  
- [ ] Maintain records for:  
  - Investment capitalized for manufacturing  
  - Employment generation (on-roll, contractual, apprentice)  
  - Revenue from operations (domestic/export, net of credits/discounts/taxes)  
  - Domestic Value Addition (%) calculation  
  - Sub-assemblies localized (with vendor details if applicable)  
- [ ] Submit Quarterly Review Reports (QRRs) within 30 days of quarter end via portal  
- [ ] Prepare Integrity Undertaking (Format B) before disbursement of incentive  
- [ ] Coordinate with statutory auditor for reconciliation of claims with GST returns, IT returns, Form 3CEB, MCA records, and audited financial statements  
- [ ] Track utilization of incentive amount for approved purposes only  
- [ ] Plan for success fee invoicing upon receipt of disbursement (as per consultancy agreement)
