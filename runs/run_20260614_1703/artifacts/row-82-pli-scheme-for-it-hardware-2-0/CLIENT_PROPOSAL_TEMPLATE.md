## 3. Our Scope of Work
1. Initial eligibility assessment based on consolidated global manufacturing revenue and investment thresholds.
2. Document collection and verification as per the scheme's required checklist.
3. Online portal registration at https://pliithw.com using CIN as user ID.
4. Preparation and submission of self-certified statements for consolidated global manufacturing revenue.
5. Compilation and upload of audited financials and annual reports (2017-18, 2018-19, 2019-20).
6. Preparation of localization plan and project report on proposed sales, investment, and domestic value addition.
7. Application fee payment via NEFT/RTGS to IFCI-PMA-PLI-ITHW account and final submission.
