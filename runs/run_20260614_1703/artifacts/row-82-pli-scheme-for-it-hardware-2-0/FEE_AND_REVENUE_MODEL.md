## Pricing Principles
1. Retainer covers initial eligibility assessment, documentation preparation, and application submission.
2. Success fee is contingent on incentive approval and disbursement, aligning consultant incentives with client outcomes.
3. Tiered pricing reflects scheme complexity and client scale; this scheme targets large enterprises with significant manufacturing capacity.
4. Scheme-specific note: The PLI Scheme for IT Hardware 2.0 has a moderate risk profile due to its six-year tenure, annual verification requirements, and localization milestones. Processing time for approvals averages 3–6 months based on PMA review cycles, and success depends on meeting incremental sales and investment thresholds, which are verifiable through audited financials.
