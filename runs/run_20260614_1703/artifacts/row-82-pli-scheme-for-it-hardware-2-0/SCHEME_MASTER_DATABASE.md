### PLI Scheme for IT Hardware 2.0
- **Scheme ID:** SCH-001
- **Category:** Subsidy
- **Geography:** Pan-India
- **Administering Body:** Ministry of Electronics and Information Technology (MeitY)
- **Portal / Application URL:** https://pliithw.com/
- **Current Status:** Closed
- **Window:** Application window initially open till 31st July 2023, which may be extended or reopened based on industry response.

#### Eligibility Criteria
- Business type: Global Company; Hybrid (Global/Domestic) Company; Domestic Company
- Age of business: Not specified
- Sector: IT Hardware (Laptops, Tablets, All-in-One PCs, Servers, Ultra Small Form Factor)
- Revenue cap: 
  - Global Companies: Consolidated global manufacturing revenue > INR 5,000 crore for target segment or > INR 10,000 crore for electronics hardware products/sub-assemblies/components
  - Hybrid Companies: Global arm revenue > INR 5,000 crore for target segment or > INR 10,000 crore for electronics hardware; Domestic arm revenue > INR 10 crore for target segment or > INR 20 crore for electronics hardware
  - Domestic Companies: Revenue > INR 10 crore for target segment or > INR 20 crore for electronics hardware products/sub-assemblies/components
- Employee count: Not specified
- Other: 
  - Must meet incremental investment thresholds post-March 31, 2021
  - Must meet net incremental sales criteria
  - Only one application per applicant allowed
  - Domestic companies defined as those where >50% capital is beneficially owned by resident Indian citizens and/or Indian companies ultimately owned/controlled by resident Indian citizens
  - For servers, applicants must use firmware from Indian or other trusted foreign sources certified by MeitY
  - Land and building expenditure not covered under scheme for eligibility determination

#### What It Offers
- Amount / Benefit: 
  - Incentive rates: 4% in Year 1, 3% in Year 2, 2% in Year 3, and 2% or 1% in Year 4 depending on localization achievement
  - Annual incentive outlay: INR 321 crore in Year 1 to INR 6,118 crore in Year 6
  - Total expected incentive outlay over six years: INR 16,939 crore
  - Per company ceiling: 
    - Global Companies: INR 4,500 crore
    - Hybrid Companies: INR 2,250 crore
    - Domestic Companies: INR 500 crore
- Disbursement mode: Direct credit upon verification of sales and investment claims by Project Management Agency (PMA)
- Duration of benefit: Six (6) years

#### Documents Required
1. Certificate of Incorporation
2. PAN Card
3. GST Registration Certificate
4. Authorization Letter from Board of Directors or Managing Director
5. Certified Shareholding Pattern
6. Profile of Directors
7. Key Managerial Personnel Details
8. Annual Reports (2017-18, 2018-19, 2019-20)
9. Self-Certification of Consolidated Global Manufacturing Revenue (Target Segment and EHP-SA-C)
10. Self-Certification of Manufacturing Revenue of Group Companies
11. Certificate of Sales of Manufactured Goods (Target Segment and Eligible Products)
12. Business Profile of the Applicant
13. Localization Plan
14. Detailed Project Report (optional)
15. Regulatory Approvals Information

#### Application Process (numbered steps)
1. Register on the PLI IT Hardware portal at https://pliithw.com using CIN as user ID
2. Login and fill the application form with company details, including CIN, PAN, and authorized signatory information
3. Upload required documents: Certificate of Incorporation, PAN, GST registration, authorization letter, business profile, and audited financials
4. Submit self-certified statement of consolidated global manufacturing revenue for FY 2021-22 (or relevant base year) for target segment and electronics hardware products/sub-assemblies/components
5. Provide details of group companies whose revenue is being considered for qualification
6. Submit baseline certificates for net sales and investment as of 31.03.2021 (or relevant date)
7. Upload project reports on proposed sales, investment, localization plan, employment generation, and domestic value addition
8. Pay application fee via NEFT/RTGS to IFCI-PMA-PLI-ITHW account (details in form)
9. Submit integrity undertaking and regulatory approvals information
10. Final submission and await approval from Project Management Agency (PMA)/MeitY based on eligibility and ranking
