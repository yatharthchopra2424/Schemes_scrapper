# Comprehensive Scheme Masterclass & File Guide

## Scheme Deep Dive

### Overview
The **PLI Scheme for IT Hardware 2.0** is a production-linked incentive scheme launched by the **Ministry of Electronics and Information Technology (MeitY)** to boost domestic manufacturing of IT hardware and attract large investments in the value chain. Implemented through the **Project Management Agency (PMA) - IFCI Ltd.**, the scheme offers financial incentives based on net incremental sales of manufactured goods in India, with a focus on localization and domestic value addition. The scheme supports the manufacturing of laptops, tablets, all-in-one PCs, servers, and ultra small form factor (USSF) devices.

### Objectives
- Boost domestic manufacturing of IT hardware  
- Attract large investments in the value chain  
- Compensate for manufacturing disabilities vis-à-vis other major manufacturing economies  
- Reduce import dependency on electronics hardware  
- Enhance India's share in global electronics manufacturing  
- Promote localization of components and sub-assemblies  
- Encourage domestic value addition in IT hardware production  

### Eligibility Matrix
Eligibility is determined by consolidated global manufacturing revenue in the base year (FY 2021-22 for PLI 2.0) and incremental investment thresholds post-March 31, 2021. Only one application per applicant is allowed.

| Category | Target Segment Revenue Threshold (Base Year) | Electronics Hardware Revenue Threshold (Base Year) | Incremental Investment Threshold (Over 6 Years) | Net Incremental Sales Threshold (Year 1) |
|---------|----------------------------------------------|----------------------------------------------------|--------------------------------------------------|------------------------------------------|
| **Global Companies** | > INR 5,000 crore | > INR 10,000 crore | INR 500 crore | INR 1,000 crore |
| **Hybrid (Global/Domestic) Companies** | > INR 10 crore (Domestic arm) & > INR 5,000 crore (Global arm for target segment) OR > INR 20 crore (Domestic arm) & > INR 10,000 crore (Global arm for EHP-SA-C) | > INR 20 crore (Domestic arm) & > INR 10,000 crore (Global arm for EHP-SA-C) | INR 250 crore | INR 500 crore |
| **Domestic Companies** | > INR 10 crore | > INR 20 crore | INR 20 crore | INR 50 crore |

> **Note**: Domestic companies are defined as those where >50% capital is beneficially owned by resident Indian citizens and/or Indian companies ultimately owned/controlled by resident Indian citizens.  
> **Source**: [Application Form](https://pliithw.com/guide/guidelines), [Gazette Notification](https://pliithw.com/guide/guidelines_second_scheme), [Guidelines PLI-ITHW2.O](https://pliithw.com/guide/guidelines_second_scheme)

### Benefits & Financial Support
Incentives are calculated as a percentage of net incremental sales (manufactured in India and covered under target segment) over the base year. The incentive rate decreases over time and is subject to localization milestones.

#### Incentive Rates by Year and Localization Achievement
| Year | Base Incentive Rate | Localization Milestone Achieved | Effective Incentive Rate |
|------|---------------------|----------------------------------|---------------------------|
| Year 1 (FY 2023-24) | 4% | Not applicable | 4% |
| Year 2 (FY 2024-25) | 3% | PCB Assembly localized domestically | 3% |
| Year 3 (FY 2025-26) | 2% | PCB Assembly + Battery Packs localized | 2% |
| Year 4 (FY 2026-27) | 2% or 1% | Full localization (PCBA, Battery, Power Adapter, Enclosure) | 2% |
| Year 4 (FY 2026-27) | 2% or 1% | Partial localization (only PCBA) | 1% |
| Year 5 (FY 2027-28) | Tapered | Continued localization | Tapered per Annexure 2 |
| Year 6 (FY 2028-29) | Tapered | Continued localization | Tapered per Annexure 2 |

### Application Process
The application process is conducted entirely online via the portal [https://pliithw.com/](https://pliithw.com/). Applicants must register using their CIN as user ID and submit a comprehensive set of documents.

```mermaid
flowchart TD
    A[Start: Register on PLI IT Hardware Portal] --> B[Login using CIN as User ID]
    B --> C[Fill Application Form with Company Details]
    C --> D[Upload Required Documents]
    D --> E[Submit Self-Certified Statement of Consolidated Global Manufacturing Revenue]
    E --> F[Provide Details of Group Companies]
    F --> G[Submit Baseline Certificates for Net Sales & Investment as of 31.03.2021]
    G --> H[Upload Project Reports on Sales, Investment, Localization, Employment, DVA]
    H --> I[Pay Application Fee via NEFT/RTGS to IFCI-PMA-PLI-ITHW Account]
    I --> J[Submit Integrity Undertaking & Regulatory Approvals Information]
    J --> K[Final Submission]
    K --> L[Await Approval from PMA/MeitY]
    L --> M{Approved?}
    M -->|Yes| N[Receive Approval Letter]
    M -->|No| O[Revise & Resubmit based on Feedback]
    N --> P[Commence Manufacturing & Localization]
    P --> Q[Submit Quarterly Review Reports (QRRs)]
    Q --> R[Annual Incentive Claim Submission]
    R --> S[PMA Verification of Claims]
    S --> T[Disbursement of Incentive]
    T --> U[End]
```

#### Required Documents
1. Certificate of Incorporation  
2. PAN Card  
3. GST Registration Certificate  
4. Authorization Letter from Board of Directors or Managing Director  
5. Certified Shareholding Pattern  
6. Profile of Directors  
7. Key Managerial Personnel Details  
8. Annual Reports (2017-18, 2018-19, 2019-20)  
9. Self-Certification of Consolidated Global Manufacturing Revenue (Target Segment and EHP-SA-C)  
10. Self-Certification of Manufacturing Revenue of Group Companies  
11. Certificate of Sales of Manufactured Goods (Target Segment and Eligible Products)  
12. Business Profile of the Applicant  
13. Localization Plan  
14. Detailed Project Report (optional)  
15. Regulatory Approvals Information  

> **Note**: Application fee is INR 1,00,000 (non-refundable), payable electronically via NEFT/RTGS to IFCI-PMA-PLI-ITHW account (details in form).  
> **Source**: [Application Form](application-form-pli-for-it-hardware-22-03-2021.pdf), [Guidelines](guidelines-pli-scheme-it-hardware-14-07-2023.pdf), [Portal](https://pliithw.com/)

### Key Caveats
- Only one application per applicant is allowed.  
- Incentive is subject to annual caps and may be reallocated if unutilized due to underperformance.  
- Eligibility does not affect or is not affected by eligibility under other schemes.  
- For servers, applicants must use firmware from Indian or other trusted foreign sources certified by MeitY.  
- Land and building expenditure is not covered under the scheme for eligibility determination.  
- Incentive rates decrease over time and are subject to localization milestones.  
- The Empowered Group of Secretaries (EGoS) may revise incentive rates, ceilings, target segments, and eligibility criteria during the scheme tenure.  
> **Source**: [Key Facts], [Gazette Notification](https://pliithw.com/guide/guidelines_second_scheme), [Guidelines PLI-ITHW2.O](https://pliithw.com/guide/guidelines_second_scheme)
