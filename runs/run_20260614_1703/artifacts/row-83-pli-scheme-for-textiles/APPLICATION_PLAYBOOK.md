# Application Playbook

## How to Use This File
For each new client case, assign it a Case ID, pick the target scheme, and follow the steps below.

---

## Stage 1: Eligibility Verification (Day 1–3)

### Checklist
- [ ] Applicant is a person including Company/Firm/LLP/Trust willing to create a separate manufacturing company under Companies Act 2013
- [ ] For Scheme Part-1: Minimum investment of ₹300 Crore (excluding land and administrative building cost) to manufacture Notified Products
- [ ] For Scheme Part-2: Minimum investment of ₹100 Crore (excluding land and administrative building cost) to manufacture Notified Products
- [ ] Applicant must form a new/separate company under Companies Act, 2013 before commencement of investment
- [ ] Investment made in anticipation from the date of Scheme Notification shall be accounted for calculation of threshold investment if proposal is approved for participation
- [ ] Participant must commit to setting up manufacturing facilities with appropriate quality and testing facilities conforming to BIS or International Standards
- [ ] Participant must ensure 'Made in India' tags on products
- [ ] Foreign investment must comply with Consolidated FDI Policy 2020
- [ ] Only manufacturing company registered in India will be eligible to participate under the scheme
- [ ] Participating company will have to undertake processing and operation activities in their own factory premises as prescribed in the scheme guidelines
- [ ] Project proposals must envisage processing and operation activities able to enhance value by not less than 60% in integrated fibre/yarn to fabric, garment & technical textiles (30% for independent fabrics processing house)
- [ ] Turnover achieved from trading and job work will not be accounted for incentives under the Scheme
- [ ] Only Notified Products sold under GST Invoice indicating 8 digit HS code shall be taken into account for calculation of threshold/incremental turnover
- [ ] Notified goods/Products manufactured by a company other than the registered participant company, even of same group, shall not be accounted for calculation of incremental turnover
- [ ] If multiple applications are filed by the same group companies, only one project under PLI will be approved for any group of companies
- [ ] Participant shall declare the Notified ITC HS lines/Technical Textiles Products as applicable they shall manufacture in the new participant company, and lines/products once selected should be adhered to till Scheme duration; any changes require prior approval of MoT
- [ ] Incentive will be available only for a period of 5 years only from the first performance year
- [ ] Scheme is valid upto 2029-30
- [ ] Gestation period for both parts of the scheme is of two (2) years i.e. FY: 2022-23 to FY: 2023-24
- [ ] Performance Year 1 is FY 2024-2025; 2nd is 2025-2026; 3rd is 2026-2027; 4th is 2027-2028; 5th is 2028-2029

**Portal Reference**: https://pli.texmin.gov.in/

## Stage 2: Document Collection (Day 3–10)

### Universal Documents (always required)
- Certificate of Incorporation / Registration
- PAN of the entity
- ITR (Income Tax Returns)
- GST registration certificate
- Bank statements (last 6 months)
- Udyam Registration (for MSME classification)

### Scheme-Specific Documents
- Letter of Authorization
- Project Report
- QRR Sample (Quarterly Review Report)
- Application Sample
- Undertaking duly Affirmed and Notarized
- Auditor’s Certificate
- Consumption and inventory register
- Letter of Approval
- Project Management Agency correspondence

### Document Quality Check
- [ ] All documents are legible and in PDF format
- [ ] File size does not exceed 10 MB per document
- [ ] Document names match the applicant's legal name exactly
- [ ] All certificates are issued by authorized bodies (e.g., Statutory Auditor, CA, etc.)
- [ ] Undertaking is notarized on stamp paper of appropriate value
- [ ] All declarations are signed and dated
- [ ] GST invoice samples show 8-digit HS code for Notified Products
- [ ] Bank statements reflect transactions in normal banking channel
- [ ] Project Report includes investment plan, turnover projections, employment generation, and value addition details
- [ ] Auditor’s Certificate certifies turnover declared, products manufactured, taxes paid, and input tax credit availed

## Stage 3: Application Drafting (Day 10–18)

### Project Report / DPR (if required)
**Required**: Yes, a Project Report is mandatory as per the scheme-specific documents list.  
**Standard Sections to Include**:
- Executive Summary  
- Promoter Details and Experience  
- Proposed Investment Breakdown (Plant, Machinery, Equipment, Civil Works)  
- Details of Notified Products to be manufactured (with 8-digit HS codes)  
- Manufacturing Process and Technology  
- Projected Turnover and Incentive Calculation for 5 years  
- Employment Generation Plan (Direct and Indirect)  
- Value Addition Calculation (minimum 60% for integrated units, 30% for independent processing)  
- Location Rationale (preference for Aspirational Districts/Tier 3/4 towns)  
- Risk Assessment and Mitigation Plan  
- Implementation Timeline (aligned with gestation period FY 2022-23 to FY 2023-24)  
- Compliance with BIS/International Standards and 'Made in India' tagging  
- Details of associated utilities and R&D investment (if applicable)  
- Undertaking and Declaration  

### Application Form Filling Protocol
1. Register on the Ministry’s PLI portal at https://pli.texmin.gov.in/ in the prescribed format.  
2. Fill applicant details accurately matching Certificate of Incorporation and PAN.  
3. Declare target segment (MMF Apparel, MMF Fabrics, Technical Textiles, or Multiple Segment).  
4. Specify proposed business activity and employment generation.  
5. Provide projected turnover aligned with scheme thresholds (e.g., ₹600 Cr for Part-1 Year 1).  
6. Upload required documents as per applicant type (refer to document checklist).  
7. Accept the Undertaking and pay the non-refundable application fee of Rs. 50,000 via NEFT/RTGS/UPI.  
8. E-sign uploaded documents via OTP validation using Aadhaar-linked mobile number.  
9. Perform final submit only after verifying all fields and attachments.  
10. Save acknowledgment with unique Application ID for tracking.  

### Internal Review Gate
- [ ] All sections of the application form are complete  
- [ ] All scheme-specific and universal documents are uploaded and legible  
- [ ] Undertaking is accepted and application fee paid  
- [ ] Documents are e-signed via Aadhaar OTP  
- [ ] Application ID is generated and stored  
- [ ] Declaration of Notified ITC HS lines/Technical Textiles Products is included  
- [ ] Investment and turnover projections meet scheme minimums  
- [ ] Value addition calculation complies with 60%/30% rule  
- [ ] No trading or job work turnover is included in projections  
- [ ] GST invoice samples reflect 8-digit HS code for Notified Products

## Stage 5: Post-Approval (Day 30–90+)

### Approval Actions
- [ ] Obtain and verify Letter of Approval from MoT  
- [ ] Confirm sanctioned amount and incentive rate as per Performance Year  
- [ ] Ensure separate books of accounts are maintained for the participant unit  
- [ ] Submit Quarterly Review Reports (QRRs) within 30 days of quarter end via portal  
- [ ] Maintain consumption and inventory register for inputs and raw materials  
- [ ] Ensure GST invoices for Notified Products reflect 8-digit HS code  
- [ ] Verify realization of sale proceeds through normal banking channel  
- [ ] Prepare annual incentive claim with audited financial statements by Statutory Auditor  
- [ ] File incentive claim online by 31st December of immediate subsequent financial year (e.g., for FY 2024-25, claim by 31.12.2025)  
- [ ] Monitor disbursement via PFMS or approved mechanism within 15 days of approval  
- [ ] Utilize incentive amount strictly for manufacturing of Notified Products as per undertaking  
- [ ] Submit Utilization Certificate post-verification of expenditure  
- [ ] Track success fee invoice as per consultancy agreement
