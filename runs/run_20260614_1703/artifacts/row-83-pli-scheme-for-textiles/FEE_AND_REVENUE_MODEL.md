## Pricing Principles
1. Retainer covers initial eligibility assessment, documentation preparation, and application submission support.
2. Success fee is contingent on incentive approval and disbursement, aligning consultant incentives with client outcomes.
3. Tiered pricing reflects scalability of effort: larger investments require more complex structuring but yield higher absolute fees.
4. Scheme-specific note: This PLI Scheme for Textiles involves a 2-year gestation period before performance year 1 (FY 2024-25), with incentives claimable annually over 5 years. The multi-year nature and documentation intensity increase engagement risk, justifying retainer stability and moderate success fees.
