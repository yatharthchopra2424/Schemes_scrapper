# Pitch & Sales Scripts

## Philosophy
We are advisors, not salespeople. We diagnose first. We only pitch a scheme if it fits the client. We never oversell success rates.

---

## 1. Discovery Call Script (First Contact — 20 min)

### Opening (2 min)
Good morning/afternoon. I specialize in helping textile manufacturers access government incentives like the PLI Scheme for Textiles, which supports companies investing in MMF Apparel, MMF Fabrics, and Technical Textiles to scale up and become globally competitive. I’d like to understand your current operations and see if this scheme aligns with your growth plans.

### Discovery Questions
1. What type of textile products are you currently manufacturing or planning to manufacture? (e.g., MMF Apparel, MMF Fabrics, Technical Textiles like Geo-textiles, Agro-textiles, or Medical Textiles)
2. Have you incorporated a separate company under the Companies Act, 2013 for this manufacturing activity?
3. What is your planned or actual investment in plant, machinery, equipment, and civil works (excluding land and administrative building)?
4. Are you targeting a minimum turnover of ₹600 Crore (for MMF/Fabric focus) or ₹200 Crore (for Technical Textiles focus) in your first performance year?
5. Do you have the required quality and testing facilities conforming to BIS or International Standards for your products?
6. Are you committed to tagging your products with 'Made in India'?
7. Have you already made any investment in anticipation of the scheme since September 24, 2021?
8. Are you open to forming a new/separate entity to participate in the scheme, as required?

### Qualification Checklist (internal — don't read aloud)
- [ ] Willing to create a separate manufacturing company under Companies Act, 2013
- [ ] Minimum investment of ₹300 Crore (for Part-1: MMF Apparel/Fabrics) or ₹100 Crore (for Part-2: Technical Textiles)
- [ ] Targeting threshold turnover of ₹600 Crore (Part-1 Year 1) or ₹200 Crore (Part-2 Year 1) in first performance year
- [ ] Commitment to manufacture only Notified Products as per scheme annexures
- [ ] Agreement to maintain separate accounts and inventory registers
- [ ] Willing to adhere to selected HSN lines/products for scheme duration
- [ ] Prepared to ensure 'Made in India' tagging and GST invoices with 8-digit HS codes
- [ ] Not relying on turnover from trading or job work for incentive eligibility

### Transition to Pitch
Based on your responses, it appears you’re aligned with the PLI Scheme for Textiles’ requirements for [mention relevant part, e.g., Part-1 with ₹300 Crore investment targeting ₹600 Crore turnover]. This scheme offers financial incentives of up to 15% on eligible turnover in Year 1, payable for 5 years. Shall I walk you through how it works?

## 2. Scheme Pitch (5 min)

### Structure: Problem → Scheme → Value → Proof → Next Step

**Problem framing:**
Textile manufacturers face high upfront capital costs and struggle to achieve scale and global competitiveness, especially in high-value segments like MMF Apparel, MMF Fabrics, and Technical Textiles. The PLI Scheme for Textiles addresses this by linking financial support directly to production and turnover achievement.

**Scheme intro:**
The PLI Scheme for Textiles, notified by the Ministry of Textiles, Government of India (Notification No. 12015/03/2020-IT dated 24/09/2021), is a production-linked subsidy offering financial incentives for 5 years from the first performance year. It provides incentives calculated on achieving threshold turnover: for Scheme Part-1 (MMF Apparel/Fabrics), Year 1 incentive is 15% on ₹600 Crore turnover; for Scheme Part-2 (Technical Textiles), Year 1 incentive is 11% on ₹200 Crore turnover. The scheme has a total outlay of ₹10,683 Crore and aims to drive fresh investment above ₹19,000 Crore and additional production turnover of over ₹3 lakh Crore in five years.
