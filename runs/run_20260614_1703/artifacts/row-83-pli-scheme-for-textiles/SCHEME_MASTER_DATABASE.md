### PLI Scheme for Textiles
- **Scheme ID:** SCH-001
- **Category:** Subsidy
- **Geography:** Pan-India
- **Administering Body:** Ministry of Textiles, Government of India
- **Portal / Application URL:** https://pli.texmin.gov.in/
- **Current Status:** Rolling
- **Window:** Always Open

#### Eligibility Criteria
- Business type: Company/Firm/LLP/Trust willing to create a separate manufacturing company under Companies Act 2013
- Age of business: New entity must be formed before commencement of investment
- Sector: Manufacturing of MMF Apparel, MMF Fabrics, and Technical Textiles products
- Revenue cap: Not applicable (incentive based on turnover achievement)
- Employee count: Not specified
- Other: 
  - Minimum investment: ₹300 Crore for Scheme Part-1 (excluding land and administrative building cost) or ₹100 Crore for Scheme Part-2
  - Must form a new/separate company under Companies Act, 2013 before commencement of investment
  - Must commit to setting up manufacturing facilities with appropriate quality and testing facilities conforming to BIS or International Standards
  - Must ensure 'Made in India' tags on products
  - Foreign investment must comply with Consolidated FDI Policy 2020
  - Only one project under PLI will be approved for any group of companies
  - Participant must declare the Notified ITC HS lines/Technical Textiles Products they shall manufacture and adhere to them till scheme duration
  - Turnover from trading and job work will not be accounted for incentives
  - Only Notified Products sold under GST Invoice indicating 8 digit HS code shall be taken into account for calculation of threshold/incremental turnover

#### What It Offers
- Amount / Benefit: Financial incentives linked to turnover achievement; rates vary by year and scheme part (e.g., 15% for Part-1 Year 1, 11% for Part-2 Year 1); total fund outlay of ₹10,683 crore
- Disbursement mode: Direct credit (via Direct Bank Transfer through PFMS or other mechanism)
- Duration of benefit: 5 years only from the first performance year

#### Documents Required
1. Certificate of Incorporation / Registration
2. PAN of the entity
3. Letter of Authorization
4. Project Report
5. QRR Sample
6. Application Sample
7. Undertaking duly Affirmed and Notarized
8. Auditor’s Certificate
9. Consumption and inventory register
10. Letter of Approval
11. Project Management Agency correspondence

#### Application Process
1. Register on the Ministry’s PLI portal developed for this purpose in the prescribed format.
2. Submit application with required documents.
3. Accept Undertaking and Pay application fee.
4. Upload required documents (as per Type of applicant).
5. E-Sign uploaded documents via OTP validation form Aadhaar.
6. Final Submit.
