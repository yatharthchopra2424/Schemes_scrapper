# Application Playbook

## How to Use This File
For each new client case, assign it a Case ID, pick the target scheme, and follow the steps below.

---

## Stage 1: Eligibility Verification (Day 1–3)

### Checklist
- [ ] Company registered in India under the Companies Act 2013
- [ ] Engaged in end-to-end manufacturing of identified specialty steel grades
- [ ] Input material melted and poured within India using iron ore/scrap/sponge iron/pellets, etc.
- [ ] Net worth of the company (including group companies) not less than 30% of total committed investment
- [ ] Value addition through third parties limited to maximum 20%
- [ ] For capacity augmentation, 50% of threshold investment shall be applicable
- [ ] Investments made after the date of notification of the scheme (04.11.2025) only considered under permissible investment
- [ ] No insolvency proceedings admitted against the applicant in the National Company Law Tribunal (NCLT)
- [ ] Accounts not declared as Non-performing assets (NPA) as per RBI guidelines or willful defaulter or reported as fraud by any bank
- [ ] Commitment to minimum unit capacity and minimum investment as per Annexure-III for the applied product sub-category
- [ ] Committed annual incremental production rate equal to or more than respective threshold incremental production rate mentioned in Annexure-III for each product sub-category

## Stage 2: Document Collection (Day 3–10)

### Universal Documents (always required)
- Certificate of Incorporation (Issued by ROC)
- Memorandum & Articles of Association (Submitted to ROC)
- PAN Card
- GST Registration Certificates
- Annual Report of Applicant company: FY 2022-23, FY 2023-24 & FY 2024-25
- Bank Details of applicant (Account holder Name, Account No., Type of Account, Name of the Bank, Branch Code, Branch IFSC Code, Branch MICR Code, Branch Name, Branch Address, Telephone/Fax nos. of Bank, Email address)
- Application Fee Payment Proof

### Scheme-Specific Documents
- Authorization Letter by Board of Directors or Managing Director or Equivalent
- Feasibility report of the project (if available)
- Integrity Undertaking (Format A) of Annexure-VIII
- Net worth certificate by Statutory auditor
- Baseline production certificate by Statutory auditor (Annexure-IX)
- Certificate by Statutory auditor stating that accounts of the company are not declared as Non-performing assets (NPA) as per RBI guidelines or wilful defaulter or reported as fraud by any bank, financial institution or non-banking financial company etc. Further, it is further declared that there is no insolvency proceedings admitted against the company in the National Company Law Tribunal (NCLT)
- Details of proposed plant units for the applied product sub-category (Unit No., Unit Location, Unit Capacity in Thousand Metric Tonne per Year, Year of Commissioning)
- Committed annual production (unit wise) for Production Year 2025-2026 to 2029-2030
- Committed investment details- unit wise (Investment made before date of notification of the PLI 1.2, Investment proposed to be made from the date of notification of the PLI 1.2, Total investment)
- Year wise phasing of investment (made after date of notification of the guidelines for PLI Scheme 1.2) in Rs Cr for FY25-26, FY26-27, FY27-28, FY28-29, FY29-30
- Details of proposed plant to be set up under targeted segment (Unit Location details: Village, Block, Dist., State, Pin; Whether land is acquired; Whether statutory clearances are taken; Major units of the proposed plant; Implementation period with start date of the project; Expected month along with year of commissioning of plant; Whether any feasibility report of the project is prepared; Whether Financial closure for the proposed project is occurred; Details of source of fund: Equity, Debt, Total)
- Proposed Plan for Employment Generation from the Project (Year-wise Employment generation: FY2024-25 to FY2029-30: On-roll labour/employees, Contractual, Apprentice, Total year-wise Employment)
- Historical Data for Net Sales of all saleable products (incl. Target segments of products as applicable) for FY 22-23, FY 23-24, FY24-25 (Export Sales, Domestic Sales, Total Sales Qty (t), Value (Rs Cr.))

### Document Quality Check
- All documents uploaded in PDF format
- All documents self-certified with seal of the company
- All monetary figures provided only in INR
- File size within portal limits
- Document names matching application details
- Language: English only
- Statutory auditor certificates include UDIN, FRN, Membership No., Signature & Seal
- Bank Guarantee format as per Annexure-X
- Undertakings on company letterhead with authorised signatory, seal, date, place

## Stage 3: Application Drafting (Day 10–18)

### Project Report / DPR (if required)
Not explicitly mentioned as required in the evidence. Flag: verify with nodal officer.

### Application Form Filling Protocol
1. Fill the application form only in English language.
2. Provide all monetary figures in INR only.
3. Do not leave any input blank; all fields are mandatory.
4. Upload documents in PDF format as per Appendix-A of Annexure-II.
5. Self-certify all uploaded documents with the seal of the company.
6. Confirm eligibility as per scheme document and PLI Scheme guidelines before filling.
7. Only one product sub-category per application is permitted; for multiple sub-categories, submit separate application forms.
8. Provide details of Authorised signatory, Statutory Auditor, and current manufacturing facilities (including GST Registration Certificates uploaded in Document Uploads section).

- [ ] All required documents uploaded and self-certified
- [ ] Declarations signed and dated

- [ ] Employment generation plan provided
- [ ] Source of fund details (Equity, Debt) provided if financial closure occurred
- [ ] Feasibility report attached if prepared

## Stage 4: Submission & Follow-Up (Day 18–30)

### Submission Protocol
- Submit application online via https://plimos.mecon.co.in within 30 days from launch date (04.11.2025)
- Take screenshot of submission confirmation with unique application ID and date
- Store reference number securely
- Submit signed copy of online application along with all uploaded documents to PMA within 10 days of online submission
- Set follow-up reminders for:
  - Application scrutiny status (within 15 working days of submission)
  - Correction window opening (to be notified after close of application submission)
  - Correction window deadline (15 days after opening)
  - MoU signing (if selected)
  - Performance security submission (0.5% of committed investment in Bank Guarantee format)
  - Undertaking consenting to audit of manufacturing facilities

## Stage 5: Post-Approval (Day 30–90+)

### Approval Actions
- [ ] Obtain approval letter from Ministry of Steel
- [ ] Verify sanctioned amount and incentive eligibility
- [ ] Sign Memorandum of Understanding (MoU) with Ministry of Steel for each applied product sub-category
- [ ] Submit Performance Security of 0.5% of committed investment in the form of Bank Guarantee (as per Annexure-X) for each product sub-category
- [ ] Submit undertaking consenting to audit of manufacturing facilities
- [ ] File annual incentive claims within 7 months from the end of the financial year to which the claim pertains (e.g., claim for FY 2025-26 to be filed before 31st October, 2026)
- [ ] Submit disbursement claim form (Annexure-V) along with:
  - Investment Certificate (Format-7) from Statutory Auditor
  - Permissible Investment Certificate (Format-8) from Chartered Engineer
  - Eligible Product Sales Certificate (Format-9) from Statutory Auditor
  - Baseline Production Certificate (Format-10) from Statutory Auditor (only for 1st year of Incentive Claim)
  - Quality Declaration of Steel Grade from Chartered Engineer
  - Others Declaration on not claiming fiscal benefit on any other PLI Scheme from Authorized Signatory
  - Indemnity Bond on Non-Judicial stamp of Rs. 100
  - Board Resolution accepting terms and conditions of PLI Scheme
  - Integrity Compliance (Format-B of Annexure-VIII) from Authorized Signatory
- [ ] Ensure performance security remains valid throughout the scheme period
- [ ] Monitor for any change in shareholding pattern and intimate to PMA after updating with Registrar of Companies
- [ ] Prepare for financial, compliance, and performance audit by Ministry of Steel (statutory audit by CAG)
- [ ] Utilize non-financial benefits: eligibility to avail benefits under other schemes such as RoDTEP or State Government schemes, and ability to undertake third-party value addition up to 20%
