## Pricing Principles
1. Retainer covers application preparation, documentation, and submission support.
2. Success fee is contingent on incentive approval and disbursement.
3. Fees are benchmarked against the scheme’s high-value nature (incentive capped at ₹200 Cr per company annually).
4. Scheme-specific note: This PLI scheme has a long processing timeline (incentive disbursement begins FY 2026-27 for production FY 2025-26), requiring sustained engagement; success is evidenced by MoUs signed and approved applications under PLI 1.1 and 1.2, indicating moderate-to-high feasibility with proper structuring.
