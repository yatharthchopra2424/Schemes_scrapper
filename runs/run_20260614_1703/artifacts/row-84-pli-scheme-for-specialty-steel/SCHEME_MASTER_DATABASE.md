### PLI Scheme for Specialty Steel
- **Scheme ID:** SCH-001
- **Category:** Subsidy
- **Geography:** Pan-India
- **Administering Body:** Ministry of Steel, Government of India
- **Portal / Application URL:** https://plimos.mecon.co.in
- **Current Status:** Open
- **Window:** Start Date: 04.11.2025, End Date: To be announced (based on PLI Scheme 1.2 launch)

#### Eligibility Criteria
- Business type: Companies registered in India under the Companies Act 2013, desirous of manufacturing identified specialty steel grades
- Age of business: Not specified
- Sector: Specialty steel manufacturing (Coated/Plated Steel Products, High Strength/Wear Resistant Steel, Specialty Rails, Alloy Steel Products and Steel Wires, Electrical Steel)
- Revenue cap: Not specified
- Employee count: Not specified
- Other: 
  - End-to-end manufacturing of applied product sub-category domestically (input material melted and poured within India using iron ore/scrap/sponge iron/pellets)
  - Net worth of the company (including group companies) shall not be less than 30% of total committed investment
  - Third party value addition allowed up to 20%
  - For capacity augmentation, 50% of threshold investment shall be applicable
  - Investments made before the date of notification of the scheme (04.11.2025) are not eligible under permissible investment

#### What It Offers
- Amount / Benefit: Incentive calculated as (Eligible incremental production) x (Weighted Average Sales Price in base year or current year, whichever is lower) x (PLI rate as applicable)/100; capped at Rs. 200 crores per eligible company per annum
- Disbursement mode: Direct credit (after claim submission and verification)
- Duration of benefit: One-time annual claims; scheme period of five years (FY 2025-26 to FY 2029-30 for production, incentive disbursement from FY 2026-27 to FY 2030-31)

#### Documents Required
1. Authorization Letter
2. GST Registration Certificates
3. Certificate of Incorporation
4. PAN of the entity
5. Audited financial statements
6. Net worth certificate
7. Details of proposed plant units
8. Committed investment details
9. Year-wise phasing of investment
10. Feasibility report (if prepared)
11. Details of source of fund (Equity and Debt)
12. Proposed plan for employment generation
13. Historical data for net sales
14. Undertaking for end-to-end manufacturing
15. Undertaking on value addition through third parties

#### Application Process (numbered steps)
1. Register on the PLI web portal (https://plimos.mecon.co.in)
2. Fill the application form online within 30 days from the date of launch of PLI Scheme 1.2
3. Pay a non-refundable application fee of Rs 1 lakh (plus applicable GST)
4. Submit the signed copy of the online application along with all uploaded documents within 10 days of online submission
5. Undergo application scrutiny by PMA for completeness of supporting documents
6. A one-time correction window of 15 days is available after closure of application submission for completeness of supporting documents only
7. Selected companies sign a MoU with the Ministry of Steel
8. Submit performance security of 0.5% of committed investment in the form of Bank Guarantee
9. Submit undertaking consenting to audit of manufacturing facilities
10. File annual incentive claims within 7 months from the end of the financial year to which the claim pertains
