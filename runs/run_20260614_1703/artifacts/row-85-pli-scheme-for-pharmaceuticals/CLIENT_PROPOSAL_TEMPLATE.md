## 1. Executive Summary
Based on our initial review of [Client]'s business profile, we have identified PLI Scheme for Pharmaceuticals — a subsidy scheme administered by Department of Pharmaceuticals, Ministry of Chemicals and Fertilizers — for which your business appears eligible. The potential benefit is financial incentive on incremental sales over base year (FY 2019-20) at rates of 10% for FY 2022-23 to FY 2025-26, 8% for 2026-27, and 6% for 2027-28 for Category 1 & 2 goods; 5% for FY 2022-23 to FY 2025-26, 4% for 2026-27, and 3% for 2027-28 for Category 3 goods, disbursed for a maximum of six years.
