## Pricing Principles
1. Retainer covers initial eligibility assessment, documentation preparation, and application submission support.
2. Success fee is contingent on incentive disbursement, aligning consultant incentives with client outcomes.
3. Tiered pricing reflects scheme value and complexity of compliance requirements.
4. Scheme-specific note: The PLI Scheme for Pharmaceuticals involves a multi-year incentive structure (up to six years) based on incremental sales, with disbursement contingent on verification of sales claims and investment thresholds. Processing time for approval and claim verification typically spans 3–6 months per cycle, introducing revenue lag. Success is evidenced by approved applicant lists and ongoing disbursements, indicating moderate to high feasibility for eligible enterprises meeting investment and growth thresholds.
