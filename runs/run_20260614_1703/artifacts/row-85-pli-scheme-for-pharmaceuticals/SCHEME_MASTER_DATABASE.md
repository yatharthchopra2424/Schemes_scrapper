### PLI Scheme for Pharmaceuticals
- **Scheme ID:** SCH-001
- **Category:** Subsidy
- **Geography:** Pan-India
- **Administering Body:** Department of Pharmaceuticals, Ministry of Chemicals and Fertilizers
- **Portal / Application URL:** https://pli-pharma.udyamimitra.in
- **Current Status:** Closed (initial window); subsequent windows may be notified
- **Window:** 60 days starting from 2nd June, 2021 to 31st July, 2021 (both dates inclusive)

#### Eligibility Criteria
- Business type: Pharmaceutical manufacturers; MSME
- Age of business: Not specified
- Sector: Pharmaceuticals (including in vitro diagnostic medical devices)
- Revenue cap: Grouped by Global Manufacturing Revenue (GMR) from pharmaceutical goods and/or in vitro diagnostic medical devices in FY 2019-20: Group A (GMR ≥ Rs 5,000 crore), Group B (GMR between Rs 500 crore and Rs 5,000 crore), Group C (GMR < Rs 500 crore, with a sub-group for MSME)
- Employee count: Not specified
- Other: 
  - Minimum cumulative investment over five years: Group A: Rs 1,000 crore, Group B: Rs 250 crore, Group C: Rs 50 crore
  - Minimum year-on-year percentage growth in sales: Group A/B/C: 200/50/10 in FY 2021-22, increasing to 1000/250/50 by FY 2025-26
  - For first year of production: threshold sales specified in guidelines
  - For subsequent years: minimum 7% year-on-year growth required
  - Applicants must not have been declared bankrupt, wilful defaulter, or reported as fraud by any bank/financial institution
  - Second-hand/used/refurbished plant, machinery, equipment, utilities, or R&D equipment is not eligible for investment consideration
  - Expenditure on consumables and raw material used for manufacturing is not considered as eligible investment
  - Investment made under other PLI schemes (e.g., PLI for Bulk Drugs) cannot be double-counted for eligibility under this scheme
  - Minimum repayment period of loan (if applicable) shall not be less than 3 years
  - At least 50% of total sanctioned loan amount must be on components eligible under the scheme for interest subvention or capital subsidy
  - Loan proposals availing benefit under this scheme cannot avail benefits under any other technology up-gradation scheme of Government or State Government or autonomous institutions/PSUs

#### What It Offers
- Amount / Benefit: Financial incentives based on incremental sales of eligible pharmaceutical goods over the base year (FY 2019-20). Incentive rates: 
  - Category 1 & 2 goods: 10% for FY 2022-23 to FY 2025-26, 8% for 2026-27, 6% for 2027-28
  - Category 3 goods: 5% for FY 2022-23 to FY 2025-26, 4% for 2026-27, 3% for 2027-28
- Disbursement mode: Direct credit (managed by Project Management Agency after verification of eligibility and sales claims)
- Duration of benefit: Maximum of six years, contingent on achieving minimum cumulative investment and year-on-year sales growth thresholds

#### Application Process
1. Applicants must submit applications during the designated Application Window (60 days starting from 2nd June, 2021 to 31st July, 2021, as per initial guidelines; subsequent windows may be notified)
2. Applications are submitted via the online portal maintained by the Project Management Agency (PMA) at https://pli-pharma.udyamimitra.in
3. Applicants must provide requisite information, supporting documents, and pay a non-refundable application fee electronically
4. PMA conducts an examination of the application within 15 working days of receipt, checking completeness via a checklist
5. If incomplete, PMA notifies the applicant to complete within 15 days; otherwise, the application is closed
6. Upon completion, PMA issues an acknowledgement of receipt (not approval)
7. Eligible applicants are ranked based on evaluation criteria: Gross manufacturing investment (30%), number of ANDA/NDA approvals (30%), and R&D expenditure as % of GMR or GMR itself (40%), depending on group and product type
8. Selection is made in order of rank, with limits: Group A (11 participants, max 4 foreign MNCs), Group B (9 participants, max 3 foreign MNCs), Group C (35 participants, min 20 MSMEs, min 5 in vitro diagnostic medical devices manufacturers)
9. Selected applicants must establish manufacturing facilities and commence production, with FY 2021-22 as the gestation period
10. Incentive claims are submitted periodically based on incremental sales, verified by PMA and SSC, and disbursed after scrutiny
