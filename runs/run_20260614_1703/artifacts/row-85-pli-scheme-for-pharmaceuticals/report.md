# Comprehensive Scheme Masterclass & File Guide

## Scheme Deep Dive

### Scheme Overview
**Scheme Name:** PLI Scheme for Pharmaceuticals  
**Scheme ID:** row-85  
**Ministry / Category:** Pharmaceuticals (PLI)  
**Scheme Type:** subsidy  
**Geographic Scope:** Pan-India  
**Implementing Agency:** Department of Pharmaceuticals, Ministry of Chemicals and Fertilizers  
**Application Portal:** https://pli-pharma.udyamimitra.in  
**Status / Deadlines:** Application Window: 60 days starting from 2nd June, 2021 to 31st July, 2021 (both dates inclusive). Subsequent windows may be notified; refer to latest notices for current status.  
**Last Updated:** 2021  
**Fund Size (Cr):** 15000  
**Contact Details:** Email: Head-PharmaPolicy[at]pharma-dept[dot]gov[dot]in | Tel: 011 23385131  

### Objectives
- Enhance India’s manufacturing capabilities by increasing investment and production in the pharmaceutical sector  
- Contribute to product diversification to high-value goods in the pharmaceutical sector  
- Create global champions out of India with potential to grow in size and scale using cutting-edge technology  
- Penetrate global value chains through enhanced manufacturing and innovation  
- Incentivize global and domestic players to enhance investment and production in high-value product categories  
- Ensure higher resilience of the Indian pharmaceutical industry to external shocks via domestic production capability in APIs/KSMs  
- Contribute to achieving higher objective of affordable healthcare in the country and globally on a sustained basis  

### Eligibility Matrix
| Group | Global Manufacturing Revenue (GMR) in FY 2019-20 | Minimum Cumulative Investment (5 years) | Minimum Year-on-Year Sales Growth (FY 2021-22) | Minimum Year-on-Year Sales Growth (FY 2025-26) | Sub-group Notes |
|-------|--------------------------------------------------|------------------------------------------|------------------------------------------------|------------------------------------------------|-----------------|
| **Group A** | GMR ≥ Rs 5,000 crore | Rs 1,000 crore | 200% | 1000% | - |
| **Group B** | GMR between Rs 500 crore and Rs 5,000 crore | Rs 250 crore | 50% | 250% | - |
| **Group C** | GMR < Rs 500 crore | Rs 50 crore | 10% | 50% | Includes MSME sub-group; MSME participants may commit investment as per application form |

> **Eligibility Caveats:**  
> - Applicants must not have been declared bankrupt, wilful defaulter, or reported as fraud by any bank/financial institution  
> - Second-hand/used/refurbished plant, machinery, equipment, utilities, or R&D equipment is not eligible for investment consideration  
> - Expenditure on consumables and raw material used for manufacturing is not considered as eligible investment  
> - Investment made under other PLI schemes (e.g., PLI for Bulk Drugs) cannot be double-counted for eligibility under this scheme  
> - Minimum repayment period of loan (if applicable) shall not be less than 3 years  
> - At least 50% of total sanctioned loan amount must be on components eligible under the scheme for interest subvention or capital subsidy  
> - Loan proposals availing benefit under this scheme cannot avail benefits under any other technology up-gradation scheme of Government or State Government or autonomous institutions/PSUs  

### Benefits & Financial Support
| Category | FY 2022-23 to FY 2025-26 | FY 2026-27 | FY 2027-28 |
|----------|----------------------------|------------|------------|
| **Category 1 & 2 Goods** | 10% | 8% | 6% |
| **Category 3 Goods** | 5% | 4% | 3% |

**Key Financial Details:**  
- Incentive is provided on **incremental sales over base year (FY 2019-20)**  
- Disbursed for a **maximum of six years**, contingent on achieving minimum cumulative investment and year-on-year sales growth thresholds  
- Total financial outlay: **Rs. 15,000 crore**, allocated as:  
  - **Group A:** Rs. 11,000 crore  
  - **Group B:** Rs. 2,250 crore  
  - **Group C:** Rs. 1,750 crore  
- Disbursement managed by the **Project Management Agency (PMA)** after verification of eligibility and sales claims  

### Application Process Flowchart
```mermaid
flowchart TD
    A[Start: Application Window Opens] --> B[Submit Application via Online Portal<br>https://pli-pharma.udyamimitra.in]
    B --> C{PMA Examination<br>(Within 15 working days)}
    C -->|Complete| D[Issue Acknowledgement of Receipt<br>(Not Approval)]
    C -->|Incomplete| E[Notify Applicant to Complete<br>Within 15 days]
    E --> F{Applicant Responds?}
    F -->|Yes| D
    F -->|No| G[Application Closed]
    D --> H[Eligible Applicants Ranked<br>Based on Evaluation Criteria]
    H --> I[Selection Made in Order of Rank<br>With Group-wise Limits]
    I --> J[Selected Applicants Establish Facilities<br>Commence Production<br>FY 2021-22: Gestation Period]
    J --> K[Submit Incentive Claims Periodically<br>Based on Incremental Sales]
    K --> L[PMA & SSC Verify Claims]
    L --> M[Disbursement After Scrutiny]
    M --> End[End: Incentive Disbursed for Max 6 Years]
```

**Evaluation Criteria for Ranking:**  
- Gross manufacturing investment (30%)  
- Number of ANDA/NDA approvals (30%)  
- R&D expenditure as % of GMR or GMR itself (40%), depending on group and product type  

**Selection Limits:**  
- **Group A:** 11 participants (max 4 foreign MNCs)  
- **Group B:** 9 participants (max 3 foreign MNCs)  
- **Group C:** 35 participants (min 20 MSMEs, min 5 in vitro diagnostic medical devices manufacturers)  

### Required Documents
1. Application form as per Appendix K  
2. Undertaking in format given in Appendix E  
3. Undertaking in format given in Appendix G consenting audit of manufacturing site/offices  
4. Supporting documents as per application form  
5. Application fee payment receipt  
6. Documents evidencing financial closure (for benefit-share cases)  
7. Duly signed funding agreement  
8. Share allotment agreement (if benefit-share via share allotment)  
9. Report validating product/technology at claimed TRL (if required)  
10. Project plan based on sequential milestones  
11. Valuation report of IP proposed to be in-licensed from Academia (if applicable)  
12. Academic intent letter (if applicable)  
13. Certificate of Incorporation/Registration  
14. PAN of the entity  
15. Details of funding received (if any)
