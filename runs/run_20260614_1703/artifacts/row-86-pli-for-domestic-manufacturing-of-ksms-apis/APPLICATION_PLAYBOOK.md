# Application Playbook

## How to Use This File
For each new client case, assign it a Case ID, pick the target scheme, and follow the steps below.

---

## Stage 1: Eligibility Verification (Day 1–3)

### Checklist
- [ ] Applicant must be a pharmaceutical manufacturer or MSME registered with Ministry of MSME, Government of India.
- [ ] Applicant must fall into one of the three groups based on Global Manufacturing Revenue (FY 2019-20) of pharmaceutical goods and/or in vitro Diagnostic Medical Devices:
  - Group A: Revenue ≥ Rs. 5,000 crore
  - Group B: Revenue between Rs. 500 crore (inclusive) and Rs. 5,000 crore
  - Group C: Revenue < Rs. 500 crore (includes MSME sub-group)
- [ ] Applicant must not have been declared bankrupt, wilful defaulter, or reported as fraud by any bank or financial institution.
- [ ] Applicant must apply within the application window: 2nd June 2021 to 31st July 2021 (both dates inclusive).
- [ ] Applicant must be engaged in manufacturing of eligible products as listed in Appendix A of the guidelines.
- [ ] Applicant must commit to incremental sales over the base year (FY 2019-20) to claim incentives.
- [ ] Applicant must be willing to undertake audit of manufacturing site/offices for verification of submitted information.

---

## Stage 2: Document Collection (Day 3–10)

### Universal Documents (always required)
- Incorporation Certificate
- PAN Card
- ITR (last 3 years)
- GST Registration Certificate
- Bank Statements (last 6 months)
- Udyam Registration (for MSME applicants)

### Scheme-Specific Documents
- Application form as per Appendix K
- Undertaking in the format given in Appendix E
- Undertaking in the format given in Appendix G
- Supporting documents (as specified in guidelines)
- Proof of application fee payment (non-refundable, as per Appendix C)

### Document Quality Check
- All documents must be legible, scanned in PDF format.
- File names must match the document type (e.g., "PAN_Card.pdf").
- File size must not exceed 5 MB per document.
- Undertakings must be signed and stamped where applicable.
- Documents must be in English or Hindi; if in another language, certified translation must be attached.
- All financial documents must be certified by a Chartered Accountant if required.

## Stage 3: Application Drafting (Day 10–18)

### Project Report / DPR (if required)
Not explicitly required for this scheme. However, applicants must submit a project proposal with details on eligible products, investment plan, and sales projections. **Verify with nodal officer if a Detailed Project Report (DPR) is required.**

### Application Form Filling Protocol
1. Use only the official application form as per Appendix K.
2. Fill all fields in block letters; do not leave any mandatory field blank.
3. Attach all required documents as per the checklist.
4. Ensure the undertaking in Appendix E and Appendix G are signed and dated.
5. Declare that the applicant has not been declared bankrupt or wilful defaulter.
6. Specify the correct group (A, B, or C) based on FY 2019-20 Global Manufacturing Revenue.
7. List only eligible products from Appendix A for which incentive is sought.
8. Submit the application fee electronically only (as per guidelines).
9. Retain a screenshot of the submitted application and the acknowledgment receipt.
10. Do not submit after the application window closes (31st July 2021).

### Internal Review Gate
- [ ] All sections of the application form are completed.
- [ ] All scheme-specific documents (Appendix K, E, G) are attached and signed.
- [ ] Universal documents are present and valid.
- [ ] Application fee payment proof is attached.
- [ ] Declarations regarding bankruptcy, wilful default, and fraud are included.
- [ ] Product list matches Appendix A of the guidelines.
- [ ] Group classification (A/B/C) is correctly calculated and mentioned.
- [ ] Application is submitted within the stipulated window (2nd June – 31st July 2021).

## Stage 4: Submission & Follow-Up (Day 18–30)

### Submission Protocol
- Submit the application via the online portal: https://pli-pharma.udyamimitra.in
- Take a screenshot of the submission confirmation page showing the application number and timestamp.
- Store the application reference number securely.
- Set a follow-up reminder for 15 working days after submission to check for acknowledgment or deficiency notice from PMA.

## Stage 5: Post-Approval (Day 30–90+)

### Approval Actions
- [ ] Obtain and verify the approval letter from the Department of Pharmaceuticals.
- [ ] Confirm the sanctioned incentive amount and eligibility group (A/B/C).
- [ ] Prepare and submit Utilization Certificate (as per GFR 12A) for fund utilization.
- [ ] Ensure timely submission of annual incremental sales claims for incentive disbursement (based on yearly threshold criteria).
- [ ] Maintain records for audit by PMA or statutory authorities.
- [ ] Track success fee invoice for consulting services upon approval.
