## Pricing Principles
1. **Value-Based Alignment**: Fees are calibrated to the scheme’s incremental sales-based incentive structure (up to 10% for Category 1&2 goods over 6 years), ensuring consultant compensation reflects the actual financial benefit delivered to clients.
2. **Risk-Adjusted Pricing**: Success fees are set at the lower end of industry ranges (2–4% for Enterprise tier) due to the scheme’s 6-year payout horizon, rigorous audit requirements, and dependency on verified incremental sales over a base year (FY 2019-20), which increases implementation complexity and revenue lag.
3. **Tiered Accessibility**: The Enterprise tier exclusively applies to this scheme, as eligibility requires Global Manufacturing Revenue ≥ ₹500 crore (Group B) or ≥ ₹5,000 crore (Group A), placing clients firmly in the mid-to-large enterprise segment.
4. **Transparency & Trust**: All fees are disclosed upfront; retainers cover application preparation and compliance setup, while success fees are contingent on verified disbursement of incentives, aligned with the scheme’s reimbursement-based disbursement model.
