# Pitch & Sales Scripts

We are advisors, not salespeople. We diagnose first. We only pitch a scheme if it fits the client. We never oversell success rates.

## 1. Discovery Call Script (First Contact — 20 min)

### Opening (2 min)
Good morning/afternoon. I specialize in helping pharmaceutical manufacturers and MSMEs access government incentives for domestic manufacturing of Key Starting Materials (KSMs), Drug Intermediates, and Active Pharmaceutical Ingredients (APIs). The PLI scheme for Domestic Manufacturing of KSMs/APIs is designed to strengthen India’s pharmaceutical self-reliance by providing financial incentives on incremental sales. I’d like to understand your current manufacturing footprint and growth plans to see if this scheme aligns with your objectives.

### Discovery Questions
1. What is your company’s Global Manufacturing Revenue (FY 2019-20) for pharmaceutical goods and/or in vitro Diagnostic Medical Devices?
2. Are you currently manufacturing any KSMs, Drug Intermediates, or APIs in India?
3. What was your base year (FY 2019-20) sales figure for the products you intend to claim incentives on?
4. Do you have plans to increase investment in manufacturing capacity for KSMs/APIs over the next 6 years?
5. Are you registered as an MSME with the Ministry of MSME, Government of India?
6. Have you been declared bankrupt, a wilful defaulter, or reported as fraud by any bank or financial institution?
7. Are you prepared to submit an undertaking consenting to audit of your manufacturing site/offices for verification?
8. What is your timeline for applying—are you aware the application window was open from 2nd June 2021 to 31st July 2021?

### Qualification Checklist (internal — don't read aloud)
- [ ] Global Manufacturing Revenue (FY 2019-20) ≥ Rs. 5,000 crore (Group A) OR between Rs. 500 crore and Rs. 5,000 crore (Group B) OR < Rs. 500 crore (Group C, includes MSME sub-group)
- [ ] Applicant is engaged in manufacturing of pharmaceutical goods and/or in vitro Diagnostic Medical Devices
- [ ] Applicant commits to incremental sales over base year (FY 2019-20)
- [ ] Applicant agrees to undertake audit of manufacturing site/offices
- [ ] Applicant is not bankrupt, wilful defaulter, or reported as fraud by any bank/financial institution
- [ ] Application submitted within the window: 2nd June 2021 to 31st July 2021 (both dates inclusive)
- [ ] Willing to pay non-refundable application fee as per Appendix C
- [ ] Prepared to submit Application Form (Appendix K), Undertaking (Appendix E), Undertaking (Appendix G), supporting documents, and application fee

### Transition to Pitch
Based on your responses, it appears you may qualify under Group [A/B/C] of the PLI for Domestic Manufacturing of KSMs/APIs scheme. This scheme offers financial incentives on incremental sales for up to 6 years, with rates ranging from 3% to 10% depending on the product category and year. Shall I walk you through how this could work for your business?

## 2. Scheme Pitch (5 min)

### Structure: Problem → Scheme → Value → Proof → Next Step

**Problem framing:**
Indian pharmaceutical manufacturers face high dependence on imported Key Starting Materials (KSMs), Drug Intermediates, and APIs, which increases costs, supply chain vulnerability, and limits value capture in the global pharmaceutical value chain. Scaling domestic manufacturing requires significant upfront investment, which many MSMEs and mid-sized firms struggle to finance.

**Scheme intro:**
The Production Linked Incentive (PLI) Scheme for Promotion of Domestic Manufacturing of critical Key Starting Materials (KSMs)/Drug Intermediates and Active Pharmaceutical Ingredients (APIs) in the Country, administered by the Department of Pharmaceuticals, Ministry of Chemicals and Fertilizers, Government of India, provides financial incentives on incremental sales over the base year (FY 2019-20). The scheme has a total outlay of Rs 15,000 crore, allocated across three groups: Group A (Rs 11,000 crore), Group B (Rs 2,250 crore), and Group C (Rs 1,750 crore). Incentive rates are 10% for FY 2022-23 to FY 2025-26, 8% for 2026-27, and 6% for 2027-28 for Category 1 & 2 goods; and 5% for FY 2022-23 to FY 2025-26, 4% for 2026-27, and 3% for 2027-28 for Category 3 goods, payable for a maximum of 6 years.

**Why they qualify (personalised):**
- Your Global Manufacturing Revenue (FY 2019-20) falls within [Group A/B/C] range, making you eligible to apply under the respective group.
- You are engaged in manufacturing of pharmaceutical goods and/or in vitro Diagnostic Medical Devices, which are covered under the scheme.
- You commit to achieving incremental sales over your base year (FY 2019-20), which is the basis for incentive calculation.
- You are willing to submit the required undertakings (Appendix E and G) consenting to audit of your manufacturing site/offices for verification.
