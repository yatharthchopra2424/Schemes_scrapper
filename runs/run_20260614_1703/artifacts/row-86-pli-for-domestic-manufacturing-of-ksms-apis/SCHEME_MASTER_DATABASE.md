### PLI for Domestic Manufacturing of KSMs/APIs
- **Scheme ID:** SCH-001
- **Category:** Subsidy
- **Geography:** Pan-India
- **Administering Body:** Department of Pharmaceuticals, Ministry of Chemicals and Fertilizers, Government of India
- **Portal / Application URL:** https://pli-pharma.udyamimitra.in
- **Current Status:** Closed
- **Window:** 2nd June, 2021 to 31st July, 2021 (Both dates inclusive)

#### Eligibility Criteria
- Business type: Pharmaceutical manufacturers and/or in vitro Diagnostic Medical Devices manufacturers
- Age of business: Based on Global Manufacturing Revenue (FY 2019-20)
- Sector: Pharmaceutical manufacturing (KSMs/APIs)
- Revenue cap: 
  - Group A: ≥ ₹5,000 crore
  - Group B: ₹500 crore to < ₹5,000 crore
  - Group C: < ₹500 crore (includes MSME sub-group)
- Employee count: [TO BE FILLED BY CONSULTANT]
- Other: Applicants must not have been declared bankrupt, wilful defaulter, or reported as fraud by any bank/financial institution/NBFC

#### What It Offers
- Amount / Benefit: Incentive rates: 
  - Category 1 & 2 goods: 10% (FY 2022-23 to FY 2025-26), 8% (2026-27), 6% (2027-28)
  - Category 3 goods: 5% (FY 2022-23 to FY 2025-26), 4% (2026-27), 3% (2027-28)
  - Additional incentive available as per para 7.2.3 of guidelines
- Disbursement mode: Based on incremental sales over base year (FY 2019-20)
- Duration of benefit: Maximum of 6 years

#### Documents Required
1. Application form as per Appendix K
2. Undertaking in the format given in Appendix E
3. Undertaking in the format given in Appendix G
4. Supporting documents
5. Application fee

#### Application Process (numbered steps)
1. The applicant is required to submit the application as per application form prescribed in Appendix K.
2. The Scheme shall be open for applications during the Application Window. No application shall be accepted after the end of the Application Window.
3. An applicant can apply for one or more products as per list of eligible products as given in Appendix A.
4. An applicant shall submit an undertaking in the format given in Appendix E and also an undertaking in the format given in Appendix G consenting audit of their manufacturing site/offices for verification of information/data submitted along with the application.
5. On receipt of an application in the prescribed format, PMA will conduct an examination as per checklist in Appendix I. The aforesaid examination shall be completed within 15 working days from the date of receipt of the application or any subsequent submission of the revised application, if the original application was returned as incomplete earlier. Thereafter, the PMA shall issue an acknowledgement of receipt of the application. This acknowledgement shall not be construed as approval under the Scheme.
6. In case, on the above-mentioned examination, an application is found to be incomplete, PMA shall inform the applicant accordingly within 15 working days of receipt of the application. An applicant must complete an incomplete application within 15 days of such communication from PMA, failing which the application will be closed under intimation to the applicant.
7. A non-refundable application fee, as mentioned in Appendix C of these guidelines, would be payable for each application. The application fee would be accepted electronically only.
8. All applications will be submitted through an online portal maintained by the PMA. In case, the portal is not available, applications may be submitted in physical form to the PMA.
