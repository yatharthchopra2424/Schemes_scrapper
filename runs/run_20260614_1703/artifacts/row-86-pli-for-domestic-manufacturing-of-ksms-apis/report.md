# Comprehensive Scheme Masterclass & File Guide

## Scheme Deep Dive

### Scheme Overview
**Scheme Name:** PLI for Domestic Manufacturing of KSMs/APIs  
**Scheme ID:** row-86  
**Ministry / Category:** Pharmaceuticals (PLI)  
**Scheme Type:** subsidy  
**Geographic Scope:** Pan-India  
**Implementing Agency:** Department of Pharmaceuticals, Ministry of Chemicals and Fertilizers, Government of India  
**Application Portal:** https://pli-pharma.udyamimitra.in  
**Status / Deadlines:** The application window shall be of 60 days starting from 2nd June, 2021 to 31st July, 2021 (Both dates inclusive).  
**Last Updated:** 2021  
**Fund Size (Cr):** 15000  
**Confidence:** high  

### Objectives
- Enhance India’s manufacturing capabilities by increasing investment and production in the sector  
- Contribute to product diversification to high value goods in the pharmaceutical sector  
- Create global champions out of India who have the potential to grow in size and scale using cutting edge technology  
- Penetrate the global value chains by enhancing domestic production capability in APIs/KSMs  
- Ensure higher resilience of the Indian pharmaceutical industry to external shocks  
- Achieve higher objective of affordable healthcare in the country and globally on a sustained basis  

### Eligibility Matrix
| Group | Global Manufacturing Revenue (FY 2019-20) | Sub-group Details |
|-------|-------------------------------------------|-------------------|
| **Group A** | ≥ Rs. 5,000 crore | Applicants having Global Manufacturing Revenue of pharmaceutical goods and/or in vitro Diagnostic Medical Devices more than or equal to Rs. 5,000 crore |
| **Group B** | Rs. 500 crore to < Rs. 5,000 crore | Applicants having Global Manufacturing Revenue of pharmaceutical goods and/or in vitro Diagnostic Medical Devices between Rs. 500 (inclusive) crore and Rs. 5,000 crore |
| **Group C** | < Rs. 500 crore | Applicants having Global Manufacturing Revenue of pharmaceutical goods and/or in vitro Diagnostic Medical Devices less than Rs. 500 crore. This group includes a sub-group for MSME applicants registered with the Ministry of MSME, Government of India |

### Benefits & Financial Support
**Total Incentive Pool:** Rs 15,000 crore (inclusive of administrative expenditure)  
**Incentive Allocation by Target Group:**  
- Group A: Rs 11,000 crore  
- Group B: Rs 2,250 crore  
- Group C: Rs 1,750 crore  

**Incentive Structure:**  
Incentives are paid for a maximum of 6 years based on incremental sales over the base year (FY 2019-20).  

**Incentive Rates by Category and Year:**  

| Financial Year | Category 1 & 2 Goods | Category 3 Goods |
|----------------|----------------------|------------------|
| FY 2022-23 to FY 2025-26 | 10% | 5% |
| FY 2026-27 | 8% | 4% |
| FY 2027-28 | 6% | 3% |

**Additional Incentive:** Available as per para 7.2.3 of the guidelines.  

**Benefit Description:** Financial incentive on incremental sales of pharmaceutical goods based on yearly threshold criteria of minimum cumulative investment and minimum percentage growth in sales.

### Key Caveats
> - The applicant should not have been declared as bankrupt or wilful defaulter or reported as fraud by any bank or financial institution or non-banking financial company.  
> - Incentive is the financial benefit to be provided to each selected participant based on the incremental sales and laid down eligibility criteria.  
> - Additional incentive is the amount that would be available to any participant in terms of para 7.2.3.  
> - The Scheme shall be open for applications during the Application Window. No application shall be accepted after the end of the Application Window.  
> - All non-creditable taxes and duties would be included in such expenditure.  

> **Portal Reference:** All applications must be submitted through the online portal maintained by the PMA at https://pli-pharma.udyamimitra.in. In case the portal is not available, applications may be submitted in physical form to the PMA.
