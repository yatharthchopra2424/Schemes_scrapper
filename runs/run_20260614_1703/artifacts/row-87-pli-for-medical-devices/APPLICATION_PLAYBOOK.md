# Application Playbook

## How to Use This File
For each new client case, assign it a Case ID, pick the target scheme, and follow the steps below.

---

## Stage 1: Eligibility Verification (Day 1–3)

### Checklist
- [ ] Applicant is a manufacturer of medical devices (including in-vitro diagnostic medical devices)
- [ ] Global Manufacturing Revenue (FY 2019-20) of pharmaceutical goods and/or in-vitro diagnostic medical devices is less than Rs. 500 crore
- [ ] Applicant is registered as a Micro, Small & Medium Enterprise (MSME) with the Ministry of MSME, Government of India (if applying under MSME sub-group)
- [ ] Applicant has not been declared bankrupt, wilful defaulter, or reported as fraud by any bank or financial institution
- [ ] Minimum cumulative investment commitment of Rs. 50 crore over 5 years (FY 2021-22 to FY 2025-26) (for non-MSME applicants); for MSME applicants, investment threshold is as committed in the application form
- [ ] Year-wise investment thresholds met (if applicable): FY 2021-22: Rs. 10 crore, FY 2022-23: Rs. 20 crore, FY 2023-24: Rs. 30 crore, FY 2024-25: Rs. 40 crore, FY 2025-26: Rs. 50 crore
- [ ] Applicant is engaged in manufacturing of eligible medical devices as per the scheme guidelines
- [ ] Second-hand/used/refurbished plant, machinery, equipment, utilities, or R&D equipment is not considered for investment eligibility
- [ ] Expenditure on consumables and raw materials used for manufacturing is not considered as investment
- [ ] Investment made under this scheme will not be considered again for other PLI schemes

## Stage 2: Document Collection (Day 3–10)

### Universal Documents (always required)
- Incorporation Certificate
- PAN Card
- ITR (last 3 years)
- GST Registration Certificate
- Bank Statements (last 6 months)
- Udyam Registration Certificate (for MSME verification)

### Scheme-Specific Documents
- Application form as prescribed under the guidelines
- Supporting documents
- Application fee (to be paid electronically)
- Undertaking in the format given in Appendix E
- Undertaking in the format given in Appendix G consenting audit of manufacturing site/offices
- Documents evidencing financial closure for the approved total project cost
- Duly signed funding agreement with the approved project plan annexed
- Where applicable, duly signed share allotment agreement for benefit-share option
- Report validating that the product or technology is at TRL claimed in the application (if required by CG)
- Certificate from Statutory Auditor along with supporting documents such as receipts for total expenditure incurred
- Proof of publication of clinical data of Clinical Investigation/Post Market Clinical Follow-up/Clinical Performance Evaluation
- Revised Schedule M certificate or WHO-GMP certificate with CA certified expenditure incurred after 01.01.2024 on eligible activities
- Permission to conduct clinical investigation/clinical performance evaluation/PMCF study issued under provisions of Medical Devices Rules 2017
- Approved Protocol by CDSCO for conducting clinical investigation/clinical performance evaluation/PMCF study
- Detailed process of conducting Clinical Investigations or Pre-Clinical animal studies for medical device or Clinical Performance Evaluation of new IVDs

### Document Quality Check
- All documents must be legible and in PDF format
- File size for each document should not exceed 10 MB
- Document names must match the checklist (e.g., "Statutory_Auditor_Certificate.pdf")
- All certificates must be valid and issued by recognized authorities
- Undertakings must be duly signed and stamped
- Financial documents must be certified by a Chartered Accountant
- All documents must be in English or accompanied by a certified translation
- Applicant name and registration number must be consistent across all documents

## Stage 3: Application Drafting (Day 10–18)

### Project Report / DPR (if required)
A Detailed Project Report (DPR) is required for this scheme. Standard sections include:  
- Executive Summary  
- Project Overview and Objectives  
- Technical Details (manufacturing process, technology used)  
- Market Analysis and Demand Assessment  
- Financial Projections (investment, sales, incentive calculation)  
- Implementation Schedule (Gantt chart)  
- Risk Assessment and Mitigation Plan  
- Compliance with Eligibility Criteria  
- Details of Incremental Sales Calculation (Base Year: FY 2019-20)  
- Undertakings and Declarations  
- Annexures (supporting documents, certificates, etc.)

### Application Form Filling Protocol
1. Ensure all fields in the application form are filled accurately; leave no blank sections unless marked as optional.  
2. Use the exact legal name as per the Incorporation Certificate and PAN.  
3. Global Manufacturing Revenue (FY 2019-20) must be derived from audited financial statements.  
4. Investment details must align with the year-wise thresholds (FY 2021-22 to FY 2025-26).  
5. Clearly specify the list of eligible medical devices to be manufactured.  
6. Attach all supporting documents as per the checklist; verify file names and formats before upload.  
7. Declarations and undertakings must be digitally signed or physically signed and scanned.  
8. Application fee must be paid electronically; retain the transaction ID and receipt.  
9. Double-check that the undertaking for audit consent (Appendix G) is included.  
10. Confirm that the applicant is not defaulter or bankrupt via bank certification.  
11. For MSME applicants, ensure Udyam registration is valid and investment commitment is stated in the form.  
12. Submit only via the official portal: https://pli-pharma.udyamimitra.in

## Stage 4: Submission & Follow-Up (Day 18–30)

### Submission Protocol
- Submit the application exclusively through the online portal: https://pli-pharma.udyamimitra.in  
- Upon submission, take a screenshot of the confirmation page showing the Application Reference Number and timestamp  
- Store the Application Reference Number securely in the case file  
- Save a PDF copy of the submitted application and all attached documents  
- Set a follow-up reminder for 15 working days post-submission to check for acknowledgment from PMA  
- If application is marked incomplete, respond within 15 days with the requested documents

## Stage 5: Post-Approval (Day 30–90+)

### Approval Actions
- [ ] Obtain and verify the approval letter from the Department of Pharmaceuticals  
- [ ] Confirm the sanctioned amount and incentive rate (5% for FY 2022-23 to FY 2025-26, 4% for 2026-27, 3% for 2027-28)  
- [ ] Ensure the funding agreement is executed and project-specific bank account is opened  
- [ ] Monitor achievement of year-wise investment thresholds and cumulative investment of Rs. 50 crore by FY 2025-26  
- [ ] Track incremental sales over base year (FY 2019-20) for disbursement eligibility  
- [ ] Prepare and submit Utilization Certificate (GFR-12A) via PFMS portal for each instalment  
- [ ] Ensure expenditure details are uploaded in the EAT-02 module of PFMS before subsequent instalments  
- [ ] Maintain records for audit by Comptroller & Auditor General of India  
- [ ] Submit annual claims for incentive disbursement within one month of financial year closure  
- [ ] Coordinate with PMA for verification of claims and disbursement of incentive  
- [ ] Ensure no duplication of investment claims under other PLI schemes  
- [ ] Plan for successful project completion within 2 years (extendable by 1 year with SSC approval)  
- [ ] Prepare for final settlement and potential refund if guidelines are not followed
