## 1. Executive Summary
Based on our initial review of [Client]'s business profile, we have identified Production Linked Incentive (PLI) Scheme for Promoting Domestic Manufacturing of Medical Devices — a subsidy scheme administered by the Department of Pharmaceuticals, Ministry of Chemicals and Fertilizers, Government of India — for which your business appears eligible. The potential benefit is financial incentive on incremental sales of eligible medical devices manufactured in India, disbursed over a period of 6 years for sales of FY 2022-23 to FY 2027-28, with incentive rates of 5% for FY 2022-23 to FY 2025-26, 4% for 2026-27, and 3% for 2027-28 on incremental sales over base year (FY 2019-20).
