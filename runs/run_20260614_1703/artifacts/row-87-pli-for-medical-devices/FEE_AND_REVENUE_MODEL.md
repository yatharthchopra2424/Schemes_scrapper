## Pricing Principles
1. Retainer covers initial eligibility assessment, documentation preparation, and application submission support.
2. Success fee is contingent on approval and disbursement of PLI incentives, aligning consultant incentives with client outcomes.
3. Tiered success fees reflect decreasing marginal effort as client scale and scheme value increase.
4. Scheme-specific note: The PLI for Medical Devices scheme involves a 6-year incentive period (FY 2022-23 to FY 2027-28) with disbursement lagged by ~1 year post-sales, resulting in moderate risk due to long approval-to-disbursement timelines and dependency on incremental sales performance; however, high success likelihood exists for eligible applicants meeting investment thresholds, as evidenced by approved applicant lists and clear disbursement mechanics.
