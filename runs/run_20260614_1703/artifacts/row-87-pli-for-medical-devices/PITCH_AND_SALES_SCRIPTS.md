# Pitch & Sales Scripts

## Philosophy
We are advisors, not salespeople. We diagnose first. We only pitch a scheme if it fits the client. We never oversell success rates.

## 1. Discovery Call Script (First Contact — 20 min)

### Opening (2 min)
Good morning/afternoon. I specialize in helping medical device manufacturers in India access government incentives that directly support domestic production and reduce import dependence. The PLI for Medical Devices scheme is designed specifically for companies like yours that are manufacturing in India and looking to scale up through incremental sales incentives. I’d like to understand your current operations to see if this is a fit.

### Discovery Questions
1. What is your company’s Global Manufacturing Revenue (GMR) from pharmaceutical goods and/or in vitro diagnostic medical devices for FY 2019-20?
2. Are you registered as an MSME with the Ministry of MSME, Government of India?
3. What is your planned cumulative investment over FY 2021-22 to FY 2025-26, and have you met the year-wise thresholds (e.g., Rs. 10 crore in FY 2021-22)?
4. Have you been declared bankrupt, a wilful defaulter, or reported as fraud by any bank or financial institution?
5. Are you manufacturing eligible medical devices in India, and do you have the necessary permissions for clinical investigations or performance evaluations if applicable?
6. Do you have documents evidencing financial closure for your approved project cost?
7. Are you planning to claim incentives based on incremental sales over base year FY 2019-20?
8. Are you aware that second-hand/refurbished plant and machinery are not eligible for investment under this scheme?

### Qualification Checklist (internal — don't read aloud)
- [ ] GMR < Rs. 500 crore (FY 2019-20) for pharmaceutical goods and/or IVD medical devices  
- [ ] MSME registration (if applicable for relaxed investment threshold)  
- [ ] Minimum cumulative investment of Rs. 50 crore over FY 2021-22 to FY 2025-26 (with year-wise thresholds met)  
- [ ] Not bankrupt, wilful defaulter, or reported as fraud by any bank/FI  
- [ ] Manufacturing eligible medical devices in India  
- [ ] No second-hand/refurbished plant/machinery claimed as investment  
- [ ] Expenditure on consumables/raw material not counted as investment  
- [ ] Investment not claimed under other PLI schemes  

### Transition to Pitch
Based on your responses, it appears you may meet the core eligibility for the **PLI for Medical Devices** scheme, which offers a 5% subsidy on incremental sales (over FY 2019-20) for six years. Let me walk you through how this works and what it could mean for your business.

## 2. Scheme Pitch (5 min)

### Structure: Problem → Scheme → Value → Proof → Next Step

**Problem framing:**
Medical device manufacturers in India face high import dependence (over 70% for key devices) and struggle to scale domestic production due to high upfront investment costs and limited incentives for incremental growth. Many are unable to compete globally without support for scaling manufacturing and achieving sales growth thresholds.

**Scheme intro:**
The **Production Linked Incentive (PLI) Scheme for Promoting Domestic Manufacturing of Medical Devices**, administered by the **Department of Pharmaceuticals, Ministry of Chemicals and Fertilizers, Government of India**, provides a **subsidy on incremental sales** (over base year FY 2019-20) of eligible medical devices manufactured in India. For Group C (GMR < Rs. 500 crore), the incentive rate is **5% for FY 2022-23 to FY 2025-26**, **4% for FY 2026-27**, and **3% for FY 2027-28**, with a total financial outlay of **Rs. 1,750 crore**.

**Why they qualify (personalised):**
- Your GMR from pharmaceutical goods and/or IVD medical devices in FY 2019-20 is below Rs. 500 crore (Group C eligibility)  
- You have committed to or are planning the minimum cumulative investment of Rs. 50 crore over FY 2021-22 to FY 2025-26 (with year-wise thresholds: Rs. 10 crore in FY 2021-22, Rs. 20 crore in FY 2022-23, etc.)  
- You are not bankrupt, a wilful defaulter, or reported as fraud by any bank or financial institution  
- You are manufacturing eligible medical devices in India and have or can obtain necessary regulatory permissions (e.g., CDSCO approval for clinical investigations)
