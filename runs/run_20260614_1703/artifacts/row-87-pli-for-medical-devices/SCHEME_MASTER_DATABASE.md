### PLI for Medical Devices
- **Scheme ID:** SCH-001
- **Category:** Subsidy
- **Geography:** Pan-India
- **Administering Body:** Department of Pharmaceuticals, Ministry of Chemicals and Fertilizers, Government of India
- **Portal / Application URL:** https://pli-pharma.udyamimitra.in
- **Current Status:** Closed
- **Window:** 2nd June, 2021 to 31st July, 2021 (Both dates inclusive)

#### Eligibility Criteria
- Business type: Medical device manufacturers
- Age of business: Not specified
- Sector: Medical devices
- Revenue cap: Global Manufacturing Revenue (FY 2019-20) of pharmaceutical goods and/or in vitro Diagnostic Medical Devices less than Rs. 500 crore
- Employee count: Not mentioned
- Other: Applicant should not have been declared as bankrupt or wilful defaulter or reported as fraud by any bank or financial institution or non-banking financial company; MSME participants in Group C have minimum cumulative investment threshold as committed in the application form

#### What It Offers
- Amount / Benefit: Financial incentive on incremental sales of eligible medical devices manufactured in India; rate of incentive is 5% for FY 2022-23 to FY 2025-26, 4% for 2026-27 and 3% for 2027-28 on incremental sales over base year (FY 2019-20); total financial outlay for Group C is Rs. 1,750 crore
- Disbursement mode: Direct credit (incentive disbursed based on achieved incremental sales)
- Duration of benefit: 6 years (for sales of FY 2022-23 to FY 2027-28)

#### Documents Required
1. Application form as prescribed under the guidelines
2. Supporting documents
3. Application fee (to be paid electronically)
4. Undertaking in the format given in Appendix E
5. Undertaking in the format given in Appendix G consenting audit of manufacturing site/offices
6. Documents evidencing financial closure for the approved total project cost
7. Duly signed funding agreement with the approved project plan annexed
8. Where applicable, duly signed share allotment agreement for benefit-share option
9. Report validating that the product or technology is at TRL claimed in the application (if required by CG)
10. Certificate from Statutory Auditor along with supporting documents such as receipts for total expenditure incurred
11. Proof of publication of clinical data of Clinical Investigation/Post Market Clinical Follow-up/Clinical Performance Evaluation
12. Revised Schedule M certificate or WHO-GMP certificate with CA certified expenditure incurred after 01.01.2024 on eligible activities
13. Permission to conduct clinical investigation/clinical performance evaluation/PMCF study issued under provisions of Medical Devices Rules 2017
14. Approved Protocol by CDSCO for conducting clinical investigation/clinical performance evaluation/PMCF study
15. Detailed process of conducting Clinical Investigations or Pre-Clinical animal studies for medical device or Clinical Performance Evaluation of new IVDs

#### Application Process (numbered steps)
1. Applicants must submit an application through the online portal maintained by the Project Management Agency (PMA).
2. The application must contain requisite information, supporting documents, and application fee as prescribed in the guidelines.
3. PMA will conduct an examination of the application as per checklist in Appendix I within 15 working days of receipt.
4. If the application is found incomplete, PMA will inform the applicant to complete it within 15 days; otherwise, the application will be closed.
5. Upon satisfactory completion, PMA will issue an acknowledgement of receipt (not approval under the Scheme).
6. All applications are to be submitted via the online portal at https://pli-pharma.udyamimitra.in.
7. Selection of applicants is based on evaluation criteria including gross manufacturing investment, number of approved manufacturing plants, and GMR from in vitro diagnostic medical devices, with weightages as specified in the guidelines.
8. Selected applicants will be ranked, and incentives will be disbursed based on achieved incremental sales and compliance with eligibility criteria.
