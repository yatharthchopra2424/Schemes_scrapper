# Comprehensive Scheme Masterclass & File Guide

## Scheme Deep Dive

### Scheme Overview
**Scheme Name:** Production Linked Incentive (PLI) for Medical Devices  
**Scheme ID:** row-87  
**Ministry / Category:** Pharmaceuticals (PLI)  
**Scheme Type:** Subsidy  
**Geographic Scope:** Pan-India  
**Implementing Agency:** Department of Pharmaceuticals, Ministry of Chemicals and Fertilizers, Government of India  
**Application Portal:** https://pli-pharma.udyamimitra.in  
**Status / Deadlines:** Application window of 60 days from 2nd June 2021 to 31st July 2021 (both dates inclusive)  
**Last Updated:** 2021  
**Fund Size:** Rs. 1,750 crore  
**Confidence:** High  

### Objectives
- Enhance India's manufacturing capabilities by increasing investment and production in the medical devices sector  
- Contribute to product diversification to high value goods in the medical devices sector  
- Create global champions out of India with potential to grow using cutting-edge technology  
- Penetrate global value chains by promoting domestic manufacturing of medical devices  
- Reduce import dependence in the medical devices sector through incentivized domestic production  
- Support eligible applicants in achieving minimum cumulative investment and sales growth thresholds  

### Eligibility Matrix
| Eligibility Criteria | Details |
|----------------------|---------|
| **Global Manufacturing Revenue (GMR)** | FY 2019-20 GMR of pharmaceutical goods and/or in vitro Diagnostic Medical Devices less than Rs. 500 crore |
| **Sub-group for MSME** | Applicants registered as Micro, Small & Medium Enterprises (MSME) with Ministry of MSME, Government of India |
| **Bankruptcy/Defaulter Status** | Applicant must not have been declared bankrupt, wilful defaulter, or reported as fraud by any bank/financial institution/NBFC |
| **Investment Restrictions** | Second-hand/used/refurbished plant, machinery, equipment, utilities, or R&D equipment not considered eligible investment; expenditure on consumables and raw materials not considered investment |
| **PLI Scheme Overlap** | Investment under this scheme shall not be considered again for other PLI schemes |
| **Tie-breaking Rule** | In case of equal scores, applicants with higher R&D expenditure marks ranked higher for Group A/B; for Group C, selection based on respective GMR for FY 2019-20 |

### Benefits & Financial Support
| Parameter | Details |
|----------|---------|
| **Incentive Basis** | Subsidy on incremental sales (over base year FY 2019-20) of eligible medical devices manufactured in India |
| **Incentive Rate (Group C)** | 5% for FY 2022-23 to FY 2025-26; 4% for FY 2026-27; 3% for FY 2027-28 |
| **Disbursement Period** | 6 years for sales of FY 2022-23 to FY 2027-28 |
| **Total Financial Outlay (Group C)** | Rs. 1,750 crore |
| **Minimum Cumulative Investment** | Rs. 50 crore over 5 years (FY 2021-22 to FY 2025-26) |
| **Year-wise Investment Thresholds** | FY 2021-22: Rs. 10 crore; FY 2022-23: Rs. 20 crore; FY 2023-24: Rs. 30 crore; FY 2024-25: Rs. 40 crore; FY 2025-26: Rs. 50 crore |
| **MSME Investment Threshold** | As committed in the application form |
| **Additional Support** | Facilitation through Project Management Agency (PMA) for application processing, eligibility verification, and disbursement of incentives |

### Scheme Tenure & Key Caveats
> **Scheme Tenure:** FY 2020-21 to FY 2028-29, including:  
> - One-year optional gestation period (FY 2021-22)  
> - Incentive period for 6 years (FY 2022-23 to FY 2027-28)  
> - FY 2028-29 for disbursal of incentive for sales of FY 2027-28  
>   
> **Key Caveats:**  
> - Incentive restricted to incremental sales over base year (FY 2019-20) and subject to achieving minimum cumulative investment and sales growth thresholds  
> - Applicants must not have been declared bankrupt, wilful defaulter, or reported as fraud by any bank/financial institution  
> - Second-hand/used/refurbished plant, machinery, equipment, utilities, or R&D equipment not considered eligible investment  
> - Expenditure on consumables and raw materials not considered investment  
> - Investment under this scheme not considered again for other PLI schemes  
> - In case of equal scores during selection, applicants with higher R&D expenditure marks ranked higher for Group A/B; for Group C, selection based on respective GMR for FY 2019-20  
> - Number of applicants to be selected for Group C: 35, of which minimum 5 must be in vitro diagnostic medical devices manufacturers

### Required Documents
1. Application form as prescribed under guidelines  
2. Supporting documents  
3. Application fee (to be paid electronically)  
4. Undertaking in format given in Appendix E  
5. Undertaking in format given in Appendix G (consenting audit of manufacturing site/offices)  
6. Documents evidencing financial closure for approved total project cost  
7. Duly signed funding agreement with approved project plan annexed  
8. Where applicable, duly signed share allotment agreement for benefit-share option  
9. Report validating product/technology at TRL claimed (if required by CG)  
10. Certificate from Statutory Auditor with supporting documents (receipts for total expenditure incurred)  
11. Proof of publication of clinical data of Clinical Investigation/Post Market Clinical Follow-up/Clinical Performance Evaluation  
12. Revised Schedule M certificate or WHO-GMP certificate with CA certified expenditure incurred after 01.01.2024 on eligible activities  
13. Permission to conduct clinical investigation/clinical performance evaluation/PMCF study under Medical Devices Rules 2017  
14. Approved Protocol by CDSCO for conducting clinical investigation/clinical performance evaluation/PMCF study  
15. Detailed process of conducting Clinical Investigations or Pre-Clinical animal studies for medical device or Clinical Performance Evaluation of new IVDs
