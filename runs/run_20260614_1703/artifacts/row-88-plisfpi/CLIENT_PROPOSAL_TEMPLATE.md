## 3. Our Scope of Work
1. Initial eligibility assessment and scheme suitability analysis  
2. Documentation preparation and verification as per scheme requirements  
3. Application submission and portal registration on https://mofpi.gov.in

5. Agreement facilitation upon approval for incentive disbursement
