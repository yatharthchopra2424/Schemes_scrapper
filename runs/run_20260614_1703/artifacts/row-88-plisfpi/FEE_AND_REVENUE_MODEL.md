## Pricing Principles

1. **Value-Based Pricing**: Fees are aligned with the financial benefit delivered to the client under PLISFPI, which provides incentives as a percentage of incremental sales and investment over five years, disbursed annually based on verified claims.
2. **Risk-Sharing Model**: Success fees are contingent on claim approval and disbursement, reflecting the scheme’s performance-linked nature and the risk of non-achievement of incremental sales/investment thresholds.
3. **Transparency & Predictability**: Retainer covers advisory, documentation, and application submission; success fee is payable only upon verified incentive disbursement, ensuring client pays for outcomes.
4. **Scheme-Specific Note**: PLISFPI has a medium confidence level in evidence and involves a five-year disbursement cycle with annual claims, introducing revenue lag and verification risk. Processing time affects cash flow, requiring clear milestone-based invoicing.
