# Pitch & Sales Scripts

## Philosophy
We are advisors, not salespeople. We diagnose first. We only pitch a scheme if it fits the client. We never oversell success rates.

---

## 1. Discovery Call Script (First Contact — 20 min)

### Opening (2 min)
Good morning/afternoon. I specialize in helping food processing units—especially SMEs focused on innovative, organic, or branded products—access government incentives that support scaling operations and market access. The Production Linked Incentive Scheme for Food Processing Industry (PLISFPI) from the Ministry of Food Processing Industries offers financial incentives based on incremental sales and investment, which could be highly relevant for your growth plans.

### Discovery Questions
1. When was your food processing unit incorporated or registered?
2. What products do you currently manufacture, and are any classified as innovative or organic?
3. What is your annual sales turnover, and what was your base year sales (for incremental calculation)?
4. Have you undertaken any branding or marketing activities, and do you have expenditure proofs for these?
5. What is your current investment in plant, machinery, or infrastructure, and do you plan to expand this?
6. Are you maintaining proper records of sales, investment, and production as required for scheme compliance?
7. What are your growth targets for the next 3–5 years in terms of sales or capacity expansion?
8. Are you looking for non-dilutive government support to scale operations without equity dilution?

### Qualification Checklist (internal — don't read aloud)
- [ ] Entity is a food processing unit engaged in manufacturing  
- [ ] Focus on SMEs, innovative/organic products, or branding/marketing activities  
- [ ] Has valid Certificate of Incorporation/Registration  
- [ ] Has PAN and GST registration  
- [ ] Can provide details of plant and machinery investment  
- [ ] Can provide sales turnover details (base year and incremental)  
- [ ] Can provide product list and manufacturing process  
- [ ] Can provide branding and marketing expenditure proof (if applicable)  
- [ ] Has bank account details and authorization letter from signatory  
- [ ] Willing to undertake as per scheme guidelines  

### Transition to Pitch
Based on your responses, it appears your unit aligns well with PLISFPI’s focus on supporting SMEs in food processing through incentives on incremental sales and investment. Let me explain how this scheme could provide financial support of up to a percentage of incremental sales and investment over five years, disbursed annually based on verified claims.

## 2. Scheme Pitch (5 min)

### Structure: Problem → Scheme → Value → Proof → Next Step

**Problem framing:**
Food processing SMEs often struggle to scale operations due to limited access to capital for investment in plant, machinery, branding, and market expansion. Without financial support, achieving incremental sales growth and maintaining compliance with record-keeping and auditing requirements becomes challenging, limiting their ability to enhance domestic manufacturing, exports, and rural employment.

**Scheme intro:**
The Production Linked Incentive Scheme for Food Processing Industry (PLISFPI), administered by the Ministry of Food Processing Industries, is a subsidy-type scheme offering financial incentives as a percentage of incremental sales over base year sales and as a percentage of investment in plant, machinery, and related infrastructure. Incentives are disbursed annually over five years based on verified claims, with support also available for branding, marketing, innovative/organic products, and scaling operations.

**Why they qualify (personalised):**
- Your unit is engaged in food processing manufacturing  
- You focus on innovative/organic products or branding and marketing activities (as discussed)
