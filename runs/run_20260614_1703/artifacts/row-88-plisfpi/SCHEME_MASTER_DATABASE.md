### PLISFPI
- **Scheme ID:** SCH-001
- **Category:** Subsidy
- **Geography:** Pan-India
- **Administering Body:** Ministry of Food Processing Industries
- **Portal / Application URL:** https://mofpi.gov.in
- **Current Status:** Rolling
- **Window:** Always Open

#### Eligibility Criteria
- Business type: Food processing units engaged in manufacturing
- Age of business: Not specified
- Sector: Food processing
- Revenue cap: Not specified
- Employee count: Not specified
- Other: Focus on SMEs, innovative/organic products, and entities undertaking branding and marketing activities

#### What It Offers
- Amount / Benefit: Incentives as a percentage of incremental sales over base year sales and as a percentage of investment in plant, machinery, and related infrastructure
- Disbursement mode: Direct credit (annual disbursement based on verified claims)
- Duration of benefit: Five years (disbursed annually over five years based on verified claims)

#### Documents Required
1. Certificate of Incorporation / Registration
2. PAN of the entity
3. GST registration
4. Details of plant and machinery investment
5. Sales turnover details (base year and incremental)
6. Product list and manufacturing process
7. Branding and marketing expenditure proof (if applicable)
8. Bank account details
9. Authorization letter from signatory
10. Undertaking as per scheme guidelines

#### Application Process (numbered steps)
1. Visit the PLISFPI portal via the Ministry of Food Processing Industries website.
2. Register as an applicant on the portal.
3. Submit application with required documents including business registration, product details, investment proof, and sales data.
4. Application is reviewed by the Ministry or its appointed Programme Management Agency.
5. Upon approval, enter into an agreement for incentive disbursement.
6. Submit periodic claims for incentives based on verified incremental sales and investment.
7. Incentives are released after validation.
