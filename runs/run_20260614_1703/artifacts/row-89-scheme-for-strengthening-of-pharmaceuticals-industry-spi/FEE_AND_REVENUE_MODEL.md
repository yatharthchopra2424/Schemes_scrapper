## Pricing Principles
1. Retainer covers initial eligibility assessment, SPV formation guidance, and document preparation support.
2. Success fee is contingent on SSC approval and disbursement of financial support under SPI.
3. Tiered pricing aligns with client scale and scheme benefit ranges (APICF: up to ₹20 Cr; RPTUAS: up to ₹2 Cr per plant; PMPDS: variable).
4. Scheme-specific note: Moderate risk profile due to 30–90 day PMC appraisal timelines and SSC approval dependencies; however, high success likelihood for well-prepared SPVs with ≥5 pharma units and 51% pharma equity, as evidenced by rolling applications and structured disbursement milestones.
