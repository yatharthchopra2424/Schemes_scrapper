# Comprehensive Scheme Masterclass & File Guide

## Scheme Deep Dive

### Scheme Overview
**Scheme Name:** Scheme for Strengthening of Pharmaceuticals Industry (SPI)  
**Scheme ID:** row-89  
**Ministry / Category:** Pharmaceuticals  
**Scheme Type:** subsidy  
**Geographic Scope:** Pan-India  
**Implementing Agency:** Department of Pharmaceuticals, Ministry of Chemicals and Fertilizers, Government of India  
**Application Portal:** https://spi.udyamimitra.in  
**Status / Deadlines:** Rolling basis — applications are accepted throughout the year as per notifications; specific windows may be opened for sub-schemes (e.g., API-CF invitation from 11.12.2024 to 13.01.2025)  
**Last Updated:** 2024  
**Fund Size (Cr):** 500  
**Confidence:** high  

### Objectives
1. To strengthen the existing infrastructure facilities in order to make India a global leader in Pharma Sector by providing financial assistance to pharma clusters for creation of Common Facilities to improve the quality and ensure the sustainable growth of cluster  
2. To upgrade the production facilities of Pharma units to meet up to date regulatory standards, by providing subsidy on reimbursement basis, which will enable them to obtain revised Schedule M and WHO_GMP certifications  
3. To promote knowledge and awareness in and about the Pharmaceutical and Medical Devices Industry by taking up studies, building databases and bringing industry leaders, academia and policy makers together to share their knowledge and experience for overall development of Pharma and Medical Devices industry  

### Eligibility Matrix
| **Eligibility Criteria** | **Details** |
|--------------------------|-------------|
| **Target Beneficiaries** | pharma clusters; MSMEs; pharma manufacturing units |
| **SPV Formation** | Pharmaceutical manufacturing units in a cluster who have come together to form a Special Purpose Vehicle (SPV) to execute the project of developing common facility |
| **Minimum SPV Members** | There shall be a minimum of 5 pharma units as members of (SPV) |
| **SPV Legal Entity** | The SPV should be a separate legal entity registered under the Companies Act or Registration of Societies Act |
| **Pharma Equity Holding** | Pharma enterprises shall hold at least 51% equity of the SPV |
| **Net Worth Requirement** | The combined net worth of members of SPV shall be equivalent to total grant amount applied for and each SPV member must have a net worth of at least 1.5 times of their proposed equity contribution |
| **Related-Party Restriction** | The SPV members shall be legally independent entities without any related-party relationship with each other as described under Accounting Standard (AS) 18 of the Companies (Accounting Standard) Rules, 2006 |

### Benefits & Financial Support
| **Sub-Scheme** | **Financial Support Details** | **Maximum Support** |
|----------------|-------------------------------|---------------------|
| **Assistance to Pharmaceutical Industry for Common Facilities (APICF)** | Capital subsidy or grant-in-aid. For APICF, the incentive is 70% of approved project cost or Rs. 20 crore, whichever is less (90% or Rs. 20 crore for Himalayan and North Eastern States) | Up to Rs. 20 crore for APICF (70% of project cost or Rs. 20 crore, whichever is less); up to Rs. 2 crore per plant for RPTUAS; support for PMPDS activities as per approval |
| **Revamped Pharmaceutical Technology Up-gradation Assistance Scheme (RPTUAS)** | Incentive is based on average turnover: 20% of investment for turnover Rs. 1-50 crore, 15% for Rs. 50-250 crore, and 10% for Rs. 250-500 crore, subject to a maximum of Rs. 2 crore per plant | Up to Rs. 2 crore per plant |
| **Pharmaceutical & Medical Devices Promotion and Development Scheme (PMPDS)** | Support is provided for studies, awareness programs, database creation, and industry promotion | Support for PMPDS activities as per approval |
| **Disbursement** | Disbursement is made in instalments based on milestone achievement, with 50% of eligible amount (up to Rs. 1 crore) released as first instalment upon submission of requisite documents | 50% of eligible amount (up to Rs. 1 crore) as first instalment |

### Application Process
```mermaid
flowchart TD
    A[Pharma units form SPV with min 5 units & ≥51% pharma equity] --> B[SPV submits application via PMC portal with DPR, gap analysis & docs]
    B --> C[PMC examines proposal, processes loan if applicable, forwards to lending institution for sanction within 90 days]
    C --> D[PMC scrutinizes proposal, verifies eligibility, submits appraisal report to Technical Committee within 1 month]
    D --> E[Technical Committee evaluates & recommends to Scheme Steering Committee (SSC)]
    E --> F[SSC approves project & sanctions financial support]
    F --> G[Upon approval, applicant submits documents (Schedule M/WHO-GMP cert, CA expenditure) for instalment release]
    G --> H[First instalment (50% of eligible amount, up to Rs. 1 crore) released within 30 days of doc submission]
    H --> I[Subsequent instalments released upon verification of milestone achievement & submission of required docs]
```

### Key Caveats
> **Warning:** Incentive disbursement is subject to SSC approval and verification of documents by PMC  
> **Warning:** Loan proposals availing benefit under RPTUAS cannot avail benefits under any other technology upgradation scheme  
> **Warning:** Minimum repayment period of loan sanctioned for availing benefit under RPTUAS shall not be less than 3 years  
> **Warning:** For APICF, SPV members must be legally independent entities without any related-party relationship  
> **Note:** In case of Himalayan States and North Eastern Region, grant-in-aid is Rs. 20 crore per cluster or 90% of project cost, whichever is less  

### Required Documents
1. Certificate of Incorporation / Registration of SPV  
2. PAN of the entity  
3. Detailed project report with gap analysis  
4. CA certified expenditure statement  
5. Revised Schedule M certificate or WHO-GMP certificate  
6. Undertaking for fund utilization  
7. Bank sanction letter for loan component (if applicable)  
8. Net worth certificate of SPV members  
9. Shareholding pattern of SPV  
10. Undertaking of legally independent entities without related-party relationships
