## Pricing Principles
1. **Value-Based Pricing**: Fees aligned with the tangible career advancement and employability benefits delivered through industry-recognized certifications.
2. **Risk-Sharing Model**: Success fees reflect the scheme’s non-financial nature — revenue is tied to measurable skill outcomes rather than direct monetary grants.
3. **Transparency**: Clear communication that FutureSkills PRIME provides skill development and certification, not direct financial support, managing client expectations appropriately.
4. **Scalability**: Tiered retainer structure accommodates individual professionals, educational institutions, and corporate clients seeking bulk upskilling programs.

*Note: This scheme carries low financial risk but moderate operational risk due to dependence on course completion and assessment success. Processing time varies by course duration (typically 3–6 months), requiring careful expectation management.*

## Invoice Templates

### Retainer Invoice
**Description**: Retainer fee for FutureSkills PRIME consulting services — including course selection guidance, enrollment support, progress tracking, and certification pathway planning for individuals or groups seeking digital technology upskilling through MeitY and NASSCOM-backed programs.

### Success Fee Invoice
**Description**: Success fee for FutureSkills PRIME consulting services — payable upon successful completion of training and assessment, resulting in industry-recognized certification aligned with NOS and NSQF, enhancing employability in roles such as machine learning engineer, data scientist, business intelligence analyst, or information security analyst.  
**Implementing Agency**: Ministry of Electronics and Information Technology (MeitY) in collaboration with National Association of Software and Service Companies (NASSCOM)
