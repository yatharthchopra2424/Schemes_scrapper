# Pitch & Sales Scripts

## Philosophy
We are advisors, not salespeople. We diagnose first. We only pitch a scheme if it fits the client. We never oversell success rates.

---

## 1. Discovery Call Script (First Contact — 20 min)

### Opening (2 min)
Good morning/afternoon! I specialize in helping students, professionals, and job seekers build in-demand digital technology skills through government-backed initiatives. Given your interest in career advancement in fields like AI, data analytics, or cybersecurity, I wanted to explore whether the Future Skills PRIME scheme—led by MeitY and NASSCOM—could support your upskilling goals with industry-recognized certification.

### Discovery Questions
- What specific digital technology domain are you looking to upskill in—such as artificial intelligence, data analytics, cloud computing, or cybersecurity?
- Are you currently a student, working professional, or actively seeking employment in the IT/ITeS sector?
- Have you explored any certification programs recently, and were they aligned with National Occupational Standards (NOS) or the National Skills Qualification Framework (NSQF)?
- What is your primary goal for upskilling—career transition, promotion, freelancing opportunities, or staying relevant in your current role?
- Are you looking for a program that offers practical, hands-on training with assessments leading to certification?
- Do you prefer self-paced online learning or structured instructor-led programs for your skill development?
- How important is it for you to earn a credential that is recognized by employers in the digital economy?
- Are you aware of any financial support or subsidies available for skill development in emerging technologies?

### Qualification Checklist (internal — don't read aloud)
- [ ] Individual is a student, professional, or job seeker
- [ ] Seeking skills in AI, data analytics, cloud computing, cybersecurity, or related digital domains
- [ ] Interested in certification aligned with NOS and NSQF
- [ ] Looking to enhance employability in digital technology roles
- [ ] Willing to complete training and assessment for certification
- [ ] Based in India (pan-India eligibility)
- [ ] Not seeking direct financial aid (scheme focuses on skill development, not grants)
- [ ] Open to enrolling via the FutureSkills Prime portal

### Transition to Pitch
Based on your responses, it sounds like Future Skills PRIME could be a strong fit—especially since it offers industry-backed certification in high-demand digital domains at no direct cost, helping you boost employability in roles like data scientist or cybersecurity analyst. Let me walk you through how it works.

## 2. Scheme Pitch (5 min)

### Structure: Problem → Scheme → Value → Proof → Next Step

**Problem framing:**
Many professionals and students struggle to find credible, industry-recognized training in emerging technologies like AI, data analytics, or cloud computing. Without NOS/NSQF-aligned certification, it’s difficult to validate skills to employers, limiting career growth in India’s fast-evolving digital economy.

**Scheme intro:**
Future Skills PRIME is a pan-India initiative led by the Ministry of Electronics and Information Technology (MeitY) in collaboration with NASSCOM. It provides access to industry-backed training programs and certification in digital technology domains such as artificial intelligence, data analytics, cloud computing, and cybersecurity—aligned with National Occupational Standards (NOS) and the National Skills Qualification Framework (NSQF). While the scheme does not offer direct financial support, it enables skill development and certification at no cost to the learner through its digital platform.

- You are a student, professional, or job seeker seeking to build skills in digital technology domains
