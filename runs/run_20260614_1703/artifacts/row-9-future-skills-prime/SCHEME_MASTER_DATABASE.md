### Future Skills PRIME
- **Scheme ID:** SCH-001
- **Category:** Employment schemes
- **Geography:** Pan-India
- **Administering Body:** Ministry of Electronics and Information Technology (MeitY) in collaboration with National Association of Software and Service Companies (NASSCOM)
- **Portal / Application URL:** https://futureskillsprime.in/
- **Current Status:** Rolling
- **Window:** Always Open

#### Eligibility Criteria
- Business type: Not applicable (individual-focused scheme)
- Age of business: Not applicable
- Sector: Digital technology domains (AI, data analytics, cloud computing, cybersecurity, etc.)
- Revenue cap: Not mentioned
- Employee count: Not mentioned
- Other: Open to students and professionals seeking to acquire skills in digital technology domains as per National Occupational Standards (NOS) and National Skills Qualification Framework (NSQF); target beneficiaries include students, professionals, and job seekers

#### What It Offers
- Amount / Benefit: No direct financial support; offers industry-recognized certification aligned with NOS and NSQF, enhanced employability in digital technology roles (machine learning engineer, data scientist, business intelligence analyst, information security analyst), access to dynamic learning environment, and validation of skills for career advancement
- Disbursement mode: Certification awarded upon successful completion of training and assessment
- Duration of benefit: One-time certification upon course completion

#### Documents Required
1. No specific documents listed in evidence for enrollment; process involves visiting portal, exploring courses, selecting, enrolling, completing training and assessment

#### Application Process
1. Visit the FutureSkills Prime portal at https://futureskillsprime.in/
2. Explore available courses in emerging technologies
3. Select a course aligned with career goals
4. Enroll in the chosen program
5. Complete the training and assessment
6. Earn certification upon successful completion
