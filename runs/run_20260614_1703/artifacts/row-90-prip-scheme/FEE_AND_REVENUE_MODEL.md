## Pricing Principles
1. Retainer covers initial eligibility assessment, documentation preparation, and application submission support.
2. Success fee is contingent on financial assistance disbursement and aligns consultant incentives with client outcomes.
3. Tiered pricing reflects scheme accessibility: MSMEs/startups (Growth tier) are explicitly eligible for Early Stage Projects, while mid-market and large enterprises benefit from Later Stage Projects.
4. Scheme-specific note: The PRIP Scheme involves moderate risk due to its 30-day application window and milestone-linked disbursement in four instalments, with the fourth instalment being at least 15% of approved financial assistance on a reimbursement basis. Success is evidenced by structured benefit-share mechanisms and clear eligibility criteria for startups/MSMEs, reducing uncertainty for consultants.
