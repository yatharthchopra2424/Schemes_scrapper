## Pricing Principles
1. **Value-Based Alignment**: Fees are tied to the actual financial benefit delivered (₹5 lakhs per family per year), ensuring consultant compensation reflects the scheme’s high impact on reducing out-of-pocket healthcare expenditure for vulnerable families.
2. **Risk Sharing**: Success fees (2–8% depending on tier) align incentives—consultants earn only upon successful beneficiary identification and card issuance, reducing client financial risk.
3. **Transparency & Compliance**: All pricing adheres to NHA guidelines; no hidden charges, and retainers cover baseline compliance activities like document verification and CSC/hospital coordination.
4. **Scheme-Specific Note**: Given the scheme’s rolling basis status and high success rate (over 5 crore hospital admissions authorized, ₹61,501 crore worth of free treatment provided), the risk profile is low to moderate. Processing time is immediate upon document verification at CSCs/empanelled hospitals, enabling near-real-time success fee realization. However, eligibility is strictly SECC-based, limiting applicability to individual consultants serving non-eligible client segments (e.g., MSMEs, startups).
