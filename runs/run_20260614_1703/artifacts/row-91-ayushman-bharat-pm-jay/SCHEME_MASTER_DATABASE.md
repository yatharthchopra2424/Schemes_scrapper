### Ayushman Bharat PM-JAY
- **Scheme ID:** SCH-001
- **Category:** Subsidy
- **Geography:** Pan-India
- **Administering Body:** National Health Authority (NHA)
- **Portal / Application URL:** https://pmjay.gov.in/
- **Current Status:** Rolling
- **Window:** Always Open

#### Eligibility Criteria
- Business type: Not applicable (individual/family-based scheme)
- Age of business: Not applicable
- Sector: Healthcare
- Revenue cap: Not applicable (based on SECC deprivation and occupational criteria)
- Employee count: Not applicable
- Other: Beneficiaries identified based on deprivation and occupational criteria from SECC database; covers poor and vulnerable families including rural households with only one room, no adult member aged 16-59, female-headed households with no adult male member, households with disabled members and no able-bodied adult, SC/ST households, landless households deriving major income from manual casual labour; in urban areas: ragpickers, beggars, domestic workers, street vendors, construction workers, etc.

#### What It Offers
- Amount / Benefit: ₹5 lakhs per family per year
- Disbursement mode: Direct credit (cashless treatment via Ayushman Card at empanelled hospitals)
- Duration of benefit: Annual (renewable yearly)

#### Documents Required
1. Proof of identity (Aadhaar card, Voter ID, PAN card, etc.)
2. Proof of address (ration card, electricity bill, etc.)
3. Ration card or SECC verification document
4. Passport-sized photographs
5. Mobile number for OTP verification

#### Application Process (numbered steps)
1. Check eligibility via the official NHA website or Ayushman App using mobile number or ration card details.
2. Visit a nearby Common Service Centre (CSC) or empanelled hospital with required documents.
3. Submit documents for verification.
4. Upon verification, the Ayushman Card is generated and issued.
5. Use the Ayushman Card for cashless treatment at any empanelled hospital by presenting it along with ID proof.
6. Hospitals submit claims to NHA through the transaction management system for reimbursement.
