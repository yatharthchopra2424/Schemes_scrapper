## Pricing Principles
1. **Value-Based Alignment**: Fees are calibrated to the actual grant quantum available under NAPDDR, which provides substantial financial support for projects like DDACs, Garima Greh, Senior Citizen Homes, and Continuous Care Homes, ensuring consultant remuneration reflects the scale of public funds accessed.
2. **Risk-Adjusted Pricing**: Success fees reflect the scheme’s moderate risk profile — while the grant is substantial and well-defined, processing involves multi-level verification (district, state, central) and document-intensive submissions, with approval timelines varying by notice (typically 3–6 months post-submission).
3. **Transparency & Predictability**: Retainer covers initial eligibility assessment, NGO-DARPAN registration, portal navigation, and document preparation; success fee is contingent only upon GIA sanction and disbursement, aligning incentives with successful outcomes.
4. **Scheme-Specific Note**: Given the scheme’s reliance on the e-Anudaan portal and strict adherence to notices (e.g., AVYAY/IPSrC applications for Gap districts accepted from 01st July 2024 to 15th August 2024), revenue recognition may lag due to fixed application windows and sequential installment disbursement (1st, 2nd, 3rd/final) based on progress reports and inspections.
