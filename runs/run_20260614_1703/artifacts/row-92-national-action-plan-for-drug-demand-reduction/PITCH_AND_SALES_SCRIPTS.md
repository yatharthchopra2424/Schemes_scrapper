# Pitch & Sales Scripts

## Philosophy
We are advisors, not salespeople. We diagnose first. We only pitch a scheme if it fits the client. We never oversell success rates.

---

## 1. Discovery Call Script (First Contact — 20 min)

### Opening (2 min)
Good morning/afternoon. I specialise in helping NGOs working in drug demand reduction, senior citizen care, and transgender welfare access government grants. The National Action Plan for Drug Demand Reduction (NAPDDR) provides Grant-in-Aid for setting up and running projects like District De-Addiction Centres (DDACs), Garima Greh shelters, Senior Citizen Homes, and Continuous Care Homes. I’d like to understand your current work to see if this scheme aligns with your goals.

### Discovery Questions
1. What type of project are you currently running or planning (e.g., DDAC, Garima Greh, Senior Citizen Home, Continuous Care Home)?
2. How long has your NGO been registered and operational in the social sector?
3. Do you have a NGO Unique ID from the NGO-DARPAN portal?
4. Is your organisation registered as an NGO, Trust, or Charitable institution?
5. Have you previously applied for grants under NAPDDR, AVYAY, or SMILE schemes?
6. What is the primary beneficiary group you serve (persons affected by substance abuse, transgender individuals, senior citizens)?
7. Are you proposing a project in a designated Gap district (for AVYAY/IPSrC sub-schemes)?
8. Do you have audited financial statements and bank account details ready for submission?

### Qualification Checklist (internal — don't read aloud)
- [ ] NGO registered for at least two years
- [ ] Minimum two years' experience in the social sector
- [ ] Registered as NGO, Trust, or Charitable institution
- [ ] Possesses NGO Unique ID from NGO-DARPAN portal
- [ ] Organisation's PAN available
- [ ] Project proposal in prescribed proforma ready
- [ ] For DDRS-related projects: UDID number or enrollment ID with disability certificate (if applicable from FY 2023-24)
- [ ] For AVYAY/IPSrC: Project proposed in designated Gap district (if applicable)
- [ ] Commitment to apply exclusively via e-Anudaan portal (https://grants-msje.gov.in/ngo-login)
- [ ] Target beneficiaries align with scheme (persons affected by substance abuse, transgender individuals, senior citizens)

### Transition to Pitch
Based on your responses, it appears your work with [mention specific project type from discovery, e.g., Senior Citizen Homes] in [region/district if mentioned] could be well-supported by the National Action Plan for Drug Demand Reduction (NAPDDR) scheme, which offers Grant-in-Aid for both recurring and non-recurring expenses to establish and operate such facilities.

## 2. Scheme Pitch (5 min)

### Structure: Problem → Scheme → Value → Proof → Next Step

**Problem framing:**
NGOs face significant financial barriers when establishing and sustaining critical services like de-addiction centres, shelters for transgender persons, and senior citizen care homes. Recurring costs for staff, utilities, and programme delivery, along with non-recurring setup costs, often strain limited resources, limiting the scale and continuity of care for vulnerable populations.

**Scheme intro:**
The National Action Plan for Drug Demand Reduction (NAPDDR), administered by the Department of Social Justice and Empowerment, Ministry of Social Justice and Empowerment, Government of India, provides Grant-in-Aid (GIA) to eligible NGOs for the establishment and operation of projects including District De-Addiction Centres (DDACs), Garima Greh shelters, Senior Citizen Homes, and Continuous Care Homes. Financial assistance covers both recurring and non-recurring expenses based on project progress and submission of required documents.

**Why they qualify (personalised):**
- Your NGO has been registered and operational for at least two years with relevant social sector experience  
- You possess a NGO Unique ID from the NGO-DARPAN portal (mandatory for e-Anudaan access)  
- Your project targets eligible beneficiaries such as persons affected by substance abuse, transgender individuals, or senior citizens  
- You are prepared to submit the application exclusively through the e-Anudaan portal (https://grants-msje.gov.in/ngo-login)
