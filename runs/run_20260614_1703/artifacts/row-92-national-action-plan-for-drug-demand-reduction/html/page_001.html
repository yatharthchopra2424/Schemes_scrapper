NGO Registration
  • * Prior to Registration on portal e-ANUDAAN of the MoSJE http://grants-msje.gov.in , it is mandatory for every NGO to register first at NGO- DARPAN portal of NITI Aayog https://ngodarpan.gov.in and to get NGO Unique ID.
  • * During registration, NGO have to submit NGO Unique ID and PAN of the organisation. Common registration details of organisation available on NGO-Darpan will automatically be auto-filled in non-editing mode. A few details like Username/ Password and scheme etc. are required to be submitted by NGO. Take care of all input fields marked with * which are mandatory to fill and follow the instructions.
  • * Registration on this portal needs to be done only once. The NGO(s) who had registered earlier on this portal need not to register again. NGO(s) who have registered multiple times and are having multiple login/userid accounts, will be shown all login accounts. NGO would select only one login/user id account to keep it active in future. All other login/user id account(s) will be deactivated however would remain available for uploading the deficient documents.
  • * For updating any NGO registration details at e-Anudaan, it is to be updated first at NGO Darpan. Update Profile option in NGO login at e-Anudaan would enable NGO to update the profile details.
  • Note: In case of any query relating to the above, kindly send email to support[dot]ngo-msje[at]nic[dot]in for the needful.
