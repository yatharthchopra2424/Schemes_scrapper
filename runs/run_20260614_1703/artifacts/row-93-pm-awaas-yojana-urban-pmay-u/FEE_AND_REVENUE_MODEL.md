## Pricing Principles
1. Value-based pricing aligned with the financial benefit delivered to the client under PMAY-U.
2. Transparent fee structure with no hidden charges; all fees disclosed upfront.
3. Success fee contingent on verified subsidy disbursement to the client’s loan account or bank account.
4. Scheme-specific note: PMAY-U involves moderate risk due to variable processing times (typically 3–6 months for CLSS vertical, longer for BLC/AHP/ISSR verticals) and dependency on ULB/CNA approvals; however, high success rates are evidenced by over 1.25 crore houses sanctioned and ₹8.78 lakh crore investment as of May 2026.
