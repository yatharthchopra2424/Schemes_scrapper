## 1. Discovery Call Script (First Contact — 20 min)

### Opening (2 min)
Good [morning/afternoon], I’m [Your Name] from [Your Firm]. We specialize in helping urban households access government housing support like Pradhan Mantri Awas Yojana - Urban (PMAY-U), which provides direct financial assistance or interest subsidies for pucca houses with basic amenities. I’d like to understand your housing situation to see if PMAY-U could support your goal of owning or improving a home.

### Discovery Questions
1. Are you currently residing in an urban area as per Census 2011 or a notified planning area?
2. Do you or any family member own a pucca (all-weather) house anywhere in India?
3. What is your household’s annual income? (This helps determine eligibility under EWS/LIG/MIG categories.)
4. Are you looking to construct a new house, enhance an existing one, redevelop a slum dwelling, or avail a home loan subsidy?
5. Do you have identity proof (Aadhaar/Voter ID/PAN), income proof, and property/ownership documents ready?
6. Are you availing benefits under any other vertical of PMAY-U currently? (Beneficiaries can use only one vertical.)
7. If applying for a home loan, is the loan amount within ₹6 lakh (EWS/LIG), ₹9 lakh (MIG-I), or ₹12 lakh (MIG-II) for interest subsidy eligibility?
8. Is the carpet area of the proposed or existing house within 30 sq.m (EWS), 60 sq.m (LIG), 160 sq.m (MIG-I), or 200 sq.m (MIG-II) to qualify for CLSS?

### Qualification Checklist (internal — don't read aloud)
- [ ] Family does not own a pucca house in any part of India (self or family member)
- [ ] Adult earning member qualifies as separate household if no pucca house owned
- [ ] Annual income within EWS (≤₹3 lakh), LIG (₹3–6 lakh), MIG-I (₹6–12 lakh), or MIG-II (₹12–18 lakh)
- [ ] Will use only one vertical of PMAY-U (BLC, AHP, ISSR, or CLSS)
- [ ] For CLSS: loan amount ≤₹6 lakh (EWS/LIG), ≤₹9 lakh (MIG-I), or ≤₹12 lakh (MIG-II)
- [ ] For CLSS: carpet area ≤30 sq.m (EWS), ≤60 sq.m (LIG), ≤160 sq.m (MIG-I), or ≤200 sq.m (MIG-II)
- [ ] Beneficiary willing to provide: identity proof, income proof, property details, bank account for DBT, affidavit of no pucca house
- [ ] Located in a statutory town or notified planning/development area (urban)

### Transition to Pitch
Based on your responses, it appears you may be eligible for financial support under Pradhan Mantri Awas Yojana - Urban (PMAY-U), which offers up to ₹1.5 lakh in direct assistance or interest subsidies up to ₹2.67 lakh on home loans. ## 2. Scheme Pitch (5 min)

### Structure: Problem → Scheme → Value → Proof → Next Step

**Problem framing:**
Many urban households struggle to afford pucca houses with basic amenities like toilet, water, and electricity due to high construction costs or limited access to formal credit. Slum dwellers and low-income families often live in kutcha houses without security or dignity, limiting their ability to invest in education, livelihoods, or health.

**Scheme intro:**
Pradhan Mantri Awas Yojana - Urban (PMAY-U), administered by the Ministry of Housing and Urban Affairs (MoHUA), Government of India, is a Centrally Sponsored Scheme (except CLSS, which is a Central Sector Scheme) offering four verticals:  
- **Beneficiary-Led Construction (BLC):** ₹1.5 lakh direct assistance for EWS families to construct or enhance homes  
- **Affordable Housing in Partnership (AHP):** ₹1.5 lakh per EWS house in projects with ≥35% EWS units and ≥250 houses  
- **In-situ Slum Redevelopment (ISSR):** ₹1 lakh per house for slum dwellers  
- **Credit Linked Subsidy Scheme (CLSS):** Interest subsidy of 6.5% (EWS/LIG), 4% (MIG-I), or 3% (MIG-II) on loans up to ₹6 lakh, ₹9 lakh, or ₹12 lakh respectively, with NPV calculated at 9% discount rate  

All houses must have drinking water, power supply, sewerage, and toilet facilities. Preference is given to women, SC/ST/OBCs, minorities, transgender persons, and persons with disabilities.

**Why they qualify (personalised):**
- You do not own a pucca house in India (self or family)  
- Your household income falls within [EWS/LIG/MIG-I/MIG-II] range  
- You are seeking to [construct/enhance/redevelop/take a home loan] for a house in an urban area  
- For CLSS: your loan amount is within ₹[6/9/12] lakh and carpet area ≤[30/60/160/200] sq.m
