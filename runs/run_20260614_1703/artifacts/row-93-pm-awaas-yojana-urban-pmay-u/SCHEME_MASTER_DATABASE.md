### PM Awaas Yojana - Urban (PMAY-U)
- **Scheme ID:** SCH-001
- **Category:** Subsidy
- **Geography:** Pan-India
- **Administering Body:** Ministry of Housing and Urban Affairs (MoHUA), Government of India
- **Portal / Application URL:** https://pmay-urban.gov.in/
- **Current Status:** Rolling
- **Window:** Always Open

#### Eligibility Criteria
- Business type: Beneficiary family (husband, wife, unmarried sons and/or unmarried daughters)
- Age of business: Not applicable (individual/family-based scheme)
- Sector: Urban housing
- Revenue cap: Household income limits: EWS (up to ₹3 lakh/annum), LIG (₹3,00,001–₹6,00,000/annum), MIG-I (₹6,00,001–₹12,00,000/annum), MIG-II (₹12,00,001–₹18,00,000/annum)
- Employee count: Not mentioned
- Other: 
  - Beneficiary family must not own a pucca house (all-weather dwelling unit) in any part of India, either in their name or in the name of any family member.
  - An adult earning member (irrespective of marital status) is treated as a separate household if they do not own a pucca house in any part of India.
  - In case of a married couple, either spouse or both jointly can be eligible for a single house, subject to household income eligibility.
  - An eligible beneficiary can avail benefits under only one of the four verticals (BLC, AHP, ISSR, CLSS).
  - Beneficiaries with pucca house having built-up area less than 21 sq.m may be included for enhancement of existing dwelling units up to 30 sq.m, if enhancement is not possible due to lack of land/space or other reasons, they may get a house under PMAY(U) elsewhere.

#### What It Offers
- Amount / Benefit: 
  - BLC vertical: Central Assistance of ₹1.5 lakh per house for EWS category
  - AHP vertical: Central Assistance of ₹1.5 lakh per EWS house (if project has ≥35% EWS houses and ≥250 total houses)
  - ISSR vertical: Slum redevelopment grant of ₹1 lakh per house for eligible slum dwellers
  - CLSS vertical: Interest subsidy up to ₹2.67 lakh per house (NPV calculated at 9% discount rate); specific subsidy rates: 6.5% for EWS/LIG (loan up to ₹6 lakh), 4% for MIG-I (loan up to ₹9 lakh), 3% for MIG-II (loan up to ₹12 lakh)
- Disbursement mode: Direct Benefit Transfer (DBT) for BLC; upfront credit to loan account via Primary Lending Institutions (PLIs) for CLSS
- Duration of benefit: One-time (for BLC, AHP, ISSR); upfront interest subsidy credit for CLSS (effective for loan tenure)

#### Documents Required
1. Identity proof (Aadhaar card, Voter ID, PAN card, etc.)
2. Income proof (salary slips, income tax returns, etc.)
3. Property details (ownership documents, encumbrance certificate, etc.)
4. Bank account details for DBT transfer
5. Affidavit declaring no pucca house ownership
6. Passport size photographs
7. For CLSS: loan application documents, property papers, income proof

#### Application Process (numbered steps)
1. Visit the PMAY-U website at https://pmay-urban.gov.in/
2. Click on 'Apply for PMAY-U 2.0'
3. Fill in the application form with personal and household details
4. Upload required documents such as identity proof, income proof, and property details
5. Submit the application and await verification by the concerned Urban Local Body (ULB)
6. Upon approval, the applicant will be registered as a beneficiary and can proceed with house construction or enhancement
7. For CLSS beneficiaries, apply through Primary Lending Institutions (PLIs) with loan application and required documents
8. Track application status via CLSS Awas Portal (CLAP) at https://pmayuclap.gov.in/
9. Receive SMS alerts at different stages of application processing
10. After subsidy approval, the amount is credited upfront to the loan account
