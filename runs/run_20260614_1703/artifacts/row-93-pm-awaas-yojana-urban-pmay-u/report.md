# Comprehensive Scheme Masterclass & File Guide

## Scheme Deep Dive

### Overview
Pradhan Mantri Awas Yojana - Urban (PMAY-U) is a flagship housing scheme launched by the Ministry of Housing and Urban Affairs (MoHUA), Government of India on 25.06.2015. The mission aims to provide pucca houses with basic amenities to urban poor, empower women, address housing requirements of slum dwellers, EWS, LIG, and MIG categories, and promote convergence with allied welfare schemes. The original mission period was up to 31.03.2022, extended till 31.12.2025 for completion of houses sanctioned up to 31.03.2022. The scheme is implemented as a Centrally Sponsored Scheme (CSS) except the Credit Linked Subsidy Scheme (CLSS) vertical, which is a Central Sector Scheme. Direct Benefit Transfer (DBT) mode is used for BLC vertical, while interest subsidy under CLSS is credited upfront to loan accounts through Primary Lending Institutions (PLIs).

### Objectives
- Provide pucca houses with basic amenities (drinking water, power supply, sewerage, toilet facility) to urban poor
- Empower women by ensuring house ownership in the name of female member or joint name (overriding priority to widow)
- Address housing requirements of slum dwellers, EWS, LIG, and MIG categories in urban areas
- Promote convergence with allied welfare schemes like PM Surya Ghar: Muft Bijli Yojana
- Facilitate home loans through Bank/HFC linkages with credit guarantee under CRGFTLIH
- Promote use of innovative construction technologies, thermal comfort, and green/sustainable processes
- Accelerate grounding, completion, and occupancy of houses under PMAY-U and PMAY-U 2.0
- Reach out to vulnerable sections and Special Focus Groups for wider scheme coverage

### Eligibility Matrix
| Criteria | Details |
|----------|---------|
| **Beneficiary Family Composition** | Husband, wife, unmarried sons and/or unmarried daughters. An adult earning member (irrespective of marital status) is treated as a separate household if they do not own a pucca house in any part of India. |
| **Pucca House Ownership** | Beneficiary family should not own a pucca house (all-weather dwelling unit) in their name or any family member's name in any part of India. |
| **Married Couples** | Either spouse or both together in joint ownership eligible for a single house, subject to household income eligibility. |
| **Enhancement Eligibility** | Person with pucca house having built-up area < 21 sq.m may be included for enhancement of existing dwelling units up to 30 sq.m. If enhancement not possible due to lack of land/space, eligible for a house under PMAY(U) elsewhere. |
| **Vertical Exclusivity** | Eligible beneficiary can take advantage of only one of the four verticals of the Mission. |
| **Income Categories** | EWS (annual household income ≤ ₹3 lakh), LIG (₹3 lakh < income ≤ ₹6 lakh), MIG-I (₹6 lakh < income ≤ ₹12 lakh), MIG-II (₹12 lakh < income ≤ ₹18 lakh). |
| **Special Focus Groups** | Preference to persons with disabilities, ST/SC/OBCs, Minorities, Transgender, and women (with overriding priority to widow). |
| **Geographic Scope** | Pan-India, covering all statutory towns (Census 2011) and towns notified subsequently, Notified Planning Areas and Notified Development Authorities (excluding rural areas), areas under Industrial Development Authority/Special Area Development Authority/Urban Development Authority or State Legislation. |

### Benefits & Financial Support
#### Vertical-wise Assistance
| Vertical | Central Assistance | Conditions | Notes |
|----------|-------------------|------------|-------|
| **Beneficiary-led Individual House Construction/Enhancement (BLC)** | ₹1.5 lakh per house | For EWS categories only | DBT mode; instalments credited directly to beneficiary's bank account as per geo-tagged stage of construction with PFMS and GIS-based MIS. |
| **Affordable Housing in Partnership (AHP)** | ₹1.5 lakh per EWS house | Projects where ≥35% houses are for EWS category and single project has ≥250 houses | Implemented through public/private sector partnership. |
| **In-situ Slum Redevelopment (ISSR)** | ₹1 lakh per house | For all houses built for eligible slum dwellers | Uses 'Land as Resource' with private developer participation; slum rehabilitation grant for slum redevelopment projects. |
| **Credit Linked Subsidy Scheme (CLSS)** | Interest subsidy up to ₹2.67 lakh per house | For EWS/LIG/MIG beneficiaries seeking housing loans from Banks/HFCs | Interest subsidy credited upfront to loan account through PLIs; NPV calculated at 9% discount rate. |

**Notes:**
- Credit linked subsidy available only for loan amounts up to specified limits; additional loans beyond limit at non-subsidized rate.
- Beneficiary can build house of larger area, but interest subvention limited to first ₹6 lakh only.
- Scheme duration: EWS/LIG from 17.06.2015 to 31.03.2022; MIG from 01.01.2017 to 31.03.2020 (extended via amendments).
- Interest subsidy calculated at 9% NPV over maximum loan tenure of 20 years or actual tenure, whichever is lower.

#### Step-by-Step Process
1. Visit PMAY-U website at https://pmay-urban.gov.in/
2. Click on 'Apply for PMAY-U 2.0'
3. Fill application form with personal and household details
4. Upload required documents (identity proof, income proof, property details, bank account details, affidavit, photographs)
5. Submit application and await verification by concerned Urban Local Body (ULB)
6. Upon approval, applicant registered as beneficiary and can proceed with house construction/enhancement
7. For CLSS beneficiaries: Apply through Primary Lending Institutions (PLIs) with loan application and required documents
8. Track application status via CLSS Awas Portal (CLAP) at https://pmayuclap.gov.in/
9. Receive SMS alerts at different stages of application processing
10. After subsidy approval, amount credited upfront to loan account (CLSS) or via DBT instalments (BLC)

### Required Documents
1. Identity proof (Aadhaar card, Voter ID, PAN card, etc.)
2. Income proof (salary slips, income tax returns, etc.)
3. Property details (ownership documents, encumbrance certificate, etc.)
4. Bank account details for DBT transfer
5. Affidavit declaring no pucca house ownership
6. Passport size photographs
7. For CLSS: loan application documents, property papers, income proof

### Key Caveats
- Beneficiaries can take advantage under one component only.
- Validity of CLSS vertical was up to 31.03.2022.
- Credit linked subsidy available only for loan amounts up to ₹6 lakhs; additional loans beyond ₹6 lakhs at non-subsidized rate.
- Carpet area of houses under CLSS should be up to 30 sq.m for EWS and 60 sq.m for LIG to avail credit linked subsidy.
- Interest subsidy limited to first ₹6 lakh only, even if beneficiary builds house of larger area.
- Houses must have drinking water, power supply, sewerage, and toilet facility.
- Preference to persons with disabilities, ST/SC/OBCs, Minorities, Transgender, and women (overriding priority to widow).
