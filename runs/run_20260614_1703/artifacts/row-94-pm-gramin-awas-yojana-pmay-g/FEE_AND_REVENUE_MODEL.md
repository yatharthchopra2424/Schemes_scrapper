## Pricing Principles
1. **Value-Aligned Fees**: Success fees are calibrated to the actual grant amount (₹1.20–1.30 lakh per beneficiary) to ensure client profitability after consulting costs.
2. **Risk-Adjusted Pricing**: Given the scheme’s dependency on geo-tagged verification and instalment-linked disbursement, success fees reflect moderate execution risk; retainers cover initial eligibility and documentation work.
3. **Tiered Accessibility**: The Growth tier (MSME/Startup equivalent) aligns with individual rural beneficiaries seeking housing support, as the scheme targets economically weaker sections without business turnover criteria — interpreted here as individual household applicants.
4. **Transparency & Trust**: Fees are disclosed upfront; no hidden charges. All DBT-linked payments are verifiable, reducing disputes.

*Note: Although PMAY-G targets individual rural households, consulting firms often serve intermediaries (NGOs, SHGs, or rural entrepreneurs) who aggregate beneficiary applications. Thus, the "Growth" tier is applicable for such service providers managing multiple applications.*
