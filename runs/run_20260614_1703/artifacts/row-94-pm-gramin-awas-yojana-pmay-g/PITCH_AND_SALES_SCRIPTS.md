# Pitch & Sales Scripts

## Philosophy
We are advisors, not salespeople. We diagnose first. We only pitch a scheme if it fits the client. We never oversell success rates.

---

## 1. Discovery Call Script (First Contact — 20 min)

### Opening (2 min)
Good morning/afternoon. I’m calling from [Your Firm Name], and we specialise in helping rural households access government housing support like the Pradhan Mantri Gramin Awas Yojana (PMAY-G). This scheme provides financial assistance to build a pucca house with basic amenities for eligible rural families. I’d like to understand your current housing situation to see if this might be a fit — is now a good time to ask a few quick questions?

### Discovery Questions
1. Where is your household located (state, district, block, village)?  
2. What type of house do you currently live in — pucca, semi-pucca, or kutcha (walls/roof made of grass, thatch, bamboo, wood, mud, etc.)?  
3. How many rooms does your current house have, and what are they made of?  
4. Do you or any family member own a pucca house anywhere in India?  
5. Are you part of any of these groups: Scheduled Caste, Scheduled Tribe, freed bonded labourer, manual scavenger, person with disability, or non-tribal forest dweller?  
6. Do you have an Aadhaar number and a bank or post office account linked to it?  
7. Are you planning to construct a new house, or are you looking to upgrade an existing kutcha house?  
8. Have you been identified as eligible through the Socio Economic and Caste Census (SECC) 2011 or verified by your Gram Sabha?

### Qualification Checklist (internal — don't read aloud)
- [ ] Household is located in a rural area (as per SECC 2011)  
- [ ] Currently houseless or living in a kutcha house (0, 1, or 2 rooms; walls/roof of grass, thatch, bamboo, wood, mud, unburnt brick, etc.)  
- [ ] Does not own a pucca house anywhere in India  
- [ ] Belongs to priority group (SC/ST, freed bonded labourer, manual scavenger, person with disability, non-tribal forest dweller, or legally released bonded labourer)  
- [ ] Has Aadhaar number and bank/post office account linked to Aadhaar  
- [ ] Willing to construct a new pucca house (not upgradation of existing structure)  
- [ ] Identified as eligible via SECC 2011 and Gram Sabha verification  
- [ ] Consent obtained for participation in the scheme  

### Transition to Pitch
Based on your responses, it appears your household may meet the core criteria for the Pradhan Mantri Gramin Awas Yojana (PMAY-G). This scheme offers up to INR 1.30 lakh in financial assistance to build a pucca house with basic amenities, released in three instalments tied to construction progress. May I share how it works and how we’ve helped similar households access this support?

## 2. Scheme Pitch (5 min)

### Structure: Problem → Scheme → Value → Proof → Next Step

**Problem framing:**
Many rural households in India live in kutcha or dilapidated houses that are unsafe, lack basic amenities like toilets, electricity, and clean water, and are vulnerable to weather and structural damage. Upgrading to a pucca house is often financially out of reach without government support.

**Scheme intro:**
The Pradhan Mantri Gramin Awas Yojana (PMAY-G), administered by the Ministry of Rural Development, Government of India, provides a grant of INR 1.20 lakh in plain areas and INR 1.30 lakh in hilly states, difficult areas, and IAP (Integrated Action Plan) districts for the construction of a new pucca house. The assistance is released in three instalments via Direct Benefit Transfer (DBT) to the beneficiary’s Aadhaar-linked bank or post office account, based on construction progress verified through geo-tagged photographs.

**Why they qualify (personalised):**
- You live in a kutcha house (or are houseless), which meets the primary housing deprivation criterion  
- You do not own a pucca house anywhere in India, satisfying the ownership requirement  
- You belong to a priority group (e.g., SC/ST, person with disability, manual scavenger) as per SECC 2011 data  
- You have an Aadhaar-linked bank/post office account, enabling DBT of funds

We offer a free, no-obligation eligibility check. If you qualify, we’ll guide you through the entire process — from consent and account setup to construction and instalment tracking — charging only a success fee upon approval. No upfront cost. Shall we schedule a 30-minute document review to confirm your eligibility?
