## 1. Executive Summary
Based on our initial review of [Client]'s business profile, we have identified Mission Mausam — a centrally funded government scheme administered by the India Meteorological Department (IMD), Ministry of Earth Sciences — for which your business appears eligible. The potential benefit is access to real-time weather forecasts, early warnings, and sector-specific advisories through IMD’s public service infrastructure.

## 3. Our Scope of Work
1. Initial eligibility assessment for Mission Mausam based on client’s sector and use case  
2. Guidance on accessing IMD’s public service channels (website, mobile apps, SMS advisories)  
3. Assistance with portal registration on https://mausam.imd.gov.in/ for data products requiring authorization  
4. Support in interpreting and applying sector-specific advisories (agriculture, aviation, power, water resources, disaster management)  
5. Training on utilizing IMD’s early warning systems (SMS, web portals, mobile apps)  
6. Facilitating access to climate data and research collaboration opportunities  
7. No DPR/project report required, as Mission Mausam is a public service initiative with no formal application process for end-users
