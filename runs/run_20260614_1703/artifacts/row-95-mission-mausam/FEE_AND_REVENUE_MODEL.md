## Pricing Principles
1. Value-based pricing aligned with the strategic importance of weather and climate services for national infrastructure and public safety.
2. Tiered retainer structure reflecting client scale and complexity of engagement with Mission Mausam’s sector-specific advisories.
3. Success fees calibrated to the scheme’s non-transfer nature — revenue is derived from enabling access to public goods, not direct financial transfers.
4. Scheme-specific note: Mission Mausam involves long-term infrastructure investment (Doppler Weather Radars, high-performance computing, satellite systems) with rolling, year-round availability; success is measured in sustained service uptake and institutional reliance, requiring multi-month engagement cycles and posing low financial risk but high operational complexity due to public service nature.
