## 8D. Internal Data Handling Policy

(Standard data handling policy — encrypted cloud storage, 2FA, limited access, no personal devices,
2-year retention post case closure, 24-hour breach notification.)

All client data shall be handled in accordance with the following internal policy:

- **Storage:** All files stored in encrypted cloud storage (AES-256 encryption at rest and in transit).
- **Access Control:** Role-based access with multi-factor authentication (2FA) required for all
  sensitive data access.
- **Device Policy:** No personal devices permitted for accessing or storing client data.
- **Access Logging:** All access to client data is logged and monitored.
- **Breach Notification:** In the event of a data breach, affected parties and relevant authorities
  will be notified within 24 hours.
- **Retention:** Client data retained for two (2) years post case closure, after which it is
  securely deleted.

**Scheme-Specific Note:** All documents submitted to Ministry of Earth Sciences under Deep Ocean Mission
must be retained for a minimum of 3 years for audit purposes. (Note: As no specific retention period
was specified in the evidence for Deep Ocean Mission, the standard 3-year audit retention period
applies per general government record-keeping guidelines.)
