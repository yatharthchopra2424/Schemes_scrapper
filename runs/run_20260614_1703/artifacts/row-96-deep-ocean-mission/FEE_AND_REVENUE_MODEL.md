## Pricing Principles
1. **Value Alignment**: Fees are structured to reflect the substantial financial and strategic value delivered through successful scheme utilization, particularly for large-scale, long-term initiatives like the Deep Ocean Mission.
2. **Risk Sharing**: Success fees are contingent on approval, aligning consultant incentives with client outcomes; given the scheme’s low confidence rating and complex inter-ministerial coordination, this mitigates client risk.
3. **Transparency**: All fee components (retainer and success) are clearly defined in writing prior to engagement, with no hidden charges.
4. **Scheme-Specific Note**: The Deep Ocean Mission involves high technical complexity, long processing timelines (typically 6–12 months based on similar Earth Sciences initiatives), and a low confidence rating in automated extraction, indicating elevated procedural and approval risk. This justifies a conservative success fee range (2–4% for Enterprise tier) and longer revenue lag assumptions in projections.
