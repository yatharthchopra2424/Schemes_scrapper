# Pitch & Sales Scripts

## Philosophy
We are advisors, not salespeople. We diagnose first. We only pitch a scheme if it fits the client. We never oversell success rates.

---

## 1. Discovery Call Script (First Contact — 20 min)

### Opening (2 min)
Good morning/afternoon. I specialise in helping organisations in the marine research, ocean technology, and deep-sea exploration sectors access government funding for innovative projects. The Deep Ocean Mission, led by the Ministry of Earth Sciences, supports pioneering work in India’s deep ocean resources and technologies — I’d like to understand if your current initiatives might align with its objectives.

### Discovery Questions
- What is the primary focus of your organisation’s current ocean-related research or technology development?
- Are you working on projects related to deep-sea mining, ocean climate services, or marine biodiversity conservation?
- What stage is your project at — concept, prototype, pilot, or scaling?
- Do you collaborate with academic institutions, ISRO, DRDO, or other government labs on ocean science initiatives?
- Is your organisation registered as a society, trust, Section 8 company, or private limited entity in India?
- Have you previously applied for or received funding from MoES, DST, or similar scientific ministries?
- What are your estimated annual operating costs for ocean research or technology development?
- Are you seeking support for manned submersibles, ocean observation systems, or deep-sea resource exploration?

### Qualification Checklist (internal — don't read aloud)
- [ ] Organisation is engaged in deep ocean science, technology, or exploration activities  
- [ ] Project aligns with Deep Ocean Mission pillars: manned submersible, ocean climate services, deep-sea mining, marine biodiversity, ocean survey, energy and freshwater from ocean  
- [ ] Applicant is an Indian entity (academic institution, R&D lab, industry, or startup)  
- [ ] Project has clear deliverables, timeline, and budget  
- [ ] Organisation has necessary regulatory clearances (if applicable, e.g., for seabed activities)  
- [ ] Preference given to collaborative projects involving multiple stakeholders  

### Transition to Pitch
Based on your responses, your work in [specific area mentioned, e.g., deep-sea technology or ocean observation] appears well-aligned with the Deep Ocean Mission’s goals. This scheme, administered by the Ministry of Earth Sciences, provides grant-based support for innovative ocean science and technology projects — let me share how it could support your initiative.

## 2. Scheme Pitch (5 min)

### Problem framing:
Organisations pursuing deep ocean research and technology face high capital and operational costs, particularly for specialised equipment like manned submersibles, autonomous underwater vehicles, and deep-sea sampling systems. Limited access to long-term funding hinders India’s ability to explore and utilise its exclusive economic zone (EEZ) for scientific and economic advancement.

### Scheme intro:
The **Deep Ocean Mission**, administered by the **Ministry of Earth Sciences (MoES)**, is a central sector scheme offering **grant-in-aid** support for research, development, and demonstration projects in deep ocean science and technology. Funding is provided based on project scope and requirements, with no fixed upper limit — support is sanctioned after technical and financial appraisal by MoES expert committees.

### Why they qualify (personalised):
- Your project focuses on **[specific area from discovery, e.g., manned submersible development or ocean observation systems]**, which is a core pillar of the Deep Ocean Mission  
- You are an **[Indian entity type, e.g., registered startup/R&D lab/academic institution]** based in India, meeting the applicant eligibility  
- Your work involves **[collaboration with national labs or academic partners]**, which is encouraged under the mission’s collaborative framework  
- You seek funding for **[technology development, pilot testing, or field trials]**, aligning with the mission’s R&D and demonstration objectives
