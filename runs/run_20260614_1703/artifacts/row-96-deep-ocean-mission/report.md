# Comprehensive Scheme Masterclass & File Guide

## Scheme Deep Dive

### Overview
The **Deep Ocean Mission (DOM)** is a flagship initiative launched by the **Ministry of Earth Sciences (MoES)**, Government of India, aimed at exploring and harnessing the potential of the deep ocean for national development. It focuses on advancing India's capabilities in deep-sea technologies, ocean observation, marine biodiversity, and sustainable utilization of ocean resources.

### Objectives
The primary objectives of the Deep Ocean Mission include:
- Development of technologies for deep-sea mining, manned submersibles, and underwater robotics.
- Enhancement of ocean observation systems for climate forecasting and disaster warning.
- Exploration and conservation of marine biodiversity.
- Sustainable utilization of oceanic non-living resources such as polymetallic nodules.
- Capacity building and human resource development in ocean science and technology.
- Promotion of innovation and entrepreneurship in blue economy sectors.

### Eligibility Matrix
The scheme primarily supports research institutions, academic organizations, industry partners, and startups engaged in ocean-related R&D. Eligibility is determined based on the nature of the project and applicant type.

| **Eligibility Criteria**       | **Details**                                                                 | **Source**                     |
|-------------------------------|-----------------------------------------------------------------------------|--------------------------------|
| **Applicant Type**            | Central/State Government agencies, PSUs, Autonomous Bodies, Universities, IITs, IISERs, CSIR Labs, Recognized R&D Institutions, Startups, Industry Partners | [MoES DOM Portal](https://moes.gov.in/schemes/dom) |
| **Project Domain**            | Deep-sea technology development, ocean observation, marine biodiversity, polar science, hydrography, ocean services, blue economy initiatives | [MoES DOM Portal](https://moes.gov.in/schemes/dom) |
| **Geographical Focus**        | Indian Exclusive Economic Zone (EEZ), Southern Ocean, Arctic, Antarctic, and global oceans as per India's international obligations | [MoES DOM Portal](https://moes.gov.in/schemes/dom) |
| **Innovation Requirement**    | Projects must demonstrate novelty, technological advancement, or pilot-scale validation in ocean science and engineering | [MoES DOM Portal](https://moes.gov.in/schemes/dom) |
| **Funding Match**             | Varies by project; some components allow 100% government funding, others require industry or institutional co-funding | [MoES DOM Portal](https://moes.gov.in/schemes/dom) |
| **Compliance**                | Must adhere to MoES financial guidelines, GFR 2017, and relevant environmental and maritime regulations | [MoES DOM Portal](https://moes.gov.in/schemes/dom) |

> **Note**: While the scheme is open to a wide range of applicants, priority is given to projects aligned with the Mission’s six pillars: (i) Development of Technologies for Deep Sea Mining and Manned Submersible, (ii) Development of Ocean Climate Change Advisory Services, (iii) Technological Innovations for Exploration and Conservation of Biodiversity, (iv) Deep Ocean Survey and Exploration, (v) Energy and Freshwater from the Ocean, and (vi) Advanced Marine Station for Ocean Biology.

### Benefits & Financial Support
The Deep Ocean Mission provides financial support through grants, infrastructure access, and collaborative R&D opportunities. Funding is project-specific and approved based on technical and financial appraisal.

| **Support Type**              | **Details**                                                                 | **Source**                     |
|-------------------------------|-----------------------------------------------------------------------------|--------------------------------|
| **Grant-in-Aid**              | Up to 100% funding for government academic and research institutions; partial funding for industry and startups based on project criticality | [MoES DOM Portal](https://moes.gov.in/schemes/dom) |
| **Infrastructure Access**     | Access to National Institute of Ocean Technology (NIOT), National Centre for Polar and Ocean Research (NCPOR), Indian National Centre for Ocean Information Services (INCOIS), and other MoES-affiliated facilities | [MoES DOM Portal](https://moes.gov.in/schemes/dom) |
| **Technology Development Funds** | Support for prototyping, sea trials, and validation of deep-sea systems (e.g., Varuna submersible, underwater gliders, mining machines) | [MoES DOM Portal](https://moes.gov.in/schemes/dom) |
| **Human Resource Development** | Funding for PhD fellowships, post-doctoral research, training programs, and skill development in ocean technology | [MoES DOM Portal](https://moes.gov.in/schemes/dom) |
| **International Collaboration** | Support for joint research programs, data sharing, and participation in global ocean observation systems (e.g., GOOS, Argo) | [MoES DOM Portal](https://moes.gov.in/schemes/dom) |
| **Incubation & Entrepreneurship** | Support for startups via seed funding, mentorship, and access to testing facilities under MoES innovation programs | [MoES DOM Portal](https://moes.gov.in/schemes/dom) |

> **Blockquote Warning**: Funding approvals are subject to annual budget allocations, project milestones, and utilization certificate (UC) submissions. Delay in UC submission may lead to fund freezing or project termination.

### Application Process
The application process for the Deep Ocean Mission involves idea submission, technical evaluation, financial appraisal, and final approval by the Project Approval and Monitoring Committee (PAMC) under MoES.

```mermaid
flowchart TD
    A[Idea Conception & Problem Identification] --> B[Pre-Proposal Submission via MoES Portal]
    B --> C{Initial Screening by MoES}
    C -->|Not Aligned| D[Rejection with Feedback]
    C -->|Aligned| E[Invitation to Submit Full Proposal]
    E --> F[Preparation of Detailed Project Report (DPR)]
    F --> G[Technical Evaluation by Expert Committee]
    G -->|Not Recommended| H[Revision Requested]
    G -->|Recommended| I[Financial Appraisal by IFD, MoES]
    I --> J[Placement before PAMC]
    J --> K{PAMC Approval}
    K -->|Approved| L[Sanction Order Issued]
    K -->|Not Approved| M[Project Closed / Resubmit Advised]
    L --> N[Fund Disbursement in Installments]
    N --> O[Project Execution & Monitoring]
    O --> P[Mid-Term Review]
    P --> Q{Progress Satisfactory?}
    Q -->|No| R[Corrective Action Plan]
    Q -->|Yes| S[Continue to Final Review]
    S --> T[Final Evaluation & Impact Assessment]
    T --> U[Submission of Utilization Certificate & Completion Report]
    U --> V[Project Closeout]
```

**Application Portal**: [https://moes.gov.in/schemes/dom](https://moes.gov.in/schemes/dom)  
**Key Guidelines**: Proposals must be submitted in the prescribed format available on the portal. All submissions require endorsement by the Head of Institution and compliance with GFR 2017 and MoES guidelines.
