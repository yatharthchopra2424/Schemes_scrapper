# Application Playbook

## How to Use This File
For each new client case, assign it a Case ID, pick the target scheme, and follow the steps below.

---

## Stage 1: Eligibility Verification (Day 1–3)

### Checklist
- [ ] The project targets a habitation of Particularly Vulnerable Tribal Groups (PVTGs)  
- [ ] The habitation has a PVTG population between 50–100  
- [ ] The habitation lacks both VDVK (Van Dhan Vikas Kendra) and Skill Centres  
- [ ] Priority is given to habitations without an Anganwadi Centre (AWC) and Health Sub-Centre (HSC)  
- [ ] If AWC and HSC are present, priority is given to habitations lacking only HSC  
- [ ] If HSC is present, priority is given to habitations lacking only AWC  
- [ ] The habitation is located in one of the 18 specified States or the Andaman & Nicobar Islands (UT)  
- [ ] The State Government has updated habitation-wise survey data on the PM Gati Shakti Portal  
- [ ] Stakeholder consultations have been conducted at Gram Sabha/Panchayat level for land, location, design, and construction oversight  
- [ ] Approximately half acre (2200 sq ft built-up area) of Government/Public land is available for MPC construction  
- [ ] Land ownership has been or will be transferred to the State Tribal Welfare Department (TWD)  
- [ ] The project will be executed by a Government agency in a transparent manner  
- [ ] All necessary clearances under Central or State Acts/Rules/Regulations will be obtained by the State  
- [ ] Technical scrutiny of cost estimates will be done by a competent authority as per DFPR applicable in the State  
- [ ] The construction cost (including furniture, fixtures, fittings) will not exceed Rs. 60 lakh per MPC  
- [ ] The State has prepared a Detailed Project Report (DPR) with cost estimates approved by competent authority  
- [ ] The proposal has been approved by a committee chaired by the District Magistrate/District Collector  
- [ ] The State/District has entered into an MOU with the identified construction agency  
- [ ] The District Magistrate/District Collector/Agency is registered on the PFMS portal  
- [ ] Approval of the State Government (PSTWD) has been obtained  

**Sources**:

## Stage 2: Document Collection (Day 3–10)

### Universal Documents (always required)
- Incorporation Certificate / Registration Certificate  
- PAN Card of the entity  
- ITR (Income Tax Returns) of the last 3 years  
- GST Registration Certificate  
- Bank Statements (last 6 months)  
- Udyam Registration (for MSMEs)  
- Aadhaar-based identity proof of authorized signatory  
- Cancelled cheque / Bank account details  

### Scheme-Specific Documents
- Location of the MPC with approval of Gram Sabha if required  
- Details of identified land furnished (including Khata/Khasra/Survey number)  
- Location of the land (GPS coordinates, village, block, district)  
- Area of the land (in acres/sq ft, must be ~half acre)  
- Approval of State Government (PSTWD – Principal Secretary/Tribal Welfare Department)  
- Name of the Agency identified by State/District for construction  
- Copy of MOU between State/District and the identified construction agency  
- Registration certificate of District Magistrate/District Collector/Agency on PFMS portal  
- Approval of Proposal by committee chaired by District Magistrate/District Collector  
- Land ownership transfer documents to State TWD (if applicable)  
- Gram Sabha resolution approving land, location, design, and construction (if required)  
- Stakeholder consultation minutes at Gram Sabha/Panchayat level  
- Updated habitation-wise survey data on PM Gati Shakti Portal (screenshot or certificate)  
- Technical scrutiny report of cost estimates by competent authority (as per State DFPR)  
- Clearance certificates from relevant Central/State authorities (environment, forest, etc., if applicable)  
- DLCC (District Level Coordination Committee) recommendation letter  
- Principal Secretary approval letter on the proposal

### Document Quality Check
- [ ] All documents are legible and in PDF format (scanned copies acceptable if clear)  
- [ ] File size of each document does not exceed 5 MB  
- [ ] Name on documents matches exactly with the applicant entity/beneficiary name  
- [ ] Land documents show clear Khata/Khasra/Survey numbers and GPS coordinates  
- [ ] Approvals are signed, stamped, and dated by the competent authority (e.g., Principal Secretary, DM/DC)  
- [ ] MOU is executed on stamp paper and registered if required by State law  
- [ ] PFMS registration screenshot shows active status of the agency  
- [ ] Gram Sabha resolution is on official letterhead and signed by the Pradhan/Panchayat Secretary  
- [ ] All documents are in English or Hindi; if in regional language, certified translation is attached  
- [ ] Utilization Certificate (UC) and Statement of Expenditure (SoE) templates are ready for post-approval use  
- [ ] DPR includes cost breakdown, site plan, execution timeline, and agency details

## Stage 3: Application Drafting (Day 10–18)

### Project Report / DPR (if required)
Yes, a Detailed Project Report (DPR) is required for this scheme.  
**Standard Sections to Include**:  
- Executive Summary  
- Project Background and Justification (linking to PVTG habitation gaps)  
- Objectives (aligned with PM-JANMAN goals: safe housing, clean water, education, health, livelihood, connectivity)  
- Location Details (village, block, district, GPS coordinates, land area ~half acre)  
- Land Ownership Proof (transfer to State TWD, Government/Public land)  
- Technical Specifications (built-up area: 2200 sq ft, room layout, amenities)  
- Bill of Quantities (BoQ) and Cost Estimates (item-wise, not exceeding Rs. 60 lakh)  
- Implementation Timeline (Gantt chart)  
- Identified Executing Agency (Government agency, with MOU copy)  
- Approval Letters (Gram Sabha, DLCC, Principal Secretary, State TWD)  
- Risk Assessment and Mitigation Plan  
- Sustainability and Maintenance Plan (post-construction, coordinated with line departments)  
- Monitoring Mechanism (link to PM Gati Shakti Portal updates)  
- Annexures: Land documents, approvals, MOUs, technical scrutiny report, clearances  

**Note**: The DPR must be prepared in consultation with the implementing agency and approved by the competent authority of the State Government.

### Application Form Filling Protocol
1. **Access the Portal**: Visit the official PM-JANMAN application portal at https://tribal.nic.in/PM-JANMAN.aspx.  
2. **Login/Register**: Use valid credentials to log in or register as a new user (State Nodal Officer/TWD representative).  
3. **Select Scheme**: Choose "PM-JANMAN" from the list of available schemes.  
4. **Fill Applicant Details**: Enter State, District, Block, and Village details exactly as per land documents.  
5. **Enter Habitation Data**: Input PVTG population (50–100), confirm absence of VDVK and Skill Centres, and mark priority based on AWC/HSC availability.  
6. **Land Information**: Fill in land location (Khata/Khasra/Survey number), area (~half acre), and confirm Government/Public land status.  
7. **Ownership Details**: State that land ownership is transferred to or in the name of State Tribal Welfare Department (TWD).  
8. **Agency Details**: Enter name and PFMS registration details of the identified construction agency.  
9. **MOU Information**: Upload copy of MOU between State/District and the agency.  
10. **Approval References**: Enter details and upload proof of:  
    - Gram Sabha approval (if required)  
    - DLCC recommendation  
    - Principal Secretary approval  
    - Committee approval chaired by DM/DC  
11. **Technical Scrutiny**: Upload the technical scrutiny report of cost estimates by competent authority (as per State DFPR).  
12. **Cost Breakdown**: Itemize construction cost (including furniture, fixtures, fittings) ensuring it does not exceed Rs. 60 lakh.  
13. **Clearances**: Upload all necessary Central/State clearances (environment, forest, etc., if applicable).  
14. **Declaration**: Sign and upload the declaration form confirming compliance with all PM-JANMAN guidelines.  
15. **Review**: Double-check all entries for accuracy before final submission.  
16. **Submit**: Click "Submit" and save the application reference number.  

**Portal-Specific Notes**:  
- The portal is hosted on the Ministry of Tribal Affairs website (tribal.nic.in).  
- A dedicated module for PM-JANMAN MPCs exists on the PM Gati Shakti Portal for progress tracking.  
- Ensure all uploaded documents are in PDF format and under 5 MB each.  
- The system may auto-save drafts; use "Save Draft" frequently to avoid data loss.

## Stage 5: Post-Approval (Day 30–90+)

### Approval Actions
- [ ] Receive and verify the approval letter from MoTA (PM-JANMAN Division) specifying sanctioned amount (up to Rs. 60 lakh)  
- [ ] Confirm that the sanctioned amount does not exceed Rs. 60 lakh and is earmarked for construction only  
- [ ] Ensure the State Nodal Officer/TWD has mapped the SNA account with PFMS for fund flow  
- [ ] Track the release of the first instalment (50% of sanctioned amount) upon approval  
- [ ] Utilize the first instalment to commence construction as per the approved DPR and timelines  
- [ ] Monitor physical and financial progress weekly and update the PM Gati Shakti Portal via the dedicated module  
- [ ] Prepare Statement of Expenditure (SoE) and Utilisation Certificate (UC) after utilizing 75% of the first instalment (or as per State TWD guidelines)  
- [ ] Submit SoE and UC to the State TWD for onward transmission to MoTA to trigger the second instalment (balance 50%)  
- [ ] Ensure the second instalment is released only after receipt and verification of SoE and UC by MoTA  
- [ ] Complete construction as per approved plans, ensuring cost does not exceed Rs. 60 lakh (including furniture, fixtures, fittings)  
- [ ] Obtain completion certificate from the executing agency and State TWD  
- [ ] Shift Anganwadi/Mini Anganwadi staff and equipment to the MPC if existing structures are kachha (temporary)  
- [ ] Coordinate with line departments (WCD, Health, Skill Development) for provision of Anganwadi, ANM, ASHA, and skill centre services  
- [ ] Finalize maintenance mechanism with State TWD and Gram Panchayat for post-construction upkeep  
- [ ] Update final physical and financial progress on PM Gati Shakti Portal  
- [ ] Submit detailed project completion report to MoTA (PM-JANMAN Division) as per Application Process Step 10  
- [ ] Retain all approvals, documents, and correspondence for audit and future reference  
- [ ] Calculate and invoice success fee as per consultancy agreement
