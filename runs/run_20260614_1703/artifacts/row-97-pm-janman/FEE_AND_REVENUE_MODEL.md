## Pricing Principles
1. Retainer covers initial eligibility assessment, documentation preparation, and proposal submission support.
2. Success fee is contingent on fund release and aligns with client risk-sharing.
3. Fees are scaled to scheme value and client capacity to pay.
4. **Scheme-specific note**: PM-JANMAN involves a 45-day processing time per the Citizens' Charter and requires multi-stage approvals (State → PAC → Ministry), introducing moderate administrative risk; however, the high success rate evidenced by approved MPCs (873 units sanctioned as of Nov 2024) and structured two-installment release mechanism supports predictable revenue realization.
