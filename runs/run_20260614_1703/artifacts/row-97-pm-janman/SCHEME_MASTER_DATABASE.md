### PM-JANMAN
- **Scheme ID:** SCH-001
- **Category:** Grant
- **Geography:** 18 States and 1 Union Territory (Andaman & Nicobar Islands)
- **Administering Body:** Ministry of Tribal Affairs
- **Portal / Application URL:** https://tribal.nic.in/PM-JANMAN.aspx
- **Current Status:** Rolling
- **Window:** Mission period: 2023-24 to 2025-26

#### Eligibility Criteria
- Business type: Not applicable (scheme targets habitations, not businesses)
- Age of business: Not applicable
- Sector: Tribal welfare / Multi-Purpose Centre construction for PVTG habitations
- Revenue cap: Not applicable
- Employee count: Not applicable
- Other: Eligible habitations are those with 50-100 PVTG population lacking VDVK centres and Skill Centres, with priority given to habitations without Anganwadi Centre and Health Sub-Centres, then those without Health Sub-Centre, and finally those without Anganwadi Centres. States must update habitation-wise survey data on the PM Gati Shakti Portal and conduct stakeholder consultations at Gram Sabha/Panchayat level for land, location, design, and construction oversight.

#### What It Offers
- Amount / Benefit: Rs. 60 lakh per Multi-Purpose Centre (MPC) for construction only
- Disbursement mode: Direct credit (funds transferred to State nodal department/Tribal Welfare Department, which authorizes funds to District Collector/District Magistrate or implementing agency)
- Duration of benefit: One-time grant per MPC (released in two instalments: 50% first, balance after Statement of Expenditure and Utilisation Certificate)

#### Documents Required
1. Location of the MPC with approval of Gram Sabha if required
2. Details of identified land furnished
3. Location of the land
4. Area of the land
5. Approval of State Government (PSTWD)
6. Name of the Agency identified by State/District for construction
7. State/District has entered in MOU with the identified Agency
8. Registration of District Magistrate/District Collectors/Agency on PFMS portal
9. Approval of Proposal by committee chaired by District Magistrate/District Collectors

#### Application Process (numbered steps)
1. State Government identifies eligible PVTG habitations/villages based on criteria (50-100 PVTG population, lacking VDVK and Skill Centres) and updates habitation-wise survey data on PM Gati Shakti Portal.
2. State conducts stakeholder consultation at Gram Sabha/Panchayat level for land, location, design, and construction oversight.
3. State ensures land availability (approximately half acre) and transfers ownership to State Tribal Welfare Department (TWD).
4. State prepares Detailed Project Report (DPR) with cost estimates approved by competent authority and agency executing the project.
5. State submits proposal through Principal Secretary/Secretary, Tribal Welfare Department for approval by Project Approval Committee (PAC) at Ministry of Tribal Affairs.
6. Proposal examined by PM-JANMAN-associated division before sending for approval by Committee chaired by Mission Director/JS of PM-JANMAN.
7. Approval communicated after financial concurrence of Internal Finance Division (IFD).
8. Funds released in two instalments: 50% first instalment, balance after SoE and UC submission.
9. State updates physical and financial progress on PM Gati Shakti Portal via dedicated module.
10. State TWD submits detailed report to MoTA after complying with stipulations (land ownership, construction on Govt/Public land, DLCC recommendation, Principal Secretary approval, transparent execution by Govt. agency, cost not exceeding Rs. 60 lakh, technical scrutiny by competent authority, clearances obtained).
