### Gyan Bharatam Mission
- **Scheme ID:** SCH-001
- **Category:** Recognition
- **Geography:** Pan-India
- **Administering Body:** Ministry of Culture, Government of India
- **Portal / Application URL:** https://gyanbharatam.com/
- **Current Status:** Rolling
- **Window:** Always Open

#### Eligibility Criteria
- Business type: Individuals and institutions holding manuscripts
- Age of business: Not specified
- Sector: Manuscript preservation, digitization, research, education
- Revenue cap: Not specified
- Employee count: Not specified
- Other: Open to citizen contributors, scholars, libraries, monasteries, temples, and academic institutions across India who wish to participate in the preservation, digitization, and sharing of manuscript heritage

#### What It Offers
- Amount / Benefit: Access to digitized manuscripts for research, education, and public engagement; opportunity to register as a Gyan Bharatam Scholar; participation in workshops, conferences, and webinars; collaboration with researchers across India; access to manuscript repositories; contribution to national digital heritage infrastructure; recognition as part of a living network of seekers, scholars, teachers, and knowledge-keepers
- Disbursement mode: Non-monetary benefits (access, recognition, collaboration)
- Duration of benefit: Ongoing (as long as participation continues)

#### Documents Required
1. Personal and institutional details (for scholar registration form)

#### Application Process (numbered steps)
1. Visit the Gyan Bharatam website at https://gyanbharatam.com/
2. Navigate to the 'Register as Scholar' section via the homepage or community page
3. Fill out the scholar registration form with personal and institutional details
4. Submit the form to join the Gyan Bharatam Parivar network
5. Upon approval, gain access to manuscript repositories and collaborative opportunities
