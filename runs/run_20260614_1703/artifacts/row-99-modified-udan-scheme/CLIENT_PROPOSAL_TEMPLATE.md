## 1. Executive Summary
Based on our initial review of [Client]'s business profile, we have identified Modified UDAN Scheme — a subsidy scheme administered by Ministry of Civil Aviation (MoCA), implemented by Airports Authority of India (AAI) — for which your business appears eligible. The potential benefit is approximately Viability Gap Funding (VGF) for up to 50% of aircraft capacity, concessional excise duty of 2% on ATF for three years, and reimbursement of GST on RCS seats (for UDAN 1-3).
