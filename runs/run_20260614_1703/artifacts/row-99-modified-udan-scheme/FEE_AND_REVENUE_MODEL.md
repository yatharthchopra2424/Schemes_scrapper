## Pricing Principles
1. **Value-Based Pricing**: Fees are aligned with the substantial financial benefits delivered under the Modified UDAN Scheme, including Viability Gap Funding (VGF) covering up to 50% of aircraft capacity, concessional ATF excise duty (2% for three years), GST reimbursement, and waivers of landing, parking, and terminal navigation charges.  
2. **Risk-Adjusted Fees**: Success fees reflect the scheme’s moderate risk profile — while VGF is awarded via competitive bidding and indexed to ATF prices/inflation, disbursement is contingent on maintaining minimum Passenger Load Factor (PLF) and operational compliance over up to 10 years, with periodic reviews and potential route withdrawal for non-performance.  
3. **Transparency & Predictability**: Retainer fees cover structured engagement phases (route proposal, bidding, agreement signing), while success fees are triggered only upon VGF disbursement and concession claims, ensuring client pays for measurable outcomes.  
4. **Scalability & Accessibility**: Tiered pricing enables participation from regional airlines (Scale tier) to large carriers and groups (Enterprise tier), matching the scheme’s eligibility for scheduled airline operators meeting technical/financial criteria set by MoCA and AAI, including both domestic and international carriers via code-sharing.
