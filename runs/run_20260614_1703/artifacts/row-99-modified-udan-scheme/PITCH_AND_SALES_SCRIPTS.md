# Pitch & Sales Scripts

## Philosophy
We are advisors, not salespeople. We diagnose first. We only pitch a scheme if it fits the client. We never oversell success rates.

## 1. Discovery Call Script (First Contact — 20 min)

### Opening (2 min)
Good morning/afternoon! I specialize in helping airline operators access government support for regional connectivity initiatives. The Modified UDAN Scheme is designed specifically to make air travel affordable and viable on underserved routes by providing financial and operational support. I’d like to understand your current operations and see if this scheme aligns with your expansion plans.

### Discovery Questions
1. What is your current incorporation date and operational history as a scheduled airline operator?
2. Which specific underserved or unserved routes are you considering for new operations?
3. Do you hold a valid Air Operator’s Permit (AOP) and aircraft airworthiness certificates?
4. What is your fleet composition and seating capacity, particularly for aircraft suitable for regional routes?
5. Have you prepared a route proposal document including operational plans and schedules?
6. Are you able to provide financial statements and bank guarantees as part of a bidding process?
7. Have you engaged with state governments for concessions such as land provision or multi-modal connectivity?
8. Are you familiar with the competitive bidding process for VGF (Viability Gap Funding) on specific routes?

### Qualification Checklist (internal — don't read aloud)
- [ ] Operator is a scheduled airline operator
- [ ] Holds valid Air Operator’s Permit (AOP)
- [ ] Possesses aircraft registration and airworthiness certificates
- [ ] Can submit financial statements and bank guarantees
- [ ] Has or can develop a route proposal document
- [ ] Meets technical and financial criteria set by MoCA and AAI
- [ ] Able to participate in transparent, competitive bidding process
- [ ] Willing to comply with scheme guidelines and periodic reporting

### Transition to Pitch
Based on your responses, it appears you meet several key criteria for the Modified UDAN Scheme, particularly around route eligibility and operational readiness. The scheme offers Viability Gap Funding (VGF) to bridge the gap between operational costs and expected revenues, which could significantly support your proposed route operations.

## 2. Scheme Pitch (5 min)

### Structure: Problem → Scheme → Value → Proof → Next Step

**Problem framing:**
Airline operators face high operational costs on regional routes, making it difficult to launch or sustain flights to underserved airports without incurring losses. This limits connectivity growth and access to remote areas.

**Scheme intro:**
The Modified UDAN Scheme, administered by the Ministry of Civil Aviation (MoCA) and implemented by the Airports Authority of India (AAI), provides Viability Gap Funding (VGF) through a competitive bidding process. VGF bridges the gap between operational costs and expected revenues, with the central government covering 80% (90% for North Eastern States and UTs) via the Regional Connectivity Fund (RCF). Benefits include VGF for up to 50% of aircraft capacity, 2% concessional excise duty on ATF for three years, GST reimbursement on RCS seats (UDAN 1-3), and waivers on landing, parking, and TNLC charges.

**Why they qualify (personalised):**
- You are a scheduled airline operator with a valid AOP
- You possess aircraft suitability and operational capability for regional routes
- You can submit required documents including financial statements, route proposal, and bid forms
- You are eligible to participate in the competitive bidding process for route allocation
