# Pitch & Sales Scripts

## Philosophy
We are advisors, not salespeople. We diagnose first. We only pitch a scheme if it fits the client. We never oversell success rates.

---

## 1. Discovery Call Script (First Contact — 20 min)

### Opening (2 min)
Good morning/afternoon! I specialize in helping startups and innovative businesses access government support through the Startup India Initiative by DPIIT. This initiative is designed specifically for early-stage, scalable ventures working on innovation—whether in tech, manufacturing, agriculture, or social impact—to reduce compliance burdens, unlock tax benefits, and access funding pathways. I’d love to learn about your venture and see if this aligns with your goals.

### Discovery Questions
1. When was your company incorporated or registered?  
2. What is your current annual turnover, and has it exceeded ₹200 crore in any financial year since incorporation?  
3. Are you registered as a Private Limited Company, LLP, Partnership Firm, or Cooperative Society?  
4. Is your entity formed by splitting up or reconstructing an existing business?  
5. Are you working towards innovation, development, or improvement of products, processes, or services?  
6. Does your business model have high potential for employment generation or wealth creation?  
7. Are you in the DeepTech sector (which allows up to 20 years and ₹300 crore turnover)?  
8. Have you already applied for DPIIT recognition or any Startup India benefits?

### Qualification Checklist (internal — don't read aloud)
- [ ] Incorporated/registered in India as a Private Limited Company, LLP, Partnership Firm, or Cooperative Society  
- [ ] Within 10 years of incorporation (20 years for DeepTech)  
- [ ] Turnover not exceeding ₹200 crore (₹300 crore for DeepTech) in any financial year since incorporation  
- [ ] Not formed by splitting up or reconstructing an existing business  
- [ ] Working towards innovation, development, or improvement of products/processes/services OR has a scalable business model with high potential for employment/wealth creation  

### Transition to Pitch
Based on your responses, it sounds like you may be eligible for the Startup India Initiative by DPIIT—specifically, DPIIT recognition, which unlocks benefits like 3 years of income tax exemption, fast-tracked patents, and access to funding schemes. Would you like me to walk you through how this works and what it could mean for your venture?

## 2. Scheme Pitch (5 min)

### Structure: Problem → Scheme → Value → Proof → Next Step

**Problem framing:**
Startups face high compliance costs, difficulty accessing funding, delays in IP protection, and barriers in public procurement—especially when competing with established firms. These challenges divert focus from innovation and growth.

**Scheme intro:**
The Startup India Initiative, administered by the Department for Promotion of Industry and Internal Trade (DPIIT) under the Ministry of Commerce and Industry, offers DPIIT recognition to eligible startups. This recognition unlocks a suite of benefits including income tax exemption for 3 consecutive years, self-certification compliance under 9 labour and environment laws, 80% rebate on patent filing fees, exemption from prior experience/turnover in government tenders, and access to funding mechanisms like the Fund of Funds for Startups (FFS) and Credit Guarantee Scheme for Startups (CGSS), which provides credit guarantees up to ₹20 crore per borrower.

**Why they qualify (personalised):**
- Incorporated as a [Private Limited Company/LLP/etc.] within the last 10 years  
- Turnover has not exceeded ₹200 crore in any financial year since incorporation  
- Focused on innovation/scalable model with employment or wealth creation potential  
- Not formed by splitting or reconstructing an existing business

**Next step:**
We offer a free, no-obligation eligibility check. If you qualify, we support you through the entire application process—document preparation, portal submission, and follow-up—on a success-fee-only model. You pay only if your application is approved.
