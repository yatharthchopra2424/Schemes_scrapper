# Pitch & Sales Scripts

## Philosophy
We are advisors, not salespeople. We diagnose first. We only pitch a scheme if it fits the client. We never oversell success rates.

---

## 1. Discovery Call Script (First Contact — 20 min)

### Opening (2 min)
Good [morning/afternoon], I’m [Your Name] from [Your Firm]. We specialize in helping startups, incubators, accelerators, mentors, and investors access government-backed support through the MeitY Startup Hub (MSH) ecosystem. I’d love to understand your current stage and goals to see if any of MSH’s programs—like SAMRIDH, TIDE 2.0, GENESIS, or corporate partnerships—could be a fit for you.

### Discovery Questions
1. What is your role and organization type (startup, incubator, accelerator, mentor, investor)?
2. If you’re a startup, when was your entity incorporated or registered?
3. Are you based in a Tier II/III city, or do you operate pan-India?
4. What stage are you at—idea, prototype, revenue-generating, or scaling?
5. Which technology domains are you working in (e.g., AI, IoT, semiconductors, deep tech, agritech, healthtech)?
6. Have you previously applied for or received funding from any MeitY schemes like SAMRIDH, TIDE 2.0, or GENESIS?
7. What kind of support are you seeking—funding, mentorship, infrastructure access, or market linkages?
8. Are you looking to expand internationally or access global networks?

### Qualification Checklist (internal — don't read aloud)
- [ ] Startup, incubator, accelerator, mentor, or investor
- [ ] Part of or willing to join the MeitY Startup Hub ecosystem
- [ ] Startup incorporated within last 10 years (if applicable to specific program)
- [ ] Operating in electronics, IT, deep-tech, or related sectors
- [ ] Seeking funding, mentorship, infrastructure, or market access
- [ ] Willing to apply via https://msh.meity.gov.in/
- [ ] Not excluded by program-specific deadlines or sector restrictions

### Transition to Pitch
Based on what you’ve shared, the MeitY Startup Hub’s Chips to Startup (C2S) initiative—particularly through accelerators like the Micron-backed CREST program—could be a strong fit. It offers up to Rs. 10 Lacs in funding per startup, along with mentorship and lab access, specifically for early-stage semiconductor and deep-tech innovators.

## 2. Scheme Pitch (5 min)

### Structure: Problem → Scheme → Value → Proof → Next Step

**Problem framing:**
Early-stage startups in deep-tech sectors like semiconductors, AI, and IoT struggle to access seed funding, specialized mentorship, and infrastructure needed to validate prototypes and reach product-market fit—especially outside Tier I cities.

**Scheme intro:**
The Chips to Startup (C2S) initiative under MeitY Startup Hub (MSH), implemented by the Ministry of Electronics and Information Technology (MeitY), Government of India, provides financial and technical support through programs like SAMRIDH, TIDE 2.0, GENESIS, and corporate partnerships. For example, the Micron-backed CREST accelerator offers up to Rs. 10 Lacs per startup in seed funding, plus access to labs, mentorship, and investor networks.
