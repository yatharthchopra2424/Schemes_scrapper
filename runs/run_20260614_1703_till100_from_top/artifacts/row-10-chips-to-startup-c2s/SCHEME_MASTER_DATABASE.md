### Chips to Startup (C2S)
- **Scheme ID:** SCH-001
- **Category:** Incubation
- **Geography:** Pan-India
- **Administering Body:** Ministry of Electronics and Information Technology (MeitY), Government of India
- **Portal / Application URL:** https://msh.meity.gov.in/
- **Current Status:** Rolling
- **Window:** Always Open

#### Eligibility Criteria
- Business type: Startups, incubators, accelerators, mentors, investors participating in the MeitY Startup Hub ecosystem
- Age of business: Not specified
- Sector: Electronics, IT, deep-tech (AI, IoT, cybersecurity, blockchain, robotics, semiconductors, etc.)
- Revenue cap: Not specified
- Employee count: Not specified
- Other: Eligibility varies by specific program (e.g., SAMRIDH, TIDE 2.0, GENESIS, corporate partnerships)

#### What It Offers
- Amount / Benefit: Up to Rs. 10 Lacs per startup (e.g., CREST accelerator by Micron); MSH has disbursed ₹49.21 crore to 51 TIDE 2.0 Centers, 23 SAMRIDH Accelerators, and 63 GENESIS Implementing Agencies
- Disbursement mode: Through incubators (not directly to startups); includes equity-linked opportunities, tailored credit lines, and seed grants
- Duration of benefit: Varies by program (e.g., 6-month accelerator programs, one-time grants)

#### Documents Required
1. Certificate of Incorporation / Registration
2. PAN of the entity
3. Pitch deck or business description
4. Authorization letter from authorized signatory
5. Details of funding received (if any)
6. Bank account details
7. GST registration (if applicable)
8. UDYAM certificate (for MSMEs)

#### Application Process (numbered steps)
1. Identify the relevant program (e.g., SAMRIDH, TIDE 2.0, GENESIS, or corporate partnership)
2. Visit the MeitY Startup Hub website (https://msh.meity.gov.in/)
3. Navigate to the specific program page
4. Click on 'Apply Now' or similar call-to-action
5. Fill out the application form with required details
6. Upload necessary documents
7. Submit the application
8. Await evaluation by the selection committee
9. If selected, proceed with onboarding and benefit activation
