    Advancing India’s Startup Ecosystem: MeitY Startup Hub & Razorpay Partnership

    MeitY Startup Hub (MSH) is thrilled to announce a transformative strategic partnership with Razorpay , a collaboration poised to revolutionize the disruptors forming the Indian startup ecosystem. This strategic collaboration is meticulously designed to give early stage startups the boost they need to scale faster and smarter.

    WATCH LAUNCH VIDEO

    Proposed Benefits offered to all start-ups affiliated with MSH Programs:

    • Turbocharge your operations with access to exclusive credits on Razorpay products: Exclusive access to MSH startups for credits on Razorpay’s 360 degree digital payments suite including Payment Gateway, Business Banking, and Payroll
    • Exclusive knowledge sessions on topics: All the Startups associated with MSH, shall get exclusive access to knowledge sessions on various pertinent topics such as investor-ready pitch decks, referral design masterclass, content mastery etc.and meticulously curated best practices providing them with the strategic insights needed to navigate their entrepreneurial journey.
    • Access to Razorpay Rize’s startup enablement program: Including access to YC resources, application review & mock interview preparations & pitch deck reviews by Razorpay Rize’s experts.
    • Company incorporation: Access to exclusively discounted pricing and streamlined company registration services in India, with priority support to startups from MSH.
    • Razorpay Rize Community: Selected startups to receive exclusive access to Razorpay Rize community which includes access to partner credits and deals.
