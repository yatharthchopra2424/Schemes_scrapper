### GENESIS Program (MeitY)
- **Scheme ID:** SCH-001
- **Category:** Grant
- **Geography:** Tier-II & Tier-III cities in India
- **Administering Body:** Ministry of Electronics and Information Technology (MeitY), Government of India
- **Portal / Application URL:** https://msh.meity.gov.in/schemes/genesis
- **Current Status:** Open
- **Window:** Always Open

#### Eligibility Criteria
- Business type: Technology startups
- Age of business: Not specified
- Sector: Technology (including deep-tech, AI, IoT, cybersecurity, blockchain, etc.)
- Revenue cap: Not specified
- Employee count: Not specified
- Other: Must be located in Tier-II or Tier-III cities in India; support limited to approximately 1600 tech startups over 5 years; scheme duration is 5 years from approval

#### What It Offers
- Amount / Benefit: 
  - Entrepreneur in Residence (EiR) Support: Up to ₹10 Lakh for early-stage
  - Pilot Funding Support: Up to ₹40 Lakh with POs/Work Orders
  - Investment Support: Up to ₹50 Lakh for market-ready
  - Deep-Tech Funding Support: Up to ₹1 Crore for deep-tech startups
- Disbursement mode: Direct credit (financial support through four components)
- Duration of benefit: One-time (per component)

#### Documents Required
1. Application form (as per Implementing Agencies list and official links)
2. Administrative Approval of Genesis (Attached PDF)
3. Genesis Scheme Brochure (Attached PDF)
4. Proof of startup registration/incorporation
5. Proof of location in Tier-II/Tier-III city
6. Business plan or pitch deck
7. Details of idea validation (for EiR)
8. Product prototype details (for Pilot Funding)
9. Fundraising details or term sheets (for Investment Support)
10. Technical details of deep-tech solution (for Deep-Tech Funding)

#### Application Process (numbered steps)
1. Refer to the Implementing Agencies list on the MeitY Startup Hub website
2. Access official links such as the Administrative Approval and Scheme Brochure (attached PDFs)
3. Follow the application procedures detailed by the respective Implementing Agency
4. Submit application through the designated channel of the Implementing Agency
5. Await evaluation and selection by the Implementing Agency and MeitY Startup Hub
