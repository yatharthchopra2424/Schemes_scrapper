# Pitch & Sales Scripts

## Philosophy
We are advisors, not salespeople. We diagnose first. We only pitch a scheme if it fits the client. We never oversell success rates.

---

## 1. Discovery Call Script (First Contact — 20 min)

### Opening (2 min)
Good morning/afternoon. I specialize in helping startups and innovators access government funding for early-stage development. The NIDHI Seed Support Program is designed specifically to provide timely financial assistance to startups through incubators, helping them get off the ground and grow. I’d like to understand your current stage and see if this could be a fit for your venture.

### Discovery Questions
1. What is your current role—are you an innovator, startup founder, student, or researcher?
2. Have you incorporated your startup, and if so, when was it registered?
3. Are you currently affiliated with or applying to a Startup Incubator or Technology Business Incubator (TBI)?
4. What stage is your innovation at—idea, prototype, or early product development?
5. Do you have a technology or knowledge-based idea with potential for commercialization?
6. Are you seeking financial support to develop a prototype, conduct product trials, or enter the market?
7. Have you previously applied for any government startup support programs?
8. Are you an Indian citizen with valid proof of nationality (e.g., passport, voter ID)?

### Qualification Checklist (internal — don't read aloud)
- [ ] Applicant is an innovator, startup, student, researcher, or entrepreneur  
- [ ] Startup is incorporated in India (if applicable)  
- [ ] Applicant is applying through a listed Startup Incubator/TBI for NIDHI-SSP  
- [ ] Innovation is technology/knowledge-based with commercialization potential  
- [ ] Applicant is an Indian citizen with valid nationality proof  
- [ ] Startup has not received support from another funding agency for the same prototype  
- [ ] Startup’s annual turnover does not exceed Rs. 25 lakhs (if already operational)  
- [ ] Startup has at least 51% equity held by Indian citizens (if a registered entity)  

### Transition to Pitch
Based on your responses, it sounds like you’re working on an early-stage innovation with potential—exactly the kind of venture the **NIDHI Seed Support Program** was created for. This scheme offers up to **INR 100 lakh** in financial support to deserving startups through incubators to help them get off the ground and grow. Let me share how it works and whether you might qualify.

## 2. Scheme Pitch (5 min)

### Structure: Problem → Scheme → Value → Proof → Next Step

**Problem framing:**
Many promising startups struggle in the critical early phase due to lack of seed funding for prototype development, product trials, and market entry. Without this support, innovative ideas often fail to progress beyond the concept stage, especially when founders lack personal capital or access to early investment.

**Scheme intro:**
The **NIDHI Seed Support Program (NIDHI-SSP)**, administered by the **Department of Science and Technology (DST), Government of India**, is a **grant** scheme that provides financial support to startups through approved **Startup Incubators/Technology Business Incubators (TBIs)**. For startups/entrepreneurs, the **maximum support is up to INR 100 lakh** per entity, released to help them get off the ground and grow, increasing their chances of success in the marketplace.

**Why they qualify (personalised):**
- You are an innovator/startup seeking early-stage support (from discovery)  
- You plan to apply through a listed Startup Incubator/TBI (as per scheme requirement)  
- Your idea is technology/knowledge-based with commercialization potential  
- You are an Indian citizen with valid proof of nationality
