### NIDHI Seed Support
- **Scheme ID:** SCH-001
- **Category:** Grant
- **Geography:** Pan-India
- **Administering Body:** Department of Science and Technology (DST), Government of India
- **Portal / Application URL:** https://nidhi.dst.gov.in/nidhissp/
- **Current Status:** Rolling
- **Window:** Applications for financial support will be accepted once per year through an online portal. A call for applications will be announced at the appropriate time.

#### Eligibility Criteria
- Business type: Startups/Entrepreneurs, Incubators/TBIs
- Age of business: Not specified
- Sector: Not specified
- Revenue cap: Not specified
- Employee count: Not specified
- Other: For Startups/Entrepreneurs: Innovators and startups can apply to one of the listed Startup Incubators/Technology Business Incubators (TBIs) for the NIDHI Seed Support Program (NIDHI-SSP). The Startup Incubators/TBIs announce periodic calls for applications, and innovators and startups are encouraged to visit the website of Startup Incubators regularly to stay informed about these calls.

#### What It Offers
- Amount / Benefit: For Incubators/TBIs: The maximum financial support that will be made available to an incubator is INR 1000 lakh. For Startups/Entrepreneurs: The maximum support that will be given to a deserving startup is up to INR 100 lakh.
- Disbursement mode: Direct credit (released in phases according to the needs, capacity, and capabilities of the incubator, following a proper process)
- Duration of benefit: Phased release (for incubators); one-time support for startups (implied)

#### Documents Required
1. Application form (online portal)
2. Supporting documents as per the call for announcement (specifics not detailed in evidence)

#### Application Process (numbered steps)
1. Applications for financial support will be accepted once per year through an online portal.
2. A call for applications will be announced at the appropriate time.
3. Innovators and startups can apply to one of the listed Startup Incubators/Technology Business Incubators (TBIs) for the NIDHI Seed Support Program (NIDHI-SSP).
4. The Startup Incubators/TBIs announce periodic calls for applications.
5. Innovators and startups are encouraged to visit the website of Startup Incubators regularly to stay informed about these calls.
