# Pitch & Sales Scripts

## Philosophy
We are advisors, not salespeople. We diagnose first. We only pitch a scheme if it fits the client. We never oversell success rates.

---

## 1. Discovery Call Script (First Contact — 20 min)

### Opening (2 min)
Good morning/afternoon. I specialize in helping researchers and academic institutions access government funding for scientific research. The SERB Grants, administered by the Science and Engineering Research Board under the Department of Science and Technology, support high-impact research projects, fellowships, and travel grants across India. I’d like to understand your current research goals and see if this scheme aligns with your work.

### Discovery Questions
1. What is your current research focus, and are you affiliated with a recognized academic institution or R&D laboratory in India?
2. Do you hold a Ph.D. in science or engineering, or are you a regular faculty member/scientist at a SERB-recognized organization?
3. Are you planning a research project that requires funding for manpower, consumables, travel, contingency, or overheads?
4. What is the expected duration of your research project—SERB grants typically support projects for up to 3 years?
5. Have you previously applied for any SERB grants such as Core Research Grant (CRG), Early Career Research Award (ECRA), or Ramanujan Fellowship?
6. Do you have institutional endorsement and a detailed project proposal with objectives, methodology, and budget breakdown ready?
7. Are you seeking funding for travel, equipment, or publishing research outcomes as part of your project?
8. Are you aware of SERB’s requirements regarding mid-term and final evaluations, and utilization of funds as per approved budget?

### Qualification Checklist (internal — don't read aloud)
- [ ] Indian national with Ph.D. in science or engineering
- [ ] Regular faculty or scientist in recognized academic institution or R&D laboratory
- [ ] Affiliated with SERB-recognized organization
- [ ] Research project aligned with SERB’s objectives (basic research, innovation, collaboration)
- [ ] Project duration compatible with 3-year funding cycle
- [ ] Availability of required documents: CV, publications, project proposal, budget breakdown, institutional endorsement, Ph.D. proof, ethics clearance (if applicable)
- [ ] Willingness to comply with SERB’s fund utilization guidelines and IPR policies

### Transition to Pitch
Based on your responses, it appears your research profile and project needs align well with the SERB Grants. This scheme provides financial support for research projects, fellowships, and travel—typically covering project costs for up to 3 years. Let me walk you through how it works and how we can help you secure this funding.

## 2. Scheme Pitch (5 min)

### Structure: Problem → Scheme → Value → Proof → Next Step

**Problem framing:**
Researchers in India often struggle to secure consistent funding for manpower, consumables, travel, and overheads, especially for early-career scientists and those in emerging research areas. Without adequate grants, high-impact projects face delays or are unable to scale, limiting innovation and collaboration opportunities.

**Scheme intro:**
The SERB Grants, administered by the Science and Engineering Research Board (SERB) under the Department of Science and Technology (DST), Ministry of Science and Technology, provide financial assistance in the form of grants for manpower, consumables, travel, contingency, and overheads. The quantum and duration depend on the specific grant scheme, with funding typically covering project costs for 3 years. Fellowships include monthly stipends and research grants.
