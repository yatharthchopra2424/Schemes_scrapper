### SERB Grants
- **Scheme ID:** SCH-001
- **Category:** Grant
- **Geography:** Pan-India
- **Administering Body:** Science and Engineering Research Board (SERB), Department of Science and Technology (DST), Ministry of Science and Technology
- **Portal / Application URL:** https://serbonline.in/
- **Current Status:** Rolling
- **Window:** Varies by scheme; specific calls are announced periodically on the SERB portal.

#### Eligibility Criteria
- Business type: Indian nationals with a Ph.D. in science or engineering, regular faculty or scientists in recognized academic institutions or R&D laboratories, individuals affiliated with SERB-recognized organizations
- Age of business: Not specified
- Sector: Science and engineering research
- Revenue cap: Not specified
- Employee count: Not specified
- Other: Eligibility varies by grant type (e.g., Core Research Grant, Early Career Research Award, Ramanujan Fellowship); specific criteria depend on the grant category

#### What It Offers
- Amount / Benefit: Financial assistance for manpower, consumables, travel, contingency, and overheads; funding typically covers project costs for 3 years; fellowships include monthly stipends and research grants
- Disbursement mode: Direct credit
- Duration of benefit: One-time (project-based) or 3 years (for project grants); fellowships are tenable for the award duration

#### Documents Required
1. Curriculum Vitae (CV)
2. List of publications
3. Project proposal with objectives and methodology
4. Detailed budget breakdown
5. Institutional endorsement letter
6. Proof of Ph.D. degree (if applicable)
7. Endorsement from host institution
8. Ethics clearance (if applicable)

#### Application Process (numbered steps)
1. Identify the appropriate SERB grant scheme based on eligibility and research objectives.
2. Register on the SERB online portal (serbonline.in).
3. Fill out the application form with project details, budget, and timeline.
4. Upload required documents such as CV, publications, and institutional endorsement.
5. Submit the application before the deadline.
6. Applications undergo peer review and evaluation by expert committees.
7. Selected applicants are notified and funds are disbursed upon approval.
