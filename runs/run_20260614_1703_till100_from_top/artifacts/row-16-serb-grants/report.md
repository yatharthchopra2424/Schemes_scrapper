# Comprehensive Scheme Masterclass & File Guide

## Scheme Deep Dive

### Scheme Overview
**Scheme Name:** SERB Grants  
**Scheme ID:** row-16  
**Ministry / Category:** Science & Technology  
**Scheme Type:** grant  
**Geographic Scope:** Pan-India  
**Implementing Agency:** Science and Engineering Research Board (SERB), Department of Science and Technology (DST), Ministry of Science and Technology  
**Application Portal:** https://serbonline.in/  
**Status / Deadlines:** Varies by scheme; specific calls are announced periodically on the SERB portal.  
**Confidence:** medium  

### Objectives
- Promote basic research in science and engineering  
- Support individual researchers through fellowships and project grants  
- Strengthen research infrastructure in academic institutions  
- Encourage innovation and scientific excellence  
- Provide travel grants for national and international collaboration  
- Fund high-impact research projects in emerging areas of science and technology  

### Eligibility Matrix
| Eligibility Criteria | Details |
|----------------------|---------|
| **General Eligibility** | Indian nationals with a Ph.D. in science or engineering, regular faculty or scientists in recognized academic institutions or R&D laboratories, and individuals affiliated with SERB-recognized organizations |
| **Specific Criteria** | Varies by grant type (e.g., Core Research Grant (CRG), Early Career Research Award (ECRA), Ramanujan Fellowship) |
| **Target Beneficiaries** | Researchers, academic institutions, scientific associations, R&D organizations |

### Benefits & Financial Support
| Support Type | Details |
|--------------|---------|
| **Financial Assistance** | Grants for manpower, consumables, travel, contingency, and overheads |
| **Quantum & Duration** | Depends on specific grant scheme; funding typically covers project costs for 3 years |
| **Fellowships** | Include monthly stipends and research grants |
| **Additional Benefits** | Access to research networks, recognition through prestigious fellowships, opportunities for national and international collaboration, support for publishing research outcomes |

### Required Documents
1. Curriculum Vitae (CV)  
2. List of publications  
3. Project proposal with objectives and methodology  
4. Detailed budget breakdown  
5. Institutional endorsement letter  
6. Proof of Ph.D. degree (if applicable)  
7. Endorsement from host institution  
8. Ethics clearance (if applicable)  

### Application Process
```mermaid
flowchart TD
    A[Identify appropriate SERB grant scheme] --> B[Register on SERB online portal (serbonline.in)]
    B --> C[Fill out application form with project details, budget, and timeline]
    C --> D[Upload required documents: CV, publications, institutional endorsement, etc.]
    D --> E[Submit application before deadline]
    E --> F[Applications undergo peer review and evaluation by expert committees]
    F --> G[Selected applicants notified]
    G --> H[Funds disbursed upon approval]
```

### Key Caveats
> - Grants are subject to mid-term and final evaluation  
> - Funds must be utilized strictly as per approved budget  
> - Any deviation requires prior approval from SERB  
> - Intellectual property rights arising from SERB-funded projects follow government guidelines  
> - Fellowship awards are non-transferable and tenable only at the approved institution
