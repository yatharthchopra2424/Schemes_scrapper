## Pricing Principles
1. Value-based pricing aligned with the scheme’s benefit of facilitating access to high-end R&D infrastructure without direct financial grants.
2. Transparent fee structure reflecting the effort required for proposal preparation, facility access coordination, and utilization reporting.
3. Success fee tied to measurable outcomes such as approved facility access and successful project execution via SAIF centers.
4. Scheme-specific note: The SAIF Facilities scheme involves no direct financial disbursement but enables access to sophisticated instruments; success is measured by approved access and utilization, resulting in moderate risk due to dependency on facility availability and user charge compliance.
