# Comprehensive Scheme Masterclass & File Guide

## Scheme Deep Dive

### Scheme Overview
**Scheme Name:** SAIF Facilities  
**Scheme ID:** row-17  
**Ministry / Category:** Science & Technology  
**Scheme Type:** other  
**Geographic Scope:** Pan-India  
**Implementing Agency:** Department of Science & Technology (DST)  
**Status / Deadlines:** Rolling basis  
**Last Updated:** 2023  
**Application Portal URL:** https://dst.gov.in/  

### Objectives
- Provide access to sophisticated analytical instruments for research and development  
- Promote shared usage of high-end equipment to avoid duplication  
- Support academic and industrial R&D through facility access  
- Enhance scientific collaboration and resource optimization  

### Eligibility Matrix
| Eligible Entity Type | Specific Criteria | Notes |
|----------------------|-------------------|-------|
| Academic institutions | Engaged in scientific research and development activities | Must submit project proposal and institutional authorization |
| National laboratories | Engaged in scientific research and development activities | Access granted based on project proposals and institutional requirements |
| Industry entities | Engaged in scientific research and development activities | Must demonstrate R&D focus and provide justification for facility use |
| Research scholars | Through parent institutions | Access facilitated via institutional sponsorship |

### Benefits & Financial Support
| Benefit Category | Specific Details | Financial Implication |
|------------------|------------------|------------------------|
| Access to sophisticated analytical instruments | Use of high-end equipment across SAIF network | No direct financial support; nominal user fees may apply |
| Shared use of high-end equipment | Avoids duplication of capital expenditure | Reduced capital expenditure for individual institutions |
| Reduced capital expenditure | Eliminates need for individual institutions to purchase expensive equipment | Cost savings on equipment acquisition |
| Enhanced research capabilities | Enables advanced experiments and analysis | Improved research output quality |
| Promotion of collaborative R&D | Facilitates inter-institutional and industry-academia partnerships | Increased joint publications and projects |
| Nominal user fees | Charged for facility utilization (if applicable) | Users settle applicable charges post-utilization |

> **Key Financial Note:** The scheme does not provide direct financial support (grants/loans). Instead, it facilitates access to existing infrastructure. Users may be charged nominal user fees for facility utilization, but no funds are disbursed under this scheme.

### Required Documents
1. Project proposal  
2. Institutional authorization letter  
3. Details of intended use of facility  
4. User charges payment proof (if applicable)  

### Key Caveats
- Access is subject to availability of equipment and facility scheduling  
- User charges may apply for facility utilization  
- Prior approval is mandatory before using any SAIF facility  

### Target Beneficiaries
- Academic institutions  
- National laboratories  
- Industry  
- Research scholars
