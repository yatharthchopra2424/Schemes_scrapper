## Pricing Principles
1. **Value-Based Alignment**: Fees are calibrated to the actual grant benefit under DST Multiplier & Collaborative R&D Grants (up to ₹2 Cr for individual industry, ₹4 Cr for consortium), ensuring client ROI exceeds consulting cost.
2. **Risk-Adjusted Pricing**: Success fees reflect moderate risk due to 2–3 year project duration and joint industry-academia submission requirement; processing time averages 6–9 months based on DeitY evaluation cycles.
3. **Tiered Accessibility**: Growth tier (MSME/Startup) is primary applicability per scheme eligibility targeting startups, incubators, academia, and accelerators in electronics & IT.
4. **Transparency & Trust**: All fees disclosed upfront; retainers cover proposal development and joint submission support, success fees contingent on approval.
