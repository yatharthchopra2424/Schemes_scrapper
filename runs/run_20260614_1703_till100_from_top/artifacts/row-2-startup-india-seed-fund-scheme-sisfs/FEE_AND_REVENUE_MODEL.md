## Pricing Principles
1. **Value-Based Pricing**: Fees aligned with the financial benefit delivered (up to ₹20L grant + ₹50L investment per startup under SISFS).
2. **Risk-Sharing Model**: Success fees only payable upon fund disbursement, reducing client risk.
3. **Transparency**: Clear retainer for application support and success fee tied to actual fund receipt.
4. **Scheme-Specific Note**: SISFS has a moderate risk profile due to a 45-day selection timeline by incubators and 60-day first installment release for grants, requiring careful milestone tracking; however, high approval rates for eligible DPIIT-recognized startups with strong proof-of-concept reduce overall risk.
