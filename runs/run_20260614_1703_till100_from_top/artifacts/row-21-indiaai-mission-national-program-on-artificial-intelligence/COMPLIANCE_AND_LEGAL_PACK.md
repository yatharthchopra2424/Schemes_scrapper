## 8D. Internal Data Handling Policy

All client data shall be handled in accordance with the following standards:

- **Encrypted Cloud Storage:** All data stored in encrypted cloud environments with AES-256 encryption at rest and in transit.
- **Two-Factor Authentication (2FA):** Mandatory for all system access involving client data.
- **Limited Access:** Access to client data restricted to authorized personnel only on a need-to-know basis.
- **No Personal Devices:** Prohibition on storing or processing client data on personal devices.
- **Retention Period:** Client data retained for 2 years post case closure, unless otherwise required by law or scheme-specific guidelines.
- **Breach Notification:** In the event of a data breach, affected parties and relevant authorities will be notified within 24 hours.

**Scheme-specific note:** All documents submitted to Ministry of Electronics and Information Technology (MeitY) under IndiaAI Mission – National Program on Artificial Intelligence must be retained for a minimum of 3 years for audit purposes.
