## Pricing Principles
1. Value-based pricing aligned with the actual financial benefit delivered to the client.
2. Transparent fee structure with clear milestones for retainer and success fees.
3. Risk-adjusted pricing reflecting the scheme’s moderate processing time and dependency on investor linkages for financial support.
4. Scalable model accommodating early-stage startups seeking incubation and funding linkages.
