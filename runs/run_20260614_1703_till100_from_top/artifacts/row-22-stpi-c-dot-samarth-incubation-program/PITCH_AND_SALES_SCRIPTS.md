# Pitch & Sales Scripts

## Philosophy
We are advisors, not salespeople. We diagnose first. We only pitch a scheme if it fits the client. We never oversell success rates.

---

## 1. Discovery Call Script (First Contact — 20 min)

### Opening (2 min)
Good morning/afternoon. I’m calling from [Your Firm Name], and we specialize in helping technology startups and entrepreneurs in emerging sectors like AI, IoT, Blockchain, FinTech, MedTech, AgriTech, Cybersecurity, and AR/VR access government-backed incubation and acceleration support. I understand you’re building something innovative in one of these areas — I’d love to learn more about your venture and see if the STPI + C-DOT Samarth Incubation Program could be a strategic fit for your growth journey.

### Discovery Questions
1. What stage is your startup currently at — idea, prototype, MVP, or early revenue?
2. When was your company incorporated or registered? (To assess eligibility as a startup under 10 years old)
3. Which emerging technology domain are you working in — AI, IoT, Blockchain, FinTech, MedTech, AgriTech, Cybersecurity, AR/VR, or another?
4. Are you based in or planning to operate from a Tier-2 or Tier-3 city? (STPI prioritizes inclusive growth beyond metros)
5. Do you have a technology-driven product or solution with potential for scalability and market impact?
6. Have you already incorporated your entity and obtained a Certificate of Incorporation or registration?
7. Do you have a business plan, team resumes, and proof of concept or prototype ready?
8. Are you seeking mentorship, infrastructure support, investor connections, or go-to-market guidance to scale your solution?

### Qualification Checklist (internal — don't read aloud)
- [ ] Startup or entrepreneur working in emerging technologies (AI, IoT, Blockchain, FinTech, MedTech, AgriTech, Cybersecurity, AR/VR, etc.)
- [ ] Engaged in technology-driven product development
- [ ] Demonstrates potential for scalability and market impact
- [ ] Based in or targeting Tier-2/Tier-3 cities for inclusive growth
- [ ] Has Certificate of Incorporation or registration (if registered entity)
- [ ] Possesses PAN card of entity or promoters
- [ ] Has bank account details ready
- [ ] Can provide business plan/project proposal
- [ ] Can provide team details and resumes
- [ ] Has proof of concept or prototype (if available)
- [ ] Has disclosed any existing funding or investment details
- [ ] Has intellectual property details (if applicable)

### Transition to Pitch
Based on what you’ve shared, it sounds like you’re building a scalable tech solution in [mention their domain] with strong potential — exactly the kind of venture the **STPI + C-DOT Samarth Incubation Program** is designed to support. This program offers selected startups access to incubation facilities, mentorship, investor networking, infrastructure labs, IPR guidance, and go-to-market strategy support — all aimed at accelerating your path to market and funding readiness.

**Problem framing:**
Many promising startups in emerging technologies struggle to access the right mix of technical infrastructure, expert mentorship, investor connections, and market validation support — especially outside major metro hubs. This gap often slows product development, limits fundraising readiness, and hinders go-to-market execution, despite strong innovation potential.

**Scheme intro:**
The **STPI + C-DOT Samarth Incubation Program**, administered by the Software Technology Parks of India (STPI) under the Ministry of Electronics and Information Technology (MeitY), is a pan-India incubation initiative that supports startups and entrepreneurs in emerging technologies. While STPI facilitates linkages to seed funding through VCs, angel investors, and government schemes, the core benefit lies in providing access to incubation facilities, mentorship from industry experts, networking with investors and financial institutions, infrastructure support (hardware/software labs), IPR and product development guidance, and market access strategies — all designed to accelerate innovation and scalability.

- You are developing a technology-driven product in [their domain, e.g., AI/IoT/FinTech], which aligns with the program’s focus on emerging technologies  
- You are based in or targeting a Tier-2/Tier-3 city, supporting the program’s goal of inclusive regional growth  
- You have or are preparing a business plan, team details, and proof of concept — key documents required for application  
- Your startup demonstrates potential for scalability and market impact, a core eligibility criterion
