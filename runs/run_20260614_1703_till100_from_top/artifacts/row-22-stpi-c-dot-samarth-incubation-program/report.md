# Comprehensive Scheme Masterclass & File Guide

## Scheme Deep Dive

### Scheme Overview
The **STPI + C-DOT Samarth Incubation Program** is an incubation scheme implemented by the **Software Technology Parks of India (STPI)**, under the **Ministry of Electronics and Information Technology (MeitY)**, Government of India. It operates on a **Pan-India** geographic scope and follows a **rolling basis** application process with no fixed deadline—applications are accepted throughout the year based on cohort availability and program cycles. The scheme was last updated in **2025**.

### Objectives
The program aims to:
- Foster innovation and entrepreneurship in emerging technologies  
- Provide incubation support to startups through infrastructure and mentorship  
- Strengthen the technology ecosystem by developing a skilled talent pool  
- Promote balanced regional growth in the IT sector beyond metros  
- Enable startups to create world-class IT/EDSM products indigenously  
- Generate direct and indirect employment through startup support  
- Facilitate access to funding, investors, and financial institutions for startups  
- Encourage collaboration between industry, academia, and government for startup growth  

### Eligibility Matrix
| Criteria | Details |
|--------|---------|
| **Target Beneficiaries** | Startups; Entrepreneurs; MSME |
| **Technology Domains** | AI, IoT, Blockchain, FinTech, MedTech, AgriTech, Cybersecurity, AR/VR, and other emerging technologies |
| **Geographic Focus** | Open to innovators from Tier-2 and Tier-3 cities to promote inclusive growth |
| **Core Requirements** | Engaged in technology-driven product development; demonstrate potential for scalability and market impact |
| **Ineligibility** | Not explicitly stated; admission subject to seat availability and selection committee evaluation |

> **Note**: The program is open to startups and entrepreneurs working in emerging technologies. Applicants must be engaged in technology-driven product development and demonstrate potential for scalability and market impact.

### Benefits & Financial Support
| Benefit Category | Details |
|------------------|--------|
| **Financial Support** | Seed funding support facilitated through linkages with venture capitalists, angel investors, and government funding schemes. Specific quantum and disbursement mechanisms are determined based on startup evaluation and available funding partners. STPI helps startups access financial support but does not directly grant funds in all cases. |
| **Incubation Facilities** | Access to STPI's incubation facilities across its Pan-India network of Centres of Entrepreneurship (CoEs) |
| **Mentorship** | Guidance from industry experts |
| **Networking** | Opportunities with investors and financial institutions |
| **Infrastructure Support** | Hardware/software labs |
| **Guidance Services** | IPR and product development guidance |
| **Market Access** | Assistance in market access and go-to-market strategies |
| **Additional Exposure** | Exposure to government schemes and policies supporting innovation and entrepreneurship |

> **Important**: Financial support is **not guaranteed** and depends on investor interest and funding availability. Continued support is contingent on meeting milestones and performance benchmarks.

### Required Documents
1. Business plan or project proposal  
2. Team details and resumes  
3. Proof of concept or prototype (if available)  
4. Certificate of Incorporation or registration (for registered entities)  
5. PAN card of the entity or promoters  
6. Bank account details  
7. Any existing funding or investment details  
8. Intellectual property details (if applicable)
