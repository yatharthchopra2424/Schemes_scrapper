## Pricing Principles
1. **Value-Based Pricing**: Fees are aligned with the grant value (up to ₹50 lakhs) and the strategic support provided for early-stage biotech startups.
2. **Risk Sharing**: Success fees reflect the competitive nature of the BIG grant, with approval rates influenced by proposal quality and due diligence requirements.
3. **Transparency**: All fees are disclosed upfront; retainers cover initial eligibility assessment and proposal preparation.
4. **Scheme-Specific Note**: The BIG grant has a moderate risk profile due to its 18-month milestone-based disbursement and rigorous technical due diligence by BIG Partners. Success is contingent on scientific validity, commercialization potential, and compliance with BIRAC’s eligibility criteria, including in-house R&D facilities and promoter shareholding rules. Processing time (typically 3–6 months from application to approval) necessitates a revenue lag in the model.
