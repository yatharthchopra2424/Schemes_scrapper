# Pitch & Sales Scripts

## Philosophy
We are advisors, not salespeople. We diagnose first. We only pitch a scheme if it fits the client. We never oversell success rates.

---

## 1. Discovery Call Script (First Contact — 20 min)

### Opening (2 min)
Good morning/afternoon. I specialise in helping biotech startups and MSMEs access non-dilutive government grants for early-stage innovation. The SBIRI scheme from the Department of Biotechnology is designed specifically for ventures like yours working on proof-of-concept or prototype development in biotechnology. I’d like to understand your current project stage and see if this could be a fit—no obligation, just a diagnostic conversation.

### Discovery Questions
1. When was your startup or MSME incorporated under the Companies Act, 2013 or LLP Act, 2008?
2. Are you currently working on a biotechnology-based innovation requiring proof-of-concept or prototype development?
3. Is your technology focused on early-stage validation with potential for commercialization?
4. What is the estimated budget you need for your proof-of-concept or prototype phase?
5. Are you seeking funding that does not require equity dilution?
6. Have you already applied for or received funding from other government schemes for this same project?
7. Do you have a project proposal detailing objectives, methodology, and expected outcomes ready?
8. Are you prepared to submit periodic progress reports and utilization certificates if funded?

### Qualification Checklist (internal — don't read aloud)
- [ ] Indian startup or MSME registered under Companies Act, 2013 or LLP Act, 2008  
- [ ] Operating in the biotechnology sector  
- [ ] Working on innovative idea requiring proof-of-concept or prototype development  
- [ ] Legal entity registered in India  
- [ ] Technology has potential for commercialization  
- [ ] Not seeking funding for salaries, infrastructure, or working capital  
- [ ] Project timeline does not exceed 18 months  
- [ ] Willing to disclose IP generated to DBT/BIRAC  

### Transition to Pitch
Based on your responses, it sounds like your project aligns well with SBIRI’s focus on early-stage biotech validation. The scheme offers up to INR 50 lakhs in grant funding—no equity, no repayment—for proof-of-concept and prototype development. Let me walk you through how it works and whether you’d qualify.

## 2. Scheme Pitch (5 min)

### Structure: Problem → Scheme → Value → Proof → Next Step

**Problem framing:**
Biotech startups often struggle to bridge the gap between lab innovation and market-ready prototypes due to lack of early-stage, non-dilutive funding. Traditional investors hesitate at the proof-of-concept stage, and using personal funds or loans can strain cash flow and divert focus from R&D.

**Scheme intro:**
The SBIRI (Small Business Innovation Research Initiative) scheme, administered by the Biotechnology Industry Research Assistance Council (BIRAC) under the Department of Biotechnology (DBT), Ministry of Science & Technology, Government of India, provides grant-based financial support of up to INR 50 lakhs per project for proof-of-concept and prototype development. The funding is disbursed in milestones based on project progress and does not require equity dilution.

**Why they qualify (personalised):**

- Your work is in the biotechnology sector with an innovative idea requiring proof-of-concept or prototype development
