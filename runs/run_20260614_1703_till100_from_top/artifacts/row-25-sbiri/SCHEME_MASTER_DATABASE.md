### SBIRI
- **Scheme ID:** SCH-001
- **Category:** Grant
- **Geography:** Pan-India
- **Administering Body:** Department of Biotechnology (DBT), Ministry of Science & Technology, Government of India
- **Portal / Application URL:** https://www.birac.nic.in/
- **Current Status:** Rolling
- **Window:** Always Open (applications accepted throughout the year with periodic review cycles)

#### Eligibility Criteria
- Business type: Indian startups and MSMEs registered under the Companies Act, 2013 or LLP Act, 2008
- Age of business: Not specified
- Sector: Biotechnology
- Revenue cap: Not specified
- Employee count: Not specified
- Other: Applicant must be a legal entity registered in India; technology must have potential for commercialization; preference given to ventures with strong technical feasibility and market potential

#### What It Offers
- Amount / Benefit: Up to INR 50 lakhs per project
- Disbursement mode: Milestone-based disbursement
- Duration of benefit: Project-based (up to 18 months from date of sanction)

#### Documents Required
1. Project proposal detailing objectives, methodology, and expected outcomes
2. Incorporation/registration certificate of the startup/MSME
3. CVs of key team members
4. Proof of concept note or preliminary data (if available)
5. Bank account details
6. Authorization letter from the signatory
7. Declaration of no duplicate funding

#### Application Process (numbered steps)
1. Register on the BIRAC website (www.birac.nic.in)
2. Log in to the SBIRI application portal
3. Fill in the online application form with project details
4. Upload required documents including project proposal, CVs of team members, and incorporation certificate
5. Submit the application for review
6. Await evaluation by the expert committee
7. If shortlisted, attend interview/presentation
8. Receive award letter upon selection
9. Sign agreement with BIRAC
10. Receive first tranche of funds upon compliance
