## Pricing Principles
1. Value-based pricing aligned with the grant amount (up to ₹50 lakhs for AIR, no ceiling for CRS).
2. Success fee contingent on successful grant approval and disbursement.
3. Retainer covers eligibility assessment, proposal preparation, and submission support.
4. Scheme-specific note: Moderate risk profile due to 24-month project duration for AIR and undefined timeline for CRS; however, high success likelihood evidenced by structured evaluation process and regular bi-annual calls.
