## Pricing Principles
1. **Value Alignment**: Fees are structured to reflect the substantial financial and operational support provided under BioNEST, which targets large-scale bioincubation infrastructure projects requiring significant capital and operational investment.
2. **Risk Mitigation**: Success fees are set at the lower end of the range (2–4%) due to the scheme’s long processing time (initial funding period up to three years, with possible two-year extension) and dependency on institutional readiness, co-funding commitments, and BioNEST committee approval, which increases implementation risk.
3. **Market Competitiveness**: Retainer fees are benchmarked against similar infrastructure and incubation consulting engagements in the public sector, adjusted for the scheme’s complexity and multi-stakeholder coordination requirements.
4. **Scheme-Specific Note**: Given that BioNEST supports the establishment or scaling of bioincubation centers at academic/research institutes, standalone incubators, or with state government partnership — often involving project costs exceeding ₹10 Cr — the Enterprise tier is the only applicable tier. The scheme’s focus on capital and operational grant-in-aid support, with minimum applicant contributions (30% for private entities, 50% for state-government-owned Section 8 companies), confirms that engagements will involve large institutional clients.
