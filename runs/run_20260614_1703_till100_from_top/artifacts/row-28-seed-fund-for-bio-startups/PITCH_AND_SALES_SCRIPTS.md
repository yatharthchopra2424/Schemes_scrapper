# Pitch & Sales Scripts

## Philosophy
We are advisors, not salespeople. We diagnose first. We only pitch a scheme if it fits the client. We never oversell success rates.

---

## 1. Discovery Call Script (First Contact — 20 min)

### Opening (2 min)
Good morning/afternoon! I specialize in helping biotech startups access non-dilutive funding through government schemes like BIRAC’s Seed Fund for Bio Startups. This scheme is designed specifically for post-proof-of-concept biotech and life sciences startups that are ready to scale but need early-stage equity support to become angel/VC investment-ready. I’d love to learn about your startup’s journey and see if this could be a strategic fit for your growth plans.

### Discovery Questions
1. When was your startup incorporated under the Companies Act, 2013?
2. What percentage of your shareholders are Indian citizens (excluding OCI/PIO)?
3. Have you achieved proof-of-concept (PoC) for your biotech innovation or technology?
4. Are you currently receiving or have you previously received SEED or LEAP fund support from BIRAC?
5. What stage is your startup at — are you seeking funding to cover equipment, supplies, hiring, or other early-stage operational costs?
6. Are you looking for equity or equity-linked funding (not a loan or grant) to avoid immediate repayment pressure?
7. Do you plan to use the funding to reach a stage where you can raise angel/VC investment or seek bank loans?
8. Are you currently incubated at or planning to apply through a BIRAC-supported BioNEST incubator?

### Qualification Checklist (internal — don't read aloud)
- [ ] Startup incorporated under Companies Act, 2013  
- [ ] At least 51% shareholding held by Indian citizens (excluding OCI/PIO)  
- [ ] Proof-of-concept (PoC) achieved  
- [ ] Not previously funded under BIRAC SEED Fund  
- [ ] Not currently receiving concurrent SEED and LEAP fund support  
- [ ] Seeking equity or equity-linked funding up to INR 30 lakhs  
- [ ] Intends to use funds for early-stage costs (equipment, supplies, hiring)  
- [ ] Aims to become angel/VC investment-ready or bank loan eligible  
- [ ] Will apply through or is already associated with a BIRAC BioNEST incubator  

### Transition to Pitch
Based on your responses, it sounds like your startup aligns well with the goals of BIRAC’s Seed Fund for Bio Startups — particularly your PoC achievement, Indian shareholding, and need for early-stage capital to reach investment readiness. This scheme offers up to INR 30 lakhs in equity or equity-linked funding through BioNEST incubators, with no repayment obligation and structured support to help you bridge to angel/VC funding. Would you like me to walk you through how it works?

## 2. Scheme Pitch (5 min)

### Structure: Problem → Scheme → Value → Proof → Next Step

**Problem framing:**
Many biotech startups struggle to cross the "Valley of Death" after proof-of-concept — they lack the capital to validate prototypes, hire key talent, or run essential trials, making it difficult to attract angel or venture capital despite having promising innovations.

**Scheme intro:**
The Seed Fund for Bio Startups, administered by the Biotechnology Industry Research Assistance Council (BIRAC), a Public Sector Enterprise under the Department of Biotechnology (DBT), Government of India, provides equity or equity-linked funding of up to INR 30 lakhs per startup. The grant-in-aid (INR 100–200 lakhs per cycle) is disbursed to selected BioNEST incubators, which then deploy the funds as investments in eligible startups. The funding must be utilized within 36 months from signing the SEED Fund Implementation Agreement (SIFA).

**Why they qualify (personalised):**
- Your startup is incorporated under the Companies Act, 2013  
- At least 51% of your shareholders are Indian citizens (excluding OCI/PIO)  
- You have achieved proof-of-concept (PoC) for your biotech innovation  
- You are seeking early-stage funding to cover costs like equipment, supplies, or hiring

We offer a free, no-obligation eligibility check. If you qualify, we’ll support you through the application process via a BioNEST incubator on a success-fee-only basis — you pay only if funding is approved.
