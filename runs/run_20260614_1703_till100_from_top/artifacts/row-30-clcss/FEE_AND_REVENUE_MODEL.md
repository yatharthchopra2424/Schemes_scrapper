## Pricing Principles
1. Value-based pricing aligned with the ₹15 lakh maximum subsidy benefit under CLCSS.
2. Transparent fee structure with retainer for advisory work and success fee contingent on subsidy approval.
3. Tiered pricing reflects client scale and complexity of technology upgradation projects.
4. Scheme-specific note: CLCSS has a moderate risk profile due to its 3–6 month processing time (from loan disbursement to subsidy release) and dependency on lending institution coordination; success is well-documented for eligible MSEs using approved technologies in the 51 sub-sectors.
