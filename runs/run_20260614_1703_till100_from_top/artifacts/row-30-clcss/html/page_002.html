Aspire to Enterprise - Loan Mela conducted at ni-msme under ATI Scheme of Ministry of MSME

  • 11 June 2026
  • Mr. Rupesh Kumar
  • 33
A Loan Mela was organized for trainees of self-employment programmes sponsored under the Assistance to Training Institutions (ATI) Scheme of the Ministry of MSME, Government of India. Representatives from 12 leading banks participated and interacted with 100+ aspiring entrepreneurs seeking financial assistance to start or scale up their businesses. Encouraged by the positive response, a larger-scale Loan Mela may be planned soon.
