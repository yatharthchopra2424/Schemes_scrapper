MSME Sustainable (ZED) Certification

MSME Sustainable (ZED) Certification is an extensive drive to create awareness amongst MSMEs about Zero Defect Zero Effect (ZED) practices and motivate and incentivise them for ZED Certification while also encouraging them to become MSME Champions.

IMPORTANT ANNOUNCEMENT

Beware of Fake Sites and unauthorized agencies or individuals. ZED Registration is Free of Cost and paperless. No online or offline agency, institution, assessor, or consultant is authorised to charge any fee or money for registration. The Fee for Certification or Handholding (Consultancy) is prescribed in the scheme guidelines. Payment if any, shall be made only through online mode as defined in the user manuals. About ZED

Addressing the nation on India's 68th Independence Day, Hon'ble Prime Minister Shri Narendra Modi urged the industry, especially the Micro, Small and Medium Enterprises (MSMEs) of India, to manufacture goods in the country with "zero defects" and to ensure that the goods have "zero effect" on the environment. "We should manufacture goods in such a way that they carry zero defect and that our exported goods are never returned to us. We should manufacture goods with zero effect that they should not have a negative impact on the environment".
