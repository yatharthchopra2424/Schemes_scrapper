# Pitch & Sales Scripts

## Philosophy
We are advisors, not salespeople. We diagnose first. We only pitch a scheme if it fits the client. We never oversell success rates.

---

## 1. Discovery Call Script (First Contact — 20 min)

### Opening (2 min)
Good morning/afternoon. I’m calling from [Your Firm Name], and we specialize in helping MSMEs navigate government support systems like the MSME Champions Scheme. This initiative by the Ministry of Micro, Small & Medium Enterprises is designed specifically to provide redressal, guidance, and handholding for businesses like yours—whether you're looking for support in finance, marketing, technology, or capacity building. I’d like to understand your current challenges and see if this scheme could be a fit.

### Discovery Questions
1. When was your business registered under Udyam, and what is your current classification (micro, small, or medium)?
2. Are you currently facing challenges in accessing finance, marketing support, technology upgrades, or skill development?
3. Have you previously sought guidance or redressal through government channels for MSME-related issues?
4. Are you located in a region where you’d benefit from support in one of the eleven regional languages offered via the Champions portal?
5. Are you looking for handholding in navigating government schemes, or do you need help with specific issues like delayed payments or market access?
6. Have you used the Champions portal before, or are you aware of the 69 State control rooms that provide localized support?
7. Are you interested in capacity-building programs or training initiatives facilitated through the Champions platform?
8. Do you currently have access to the information and guidance you need to grow your business, or are you seeking structured support?

### Qualification Checklist (internal — don't read aloud)
- [ ] Business is a registered MSME (Udyam registered)
- [ ] Business operates in India (Pan-India scheme)
- [ ] Seeking support in finance, marketing, technology, or capacity building
- [ ] Interested in redressal, guidance, or handholding services
- [ ] Willing to access the Champions portal via msme.gov.in
- [ ] Open to receiving information in one of eleven regional languages
- [ ] Not currently receiving adequate handholding from existing channels
- [ ] Business is active and seeking growth or stabilization

### Transition to Pitch
Based on what you’ve shared, it sounds like the MSME Champions Scheme could be a valuable resource for you—especially since it offers free, structured guidance and handholding through the Ministry’s portal. Let me walk you through how it works and how we’ve helped similar MSMEs benefit from it.

## 2. Scheme Pitch (5 min)

### Structure: Problem → Scheme → Value → Proof → Next Step

**Problem framing:**
Many MSMEs struggle to access timely guidance, redressal for grievances, and structured support in areas like finance, marketing, and technology—especially when navigating complex government schemes or facing operational hurdles without expert handholding.

**Scheme intro:**
The MSME Champions Scheme, administered by the Ministry of Micro, Small & Medium Enterprises, is a Pan-India initiative that provides redressal, guidance, and handholding to MSMEs through the Champions portal (https://msme.gov.in). It does not offer direct financial support but facilitates access to finance and other resources via 69 State control rooms, delivering information in eleven regional languages.

**Why they qualify (personalised):**
- You are a registered MSME (Udyam certified), making you eligible under the scheme’s core criteria  
- You sought support in finance, marketing, technology, or capacity building—key benefit areas of the Champions portal  
- You are interested in redressal and handholding services, which the scheme provides via State control rooms  
- You operate in India, ensuring access to the Pan-India network of support centers
