# Pitch & Sales Scripts

## Philosophy
We are advisors, not salespeople. We diagnose first. We only pitch a scheme if it fits the client. We never oversell success rates.

---

## 1. Discovery Call Script (First Contact — 20 min)

### Opening (2 min)
Good morning/afternoon. I’m calling from [Your Firm Name], and we specialize in helping innovative startups and MSMEs access government support schemes like the MSME Incubation Scheme. This scheme is designed specifically for technology-driven ideas with scalability potential—whether you're an individual entrepreneur, a registered startup, or an MSME looking to validate and scale your innovation. I’d love to understand your current stage and see if this could be a fit.

### Discovery Questions
1. What is the nature of your business idea or innovation, and is it technology-driven?
2. Are you currently registered as an MSME, or are you in the process of obtaining Udyam registration?
3. How long has your venture been operational, or when was it incorporated?
4. What stage is your idea at—idea validation, prototype development, or ready for market testing?
5. Do you have a detailed project proposal outlining your innovation, market potential, and financial plan?
6. Are you seeking support for mentorship, infrastructure access, prototype development, or IPR filing assistance?
7. Does your idea have potential for scalability and employment generation?
8. Are you located in an aspirational district, or do you belong to a priority group such as SC/ST, women, or differently-abled individuals?

### Qualification Checklist (internal — don't read aloud)
- [ ] Applicant is an individual, startup, or MSME
- [ ] Business idea is innovative and technology-driven
- [ ] Idea has potential for scalability and employment generation
- [ ] Applicant is registered as MSME or in process of registration
- [ ] Idea is novel, feasible, and capable of generating economic value
- [ ] Preference may be given to SC/ST, women, differently-abled, or aspirational districts (if applicable)
- [ ] Idea is not pure trading or service-only without innovation
- [ ] Applicant has or can prepare a project proposal, identity proof, MSME registration proof, idea details, financial plan, promoter background, and any existing IP

### Transition to Pitch
Based on what you’ve shared, your innovative, technology-driven idea and your current stage align well with the MSME Incubation Scheme. This scheme provides grant-in-aid support through approved incubators for activities like mentorship, infrastructure use, and prototype development—exactly what you’re looking to advance.

## 2. Scheme Pitch (5 min)

### Structure: Problem → Scheme → Value → Proof → Next Step

**Problem framing:**
Early-stage innovators often struggle to access mentorship, infrastructure, and funding needed to validate their ideas and build prototypes, especially without collateral or extensive financial history.

**Scheme intro:**
The MSME Incubation Scheme, administered by the Ministry of Micro, Small and Medium Enterprises, provides grant-in-aid to approved incubators who then support incubatees with financial assistance for mentorship, infrastructure usage, prototype development, and other pre-incubation and incubation activities. The quantum of support is determined based on the project proposal and stage of the idea, with a focus on early validation and proof-of-concept.

**Why they qualify (personalised):**
- You have an innovative, technology-driven business idea with scalability potential  
- You are either already registered as an MSME or in the process of registration  
- Your idea is novel, feasible, and capable of generating employment and economic value  
- [If applicable] You belong to a priority group such as SC/ST, women, differently-abled, or are from an aspirational district

Many innovators have successfully accessed support for prototype development and market linkage through this route. |
| "How much do you charge?" | [TO BE FILLED BY CONSULTANT] |
| "I'll try it myself first" | Common mistakes include submitting incomplete project proposals (missing financial plans or promoter background) and applying without first identifying an approved incubator—both of which lead to delays or rejection. |
| "We're too small / too big" | The scheme is open to individuals, startups, and MSMEs—there’s no strict revenue or employee threshold. Eligibility hinges on having an innovative, technology-driven idea and being registered or in the process of registering as an MSME. |
| "I don't have the documents ready" | The three most commonly missing documents are: a detailed project proposal with innovation and market potential, proof of MSME registration or application, and a financial plan with budget estimate. |
| "We already work with a CA" | Great — we work alongside CAs. They handle your accounts; we handle the scheme strategy and applications. |
