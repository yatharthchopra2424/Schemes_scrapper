## Pricing Principles
1. Retainer covers initial eligibility assessment, documentation preparation, and portal registration support.
2. Success fee is contingent on fund disbursement, aligning consultant incentives with client outcomes.
3. Tiered pricing reflects client capacity to pay, with lower retainers for micro enterprises and customized fees for large entities.
4. Scheme-specific note: SFURTI has a moderate risk profile due to its 12–18 month processing time for Regular and Major Clusters respectively, and documented success in fund disbursement to Implementing Agencies through Nodal Agencies, reducing revenue lag risk.
