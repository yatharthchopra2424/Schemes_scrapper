### TREAD for Women
- **Scheme ID:** SCH-001
- **Category:** Grant
- **Geography:** Pan-India
- **Administering Body:** Ministry of Micro, Small and Medium Enterprises
- **Portal / Application URL:** https://msme.gov.in/tread
- **Current Status:** Rolling
- **Window:** Always Open

#### Eligibility Criteria
- Business type: Women entrepreneurs engaged in or planning to set up micro and small enterprises in the non-farm sector
- Age of business: Not specified (applicable to those planning to set up as well)
- Sector: Non-farm sector
- Revenue cap: Not specified
- Employee count: Not specified
- Other: Women entrepreneurs above 18 years of age; preference given to those from SC/ST, OBC, minority, and differently abled communities

#### What It Offers
- Amount / Benefit: Grant of up to 30% of the project cost, with a maximum ceiling of Rs. 10 lakhs per enterprise
- Disbursement mode: Installments (based on project progress and utilization)
- Duration of benefit: One-time grant disbursed in installments

#### Documents Required
1. Project report
2. Proof of identity (Aadhaar, PAN, etc.)
3. Address proof
4. Bank account details
5. Educational qualification certificates (if applicable)
6. Caste certificate (for SC/ST/OBC beneficiaries)
7. Disability certificate (if applicable)
8. Quotations for machinery and equipment
9. Land/building ownership or lease documents
10. No objection certificate from pollution control board (if applicable)

#### Application Process (numbered steps)
1. Identify a viable business idea and prepare a project report.
2. Approach the designated implementing agency (such as KVIC, Coir Board, or MSME-DFO).
3. Submit the application form along with project report and required documents.
4. Undergo evaluation and screening by the agency.
5. Upon approval, receive sanction letter and disbursement of grant in installments.
6. Utilize funds for setting up/expanding the enterprise as per the approved project plan.
7. Submit utilization reports and progress updates as required.
