5. **Retention:** Client data is retained for 2 years post case closure, unless longer retention is
   required by law or scheme guidelines.
6. **Breach Notification:** In the event of a data breach, affected parties and regulators will be
   notified within 24 hours.
7. **Scheme-specific note:** All documents submitted to Department of Agriculture, Cooperation & Farmers Welfare, Ministry of Agriculture and Farmers Welfare, Government of India under RKVY-RAFTAAR
   must be retained for a minimum of 3 years for audit purposes.
8. **Disposal:** After retention period, data is securely deleted using certified data destruction
