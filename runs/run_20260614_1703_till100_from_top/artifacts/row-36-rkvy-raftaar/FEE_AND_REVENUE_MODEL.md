## Pricing Principles
1. **Value-Aligned Fees**: Success fees are calibrated to the actual grant amount sanctioned under RKVY-RAFTAAR, ensuring clients pay only for realized value.
2. **Risk-Adjusted Pricing**: Given the scheme’s dependency on State Level Sanctioning Committee (SLSC) approvals and periodic state-level application windows, retainer fees reflect the effort required for DPR preparation and compliance with evolving state-specific guidelines.
3. **Tiered Accessibility**: The Growth tier is the primary applicability band, as RKVY-RAFTAAR explicitly targets agri-startups, agri-entrepreneurs, and MSMEs in agriculture and allied sectors — aligning with the ₹10L–₹1Cr grant range commonly sanctioned under the scheme.
4. **Transparency & Trust**: All fees are disclosed upfront; success fees are contingent on grant sanction, with no hidden charges. The scheme’s grant-based nature (non-repayable) reduces client financial risk, justifying success-based consulting fees.

> *Scheme-Specific Note*: Processing times for RKVY-RAFTAAR applications vary by state due to SLSC meeting cycles and fund availability. Evidence indicates approvals typically take 3–6 months from submission, introducing a revenue lag that must be factored into cash flow planning. Success is evidenced by regular grant disbursements to agri-startups and FPOs, though exact approval rates are not publicly aggregated — necessitating conservative success fee assumptions in early engagements.
