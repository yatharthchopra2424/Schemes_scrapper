# Pitch & Sales Scripts

## Philosophy
We are advisors, not salespeople. We diagnose first. We only pitch a scheme if it fits the client. We never oversell success rates.

---

## 1. Discovery Call Script (First Contact — 20 min)

### Opening (2 min)
Good morning/afternoon. I specialise in helping agri-startups, farmers, and agri-entrepreneurs access government grants for innovative agriculture projects. Many businesses in the agriculture and allied sectors miss out on funding like RKVY-RAFTAAR because they don’t know they qualify or how to apply. I’d like to understand your current agri-project or business to see if this scheme could support your goals — no obligation, just a diagnostic conversation.

### Discovery Questions
1. What is the nature of your agri-business or project (e.g., farming, processing, value addition, technology adoption)?
2. When was your entity incorporated or registered (for startups, FPOs, NGOs, cooperatives)?
3. Are you currently engaged in any innovative agri-project focused on value addition, post-harvest management, or market linkage?
4. What is your approximate annual turnover or scale of operations (to assess MSME eligibility)?
5. Do you have a Detailed Project Report (DPR) prepared, or would you need support in creating one?
6. Are you seeking financial support for equipment, machinery, or project implementation costs?
7. Have you previously applied for any government agriculture grants or schemes?
8. Are you open to co-funding or private investment as part of the project, as encouraged by the scheme?

### Qualification Checklist (internal — don't read aloud)
- [ ] Entity is an agri-startup, agri-entrepreneur, farmer, FPO, SHG, cooperative, NGO, or MSME
- [ ] Engaged in agriculture and allied sectors
- [ ] Project is innovative, feasible, and has potential for scalability and impact on farmers' income
- [ ] Project promotes value addition, post-harvest management, or market linkage (preferred)
- [ ] Not involved in pure trading activities or land purchase
- [ ] Willing to submit utilization certificates and progress reports
- [ ] Project timeline aligns with sanctioned DPR milestones
- [ ] Applicant has or can obtain: DPR, incorporation/registration certificate, PAN, Aadhaar of signatory, bank details, land documents (if applicable), equipment quotations, undertaking of compliance

### Transition to Pitch
Based on your responses, it sounds like your project in [mention specific area, e.g., value addition or post-harvest management] could align well with RKVY-RAFTAAR’s objectives. This scheme offers grant support for innovative agri-projects like yours, with funding sanctioned by the State Level Sanctioning Committee based on project merit. Let me explain how it works.

## 2. Scheme Pitch (5 min)

### Structure: Problem → Scheme → Value → Proof → Next Step

**Problem framing:**
Many agri-businesses struggle to scale innovative projects due to lack of upfront capital for technology, equipment, or processing units. Pure trading or land purchase isn’t funded, and without grant support, promising ideas in value addition or post-harvest management often stall at the idea stage.

**Scheme intro:**
RKVY-RAFTAAR (Rashtriya Krishi Vikas Yojana - Remunerative Approaches for Agriculture and Allied sector Rejuvenation) is a central government grant scheme administered by the Department of Agriculture, Cooperation & Farmers Welfare, Ministry of Agriculture and Farmers Welfare. It provides financial support as grants for innovative agri-projects, with amounts varying based on project proposal and sanctioned by the State Level Sanctioning Committee (SLSC). Funds are released in installments tied to milestones and utilization certificates.

**Why they qualify (personalised):**
- You are engaged in agriculture and allied sectors as an [agri-startup/farmer/FPO/MSME, etc.]
- Your project focuses on [value addition/post-harvest management/market linkage/technology adoption], which is preferred under the scheme
- Your entity is eligible to apply (based on incorporation, PAN, Aadhaar, and bank details readiness)
- The project is innovative and feasible, with potential to impact farmers' income and scalability

We offer a free, no-obligation eligibility check and document review. If you’re eligible, we support end-to-end application — from DPR preparation to submission and follow-up — on a success-fee-only basis. You pay only if the grant is approved.
