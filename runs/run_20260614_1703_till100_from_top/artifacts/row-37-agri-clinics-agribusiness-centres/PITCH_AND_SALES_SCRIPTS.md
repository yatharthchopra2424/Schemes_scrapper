# Pitch & Sales Scripts

## Philosophy
We are advisors, not salespeople. We diagnose first. We only pitch a scheme if it fits the client. We never oversell success rates.

---

## 1. Discovery Call Script (First Contact — 20 min)

### Opening (2 min)
Good morning/afternoon. I specialise in helping agripreneurs and agricultural startups access government support designed specifically for setting up agri-clinics and agribusiness centres. Many professionals in agriculture and allied sectors aren’t aware of this NABARD-backed scheme that provides training and financial linkage support. I’d like to understand your background and goals to see if this could be a fit for you.

### Discovery Questions
- What is your educational or professional background in agriculture, veterinary science, horticulture, or allied fields?
- Are you looking to establish an agri-clinic or agribusiness centre to provide advisory services to farmers?
- Have you completed or are you planning to undergo the mandatory training under this scheme?
- What is your current stage — are you in the ideation phase, or have you already taken steps toward setting up the centre?
- Do you have a clear idea of the services you intend to offer (e.g., soil testing, crop advice, input linkages)?
- Are you based in a rural or semi-urban area where farmer outreach is feasible?
- Are you seeking financial support through bank linkage for setting up your agribusiness centre?
- What is your timeline for launching the agri-clinic or agribusiness centre?

### Qualification Checklist (internal — don't read aloud)
- [ ] Applicant is a graduate in agriculture, veterinary science, horticulture, sericulture, agricultural engineering, fisheries, dairy technology, or allied subjects  
- [ ] Applicant is willing to undergo the mandatory training under the scheme  
- [ ] Applicant intends to set up an agri-clinic or agribusiness centre for providing paid advisory services to farmers  
- [ ] Applicant is an individual entrepreneur (not a company, NGO, or institution)  
- [ ] Applicant is an Indian citizen  
- [ ] Applicant has not previously availed benefits under this scheme  

### Transition to Pitch
Based on your responses, it sounds like you’re aiming to launch a professional agri-advisory service — which is exactly what the **Agri Clinics & Agribusiness Centres** scheme supports. This NABARD-implemented scheme offers training and facilitates bank loans for setting up such centres. Let me walk you through how it works and whether you qualify.

## 2. Scheme Pitch (5 min)

### Structure: Problem → Scheme → Value → Proof → Next Step

**Problem framing:**
Many agriculture graduates possess technical knowledge but struggle to convert it into a sustainable livelihood due to lack of entrepreneurial training, access to finance, and guidance on establishing a service-based agribusiness. Farmers, in turn, face gaps in timely, reliable advisory services at the village level.

**Scheme intro:**
The **Agri Clinics & Agribusiness Centres** scheme, implemented by the National Bank for Agriculture and Rural Development (NABARD), provides free training to eligible agriculture graduates and facilitates linkage with banks for credit support to establish agri-clinics or agribusiness centres that offer paid advisory services to farmers.

**Why they qualify (personalised):**
- You hold a qualifying degree in agriculture or allied sciences (as per scheme guidelines)  
- You are planning to set up a centre to provide fee-based advisory services to farmers  
- You are willing to undergo the mandatory training component of the scheme  
- You are an individual entrepreneur seeking to launch this venture independently
