## 8D. Internal Data Handling Policy

(Standard data handling policy — encrypted cloud storage, 2FA, limited access, no personal devices,
2-year retention post case closure, 24-hour breach notification.)

All client data shall be handled in accordance with the following standards:

- **Storage:** Encrypted cloud storage (AES-256) with access controls.
- **Authentication:** Two-factor authentication (2FA) required for all system access.
- **Access Control:** Role-based access; limited to authorized personnel only.
- **Device Policy:** No personal devices permitted for accessing or storing client data.
- **Retention:** Client data retained for 2 years post case closure, then securely deleted.
- **Breach Notification:** Any suspected or confirmed data breach must be reported internally
  within 24 hours; affected clients notified per applicable data protection laws.

**Scheme-specific note:** All documents submitted to NABARD under NABARD Startup & Innovation Fund
must be retained for a minimum of 3 years for audit purposes. (Note: As no specific retention
period was stated in the evidence for this scheme, the default 3-year audit retention period
is applied as a prudent standard for government scheme documentation.)
