## Pricing Principles
1. Value-based pricing aligned with the transformative impact of digital agriculture solutions on farm productivity and policy decision-making.
2. Tiered retainers reflect scalability of service delivery, from startup advisory to statewide system implementation.
3. Success fees tied to measurable outcomes such as fund disbursement, pilot approval, or infrastructure deployment.
4. Scheme-specific note: The Digital Agriculture Mission has a moderate risk profile due to its 12–24 month project lifecycle (evidence: stipulated time of 1 year, extendable up to 2 years) and dependency on state-level execution and PFMS reporting. Success is evidenced by multiple state DPR approvals (Puducherry, Odisha, Telangana, etc.) and central cabinet approval of ₹2817 Cr outlay.

## Invoice Templates

### Retainer Invoice
**Description:** Retainer for consulting services under the Digital Agriculture Mission (Scheme ID: row-39) to support proposal preparation, DPR development, and technology advisory for states/UTs, agri-startups, or research institutions seeking funding for digital crop survey, AI/ML solutions, or blockchain-based traceability.

### Success Fee Invoice
**Description:** Success fee for securing approval and fund release under the Digital Agriculture Mission implemented by the Department of Agriculture & Farmers Welfare (DA&FW), Ministry of Agriculture & Farmers Welfare, upon successful sanction of the client’s proposal and release of the first tranche of funds via PFMS.
